2. **Disagreement with codex**: If codex was strongly positive but you see

   recovery path? Cheap recovery (git revert, restart inner) tilts

for routine actions on a degraded pipeline. The cost of one missed
optimization is much smaller than the cost of one autonomous bad edit.

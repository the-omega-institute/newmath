The direction is to deepen biological reasoning from the DNA-to-protein ladder
while keeping each realization layer explicit. Curated genetic-code tables can
support code readback. Internal codon-window geometry can support coordinate,
closure, spectrum, and relation probes. Neither source by itself licenses a
claim about translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility,
or biological function.

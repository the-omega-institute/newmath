\subsection{Cross-Organism CUN/UUR Leucine Gate}
\label{sec:cross-organism-cun-uur-leu-gate-translation-realization}

The carrier is the organism-indexed residual comparing the CUN leucine block with the UUR leucine block, together with an organism label, a tRNA-Leu copy-number ratio, and an amino-acid-composition control. The packet therefore treats codon usage and tRNA-gene annotation as paired finite coordinates, not as a direct observation of translation kinetics.

\paragraph{Interpretation.}
The packet should be read as a cross-organism usage-and-copy-number contact, not as a mechanism.  The positive finite statistic says that the organism label matters for the CUN/UUR leucine split in the available rows, and that the recorded tRNA-Leu copy-number coordinate tracks that split under the supplied control. \paragraph{Local scope boundary.}

\subsection{Window-six local tile boundary for standard-code readback}
\label{subsec:codon-window-six-local-tile-boundary}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}

\paragraph{Shape and null checks.}
The shape comparison was run with $N=2000$ sampled alternatives and seed $42$. Among $57377$ distinct-sample attempts, $55377$ were duplicate retries. The exact-shape count was $1$, giving $p_{exact}=0.0005$, and the high-Jaccard count at threshold $0.9$ was $25$, giving $p_{J\geq 0.9}=0.0125$. These numbers support a local finite-row anomaly relative to the sampled null families. They do not assert that the anomaly is a global rule of biology.

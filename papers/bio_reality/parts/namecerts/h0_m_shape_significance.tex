\paragraph{Shape significance.}
The local shape test uses $N=2000$ sampled alternatives with seed $42$.  Because distinct samples were required, the run made $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries.  The observed shape appeared exactly once among the $2000$ samples, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
\#\{\hbox{exact shape matches}\}&=1, & p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005.
\end{aligned}
$$
A high-overlap relaxation at Jaccard threshold $0.9$ found $25$ matches, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
\#\{J\geq 0.9\}&=25, & p_{J\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
Both reported significance scalars are the same as the corresponding empirical values: $p_{\mathrm{value,exact}}=0.0005$ and $p_{\mathrm{value,high\ Jaccard}}=0.0125$.  In an amino-acid-class-preserving null model with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, the observed distinguished tile has size $|M|=20$.  That null run records zero exact shape matches, so $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,exact}}=0.0$, and it records $7$ samples in the lower tail at or below the observed $M$-size, giving $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,lower\ tail}}=0.0035$.  The supplied finite row therefore supports a code-layer statement: the observed local tile shape is rare relative to the sampled nulls, including a label-class-preserving null.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The verified finite rows do not promote the local tile into a global biological law.  Window-six geometry alone is not translation, not folded protein structure, not structural order, not physical admissibility, not biological function, not mechanism, and not universality.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback remains a code-layer observation unless a separate higher-layer reality contact is supplied.  In particular, the curated standard-code table is a bridge for codon-to-label assignments; it is not an experiment observing ribosomal translation dynamics, protein folding, viable molecular structure, or organismal function.  The correct verdict for this row is therefore a bounded positive one: the window-six codon coordinates expose a statistically rare local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table, while the table itself blocks any promotion beyond code-read contact.

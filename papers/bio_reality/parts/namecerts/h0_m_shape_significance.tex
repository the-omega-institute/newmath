\paragraph{Claim and carrier.}
This record treats the standard-code codon table as a finite external contact with a six-bit codon display, not as an internally generated biological mechanism. The BEDC carrier is the codon cube $Q_6$, read through local tile faces, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes. The observed biological object is the curated standard-code label table: each codon is assigned an amino-acid or stop label. The claim is deliberately restricted to the code-read layer. Window-six coordinates may expose a local boundary-tile pattern, but the curated labels remain external reality contacts.

\paragraph{Shape significance.}
The shape-significance run used seed $42$ and $N=2000$ sampled comparators. It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and encountered $55377$ duplicate retries. Exactly one sampled comparator matched the observed shape, while $25$ comparators had Jaccard overlap at least $0.9$. The reported finite-sample tail values are
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{J\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same values are recorded as the exact-shape and high-Jaccard significance values. These numbers support a narrow finite-row statement: the observed local tile is uncommon under the sampled comparison family. They do not by themselves promote the tile to a law of biology.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class preserving null.}
A separate amino-acid-class preserving null used the same seed $42$ and $N=2000$. The observed $M$-size was $20$. The exact-shape count was $0$, giving reported exact-shape value $0.0$ over the sampled run, while the lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$-size was $7$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{class\ exact}}&=0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{class\ lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
This second null is stronger for scope control because it preserves label-class information while still finding the observed local form rare in the sampled population. The statement remains empirical and finite: it is a row-level comparison against sampled alternatives, not an exhaustive theorem about all possible genetic codes.

\paragraph{Reality contact and cannot-claim boundary.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code table. It can test codon-label readback and the local codon-tile label split. It cannot test translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, function realization, universal mechanism, or biological necessity. Therefore no translation, no folding, no physical admissibility, no biological function, no mechanism, and no universality follows from this chapter unless a separate contact supplies that higher layer. Amino-acid label-sequence readback is still only label readback. A local codon tile is not a global biological law, and window-six geometry alone is not translation, structure, admissibility, or function.

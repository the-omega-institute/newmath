It does not supply translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. The BEDC-side distinctions used here are the six-bit codon display, a local tile face, a singleton label corner, and a three-point label class. \paragraph{Shape significance against finite null samples.} The exact-shape test used $N=2000$ sampled comparison rows with seed $42$. The sampler required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and encountered $55377$ duplicate retries, indicating that the finite sampling space was repeatedly revisiting already-seen candidates. $$\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{J\ge 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}$$
Both are treated here as code-read evidence for a non-generic local tile pattern in the curated standard-code table. They are not treated as evidence for a global biological law.

\subsection{Window-Six Codon Tile Shape Significance}\label{sec:h0-m-shape-significance}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback.}
This BioReality record treats the standard-code codon table as a finite code-read contact on the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$.  The carrier is not a mechanism for translation; it is the finite display on which codon labels can be read back from a curated standard genetic-code table.  The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$ and is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is recorded as a punctured-cube object with face vector $(13,16,4)$.  Its module partition is complete: the $\mathrm{agr}$ module is $\{\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG}\}$, the $\mathrm{ar\_lys}$ module is $\{\mathrm{AAA}\}$, the $\mathrm{cun}$ module is $\{\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU}\}$, the $\mathrm{ile\_met}$ module is $\{\mathrm{AUA}\}$, the $\mathrm{ssn}$ module is $\{\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UUA}\}$, and the $\mathrm{stop\_trp}$ module is $\{\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UGA}\}$.  The classification reports $R\_\mathrm{size}=13$, assigned count $13$, no extra module codons, no missing module codons, and no unassigned codons.

\paragraph{Observed shape row and randomization scale.}
The tested shape row is the observed $M$-row of size $20$.  In the finite randomization audit with seed $42$, $N=2000$ distinct samples were evaluated after $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries.  The exact-shape count was $1$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}} &= 0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9} &= 0.0125,
\end{aligned}
$$
with $25$ samples at Jaccard similarity at least $0.9$.  Thus the observed window-six shape is not being reported merely as a visible tile; it is being reported as a rare finite row under the stated sampling scheme.  The companion amino-acid-class-preserving null used the same sample size $N=2000$ and seed $42$.  Under that null, the exact-match count was $0$, the lower-tail count at or below observed $M$-size $20$ was $7$, and the recorded probabilities were
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}} &= 0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}} &= 0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
The exact-shape and high-Jaccard finite-row statistics therefore support a code-layer shape claim, not a biochemical law.

\paragraph{Leave-one contacts.}
Two finite leave-one audits test whether the local tile is stable as a code-read predictor.  In the leave-one-code-out audit, $23$ tables were evaluated across $63$ total targets.  The baseline hit rate was $0.19821739130434785$, whereas the hit rate above baseline was $0.9841269841269841$.  The hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ was $0.9365079365079365$, and the hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ plus boundary was $0.9841269841269841$.  By family, the hit rates above baseline were $0.8333333333333334$ for alternative yeast, $1.0$ for bacterial plastid, $1.0$ for candidate other, $1.0$ for mitochondrial, and $1.0$ for nuclear ciliate, over $5$ families.  In the leave-one-codon-out audit, the anchor count was $0$, boundary count $5$, predictable count $8$, high-closure coverage $8$, and recovery rate $1.0$.  These rows support local codon-label recovery and boundary placement within the code-read layer.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The reality contact is the curated standard-code label table.  It observes that codons are assigned to amino-acid or stop labels, and it has codon-label readback resolution.  It cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, function realization, folded protein structure, or any universal biological mechanism.  The supported statement is therefore narrow: window-six coordinates expose a statistically rare local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table, with a $13$-codon punctured-cube boundary, an observed $M$-row of size $20$, rare exact and high-Jaccard randomized matches, and stable leave-one recovery.  The blocked statement is equally local: no translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality follows unless a separate higher-layer contact supplies that evidence.

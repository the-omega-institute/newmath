\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}

The finite carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read through a curated standard-code table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  The BEDC distinction stack used here is finite and local: six-bit codon display, local tile face, singleton label corner, and three-point label class.  The relevant internal structure is coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation.  The contact does not observe translation, protein folding, physical admissibility, biological function, a biochemical mechanism, or universality; it observes only codon-label readback and local split checks.

\paragraph{Shape significance.}
The main significance run for $M$ used seed $42$ and sampled $N=2000$ distinct null draws, requiring $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries.  The observed shape appeared exactly once among the $2000$ draws, while $25$ draws reached Jaccard similarity at least $0.9$.  The resulting finite-row estimates are
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
These numbers support a narrow claim: the observed $M$-shape is unusually specific under this finite sampling protocol.  They do not identify a mechanism.  They also do not promote the local codon tile beyond code-read geometry.  The row records shape significance for the displayed coordinate-and-label construction, with the curated table acting as the external readback bridge.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class preserving null.}
A second null model preserves amino-acid class structure.  In that run, again with seed $42$ and $N=2000$, the observed $M$ size was $20$.  The exact-shape count was $0$, and the lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$ size was $7$.  The finite-row probabilities were
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}}&=0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
The amino-acid-class preserving run therefore strengthens the local code-read anomaly: even after preserving a label-class constraint, the exact observed shape was not recovered in $2000$ samples, and only $7$ samples reached the observed-or-smaller size threshold.  This is still a statement about the table-shaped coordinate row, not a statement about translation dynamics or protein realization.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}

\paragraph{Monte Carlo shape significance.}

The exact shape was matched once, and a high-overlap Jaccard threshold $\geq 0.9$ was matched $25$ times.  The resulting empirical probabilities are
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The chapter therefore records a significant finite-row coincidence at the code-read layer: the observed local tile shape is rare under this sampling protocol.  The statement is deliberately finite and local.  It says that the window-six coordinates expose a boundary-tile pattern in the curated standard-code label table; it does not say that geometry alone generates translation, folding, admissible chemistry, or function.

\paragraph{Carrier and observational contact.}

The observation used here is intentionally narrow: a curated standard-code table assigns each codon to an amino-acid label or to a stop label.  The table is therefore a contact for label readback, local class comparison, and codon-set arithmetic.  It is not an observation of translation dynamics, folded molecular structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Any statement crossing those layers would require an independent contact of the corresponding kind.

Among the $2000$ alternatives, there was exactly $1$ exact shape match and $25$ samples with Jaccard similarity at least $0.9$.  The corresponding values are
$$
\begin{aligned}
N&=2000,\\
\#\{\text{exact shape matches}\}&=1,\\
\#\{\text{Jaccard}\geq 0.9\}&=25,\\
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.

$$
This is evidence that the observed local tile is rare under the sampled comparison class.  It does not say that the cube coordinate system causes the genetic code, nor that the local tile determines molecular function.

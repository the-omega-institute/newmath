$$
\begin{aligned}

p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9} &= 0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}

p_{\mathrm{high\ Jaccard\ significant}} &= 0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The evidence is therefore a finite combinatorial rarity statement for the observed local tile shape.  It does not say that the codon table had to arise from this geometry, and it does not promote the tile into a biological law.

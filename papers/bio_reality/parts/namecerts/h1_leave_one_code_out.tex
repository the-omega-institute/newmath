\subsection{Leave-One-Code-Out Boundary Test for the Window-Six Codon Tile}\label{sec:h1-leave-one-code-out}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{NameCert carrier.}
The finite carrier for this record is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a codon display with local tile faces, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes.  The biological contact is deliberately narrower than the carrier: the external row is a curated standard-code label table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  Thus the readback is a code-layer readback, not a translation experiment and not a structural or functional assay.  The tested claim is that the window-six coordinates expose a reproducible local boundary-tile signal around code-label changes while the labels themselves remain external reality contacts.

\paragraph{Boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  The verified row is
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is verified as a punctured-cube object with face-vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(R)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
The module partition accounts for every element of $R$ with no extras and no omissions: $\mathrm{AGR}=\{\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG}\}$, $\mathrm{AR/Lys}=\{\mathrm{AAA}\}$, $\mathrm{CUN}=\{\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU}\}$, $\mathrm{Ile/Met}=\{\mathrm{AUA}\}$, $\mathrm{SSN}=\{\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UUA}\}$, and $\mathrm{Stop/Trp}=\{\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UGA}\}$.  The classification certificate reports $R$-size $13$, assigned count $13$, empty extra-module codons, empty missing-from-modules rows, and empty unassigned codons.

\paragraph{Spectral and shape checks.}
The finite spectrum attached to the tile has measured values
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lambda_M&=0.6752479308830213,\\
\lambda_R&=0.4758352843197002,\\
\mu&=3.051487585298128.
\end{aligned}
$$
The cubic radial polynomial residual is $-6.224354365258478\times 10^{-12}$, so the finite numerical check is at numerical zero for this row.  The shape-significance run used $N=2000$ distinct sampled null rows, requiring $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries under seed $42$.  It found $1$ exact shape match and $25$ matches with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{J\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
A separate amino-acid-class-preserving null run, also with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, observed $M$-size $20$, exact-match count $0$, and lower-tail count $7$, yielding exact-shape probability $0.0$ and lower-tail probability $0.0035$.  These finite rows support a non-random local tile shape at the code-label layer; they do not by themselves identify a biochemical cause.

\paragraph{Leave-one-code-out row.}
The leave-one-code-out test evaluated $23$ code tables and $63$ total targets.  Its baseline hit rate was $0.19821739130434785$.  The observed hit rate above baseline was $0.9841269841269841$, the hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ was $0.9365079365079365$, and the hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ plus the boundary was $0.9841269841269841$.  Family-level rows preserve the same pattern across five families: alternative yeast gives $0.8333333333333334$, bacterial plastid gives $1.0$, candidate other gives $1.0$, mitochondrial gives $1.0$, and nuclear ciliate gives $1.0$.  The leave-one-codon-out companion row reports anchor count $0$, boundary count $5$, closure-coverage-high count $8$, predictable count $8$, and recovery rate $1.0$.

\paragraph{Interpretation.}
The finite evidence says that the codon cube coordinate system detects a stable local label-boundary pattern.  The strongest local statement is the following: when standard-code labels are held as the external contact, the window-six tile recovers nearly all leave-one-code-out targets relative to a baseline of about $0.1982$, and the boundary row $R$ forms a complete thirteen-codon punctured-cube module account.  This is a code-read claim.  It is not a claim that DNA is forced by this geometry, that the tile realizes translation, that amino-acid label sequences fold, that physical admissibility follows, that biological function follows, that a mechanism has been identified, or that a universal law has been established.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The curated standard-code table supplies codon-to-label assignments only.  No translation realization, folding contact, physical-admissibility contact, function assay, mechanism row, or universality witness is present in this finite packet.  Therefore any promotion from local codon tile to protein realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law is blocked unless a separate reality contact supplies that higher-layer observation.  The valid conclusion remains local and finite: $Q_6$ coordinates, the thirteen-codon row $R$, the measured spectrum, and the leave-one-code-out hit rows jointly certify a code-layer boundary pattern against the supplied null models.

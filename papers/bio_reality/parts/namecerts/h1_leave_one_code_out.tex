\paragraph{Finite carrier and reality contact.}

The external contact is the curated standard genetic-code table: it supplies codon-to-label assignments for amino-acid and stop labels, but it does not supply a translation mechanism, a folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, or universality.  Thus every numerical statement below is a statement about finite code-label readback inside a window-six coordinate presentation.

\paragraph{Local verdict and cannot-claim boundary.}
The ready claim is therefore a code-read claim: window-six codon coordinates expose a finite local boundary-tile structure in the curated standard-code label table.  The contact supports codon-label readback and local split checks.  It does not support the stronger assertions that translation is realized by this geometry, that folded protein structure follows from amino-acid label sequence, that the row is physically admissible, that it determines biological function, that it gives a universal mechanism, or that DNA obeys the coordinate geometry as a biological necessity.  Crossing any of those boundaries requires a separate reality contact at the relevant layer.

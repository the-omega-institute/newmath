\paragraph{Reality-contact boundary.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard-code table assigning codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  That contact can test code readback and the local codon tile label split.  It cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, folded protein structure, biological function, or a universal mechanism.  Therefore the admissible conclusion is deliberately narrow: window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure that is unusually stable under the stated leave-one-code-out and leave-one-codon-out finite probes, but the amino-acid labels remain external reality contacts supplied by the curated table.  No translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality follows unless a separate contact supplies that higher-layer evidence.

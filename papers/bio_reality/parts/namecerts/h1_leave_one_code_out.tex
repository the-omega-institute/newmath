\subsection{Leave-one-code-out recovery for the window-six codon tile}
\label{sec:bior-reality-h1-leave-one-code-out}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}
The carrier for this record is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as the finite set of codon displays over the standard four-letter alphabet. The reality contact used here is only the curated standard-code label table: it assigns each codon to an amino-acid label or to a stop label. This contact is a code-read bridge. It is not a translation experiment, not a protein-structure observation, not a physical-admissibility measurement, not a function assay, not a mechanism, and not a universality certificate.

\paragraph{Recovered boundary row.}
The finite boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$. In lexicographic codon notation the row is
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The punctured-cube audit returns the same row and gives the finite face data
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the local object being used by the probe is not the full codon table. It is a thirteen-codon boundary row with sixteen observed one-dimensional adjacencies and four observed two-dimensional faces inside the window-six display.

\paragraph{Null-model pressure.}
The shape-significance audit used $N=2000$ null samples with seed $42$. It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and incurred $55377$ duplicate retries. Among those samples there was $1$ exact shape match and $25$ matches with Jaccard score at least $0.9$. The resulting finite-row probabilities were
$$
\begin{aligned}
 p_{\mathrm{exact}} &= 0.0005,\\
 p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9} &= 0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
A second null audit preserving amino-acid class structure also used $N=2000$ and seed $42$. It reported observed $M$-size $20$, exact-match count $0$, and lower-tail count $7$ for rows no larger than the observed $M$-size. Its probabilities were
$$
\begin{aligned}
 p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}} &= 0.0,\\
 p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}} &= 0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
These values support the claim that the observed local row is not a routine output of the tested finite null models. They still remain code-layer evidence: the null models compare finite codon-label arrangements and do not supply translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.

\paragraph{Leave-one-code-out statistic.}
For the leave-one-code-out probe, $23$ genetic-code tables were evaluated and $63$ target changes were counted. The baseline hit rate was $0.19821739130434785$. The row-based recovery rate above baseline was
$$
\begin{aligned}
 h_{\mathrm{above\ baseline}} &= 0.9841269841269841.
\end{aligned}
$$
Inside $M\setminus T$ the hit rate was $0.9365079365079365$, while inside $M\setminus T$ together with the boundary it was again $0.9841269841269841$. The operative finite-row statement is therefore narrow: when one code table is withheld, the local window-six tile plus the boundary accounts for almost all recorded code-layer target changes in this finite audit, and it does so far above the listed baseline rate.

\paragraph{Local verdict.}
The finite-row verdict is that the window-six codon display exposes a strong local boundary-tile signal for standard-code label readback and leave-one-code-out recovery. The evidence combines the thirteen-codon row $R$, the punctured-cube face vector $(13,16,4)$, the spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$ and $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, null-model probabilities $0.0005$, $0.0125$, and $0.0035$, and a leave-one-code-out hit rate $0.9841269841269841$ above a $0.19821739130434785$ baseline. The result is deliberately bounded: it says that curated codon-label tables exhibit a local code-read structure in this finite audit. No translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows unless a separate higher-layer reality contact supplies that claim.

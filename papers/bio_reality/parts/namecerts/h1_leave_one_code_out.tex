\paragraph{Leave-one-code-out row.}

$$\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}$$
The hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ is
$$\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}$$

$$\begin{aligned}

The finite readback is therefore sharp: almost every target in the leave-one-code-out table is recovered by the local $M\setminus T$ row once the boundary contribution is admitted.  The result is a code-label readback statement only.  It says that the curated codon-label table and the window-six tile agree on a finite boundary pattern; it does not say that the coordinate system is the cause of the biological code.

\paragraph{Reality contact and boundary.}
The sole reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code bridge: a table assigning codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  This contact can test code-read behavior and a local codon-tile label split.  It cannot test translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or the claim that DNA must obey this geometry as biological necessity.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.

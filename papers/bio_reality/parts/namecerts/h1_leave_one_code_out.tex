\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}
The finite carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a codon display with local tile faces, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes.  The reality contact used here is only the curated standard-code table: it assigns each codon to an amino-acid label or to stop.  This contact is a code-layer readback.  It is not a contact with translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Thus the object of the row is the standard-code codon table as a finite label table, not protein structure and not a global biological law.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and label contact.}
The leave-one-codon-out packet treats the standard-code table as a finite code-read contact on the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$. The carrier is not a biochemical mechanism. It is a coordinate display whose finite distinctions are the six-bit codon display, a local tile face, a singleton label corner, and a three-point label class. The external contact is the curated standard genetic-code table: it assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels, and it supplies only codon-label readback. Thus the object under analysis is the standard-code label table as read through finite coordinates, closure, spectrum, and relation data.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The admissible conclusion is only a code-read conclusion. The curated table supports codon-label readback and local split checks; it does not observe translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure. A local codon tile is not a global biological law. Window-six geometry alone is not translation, structure, physical admissibility, function, or a universal mechanism. Any claim crossing those boundaries requires a separate higher-layer reality contact, not merely the finite codon-table contact used here.

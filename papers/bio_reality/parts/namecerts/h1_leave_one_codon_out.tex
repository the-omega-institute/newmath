\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}
This record treats the standard-code codon table as a finite code-read contact on the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$.  The carrier is not a model of translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality.  It is only a finite coordinate display whose external contact is the curated standard genetic-code table: each codon receives an amino-acid or stop label, and the only readback claimed here is codon-label readback.  The local tile under study is the punctured-cube set
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The row count is $|R|=13$, and the finite face data give the $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$.  Thus the code-read object is small enough to state without asymptotic language: the contact is a thirteen-codon local region inside $Q_6$, with labels supplied by the curated table rather than by the geometry itself.

\paragraph{Leave-one-codon test.}
The leave-one-codon probe removes one codon from the local tile and asks whether the remaining finite closure data recover the held-out codon as either predictable or boundary-supported.  In the recorded run the anchor count is $0$, the boundary count is $5$, the high-coverage closure count is $8$, and the predictable count is $8$.  The recovery rate is therefore
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{recovery\ rate}=1.0,
\qquad
\mathrm{recovery\ rate\ above\ threshold}=1.0.
\end{aligned}
$$
This is a code-layer statement.  It says that, for this finite tile and this curated label table, every held-out codon in the tested local row is recovered by the local closure-or-boundary criterion.  It does not say that the biological system must realize this geometry, and it does not promote a codon-label split into a claim about protein structure or function.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The accepted conclusion is narrow.  Window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile pattern in which the thirteen-codon punctured cube is recovered under leave-one-codon removal with recovery rate $1.0$, and its six-module accounting is complete.  The curated standard-code table supplies the labels needed for readback.  No translation realization follows from this.  No folded protein structure follows from this.  No physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or law of DNA geometry follows from this.  Any crossing from code-read structure to translation, folding, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact whose data actually live at that layer.

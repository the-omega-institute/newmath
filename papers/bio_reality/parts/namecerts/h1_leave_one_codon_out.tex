\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
The carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$ equipped with the standard-code label table as an external readback contact.  The internal BEDC side supplies only coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation data on the codon display; the amino-acid and stop labels are not generated internally.  They enter through the curated standard-code table, whose resolution is codon-label readback.  Thus the object under test is a code-layer boundary phenomenon: a local tile face with singleton label corners and three-point label classes, not a biological mechanism.

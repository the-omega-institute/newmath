\paragraph{Local biological reading.}
The admissible reading is narrow.  The curated standard-code table supplies the contact that lets the finite codon points be read as amino-acid or stop labels.  The packet may therefore say that window-six coordinates expose a local boundary-tile pattern in the standard-code label table, and that leave-one-codon recovery is complete in the supplied finite row.  It may not say that the geometry is a biological law, a translation mechanism, a folded-protein structure, a physical-admissibility criterion, a functional assay, or a universal mechanism.  Amino-acid label readback is still label readback.  Any claim about folding, structure, biochemical feasibility, organism-level function, or translation realization would require a separate contact measuring that layer directly.  The present packet is consequently a finite code-layer result: strong local recovery and module coverage inside the standard-code label table, with all higher biological layers explicitly left outside the certified scope.

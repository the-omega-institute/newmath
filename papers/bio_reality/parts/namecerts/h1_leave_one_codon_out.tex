\paragraph{Local verdict and boundary.}
The claim supported here is that window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure for the standard-code label table.  The support condition is narrow: the curated table supplies codon-to-label assignments, and the coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation rows organize those assignments into the finite pattern above.  The data do not show translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or biological necessity.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is therefore not folded protein structure, and a local codon tile is not a global biological law.  Any future claim crossing the code-read boundary would require a separate contact that directly observes the higher layer being claimed.

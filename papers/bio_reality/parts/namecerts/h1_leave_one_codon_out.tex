\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}

The only external contact used in this record is the curated standard-code table, which assigns each codon to an amino-acid label or to stop.  This contact is a code-layer readback bridge.  It supplies labels for finite rows; it does not supply translation dynamics, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.

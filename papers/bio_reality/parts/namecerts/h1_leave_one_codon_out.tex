\subsection{Leave-one-codon-out boundary recovery}
\label{subsec:h1-leave-one-codon-out}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback.}
The finite object in this record is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$ equipped with the standard-code label readback.  Each codon is treated as a six-coordinate display, with a local tile face, a singleton label corner, and a three-point label class available as internal distinctions.  The biological contact used here is only the curated standard-code table: it assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels, and it does not by itself observe translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  The local claim is therefore a code-layer claim: when one codon is left out, the window-six coordinate system recovers a boundary or predictable placement for that held-out codon relative to the finite tile geometry.

The finite row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  In the verified standard-code readback it is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\

The same row is reported as a punctured-cube pattern with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$.  Thus the finite observation is not merely a list of labels: it carries a local combinatorial boundary shape with $13$ vertices, $16$ one-dimensional adjacencies, and $4$ two-dimensional faces in the induced punctured-cube account.  The chapter uses this row as a finite witness for the leave-one-codon-out test, not as a global biological law.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The contact is the curated standard-code table, whose resolution is codon label readback.  Consequently the supported statement is only this: window-six coordinates expose a local boundary-tile pattern in which the $13$-codon row is completely classified by modules and the held-out codon is recovered through predictable-or-boundary placement at rate $1.0$.  The unsupported promotions are explicit.  No translation process is observed.  No folded protein structure is observed.  No physical admissibility constraint is measured.  No biological function is inferred.  No universal mechanism is established.  Any such higher-layer claim requires a separate reality contact with its own finite rows, measurement protocol, and mismatch audit.

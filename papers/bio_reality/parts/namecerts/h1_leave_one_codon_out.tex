\paragraph{Reality contact and scope.}
The only empirical contact used here is a curated standard-code table assigning codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  That contact is adequate for checking code-layer label readback and local split behavior.  It is not an observation of ribosomal translation, molecular folding, thermodynamic admissibility, organismal function, or a universal generative law.  Therefore the supported conclusion is local and finite: in the standard-code label table, the thirteen-codon residual pattern in $Q_6$ has a punctured-cube form, nonrandom shape statistics under the supplied null rows, complete module coverage, and full leave-one-codon recovery by predictable-or-boundary status.  Any move from this code-layer statement to a claim about translation, structure, physics, function, mechanism, or universality would require a separate contact that is not present in this row.

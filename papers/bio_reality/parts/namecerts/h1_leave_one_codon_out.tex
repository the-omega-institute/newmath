The finite evidence supports the statement that window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure for the standard-code label table.  The strongest local row is the leave-one-codon-out result with $8$ predictable recoveries, $5$ boundary cases, $0$ anchors, and recovery rate $1.0$.  The claim stops there.

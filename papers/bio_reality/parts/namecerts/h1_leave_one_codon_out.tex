\begin{aligned}

\paragraph{Local biological scope.}
The reality contact is the curated standard-code table: it supplies codon-to-amino-acid or codon-to-stop labels.  That contact can test code-layer readback and local label splitting, but it cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, protein function, or a universal mechanism.  Amino-acid label readback is not folded protein structure.  A CUN/UUR or tRNA-Leu wording route would require its own organism-matched contact and cannot be imported from this codon-table row.  Consequently, the ready claim is narrowly this: in the finite window-six codon coordinate system, the thirteen-codon residual tile has a complete module classification and the leave-one-codon-out test recovers the omitted codon at rate $1.0$ in the verified run, with the support confined to curated code-label readback and local finite geometry.

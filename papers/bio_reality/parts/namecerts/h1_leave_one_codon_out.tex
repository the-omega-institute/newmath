\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
This BioReality record works on the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a finite display of the standard-code codon table.  The biological contact is deliberately narrow: a curated standard genetic-code bridge supplies codon-to-label readback, meaning that each codon is assigned an amino-acid or stop label.  The contact does not supply translation dynamics, molecular structure, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality.  The BEDC-side object is therefore a local coordinate and closure probe on codon labels, not a biological law about DNA or protein realization.

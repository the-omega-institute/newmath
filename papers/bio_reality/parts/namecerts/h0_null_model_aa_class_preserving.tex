\paragraph{Finite coordinate object.}
The record concerns the standard-code codon table after it has been placed in a six-coordinate codon display.  The biological contact is deliberately narrow: a curated table assigns each codon to an amino-acid label or to stop, and the construction asks whether that label readback exposes a local boundary-tile pattern.  The coordinate object is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, with local faces, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes available as finite distinctions.  The contact does not observe translation dynamics, protein folding, chemical admissibility, biological function, a generative mechanism, or a universal law.  Those are separate empirical layers and are not inferred from the table used here.

\paragraph{Unrestricted shape null.}
Under the shape-significance sampling row, $N=2000$ distinct sampled alternatives were evaluated.  Because the sampler rejected duplicates, the run required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries. $$\begin{aligned}
N&=2000,\\
\#\{\mathrm{exact}\}&=1,\qquad p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005,\\

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class-preserving null.}
The claim-specific null keeps amino-acid-class information fixed while testing whether the observed tile shape is recovered by chance within that restricted comparison.  In this run, $N=2000$, the observed $M$ size was $20$, and no sampled alternative had the exact observed shape.  The exact-shape count was therefore $0$, with the finite-run estimate $p=0.0$ at the resolution of $2000$ samples.  The lower-tail statistic counted $7$ sampled alternatives with size at most the observed $M$ size, giving $p=0.0035$:
$$\begin{aligned}
N&=2000,\\
|M|_{\mathrm{obs}}&=20,\\
\#\{\mathrm{exact\ shape\ matches}\}&=0,\\
\#\{|M_{\mathrm{sample}}|\leq 20\}&=7,\\
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.
\end{aligned}$$
The exact-shape value should be read as a finite Monte Carlo outcome, not as a proof that the true probability is mathematically zero.  What the row supports is stronger than the unrestricted comparison in one respect: even after preserving amino-acid-class structure, the observed local tile is not reproduced in the sampled exact-shape test, and the lower tail is small.

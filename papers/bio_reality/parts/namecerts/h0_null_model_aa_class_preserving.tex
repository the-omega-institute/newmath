\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}
The carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read through the standard-code label table as an external reality contact.  The contact is deliberately narrow: it gives a curated codon-to-amino-acid-or-stop label assignment, and it does not give translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or universality.  The BEDC-side display records four distinctions: the six-bit codon display, a local tile face, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes.  The internal structure used by the row is coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation; the readback is the standard-code label table only.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class preserving null.}
The null model for this row preserves amino-acid class while randomizing the local shape test.  With seed $42$ and $N=2000$ samples, the observed local object has $|M|=20$.  The exact-shape count under the amino-acid-class preserving null is $0$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,exact}}=0.0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$ size is $7$, so
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,lower\ tail}}=\frac{7}{2000}=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
This is the central finite-row fact: preserving amino-acid class does not by itself reproduce the observed window-six tile.  The row is therefore a sharp local code-read signal against this null, but the signal is still only a signal about codon-label geometry.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The supported statement is limited to the following: window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure whose labels can be read against the curated standard-code table, and the amino-acid-class preserving null does not reproduce the exact shape in $2000$ samples.  No translation process follows from this row.  No folded protein structure follows from this row.  No physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows from this row.  An amino-acid label sequence readback is not a folded protein structure.  Any such higher-layer claim would require a separate reality contact beyond the curated standard-code table.

The packet studies the standard genetic-code table only at the code-read layer.  Its finite carrier is the six-coordinate codon cube $Q_6$, where a codon is read as a six-bit window and the observed labels are the curated amino-acid or stop assignments of the standard code.  The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$ and is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is observed as a punctured-cube pattern with face-vector $(13,16,4)$. The larger marked set $M$ has observed size $20$ in the amino-acid-class-preserving test below; the available row gives this size but does not supply a separate explicit list of all twenty $M$ codons.

\paragraph{Unrestricted shape comparison.}
Against an unrestricted finite null sample of $N=2000$ trials with seed $42$, the exact local shape appeared once.  The corresponding exact-shape probability estimate is
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}=\frac{1}{2000}=0.0005.
\end{aligned}
$$
The high-overlap comparison recorded $25$ samples with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving $p_{J\ge 0.9}=0.0125$.  The sampler attempted $57377$ distinct draws and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries, so the row is a finite randomized comparison with substantial duplicate rejection rather than an unconstrained analytic enumeration.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class-preserving null row.}
The claim-specific null model preserves amino-acid class structure while testing whether the observed local shape is still exceptional.  In $N=2000$ samples, the exact match count is $0$, so the row reports
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,exact}}&=0.0,\\
\#\{\mathrm{samples\ with\ exact\ shape}\}&=0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The observed marked-set size is $|M|=20$.  The lower-tail count for samples with size at most the observed $M$ size is $7$, yielding
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,lower\ tail}}=\frac{7}{2000}=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
Both recorded significance fields reproduce these finite values: exact-shape significance is $0.0$, and lower-tail significance is $0.0035$.  The result is therefore sharper than the unrestricted exact-shape row in one respect: after preserving amino-acid class, no exact copy of the observed tile was seen in the two thousand sampled alternatives.

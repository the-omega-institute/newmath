\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The claim supported here is narrow.  Window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure in the curated standard-code label table, and the amino-acid-class-preserving null run makes the observed tile rare within the tested finite randomization.  Nothing in this row supplies translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a biochemical mechanism, or universality across all genetic-code contexts.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is still only label readback.  Any promotion from this local codon-tile observation to translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact designed to test that higher layer.

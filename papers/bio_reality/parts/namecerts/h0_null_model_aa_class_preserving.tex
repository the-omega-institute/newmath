\subsection{Amino-Acid-Class-Preserving Null Model for the Window-Six Codon Tile}\label{sec:h0-null-model-aa-class-preserving}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and reality contact.}
This record concerns the six-bit codon display of the standard-code codon table, read only as a finite code-layer object.  The carrier is the codon cube $Q_6$, whose coordinates encode codon rows and whose labels are supplied by the curated standard genetic-code table.  The table is used as a reality contact for codon-to-label readback: it assigns each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  It is not used as a mechanism, as a translation experiment, or as a protein-structure contact.  The finite claim tested here is therefore narrow: the observed local tile and its boundary behavior are compared with amino-acid-class-preserving null relabelings, while the biological interpretation remains at the code_read layer.

\paragraph{Observed boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  Its codons are
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\texttt{AAA},\texttt{AGA},\texttt{AGG},\texttt{AUA},\texttt{CUA},\texttt{CUC},\texttt{CUG},\texttt{CUU},\\
&\texttt{UAA},\texttt{UAG},\texttt{UCA},\texttt{UGA},\texttt{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same finite row is read as a punctured-cube object with $f$-vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the row is not merely a list of exceptional codons: it is also a finite local cell complex inside the window-six coordinate system.  The supplied finite-row data do not include the explicit codon list for $M$, but they do give its observed size, $|M|=20$, and the null-model comparison is stated against that observed size.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class-preserving null model.}
The null model preserves amino-acid class while sampling alternative label arrangements.  In the run with seed $42$, $N=2000$ null samples were evaluated.  None of the samples reproduced the exact observed shape, so the exact-shape count was $0$ and the reported exact-shape tail probability was
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,exact}}=0.0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The lower-tail statistic compares null outcomes whose $M$-size is at most the observed value $20$.  There were $7$ such outcomes among $2000$ samples, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,lower\ tail}}=\frac{7}{2000}=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
This is the core finite-row result for the present claim: after preserving amino-acid class, the observed tile remains a lower-tail event in the sampled null distribution, and the exact shape is not hit in the sampled population.

\paragraph{Shape and spectrum checks.}
A separate shape-significance run used $N=2000$ samples with seed $42$.  It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and incurred $55377$ duplicate retries.  There was $1$ exact shape match, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact\ shape}}=\frac{1}{2000}=0.0005.
\end{aligned}
$$
There were $25$ samples with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}=\frac{25}{2000}=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The spectral readout is also finite and numerical.  The recorded values are
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lambda_M&=0.6752479308830213,\\
\lambda_R&=0.4758352843197002,\\
\mu&=3.051487585298128.
\end{aligned}
$$
The radial cubic residual is $-6.224354365258478\times 10^{-12}$, matching the separately reported cubic-polynomial residual, and the spectral radius in tolerance is $0.6752479308830213$.  These numbers do not promote the tile to a biological mechanism; they only record that the finite coordinate object has a stable local spectral signature under the stated computation.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The cannot-claim boundary is part of the result.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.  Window-six geometry alone is not translation, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.  The curated code table supports code_read and local codon-label split checks only.  Any assertion about translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact designed for that higher layer.

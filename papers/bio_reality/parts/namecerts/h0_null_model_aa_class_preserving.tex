$$
\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}
$$
The finite-row conclusion is narrow: preserving amino-acid classes does not make the observed local tile typical in this sample. The evidence is a code-layer discrepancy against this null model, not a mechanism of translation or a claim about folded protein order.

\paragraph{Shape controls.}

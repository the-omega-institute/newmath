\subsection{Amino-acid-class-preserving null model for the window-six codon tile}
\label{sec:bioreality-h0-null-model-aa-class-preserving}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact.}
The finite carrier is the window-six codon display $Q_6$, read as the standard-code codon table through a curated codon-label contact.  The contact supplies only the code-layer assignment of each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  It does not supply a translation process, a folding experiment, a physical-admissibility witness, a function assay, a mechanism, or a universality theorem.  The present row is therefore a finite code-read record: it asks whether the local tile singled out by the window-six coordinates remains exceptional after the null model is constrained to preserve amino-acid label classes.

\paragraph{Observed boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has exactly thirteen codons.  In lexicographic codon notation the observed row is
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is detected as a punctured-cube pattern with finite face data
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the finite readback is not merely a list of thirteen exceptional codons: it carries a local tile signature with thirteen vertices, sixteen edges, and four two-dimensional faces inside the codon-coordinate display.  The complementary measured object $M$ has observed size $|M|=20$ in the amino-acid-class-preserving null experiment.  The finite-row data supplied here do not include a codon-by-codon list for $M$, so this chapter uses its reported size and shape statistics rather than reconstructing an unavailable list.

\paragraph{Amino-acid-class-preserving null test.}
The null model keeps amino-acid label classes fixed and randomizes within that constraint.  With seed $42$ and $N=2000$ sampled null rows, the observed exact tile shape was never reproduced:
$$
\begin{aligned}
N&=2000,\\
\mathrm{exact\_match\_count}&=0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,exact}}&=0.0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The lower-tail comparison for the observed size $|M|=20$ found seven null samples at or below the observed size, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{lower\_tail\_count}&=7,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class,lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
Within this finite experiment, the local window-six shape is therefore not explained away by preserving the amino-acid class partition alone.  The result is a code-read anomaly relative to this null, not a biological mechanism.  Its evidential force is restricted to the curated standard-code label table and the finite coordinate probes used in the row.

\paragraph{Shape and spectrum checks.}
An independent shape-significance run used $N=2000$ samples with seed $42$.  It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and incurred $55377$ duplicate retries.  The exact-shape count was $1$, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$, while the high-overlap threshold $J\geq 0.9$ was met $25$ times, giving $p_{J\geq 0.9}=0.0125$.  These figures are consistent with the amino-acid-class-preserving test: exact reproduction is rare, and near-reproduction remains uncommon.

The reported spectral row gives
$$
\begin{aligned}
\lambda_M&=0.6752479308830213,\\
\lambda_R&=0.4758352843197002,\\
\mu&=3.051487585298128.
\end{aligned}
$$
The radial polynomial residual is $-6.224354365258478\times 10^{-12}$, the same value reported for the cubic-polynomial check.  At the numerical tolerance of the finite run, this residual is effectively zero for the stated spectral calculation.  The spectrum is not used here as a physical frequency or energetic law; it is a coordinate invariant attached to the finite tile construction.

\paragraph{Local verdict and boundary.}
The finite evidence supports a narrow claim: in the curated standard-code table, the window-six coordinate system exposes a local boundary-tile structure whose exact shape is rare under the amino-acid-class-preserving null model, whose row has a punctured-cube face signature, and whose codons admit a complete finite module classification.  The evidence does not license any higher-layer promotion.  No translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or necessity claim follows from this row.  Such claims would require a separate contact of the appropriate kind.  Until that contact is supplied, the chapter remains a code-read record about a finite codon-label table and its local window-six tile.

$$
\begin{aligned}

p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact\ shape}}&=0.0.
\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}

p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.

$$
Thus the finite-row readback is not merely saying that the standard code has amino-acid classes.  It says that, even after preserving those classes, the specific local codon-tile boundary is unusually sharp in this sampled null ensemble.

\paragraph{Finite object and contact layer.}
This record concerns the standard-code codon table read only as a finite code-layer contact.  The carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, with each codon displayed as a coordinate word and with the curated standard-code table supplying the external labels.  The contact is deliberately narrow: it says that a codon is assigned an amino-acid or stop label in the standard table.  It does not say that the local geometry realizes translation, protein folding, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or a universal law.  The tested claim is therefore a code-read claim about a local tile boundary, not a biological promotion beyond the label table.

\paragraph{Unrestricted and amino-acid-class-preserving nulls.}
Against the unrestricted shape null, the observed local shape is rare in the finite sample: with seed $42$ and $N=2000$ sampled tables, there was $1$ exact shape match and $25$ matches with Jaccard similarity at least $0.9$.  The resulting p-values are
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}} &= 0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9} &= 0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The sampler required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries.  The amino-acid-class-preserving null is sharper for this chapter.  With seed $42$ and $N=2000$, the observed $M$-row size is $20$, the exact match count is $0$, and only $7$ sampled rows fall in the lower tail at or below the observed $M$ size.  Hence
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact\ shape}} &= 0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}} &= 0.0035,\\
|M|_{\mathrm{observed}} &= 20.
\end{aligned}
$$
This supports the finite statement that the observed local tile is not explained by the tested amino-acid-class-preserving randomization at the sampled resolution.  It does not turn the coordinate geometry into a law of molecular biology.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The boundary of the result is part of the result.  The curated standard-code contact supplies codon-to-label assignments, and the probes test only whether the window-six coordinate display exposes a local boundary-tile structure in those labels.  No translation realization follows.  No folded protein structure follows.  No physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality follows.

\subsection{Window-Six Codon Spectrum and Local Boundary Readback}
\label{sec:bioreality-h0-spectrum}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier.}
The record concerns the standard-code codon table read through a six-bit codon display.  The carrier is the finite codon cube $Q_6$, and the only external contact used here is a curated standard genetic-code table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  The claim is therefore a code-layer claim: the table may be read as a labelled finite row system, and window-six coordinates may expose a local boundary-tile pattern inside that row system.  The contact does not supply translation dynamics, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.

\paragraph{Boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  Its observed members are
$$
\begin{aligned}
R = \{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is reported as a punctured-cube face pattern with finite face-vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(R)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
This is a local combinatorial statement about the row extracted from the codon display.  It says that the listed codons form the observed boundary tile in the window-six coordinate system; it does not say that biological translation is governed by this geometry.

$$
The reported cubic residual is $-6.224354365258478\cdot 10^{-12}$, and the radial polynomial residual is the same number.  The spectral-radius value retained by the tolerance check is $0.6752479308830213$.  Thus the finite computation supports a stable local spectrum for the measured row system: the row labelled $M$ has observed spectral radius $\lambda_M$, while the boundary row $R$ has observed value $\lambda_R$.  The scalar $\mu$ belongs to the same finite spectral certificate and is read here only as part of the coordinate-side spectrum.

\paragraph{Shape significance.}
The finite-row shape check used $N=2000$ sampled controls with seed $42$.  It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries.  In that run there was $1$ exact shape match, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005.
\end{aligned}
$$
There were $25$ controls with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
A second amino-acid-class-preserving null model also used $N=2000$ and seed $42$.  In that model the observed $M$ size was $20$, the exact-shape count was $0$, and the lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$ size was $7$.  The reported values were
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}} &= 0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}} &= 0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
These finite controls support the claim that the measured local shape is not a common event under the stated randomizations.  They do not identify a biochemical cause.

The hit rate above baseline was $0.9841269841269841$, the hit rate inside $M-T$ was $0.9365079365079365$, and the hit rate inside $M-T$ plus the boundary was $0.9841269841269841$.  By family, the reported hit-rate-above-baseline values were $0.8333333333333334$ for alternative yeast, $1.0$ for bacterial plastid, $1.0$ for candidate other, $1.0$ for mitochondrial, and $1.0$ for nuclear ciliate, across $5$ families.  These numbers are read as code-table label recovery statistics, not as organism-level universals.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.}
The supported conclusion is narrow.  Window-six coordinates, the $13$-codon punctured boundary row, the spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and the finite recovery statistics jointly support a local code-read pattern in the curated standard-code label table.  The result does not promote the local tile to translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or biological necessity.  Any such claim would require a separate contact supplying that higher-layer observation.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback remains label readback; it is not folded structure.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.} The contact used here is the curated standard-code table, which observes that codons are assigned to amino-acid or stop labels. That contact can test code readback and local label splits. It cannot test translation realization, structural order, folded protein geometry, physical admissibility, biological function, or a universal biological law. Consequently, amino-acid label sequences produced from this table remain label sequences. A separate experimental or curated contact would be required before making any structural, physical, mechanistic, functional, or universal claim.

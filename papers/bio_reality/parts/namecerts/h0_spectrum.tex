\subsection{Window-six spectral row for the standard-code table}
\label{sec:bioreality-h0-spectrum}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and reality contact.}
The record is a code-read statement over the finite codon cube $Q_6$.  A codon is displayed as a six-bit coordinate, with the curated standard-code table used only as an external contact that assigns each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  The BEDC-side object is therefore not a mechanism of translation and not a model of folding.  It is a finite row computation with four local distinctions: the six-bit codon display, a local tile face, a singleton label corner, and a three-point label class.  The allowed readback is the standard-code label table, and the forbidden promotions are fixed inside the record: no translation realization, no folded structure, no physical admissibility, no biological function, no universal mechanism, and no biological necessity follows from the window-six geometry unless a separate contact supplies that higher-layer fact.

\paragraph{Boundary row $R$.}
The distinguished boundary row has cardinality $13$.  Its codons are
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
    &UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is observed as a punctured-cube pattern with finite face vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
f(R)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the row is not being described by a continuous approximation or by a hidden asymptotic limit.  It is a concrete finite subset of the codon cube, and the face data records the local adjacency structure exposed by the six-bit coordinate.  The row contains stop-related codons $UAA$, $UAG$, and $UGA$, arginine-related codons $AGA$ and $AGG$, leucine-related codons $CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU$ and $UUA$, the isoleucine-methionine boundary codon $AUA$, the lysine-side codon $AAA$, and the serine-side codon $UCA$.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.}
The conclusion is deliberately narrow.  The finite-row evidence says that the standard-code label table, when read through the six-bit codon coordinate, contains a locally structured boundary row with cardinality $13$, punctured-cube face vector $(13,16,4)$, spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$, and rare-shape behavior under the sampled null rows.  The contact does not contain a translation apparatus, a protein structure, a thermodynamic admissibility test, or a functional assay.  Therefore the admissible claim is a code-read claim about a finite codon-label table.  Any claim about translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality must be supplied by a separate reality contact and cannot be inferred from this local tile alone.

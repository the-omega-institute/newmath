\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The conclusion is deliberately local.  The window-six spectrum, the punctured-cube row, the null-model counts, and the leave-one probes all live at the code-read layer.  They may show that the curated standard-code labels align with a local boundary-tile structure in $Q_6$.  They do not show that DNA obeys this geometry as a global biological law.  They do not establish translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Crossing any of those boundaries requires a separate reality contact with its own finite observations.

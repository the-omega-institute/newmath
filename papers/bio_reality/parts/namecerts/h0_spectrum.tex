\paragraph{Carrier and contact.}
The finite carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as the ordinary codon display over the alphabet $\{A,C,G,U\}$ after the coordinate encoding has been fixed.  The reality contact is only the curated standard-code label table: each codon is assigned an amino-acid label or a stop label.  This contact supplies code-layer readback, not translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or universality.  The local object studied here is therefore a finite label split inside $Q_6$, together with its closure and spectral rows.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.}
The admissible claim is narrow.  Window-six coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table, with a punctured-cube row of size $13$, spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$, significant finite null behavior, and complete module assignment.  The inadmissible promotion is equally explicit: no translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows from these rows.  Crossing any of those boundaries requires a separate reality contact beyond the curated codon-to-label table.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
The finite object in this record is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read only through a curated standard-code table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label. The BEDC-minimal carrier is therefore not a biological mechanism but a finite coordinate display with four internal roles: coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation. The contact supports code-layer readback and local split tests. It does not by itself observe translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or any necessity claim about DNA. Those higher-layer statements remain unavailable unless a separate reality contact supplies them.

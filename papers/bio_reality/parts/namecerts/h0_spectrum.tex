\subsection{Spectral Boundary of the Window-Six Codon Tile}\label{sec:bioreality-h0-spectrum}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and external contact.}
This BioReality record concerns the six-bit codon display $Q_6$ and its finite readback through the curated standard-code label table. The carrier is not taken as a biological mechanism. It is a coordinate object whose rows are codons, whose local distinctions are tile faces, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes, and whose only external contact in this record is the codon-to-label table. The contact says that the standard genetic code assigns each codon an amino-acid or stop label. It does not say that the coordinate geometry translates, folds, admits physically realized proteins, produces function, or supplies a universal biological law.

\paragraph{Boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$. In the finite table used for this record it is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is recorded as a punctured-cube object with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$. Thus the local tile has thirteen vertices, sixteen one-dimensional adjacencies, and four two-dimensional faces in the observed finite complex. The data support a local code-layer split, not a claim about translation chemistry or protein structure.

\paragraph{Shape significance.}
Against the sampled null row, the observed $M$ shape has size $20$. In $N=2000$ sampled tables, there was $1$ exact shape match, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$. There were $25$ samples with Jaccard similarity at least $0.9$, giving $p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}=0.0125$. The sampling process required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries with seed $42$. A second amino-acid-class-preserving null model, also with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, gave $0$ exact matches for the observed shape, a lower-tail count of $7$ at or below observed $M$ size $20$, $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}}=0.0$, and $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}=0.0035$. These numbers support the claim that the row is non-generic under the stated finite nulls. They do not identify a biochemical cause.

\paragraph{Local cannot-claim boundary.}
The conclusion is deliberately narrow. The window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code readback, with a thirteen-codon punctured-cube row, a twenty-codon $M$ shape, spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$, and non-generic behavior under the stated finite nulls. Nothing in this record supplies translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Any such claim would require a separate reality contact beyond the curated standard-code label table.

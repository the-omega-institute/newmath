\paragraph{Reality contact and boundary.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code table, whose observed fact is that codons are assigned to amino-acid or stop labels.  That contact can test code-read behavior and local codon-tile label splits.  It cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, function realization, folded protein structure, or universal mechanism.  Therefore the row is ready only as a finite code-read record: window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure against curated labels, while the labels themselves remain external reality contacts.  Any later claim about translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate contact that directly observes that higher layer.

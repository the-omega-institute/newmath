\paragraph{Scientific reading.}
The chapter therefore records a finite, code-layer result.  The six-coordinate codon display exposes a thirteen-codon punctured-cube region with a verified $f$-vector, stable spectral values, low randomization probabilities, strong leave-one-code-out recovery, and a complete module partition. \paragraph{Local boundary of the claim.}
No statement in this row licenses promotion beyond the code-label layer.  The curated table tells us which labels are assigned to codons; it does not observe ribosomal realization, kinetic accessibility, folded protein structure, thermodynamic admissibility, organismal function, or a universal mechanism.  Amino-acid label strings are not folded structures, and cross-family codon-label regularities are not by themselves biological laws.  Any later claim about translation, folding, physical feasibility, function, or universality must bring in a separate contact that directly measures that layer.  Without such a contact, the present row remains a finite local boundary statement about codon labels in $Q_6$.

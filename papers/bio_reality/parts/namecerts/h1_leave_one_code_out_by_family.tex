\paragraph{Finite-row object.}
This BioReality record concerns the standard-code codon table as a finite code-read contact over the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$.  The carrier is not a biological mechanism; it is a finite coordinate display whose rows are codons and whose observed labels are amino-acid or stop labels supplied by the curated standard genetic-code table.  The tested distinction is a local tile boundary inside the codon cube, with singleton label corners and three-point label classes read back from the external table.  The reality contact is therefore limited to codon-label readback.

The finite exceptional row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  In the observed row it is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R = \{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is reported as a punctured-cube object with $f$-vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
The module partition covers all $13$ elements of $R$ with no missing, extra, or unassigned codons.  The six modules are
$$
\begin{aligned}

\mathrm{ar\_lys} &= \{\mathrm{AAA}\},\\

Thus the finite-row claim is not merely that a set of codons is selected; it is that the selected row is completely accounted for by the observed module classification.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The claim established here is a code-read claim.  The curated standard-code table supplies codon-to-label assignments, and the window-six coordinate system exposes a local tile boundary that is stable under the listed finite probes.  No stronger biological layer follows from these rows.  In particular, this chapter does not claim translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or that DNA obeys the displayed geometry as a biological necessity.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.  Any promotion beyond code_read would require a separate reality contact with its own finite rows and its own failure conditions.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
This BioReality record studies a finite window-six codon display on the codon cube $Q_6$.  The carrier is not a mechanism for translation; it is a finite coordinate object whose readback is compared with a curated standard-code label table.  The distinguished boundary row is the $13$-codon set
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is observed as a punctured-cube face with finite $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$.  Thus the geometric datum used below is a local tile boundary, not a global biological law.  The only reality contact used here is the curated codon-to-label table, which supplies code-layer labels for amino acids and stop symbols but supplies no folded structure, no energetic admissibility test, and no functional assay.

$$

In the shape null with $N=2000$ samples and seed $42$, there was $1$ exact shape match and $25$ matches at Jaccard threshold at least $0.9$.  The corresponding empirical rates were
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{J\geq 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
The distinct-sample accounting was also finite: $57377$ distinct sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries.  Under the amino-acid-class-preserving null, again with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, the observed $M$-row size was $20$, the exact-match count was $0$, and the lower-tail count at or below the observed size was $7$, giving $p=0.0035$ for that lower tail.  These numbers justify treating the tile as a non-generic code-read pattern, while still leaving its biological interpretation bounded by the contact.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The support condition is only this: the curated standard-code table supports codon-label readback and local split checks for the window-six tile.  The break condition is promotion beyond that layer.  No translation realization, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or biological necessity follows from these rows.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded structure.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.  A separate higher-layer contact would be required before any claim about translation, structure, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality could be made.

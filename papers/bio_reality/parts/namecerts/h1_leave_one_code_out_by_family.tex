\subsection{Leave-One-Code-Out Family Stability for the Window-Six Codon Tile}
\label{sec:h1-leave-one-code-out-by-family}

\paragraph{Finite object.}
The finite carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read through the standard four-letter codon display and then through a curated standard-code label table.  The row under audit is not a claim about translation as a physical process.  It is a finite code-read claim: after one code table is held out, the window-six coordinate structure is asked whether the held-out codon-label differences fall back into the same local tile boundary already visible in the standard-code readback.  The external reality contact is therefore only the curated codon-to-amino-acid-or-stop table.  The BEDC-side distinctions are the six-bit codon display, local tile face, singleton label corner, and three-point label class; the internal row structure is coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation.

\paragraph{Recovered boundary row.}
The distinguished boundary row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  The verified codons are
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The same row is verified as the punctured-cube row with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$.  Thus the local tile is finite and explicitly inspectable: thirteen codon vertices, sixteen one-dimensional boundary incidences, and four two-dimensional faces.  The row is not inferred from biological necessity; it is the finite subset selected by the coordinate-and-label readback.

\paragraph{Null rows and significance.}
The shape row was tested against $N=2000$ sampled null rows with seed $42$.  There was $1$ exact shape match, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$, and $25$ rows with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving $p_{J\ge 0.9}=0.0125$.  The sampler records $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries.  In the amino-acid-class-preserving null, again with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, the observed $M$ size is $20$, the exact shape count is $0$, and the lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$ size is $7$.  The corresponding reported values are $p=0.0$ for exact shape and $p=0.0035$ for the lower tail.  The supplied finite data give the size of $M$ but not its full codon list, so the present row records the $M$-shape count and null behavior rather than a separate enumeration of the twenty $M$ codons.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The support condition is strictly local.  The curated standard-code table supports codon-label readback and local split checks; it does not supply a translation realization, protein structure, physical admissibility test, function assay, universal mechanism, or global biological law.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is also not folded protein structure.  Consequently, this chapter claims only that the window-six coordinate row exposes a stable local boundary-tile pattern for code-read comparisons, with strong leave-one-code-out behavior across the five named code-table families.  Any promotion beyond code_read requires a separate higher-layer reality contact.

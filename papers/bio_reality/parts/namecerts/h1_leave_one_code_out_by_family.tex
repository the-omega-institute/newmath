\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The claim established by this row is only a code-read claim.  Window-six coordinates expose a local tile and family-stratified recovery pattern for curated codon labels.  No translation process, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality follows from these data.

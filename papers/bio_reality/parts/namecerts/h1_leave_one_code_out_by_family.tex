The carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, with codons read as finite labels and not as molecular mechanisms.  The external contact is the curated standard genetic-code table: it supplies codon-to-amino-acid-or-stop labels, and it supplies only that code-layer readback. \begin{aligned}

This is a coordinate fact about the codon-label table, not a claim that biological translation is determined by cube geometry.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The claim is therefore local and finite: window-six coordinates expose a stable boundary-tile pattern in the curated codon-label table, and the family-resolved leave-one-code-out row shows that the pattern remains above baseline across the five supplied families.  Nothing in this row proves translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or biological necessity.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not protein structure.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.  Any promotion beyond code_read requires a separate reality contact designed for that higher layer.

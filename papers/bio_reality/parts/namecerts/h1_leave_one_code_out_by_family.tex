\paragraph{Local verdict and cannot-claim boundary.}
The finite data support the following restricted statement.  Window-six codon coordinates expose a local tile boundary in the curated standard-code label table, and the leave-one-code-out family audit preserves that boundary with family hit rates $0.8333333333333334,1.0,1.0,1.0,1.0$ across $5$ families.  The statement is intentionally local.  No translation realization follows from these rows.  No folded protein structure follows from amino-acid label readback. Any such higher-layer claim would require a separate reality contact beyond the curated codon-label table.

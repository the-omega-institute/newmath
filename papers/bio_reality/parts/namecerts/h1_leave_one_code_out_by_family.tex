These numbers make the row non-generic with respect to the sampled finite alternatives, but the inference remains a code-read inference: the calculation detects finite coordinate concentration, not biochemical necessity.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The supported statement is narrow.  Window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile pattern in the standard-code label table, and the leave-one-code-out family test shows strong finite recovery relative to its recorded baseline.  The chapter does not claim translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or a law of DNA geometry.  Amino-acid label readback is not protein structure.  A separate higher-layer reality contact would be required before any claim about translation, folding, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality could be made.

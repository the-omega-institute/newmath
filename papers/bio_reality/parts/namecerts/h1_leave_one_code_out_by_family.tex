\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The reality contact is the curated standard-code table, with resolution at codon label readback.  It can test code-read behavior and local codon-tile label splits.  It cannot test translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or any claim that DNA must obey BEDC geometry as biological necessity.  In particular, amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.  The local pattern may be used to formulate a code-layer probe, but no translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality follows unless a separate higher-layer contact supplies that information.  The finite result is therefore aligned rather than promoted: it supports a local code-read boundary split and blocks escalation beyond the observed contact.

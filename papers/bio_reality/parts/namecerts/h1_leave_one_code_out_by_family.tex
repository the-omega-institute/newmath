\paragraph{Reality contact and scope boundary.}
The only biological contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code table, interpreted as a bridge for codon-to-label readback.  That contact can test code-read facts and local codon-tile label splits.  It cannot test translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Consequently, the claim certified by this row is narrow: window-six coordinates expose a finite local boundary-tile pattern whose leave-one-code-out recovery remains high when grouped by code family, while the amino-acid or stop labels are external reality contacts supplied by the curated table.  No statement about protein realization follows from these numbers.  No statement about folded protein structure follows from an amino-acid label sequence.  No physical admissibility, functional role, universal mechanism, or biological necessity follows unless a separate higher-layer contact is supplied and audited on its own finite rows.

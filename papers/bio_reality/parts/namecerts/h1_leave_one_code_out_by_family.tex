\subsection{Leave-one-code-out family stability for the codon boundary tile}\label{sec:bioreality-h1-leave-one-code-out-by-family}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite object under test.}
The record concerns a window-six display of the standard-code codon table, where each codon is treated as a point of a six-coordinate cube and the observed labels are read only as curated amino-acid or stop assignments. The distinguished boundary set is the thirteen-codon row
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
This set has cardinality $13$ and is observed as a punctured cube with face data $f=(13,16,4)$. Thus the carrier is not being treated as a biochemical mechanism; it is a finite coordinate presentation whose label readback is supplied by the curated standard-code table.

\paragraph{Spectral and shape measurements.}
The associated finite operators give $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$. The radial cubic check leaves residual $-6.224354365258478\times 10^{-12}$, which is within the stated numerical tolerance for the computed spectral radius. The shape test used $N=2000$ comparison samples with seed $42$; among $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and $55377$ duplicate retries, there was $1$ exact shape match and $25$ matches at Jaccard threshold at least $0.9$. The corresponding empirical values were
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
A stricter amino-acid-class-preserving null run, also with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, found $0$ exact shape matches, $7$ lower-tail samples at or below the observed $M$-size, and observed $|M|=20$. Its reported probabilities were $0.0$ for exact-shape recovery and $0.0035$ for the lower tail. These numbers say that the coordinate shape is not behaving like a typical draw under the tested null ensembles, but they do not identify any molecular cause.

\paragraph{Leave-one-code-out family row.}
The family-level leave-one-code-out row evaluates whether the local boundary pattern remains useful when whole code families are held out. Five families were evaluated. The observed above-baseline hit rates were
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{alternative\_yeast}&:0.8333333333333334,\\
\mathrm{bacterial\_plastid}&:1.0,\\
\mathrm{candidate\_other}&:1.0,\\
\mathrm{mitochondrial}&:1.0,\\
\mathrm{nuclear\_ciliate}&:1.0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The aggregate leave-one-code-out run covered $23$ tables and $63$ total targets. Its baseline hit rate was $0.19821739130434785$, while the hit rate above baseline was $0.9841269841269841$. The hit rate inside $M\setminus T$ was $0.9365079365079365$, and the rate inside $M\setminus T$ plus the boundary was again $0.9841269841269841$. The family row therefore records a strong finite regularity: after a code-family holdout, the local tile and its boundary still recover nearly all evaluated targets above the baseline rule, with the alternative-yeast family being the only listed family below perfect recovery.

This is the finite bookkeeping reason the family-level result is interpretable: the relevant boundary is not a vague visual feature, but a closed list of codons with a complete module partition.

\paragraph{Reality contact and scope.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard-code table, understood as a codon-to-label table. It supports code-layer readback and local split checks. It does not observe translation dynamics, folded protein geometry, thermodynamic admissibility, biological role, a causal mechanism, or a universal law across all biological systems. In particular, the amino-acid label sequence obtained from a codon table is not a protein structure, and a codon tile found in a six-coordinate display is not by itself a claim about physical realization. Any step from this code-level row to translation, folding, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality would require a separate empirical contact of the appropriate kind.

\paragraph{Verdict.}
The finite row is ready as a code-read claim. The strongest statement licensed by the data is that the window-six codon coordinate system exposes a stable local boundary tile for the standard-code label table, and that the leave-one-code-out family test preserves above-baseline recovery across five evaluated families, with four families at $1.0$ and alternative yeast at $0.8333333333333334$. The row should not be promoted beyond codon-label readback without an additional non-table contact.

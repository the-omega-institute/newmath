The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code bridge, whose observed fact is that a standard-code table assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels. That contact can test code readback and a local codon-tile label split. Therefore the admissible conclusion is narrow: window-six codon coordinates expose a finite local boundary-tile structure whose label recovery is strong under the listed leave-one-code-out and leave-one-codon-out probes.

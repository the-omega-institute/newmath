\paragraph{Boundary.}

The codon-usage table is a sequence-summary contact, and the tRNA gene-copy table is a genome-annotation contact. Neither one observes a ribosome, a folded product, an activity assay, a viability constraint, or a law of directionality across organisms. The recorded non-uniform sign also cannot be read as a general reversal theorem. It is a context-dependent finite result tied to the listed rows and to the available paired statistic.

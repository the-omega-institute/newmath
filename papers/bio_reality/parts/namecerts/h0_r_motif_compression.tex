\paragraph{Local cannot-claim boundary.}
The conclusion supported here is limited to finite ORF-eligibility classification under the curated interval contact just described. The motif equality for $R$, the twenty-row frame $M$, the saturation computation, the quotient geometry, the one-window start/stop audit, and the $168$-row curated interval contact do not imply translation realization. They do not imply folded protein structure.

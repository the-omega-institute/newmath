\paragraph{Local scientific reading.}
The finite rows support a narrow reading. The motif row proves that the named set $R$ is exactly the thirteen listed codons. The ambient and saturation rows place that set inside a twenty-codon finite domain and show how the $f3$ saturation covers all of $M$ except $ACA$ and $ACG$. \paragraph{What is not concluded.}
No claim in this packet converts the motif equality, saturation, quotient coordinates, or start/stop enumeration into translation realization. No claim identifies a folded protein, a physical admissibility certificate, a cellular mechanism, a biological function, or a genome-wide universal rule. Any stronger biological assertion would require a separate contact of the appropriate kind, with its own input records and its own failure conditions.

\paragraph{ORF-eligibility seed boundary.}
The biological object named by the conjecture is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration.  At this layer, the row is deliberately weaker than a claim of an observed ORF.  A start--stop window can be an eligibility pattern only after a separate biological contact supplies an interval or comparable sequence evidence.  Without such a contact, the row remains a proposal for what would have to be tested against a sequence, transcript, or curated annotation source.  This local rule blocks the following inferences: no translation realization follows; no folded molecular structure follows; no physical admissibility follows; no biological function follows; no mechanism follows; and no universal law about genomes follows.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.}
The supported claim stops at code-read geometry.  The curated table supplies codon-to-label readback and allows local tile checks, but it does not observe translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is therefore not protein-structure readback.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law, and window-six geometry alone is not a theory of translation or function.  Any future promotion beyond this code layer would require a separate reality contact that directly measures the higher layer being claimed.

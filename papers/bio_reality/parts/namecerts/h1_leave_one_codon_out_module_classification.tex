Its carrier is the codon display, its local distinctions are a tile face, singleton label corners, and three-point label classes, and its readback contact is the curated standard-code label table. Window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure whose $13$ codons, punctured-cube counts, spectral row, null-model contrasts, leave-one-code-out behavior, leave-one-codon-out recovery, and six-module classification are mutually consistent with standard-code label readback.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The claim established by these finite rows is the following local code-read statement: window-six codon coordinates expose a structured boundary-tile pattern whose thirteen rows admit a complete six-module classification, and whose supporting finite statistics are nontrivial under the recorded null checks. The claim does not say that DNA obeys this geometry as a biological necessity. It does not say that codon geometry alone realizes translation, determines protein structure, proves physical admissibility, supplies biological function, gives a universal mechanism, or predicts folded proteins. The standard-code table is a reality bridge for codon-to-label readback only. Amino-acid label sequences are not folded structures, and local codon tiles are not global biological laws.

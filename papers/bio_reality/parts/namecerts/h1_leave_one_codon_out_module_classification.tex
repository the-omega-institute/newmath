\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The finite conclusion is that the standard-code table, read as external label contact on $Q_6$, exhibits a complete thirteen-row residual module classification in which every module has predictable-or-boundary support, with strong leave-one-out recovery and small null frequencies in the supplied finite comparisons.  The conclusion is local to code readback.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law, and window-six geometry alone is not translation, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or an account of why the standard table is realized in living systems.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is therefore not folded protein structure.  Any crossing from this code-label layer into translation, structure, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact supplying that higher-layer evidence.

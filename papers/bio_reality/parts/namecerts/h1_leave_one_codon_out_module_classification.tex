\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The contact used here is only a curated standard genetic-code table.  It supports codon-label readback and local label-split tests; it does not observe translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Therefore no statement in this record promotes the local tile to a global law of biology.  Amino-acid label sequences are not folded protein structures, and a boundary module in $Q_6$ is not a mechanism for protein function.  Any claim beyond the code-read layer requires a separate contact supplying that higher-layer evidence.

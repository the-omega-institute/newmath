\paragraph{Finite carrier.}
The record is a finite code-read claim over the six-bit codon display $Q_6$.  The biological contact is only the curated standard-code table, read as a finite assignment of each codon to an amino-acid or stop label.  The internal BEDC side supplies coordinates, closure, spectrum, and relation data; the external side supplies the label table.  The carrier is therefore not a molecular mechanism.  It is the codon cube together with a finite label readback.

The distinguished row set has cardinality $13$ and is exactly
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The punctured-cube check returns the same thirteen codons and the finite face vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the claim is not an assertion about all of biology; it is a finite-row statement about a local tile in $Q_6$ and its code-label readback.

$$
\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}
$$

$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9} &= 0.0125.

A second null model preserving amino-acid class structure used $N=2000$ and observed $0$ exact matches.  Its lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$ size was $7$, with observed $M$ size $20$, hence
$$
\begin{aligned}

p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}} &= 0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
These finite tests support non-random local structure under the supplied null models.  They do not identify a physical cause.

\paragraph{Readback and boundary.}
The readback layer is $\mathrm{code\_read}$.  The curated standard-code table can test codon-label assignment and local label splits; it cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, function realization, or universality.  Therefore the correct conclusion is bounded: window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile organization of the standard-code label table, and the leave-one-codon-out module classification is complete on the finite set $R$.  No claim about translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows unless a separate reality contact supplies that higher-layer observation.

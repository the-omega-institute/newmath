\paragraph{Local interpretation and stopping rule.}
The scientific reading is intentionally restrained.  The calculation says that window-six coordinates expose a local boundary-tile organization in the standard-code table, and that the module decomposition of $R$ is complete for the thirteen selected codons.  The curated table supplies the label contact needed to name the modules, but it supplies no experiment on ribosomal realization, no structural assay, no folding contact, no admissibility test in a physical medium, no function assay, and no reason to call the pattern a universal law.  Any such promotion would require a separate observation designed for that higher layer.  Within the present finite row, the admissible claim is only that the local codon-tile pattern classifies the thirteen selected codons into the six displayed modules with complete coverage and with the recorded leave-one behavior.

\paragraph{Scope boundary.}
The support condition is deliberately narrow. The curated standard-code table supports codon-label readback and local split checks. It does not observe translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or universality. Amino-acid label-sequence readback is therefore not protein-structure readback. A local codon tile is not a global biological law. Window-six geometry alone is not translation, folding, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality. Any claim crossing those boundaries would require a separate contact that directly supplies the higher-layer observation. In this record, no such contact is present; the result remains a code-read statement about a finite codon row, its local closure behavior, and its complete module classification.

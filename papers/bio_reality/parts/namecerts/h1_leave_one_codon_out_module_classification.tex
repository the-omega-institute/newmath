It tests code-layer readback and local codon-tile label splits; it does not test translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. \paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The supported conclusion is deliberately narrow.  Window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table, and the leave-one-codon-out module table gives a complete six-module partition of the $13$ residual codons.  The curated table supplies codon-label readback only.  No translation process, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or law of biology follows from this contact.  Any claim crossing from code labels to translation, folding, admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact with its own finite-row audit.

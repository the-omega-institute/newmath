\paragraph{Finite carrier and code-layer contact.}
The carrier for this record is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a finite coordinate display for the $64$ codons of the standard code.  The external contact is only the curated standard-code table: it assigns each codon an amino-acid or stop label, and therefore supports code-layer readback.  It is not a translation experiment, not a folding experiment, not a physical admissibility test, not a functional assay, not a mechanism, and not a universality proof.  All claims below are therefore confined to the code-read layer.

\begin{aligned}

Thus the record is not merely a list of exceptional labels; it is a finite subcomplex datum inside the window-six coordinate cube.  The geometric language here is descriptive of the finite coordinate object.  It does not assert that biological translation is generated by this geometry.

\paragraph{Module classification.}
The complete module classification assigns all $13$ codons of $R$ and leaves no residue:
$$
\begin{aligned}
R_{\mathrm{size}}&=13,\\
\mathrm{assigned\ count}&=13,\\
\mathrm{extra\ module\ codons}&=\varnothing,\\
\mathrm{missing\ from\ modules}&=\varnothing,\\
\mathrm{unassigned\ codons}&=\varnothing.
\end{aligned}
$$
The six reported modules are
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{AGR}&=\{\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG}\},\\
\mathrm{AR\_Lys}&=\{\mathrm{AAA}\},\\
\mathrm{CUN}&=\{\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU}\},\\

$$
Each module has at least one predictable-or-boundary witness, and the union of the modules is exactly $R$.  This is the finite-row content of the claim: the leave-one-codon-out row is not an unstructured residue, but a partition into familiar codon-label modules at the code-read layer.

The local tile supports the following bounded conclusion.  In the window-six codon cube, the standard-code label table determines a $13$-codon punctured-cube row with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$, nontrivial spectral scalars, low null-model exact-shape rates, strong leave-one-code-out recovery, perfect reported leave-one-codon-out recovery, and a complete six-module classification.  The claim stops there.  No translation realization, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows from this record unless a separate contact supplies that higher-layer evidence.

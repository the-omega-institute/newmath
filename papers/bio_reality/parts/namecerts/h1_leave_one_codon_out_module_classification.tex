\paragraph{Finite carrier and reality contact.}
This record treats the standard-code codon table as a code-read object over the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$. The finite carrier is not a biochemical mechanism. It is a coordinate display in which codons are represented as vertices, local tile faces are read as finite boundary data, singleton label corners and three-point label classes are distinguished, and the only external contact is the curated standard-code table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label. The contact therefore supports codon-label readback and local split checks, but it does not by itself observe translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or any law forcing DNA to obey the displayed geometry.

The exact shape was matched once, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$, and $25$ samples had Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving $p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}=0.0125$. Under the amino-acid-class-preserving null model, again with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, the observed $M$-size is $20$, the exact-match count is $0$, and the lower-tail count at or below the observed $M$-size is $7$. The corresponding reported values are
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}}&=0.0,\\
p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}&=0.0035.
\end{aligned}
$$
These controls say that the observed finite shape is not typical in the tested row ensembles. They do not identify the historical, chemical, or evolutionary cause of the standard code.

\begin{aligned}

\mathrm{missing\ from\ modules}&=0,\\
\mathrm{extra\ module\ codons}&=0,\\
\mathrm{unassigned\ codons}&=0.
\end{aligned}
$$
The concrete module classes are
$$
\begin{aligned}

The classification is finite and row-local: it says that the window-six coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation data organize the residual standard-code labels into these six modules.

Nothing in this record claims that the local codon tile is a global biological law. Nothing here claims translation realization, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Amino-acid label-sequence readback remains label readback.

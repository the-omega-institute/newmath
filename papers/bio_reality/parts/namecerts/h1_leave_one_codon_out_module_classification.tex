\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
The finite carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a codon display with three nucleotide positions and two bits per position. The reality contact used here is only the curated standard-code label table: it assigns each codon to an amino-acid or stop label, and it is not a contact with translation dynamics, folded structure, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality. The row-level object isolated by the window-six coordinate calculation is the thirteen-codon set
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA, AGA, AGG, AUA, CUA, CUC, CUG, CUU,\\
&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The finite audit gives $|R|=13$. As a subcomplex of the local codon cube, this row has face data
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4),
\end{aligned}
$$
so the observed object is not merely a list of exceptional labels; it is a punctured local tile with thirteen vertices, sixteen local edges, and four local square faces. This is the BEDC minimal form for the present claim: carrier $Q_6$, distinctions given by six-bit display, local tile face, singleton label corner, and three-point label class, internal structure by coordinate, closure, spectrum, and relation, with readback through the standard-code label table.

\paragraph{Module classification.}
The module classification partitions all thirteen row codons and leaves no residue. The recorded modules are
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{agr}&=\{AGA,AGG\},\\
\mathrm{ar\_lys}&=\{AAA\},\\
\mathrm{cun}&=\{CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU\},\\
\mathrm{ile\_met}&=\{AUA\},\\
\mathrm{ssn}&=\{UCA,UUA\},\\
\mathrm{stop\_trp}&=\{UAA,UAG,UGA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The completeness row is exact: $R_{\mathrm{size}}=13$, assigned count $13$, extra module codons $\emptyset$, missing-from-modules set $\emptyset$, and unassigned-codons set $\emptyset$. Equivalently, the module union is the row itself,
$$
\begin{aligned}
R&=\mathrm{agr}\cup\mathrm{ar\_lys}\cup\mathrm{cun}\cup\mathrm{ile\_met}\cup\mathrm{ssn}\cup\mathrm{stop\_trp},\\
|R|&=13,
\end{aligned}
$$
with no observed codon outside the six named classes and no row codon omitted by them. Each named module has at least predictable-or-boundary status in the finite audit: the two AGR codons $AGA,AGG$, the singleton arginine-lysine corner $AAA$, the four CUN codons $CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU$, the singleton isoleucine-methionine corner $AUA$, the SSN pair $UCA,UUA$, and the stop-tryptophan triple $UAA,UAG,UGA$ are all accounted for inside the local boundary classification.

\paragraph{Verdict and boundary.}
The chapter-level claim is therefore ready at the code-read layer: window-six codon coordinates expose a finite local boundary-tile structure whose thirteen codons are completely assigned to six biologically named code-label modules, and the leave-one audits recover the same row with high finite-row rates. The claim is deliberately local. It says that the curated standard-code table supplies enough external contact to read codon labels and to test the local split. It does not say that DNA obeys the window-six geometry as a biological necessity, that a local codon tile is a global biological law, that amino-acid label readback is folded protein structure, or that the observed partition proves translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Any such promotion requires a separate higher-layer reality contact.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and contact layer.}
The carrier for this record is the six-bit codon display $Q_6$, read only through the standard-code label table.  The biological contact is therefore a codon-to-label contact: it says that a curated standard genetic-code table assigns each codon an amino-acid or stop label.  It does not say that the window-six geometry is translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or a universal law.  The distinction used here is narrower: a local tile face in $Q_6$ is compared with singleton label corners and three-point label classes, and the readback is the standard-code label table itself.

\paragraph{Recovered residual row.}
The residual codon row $R$ has cardinality $13$.  Its verified members are
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.

$$
The same row is certified as a punctured-cube object with finite face vector $(13,16,4)$.  Thus the row is not being treated as an informal visual resemblance: the verified finite object has $13$ vertices, $16$ one-dimensional incidences, and $4$ two-dimensional faces in the recovered local carrier.  The module-classification run assigns all $13$ codons in this row, with $R\_\mathrm{size}=13$, assigned count $13$, no extra module codons, no missing module codons, and no unassigned codons.

This is the chapter-specific content of the claim: the local $Q_6$ tile separates the $13$-codon residual row into named finite modules that remain within the code-read layer.

\paragraph{Boundary statement.}
The verdict of this chapter is a finite local classification, not a global biological theory.  The contact supplies codon-label readback and local split checks only.  Therefore no claim is made here that DNA obeys the $Q_6$ geometry as a biological necessity, that a codon-tile split realizes translation, that an amino-acid label sequence is a folded protein, or that the displayed row proves structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Any such higher-layer assertion would require a separate contact whose observed fact lives at that higher layer.  In the present record, the supported statement is exactly that the curated standard-code labels, when read through the six-bit codon carrier, expose a $13$-codon punctured-cube residual row whose six modules are completely assigned and whose members are predictable or boundary codons under the leave-one-codon-out module classification.

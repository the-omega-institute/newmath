\paragraph{Boundary status before contact.}
The conjectural layer is $\mathrm{orf\_eligibility}$.  Its seed statement is intentionally weaker than an ORF claim: a start--stop-window eligibility packet remains at boundary status until a separate sequence, transcript, or curated annotation contact supplies an observed interval.  The probe $\mathrm{orf.eligibility.boundary.requires.contact}$ is broken if the pipeline asserts ORF eligibility, translation, folding, physical admissibility, or function from start--stop enumeration alone.  The companion probe $\mathrm{orf.eligibility.contact.gap.check}$ is broken if the packet records an ORF eligibility claim while no sequence database entry, transcript evidence, or curated annotation can test the proposed interval.  Thus the local rule is strict: codon geometry may describe what would be tested, but the biological interval must come from outside this enumeration.

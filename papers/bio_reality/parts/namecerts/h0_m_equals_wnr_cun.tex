This is the whole finite object used by the present chapter. The biological reading considered here is not that these codons are already realized in a transcript, translated product, folded protein, or organismal function. The reading is narrower: the row supplies a closed codon-side candidate geometry against which an independently supplied interval annotation may later be tested for open-reading-frame eligibility.

\subsection{Open-reading-frame boundary from the finite codon row}
\label{sec:bioreality-h0-m-equals-wnr-cun}

\paragraph{Finite row under audit.}
The audited finite row isolates the codon set $M$ obtained from the stated $WNR \cup CUN$ enumeration.  The observed row gives $|M|=20$ and records the complete list
$$
\begin{aligned}
M=\{&AAA,AAG,ACA,ACG,AGA,AGG,AUA,AUG,CUA,CUC,\
& CUG,CUU,UAA,UAG,UCA,UCG,UGA,UGG,UUA,UUG\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
This is a finite combinatorial datum.  It is not, by itself, a biological observation of an expressed interval, a translated product, a folded molecule, an admissible physical state, a molecular mechanism, a biological function, or a universal rule about genomes.  Its role in this chapter is narrower: it supplies the start and stop vocabulary against which an open-reading-frame eligibility question can be posed once a separate curated biological interval is supplied.

\paragraph{Motif compression and saturation facts.}

$$
The finite check reports no missing motif entries and no extra motif entries.  The $f_3$ saturation row gives
$$
\begin{aligned}

\begin{aligned}

\end{aligned}

Thus the codon-level input is not an unconstrained biological assertion.  It is a closed finite enumeration with a thirteen-element motif core, an eighteen-element saturation row, an empty saturation boundary, and two explicitly named residual codons.

\paragraph{ORF eligibility contact boundary.}
The open-reading-frame layer is stricter than the codon geometry.  A start codon and a stop codon can define a window-shaped test, but a window-shaped test is not yet an observed open reading frame.  The row named $h2.orf.start\_stop.window\_eligibility$ loaded one external window dataset with one input window, classified that one window by start--stop enumeration, and explicitly scoped the result to external-window start--stop enumeration only.  The recorded label for the non-promotion rule is $orf\_eligibility\_only$.  Hence this chapter may say that the finite classifier can recognize the syntactic start--stop pattern in the supplied window, but it may not say that translation, folding, physical admissibility, mechanism, function, or universality follows from the codon geometry alone.

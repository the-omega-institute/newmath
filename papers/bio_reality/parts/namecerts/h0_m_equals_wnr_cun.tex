\paragraph{Local claim boundary.}
The local conclusion is that ORF eligibility may be recorded only where the finite start--stop classifier is paired with the curated interval contact just described.  The codon row $M$, the motif row $R$, the saturation result, and the quotient representatives do not independently establish translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or a general biological law. Consequently, this chapter certifies a contacted ORF-eligibility record for the stated finite dataset and refuses every stronger biological reading unless another contact, with its own audit trail, supplies it.

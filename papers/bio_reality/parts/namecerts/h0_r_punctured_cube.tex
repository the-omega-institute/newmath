\paragraph{Finite carrier.}

$$
\begin{aligned}

&UAA, UAG, UCA, UGA, UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the row cardinality is $|R|=13$. In the cubical incidence structure induced by the window-six coordinates, this row has finite face data

The phrase ``punctured cube'' is used here in this finite sense: the row contains thirteen codon vertices, sixteen local codon-adjacency edges, and four local tile faces. No continuum, ambient biological law, or global geometric necessity is being asserted.

\paragraph{Code-label contact.}
The only external contact used by this record is a curated standard-code table assigning each codon to an amino-acid or stop label. This contact can test code-layer readback and a local codon-tile label split. It cannot test translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Consequently, the row $R$ is a finite code-read object: it is allowed to say that the standard-code labels make a local boundary pattern visible, but it is not allowed to say that this pattern is a protein structure or a biological mechanism.

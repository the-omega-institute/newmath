\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The contact used here is the curated standard-code label table.  It supports codon-label readback and local tile checks.  It does not test translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  In particular, amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure, and a window-six local tile is not a global biological law.  Any claim crossing from this code-read row into translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate contact designed for that layer.

\paragraph{Finite carrier.}

$$
\begin{aligned}

&UAA,UAG,UCA,UGA,UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The independent cardinality row reports the same set and gives $|R|=13$.  Thus the finite-row content is not a qualitative picture alone: the row names all thirteen codons, and the matching cardinality check returns exactly $13$.

\paragraph{Punctured-cube shape.}
The primary shape assertion is that $R$ realizes a punctured-cube boundary in the local codon display.  Its recorded face data have $f$-vector

\paragraph{Reality contact and boundary.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code table, which assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  That contact is sufficient for code-read label checks and for local tile split tests.  It is not a contact for translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or biological necessity.  Consequently the verdict is local: window-six coordinates expose a punctured-cube boundary row with strong finite-row support, but the row remains a code-layer boundary unless a separate higher-layer contact is supplied.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is still label readback; no protein fold, structural order, admissible physical dynamics, function, mechanism, or universality follows from the punctured-cube row alone.

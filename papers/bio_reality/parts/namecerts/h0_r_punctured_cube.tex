It does not supply translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  In particular, amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.  Any movement from this local codon tile to translation, folding, energetic admissibility, function, mechanism, or a global biological law requires a separate reality contact carrying that higher-layer information.

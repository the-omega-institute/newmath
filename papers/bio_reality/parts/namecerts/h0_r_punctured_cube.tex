\paragraph{Finite carrier and row set.} The record concerns the standard-code codon table only at the code-read layer. The finite carrier is the six-bit codon display $Q_6$, read as the ordinary codon cube after the nucleotides have been encoded into window-six coordinates. Within this carrier the distinguished boundary row is the thirteen-codon set
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA,AGA,AGG,AUA,CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU,\\
&UAA,UAG,UCA,UGA,UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The independent cardinality row gives $|R|=13$, with the same ordered codon support $AAA,AGA,AGG,AUA,CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU,UAA,UAG,UCA,UGA,UUA$. The punctured-cube row asserts that this $R$ is not merely a selected list but realizes a local punctured-cube tile in the codon-coordinate carrier. Its face data are summarized by the finite $f$-vector
$$
\begin{aligned}
(f_0,f_1,f_2)=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the object being recorded is a local tile with thirteen vertices, sixteen one-dimensional incidences, and four two-dimensional faces. The biological readback is the curated standard-code label table: each codon has an amino-acid or stop label, but that label table is a reality contact for code labels rather than a mechanism for translation.

\paragraph{Reality contact and cannot-claim boundary.} The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code bridge, whose observed fact is that a standard-code table assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels. This contact can test code-read and local codon-tile label split claims. It cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, folded protein structure, biological function, or mechanism-level universality. Therefore the local claim is exactly this: in window-six codon coordinates, the standard-code label table exposes a thirteen-codon punctured-cube boundary row with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$, spectral values $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, $\mu=3.051487585298128$, significant null-model rows, complete module assignment, and strong leave-one recovery statistics. Nothing in this chapter promotes that local code-read tile into translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. Any such promotion requires a separate higher-layer contact.

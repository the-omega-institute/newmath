\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
The result is a local codon-tile statement at the code-read layer.  It does not assert translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or a global biological law.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded protein structure.

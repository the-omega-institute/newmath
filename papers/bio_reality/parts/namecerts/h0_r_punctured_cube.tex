\paragraph{Finite carrier and reality contact.}

The biological contact is deliberately narrow: the table assigns each codon to an amino-acid or stop label, and therefore supports code-layer readback and local label-split checks. It does not by itself observe translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality. The claim tested here is the finite-row statement that a distinguished boundary set $R$ inside the codon cube is exactly a punctured local cube pattern, not that the cell geometry is a global biological law.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The result is a local codon-tile boundary statement over a curated standard-code label table. It supports the claim that the window-six coordinate display exposes a distinguished thirteen-codon punctured-cube row with finite spectral and recovery structure. It does not support any stronger claim without a separate contact. In particular, no translation process, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or necessity law follows from this record alone. Amino-acid label-sequence readback remains a code-layer readback, not a structure or function observation.

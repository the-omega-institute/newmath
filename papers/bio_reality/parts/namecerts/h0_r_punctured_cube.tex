\subsection{The punctured codon cube row}\label{subsec:h0-r-punctured-cube}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and observed row.}
This row treats the standard-code codon table as a curated code-layer contact and asks what is visible after codons are placed in a six-bit window coordinate. The observed residual set is the thirteen-codon subset
$R=\{\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}$.
The finite check records the same set under the cardinality row, with $|R|=13$, and under the punctured-cube row, with cell count vector $(13,16,4)$. In this chapter the word ``punctured'' is only a coordinate description: it says that the listed codons occupy a local cube-like boundary pattern after the window-six display is fixed. It does not say that the genetic code is generated by that geometry, nor that the geometry is a biological mechanism.

\paragraph{Local combinatorics.}
The row is small enough to state as an explicit finite object. The verified equality is
$$
\begin{aligned}
R
&=\{\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\},\\
(f_0,f_1,f_2)&=(13,16,4).
\end{aligned}
$$
Thus the row contributes a finite local boundary signature rather than a narrative about translation. The singleton corner and three-point label class are read as code-label phenomena in the curated table. The associated module audit partitions all thirteen codons without leftovers: $\mathrm{agr}=\{\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG}\}$, $\mathrm{ar\_lys}=\{\mathrm{AAA}\}$, $\mathrm{cun}=\{\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU}\}$, $\mathrm{ile\_met}=\{\mathrm{AUA}\}$, $\mathrm{ssn}=\{\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UUA}\}$, and $\mathrm{stop\_trp}=\{\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UGA}\}$. The module-classification row reports $R$-size $13$, assigned count $13$, no extra module codons, no missing codons, and no unassigned codons.

\paragraph{Spectral and sampling checks.}
The spectrum row gives a numerical companion to the finite shape. The recorded values are $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$. The radial cubic residual is $-6.224354365258478\cdot 10^{-12}$, the same value recorded as the cubic-polynomial residual, so the numerical certificate is at roundoff scale rather than at biological scale. The row for shape significance used $N=2000$ samples with seed $42$ and $57377$ distinct-sample attempts, including $55377$ duplicate retries. It found $1$ exact-shape match and $25$ matches with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving
$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{exact}}&=0.0005,\\
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}&=0.0125.
\end{aligned}
$$
A second null row preserving amino-acid classes recorded observed $M$-size $20$, $N=2000$, no exact matches, and $7$ samples in the lower tail at or below the observed $M$-size. Its reported probabilities are $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}}=0.0$ and $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}=0.0035$. These numbers support the claim that the local pattern is not a common output of the tested finite shuffles, but they still remain code-layer evidence.

These rows make the punctured-cube signal operational: when a codon or code row is withheld, the finite coordinate and boundary data still recover a large fraction of the code-label targets under the stated tests.

\paragraph{Reality contact and blocked promotions.}
The only contact used here is the curated standard-code label table. It says which codons carry which amino-acid or stop labels in the standard code. It does not observe ribosomal realization, folding, structural order, admissible chemistry, function, or a universal law of coding geometry. Consequently, the chapter licenses the following narrow statement: the listed thirteen codons form a verified local punctured-cube row in the six-bit coordinate, and the associated finite tests show nontrivial code-label regularity. The chapter does not license the stronger statements that translation is explained, that protein structure is predicted, that physical admissibility follows, that biological function follows, that all organisms must obey this local tile, or that amino-acid label readback is already folded structure. Any such promotion would require a separate contact that actually measures the higher layer.

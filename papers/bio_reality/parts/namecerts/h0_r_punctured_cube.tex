The readback contact is the curated standard-code table, so every biological label used here is an external reality contact at the code-read layer rather than a consequence of the cube geometry itself.

\paragraph{Null-model pressure.}
The shape-significance run used $N=2000$ sampled alternatives with seed $42$.  It required $57377$ distinct-sample attempts and recorded $55377$ duplicate retries.  There was $1$ exact shape match, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$, and $25$ samples with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving $p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}=0.0125$.  A separate amino-acid-class-preserving null model, again with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, reported observed $M$-size $20$, exact match count $0$, lower-tail count $7$, $p_{\mathrm{class\ exact}}=0.0$, and $p_{\mathrm{class\ lower\ tail}}=0.0035$. This is not a statement that the geometry is a universal law of coding, a law of translation, or a sufficient explanation of molecular function.

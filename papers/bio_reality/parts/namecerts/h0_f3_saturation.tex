\paragraph{Boundary of interpretation.}
The finite saturation row is allowed to say that, for this codon-level enumeration, $\mathrm{ACA}$ and $\mathrm{ACG}$ are precisely the two elements of $M$ absent from $\mathrm{Sat}_{f_3}(R)$, and that the recorded boundary for $f_3$ is empty.  It is also allowed to coexist with a separate curated interval contact whose row count, split counts, and binomial values are explicitly recorded.  It is not allowed to say that codon geometry alone has produced an observed ORF, a translated peptide, a folded structure, physical viability, cellular function, a biochemical mechanism, or a general law across genomes.  Any such assertion would require a separate contact whose data type actually tests that layer.  The local conclusion is therefore an ORF-eligibility boundary conclusion: finite start/stop-style enumeration can be audited here, while every stronger biological reading remains outside the claim.

\paragraph{Finite packet.}
The record named $h0.f3.saturation$ is a finite codon-level saturation observation used here only to delimit an open-reading-frame eligibility question. \paragraph{Local conclusion.}
The finite saturation fact $h0.f3.saturation$ supplies an exact codon enumeration and quotient-compression boundary for an ORF-eligibility seed.  Before interval evidence is attached, it is only a testable boundary question.  With the curated interval contact, the chapter may state that this particular external interval dataset supports start--stop ORF eligibility under the frozen classifier and controls described above.  Even after that contact, the conclusion remains layer-limited: it is not evidence of translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Any one of those stronger claims would need its own separately recorded contact and its own finite controls.

The local rule for this chapter is therefore strict. The quotient geometry verifies finite codon-row and quotient-row data. The ORF window enumeration verifies a one-window classification input. The self-check records three checked gates and explicitly states that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers. Promotion beyond this boundary requires a separate reality contact, such as a curated ORF interval annotation, sequence database contact, transcript evidence, or another external record capable of testing the proposed biological interval.

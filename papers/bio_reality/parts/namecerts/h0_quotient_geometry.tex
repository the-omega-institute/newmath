The finite geometry is linked to two boundary probes.  Probe \texttt{orf.eligibility.boundary.requires.contact} breaks if the pipeline asserts ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, or biological function from start/stop-window enumeration alone.  Probe \texttt{orf.eligibility.contact.gap.check} breaks if an ORF-eligibility claim is recorded while no sequence database, transcript evidence, or curated annotation contact can test the proposed interval.

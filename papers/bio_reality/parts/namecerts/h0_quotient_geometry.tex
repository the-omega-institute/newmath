The only admissible reading is conditional: the finite geometry says what kind of boundary coordinate would have to be connected to an external biological interval before an ORF-eligibility claim could be tested. The packet has no reality-contact reference. Therefore the carrier value in the BEDC-minimal form remains awaiting reality contact, the listed distinction remains awaiting reality contact, the internal structure is recorded as none, and the readback remains awaiting reality contact.

It also breaks if an ORF-eligibility claim is recorded while no sequence database entry, transcript evidence, curated interval annotation, or comparable external contact is available to test the interval.

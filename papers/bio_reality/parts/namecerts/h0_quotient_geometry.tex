\paragraph{Local admissible reading.}
The admissible statement is narrow. The finite computation supplies a codon set $M$ of size $20$, a motif-derived subset $R$ of size $13$, an $18$-element saturation list missing $\mathrm{ACA}$ and $\mathrm{ACG}$ from $M$, an $M$-level $f$-vector $(20,38,26,8,1)$, a quotient of size $10$, and a quotient $f$-vector $(10,14,6,1)$.

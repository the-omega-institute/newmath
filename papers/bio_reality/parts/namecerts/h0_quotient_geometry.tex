The admissible claim is narrow. Combining these data permits a statement about enumerated eligibility boundaries: a curated finite window can be tested for the presence of the required start/stop pattern, and in this packet exactly one such input window was classified. That self-check is part of the scientific content of the record: the geometry is allowed to organize finite codon distinctions, but it is not allowed to manufacture biological realization.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
No translation, folding, physical admissibility, molecular mechanism, organism-level function, or universal biological law follows from these rows.  The quotient table does not supply a ribosome, a transcript, an expression assay, a protein product, a folding measurement, a thermodynamic admissibility test, or a functional phenotype.

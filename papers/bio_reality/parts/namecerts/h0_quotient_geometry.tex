\paragraph{ORF eligibility is a contact-dependent layer.}
The biological object named by the row is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration. Therefore the quotient geometry may organize finite codon windows, but it does not by itself supply an observed interval, a transcript product, a translated peptide, a folded molecule, a physical admissibility statement, a cellular role, a mechanism, or a universal biological law.

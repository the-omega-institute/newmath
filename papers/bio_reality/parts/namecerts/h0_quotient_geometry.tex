\paragraph{Relation to open-reading-frame eligibility.}
The biological label attached to the seed is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration.  At this level the packet records only a combinatorial prerequisite: it can name finite start/stop-window patterns and quotient rows that would be relevant to a test.  It cannot, by itself, declare that a window is an observed open reading frame.  It also cannot declare translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality.  Each of those stronger readings would require its own contact with biological data capable of testing that reading.

\paragraph{ORF-eligibility interpretation.}
The biological layer considered here is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration. The one-window start/stop enumeration check reports one loaded external window and one classified window, with scope restricted to external window start/stop enumeration only. The recorded snapshot date for that window check is $2026$-$05$-$25$, and the finite row explicitly states that the result remains at the $\mathrm{orf\_eligibility}$ layer. Thus the quotient rows may be used only as a way to organize which finite start/stop-window questions are being asked. They do not certify translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.

\paragraph{Local boundary of the claim.}
The self-check records three checked gates and states that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers. The finite quotient geometry therefore has the following local status: it is a finite symbolic organization of $M$ and $\pi M$, and it can be paired with a separately supplied curated interval contact for the restricted question of ORF-window eligibility. It cannot, from its own coordinates or from start/stop enumeration alone, assert translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or a universal biological law. Any such stronger assertion would require a distinct contact whose evidence is appropriate to that stronger layer.

\paragraph{Biological boundary.}
The self-check states that the packet is a BioReality self-check only and that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers; the checked gate count is $3$. Consequently, the local claim boundary is strict. Start/stop-window geometry, motif compression, saturation, and quotient representatives do not imply translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality. They also do not by themselves establish that any sequence contains an open reading frame. A separate contact, such as a curated ORF interval annotation or a sequence/transcript record capable of testing the interval, is required before an $\mathrm{orf\_eligibility}$ claim can be made. Until such a contact is attached, this chapter records only the finite quotient geometry of the boundary probe.

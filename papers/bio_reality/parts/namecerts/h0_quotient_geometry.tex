\paragraph{Boundary interpretation.}
The biological object named by the packet is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration. At this layer, the coordinate geometry supplies a testable shape for a possible eligibility rule, not evidence that any interval is present in a genome, transcribed, translated, folded, physically viable, functional, mechanistic, or universal. The self-check row records three checked gates and states that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers. This local restriction is part of the mathematical content of the packet: a start/stop window may be represented as a finite enumerated candidate only after an independent biological interval contact supplies the interval to be tested.

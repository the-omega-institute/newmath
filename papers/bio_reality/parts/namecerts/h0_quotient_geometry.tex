\paragraph{Finite-row object.}
The record named \texttt{h0.quotient.geometry} is a finite coordinate statement about a start/stop-window enumeration packet, not an observed biological open reading frame. Its biological target is \texttt{orf\_eligibility}, but the carrier is explicitly still awaiting reality contact. The finite row is therefore read as a boundary object: it records what has been enumerated inside the BEDC coordinate and what cannot yet be promoted to biology. The attached quotient geometry has ambient $M$-row data with $20$ codon rows and face vector

\end{aligned}
$$

\paragraph{Reality-contact gap.}
The contact ledger for this row is empty. The required contact class is a curated ORF interval annotation, or an equivalent external sequence or transcript contact able to test an observed interval. Because no such contact is attached, the packet remains at seed-boundary status. The local support condition is therefore conditional and negative: a start/stop-window ORF eligibility seed may be retained only as a boundary probe until an external sequence, transcript, or curated annotation supplies the biological interval to be tested. The local break condition is equally explicit: the packet would fail its own boundary discipline if it asserted ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality from start/stop-window enumeration alone.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The quotient geometry supplies a finite coordinate with $20$ ambient rows, $10$ quotient rows, face vectors $(20,38,26,8,1)$ and $(10,14,6,1)$, and the codon-set bookkeeping listed above. Nothing more follows locally. In particular, no translation event follows; no folded product follows; no physical admissibility follows; no biochemical function follows; no molecular mechanism follows; and no universality claim follows. The row is admissible only as a BioReality boundary record for \texttt{orf\_eligibility}: it identifies the exact finite data that would have to meet reality, while marking that the reality contact has not yet occurred.

It does not say that a biological interval has been seen.

\paragraph{ORF-eligibility boundary.}
The biological object attached to this packet is open-reading-frame eligibility from start and stop codon enumeration.  At the seed boundary, start--stop-window geometry is allowed to specify what would have to be tested, but it is not itself an observation of translation or of an annotated coding interval.  The start--stop enumeration contact later loaded one external nucleotide-window dataset with one input window and classified one window under the declared start/stop rule; its scope was explicitly $orf\_eligibility\_only$ and its snapshot date was 2026-05-25.  Thus the coordinate packet may be compared with a curated window record, but the coordinate packet is not the record.

\paragraph{Curated interval contact.}
A stronger curated-interval contact was separately recorded with $168$ rows from NCBI RefSeq GenBank XML CDS intervals for \textit{Escherichia coli} K-12 MG1655 accession NC\_000913.3, snapshot date 2026-05-30.  Its input classes were $72$ positive curated ORF rows, $72$ matched non-ORF decoys, and $24$ boundary cases.  The frozen classifier accepted $72$ of $72$ positives and rejected $72$ of $72$ matched decoys.  The exact one-sided binomial upper-tail value reported for positive acceptance was $2.117582368135751\times 10^{-22}$, and the same value was reported for matched-decoy rejection and for the predeclared pairwise sign-test advantage with $72$ wins out of $72$ pairs.  The three held-out folds each had $24$ positives and $24$ decoys, with positive accept rate $1.0$ and decoy reject rate $1.0$ in the left-locus, middle-locus, and right-locus folds.  The boundary controls had $24$ rows, with $0$ promoted and promoted fraction $0.0$.

\paragraph{Local interpretation.}
The evidence therefore supports a narrow claim: the finite quotient geometry is a reproducible coordinate calculation, and a separately supplied curated interval dataset can test start--stop eligibility under a frozen classifier.  The self-check recorded three checked gates and states the boundary as no promotion across biological layers.  No translation event, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, molecular mechanism, or universal biological law follows from the quotient geometry, from the codon lists, or from start/stop enumeration alone.  Such claims would require a distinct observation or curated contact whose data directly bear on the higher layer being asserted.

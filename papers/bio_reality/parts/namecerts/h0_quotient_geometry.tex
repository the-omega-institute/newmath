\paragraph{Finite row and biological reading.}
This BioReality packet records a finite-row geometry attached to open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration. Its biological object is not an observed open reading frame, not a translated product, and not a functional sequence. The packet is a boundary record: it describes what the finite coordinate system can say before any curated sequence interval, transcript contact, or external annotation has been attached. The carrier is therefore recorded as awaiting reality contact, with no internal biological structure beyond the finite codon-level enumeration. In this chapter, the phrase ORF-eligibility means only the start/stop-window eligibility boundary induced by the supplied finite row.

The verified middle set $M$ has cardinality $20$ and is exactly the listed union of the WNR and CUN rows:

\paragraph{Reality-contact gate.}
The packet contains no reality contact. In particular, it has no curated ORF interval annotation, no transcript evidence, no sequence-database interval, and no external biological assay. The two boundary probes require such a contact before ORF eligibility may be asserted as more than a testable boundary condition. The first probe breaks if the pipeline asserts ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, or biological function from start/stop-window enumeration alone. The second breaks if the packet records an ORF-eligibility claim while no sequence database, transcript evidence, or curated annotation can test the proposed interval.

\paragraph{Local cannot-claim boundary.}
The finite quotient geometry therefore supports only this restricted conclusion: the supplied start/stop-window enumeration has the verified finite rows and quotient data displayed above, and these data define a boundary probe for a possible ORF-eligibility contact. No translation follows. No folding follows. No physical admissibility follows. No biological function follows. No mechanism follows. No universality follows. The self-check records the same boundary in operational form: BioReality performs only a self-check here, and no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers.

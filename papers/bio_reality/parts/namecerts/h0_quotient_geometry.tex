\paragraph{ORF-eligibility gate.}
The local biological object is open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration. The contact list is empty, and no reality-contact reference is attached. For that reason the carrier, distinctions, and readback of the BEDC minimal form remain at boundary status rather than at biological realization status. \paragraph{Local falsifiability condition.}

Thus the record is ready as a BioReality chapter precisely because it separates a verified finite geometry from every higher biological claim that would require independent reality contact.

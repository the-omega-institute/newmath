\paragraph{ORF-eligibility gate.}
The biological object named by this record is open-reading-frame eligibility from start--stop codon enumeration.  The verified packet, however, contains no sequence database contact, no transcript evidence, no curated interval annotation, and no experimentally observed biological interval.  Consequently the layer $\mathrm{orf\_eligibility}$ remains at boundary status.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The local boundary is strict. From the twenty codon rows, the thirteen-row motif set, the eighteen-row saturation set, the ten quotient rows, the face vectors $(20,38,26,8,1)$ and $(10,14,6,1)$, and the one classified external start/stop window, it does not follow that translation occurs. It does not follow that any peptide folds. It does not follow that a physical conformation is admissible, that a molecular mechanism is present, that a cellular function is realized, or that the pattern is universal across organisms, transcripts, reading frames, or genetic codes. Such conclusions require a separate reality contact, for example a curated ORF interval annotation, sequence or transcript evidence, translation evidence, structural evidence, or a functional assay, depending on the promoted layer. In the present packet the reality-contact list is empty and the mismatch list is empty, so the correct reading is a contact-gap boundary record: the finite geometry is ready to constrain a future ORF-eligibility test, but it is not itself the test result.

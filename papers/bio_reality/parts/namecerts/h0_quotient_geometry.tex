\paragraph{Local biological interpretation.}
The biological layer under discussion is only \texttt{orf\_eligibility}. The finite geometry can describe start--stop-window eligibility conditions, but it does not observe expression, ribosome engagement, peptide production, folding, biochemical viability, cellular role, mechanism, or general law. In this row the declared carrier and readback remain awaiting contact, and the listed contacts and mismatches are both empty. Therefore the correct local statement is conditional: if a separate curated interval, transcript, or sequence observation is attached, then the finite start--stop enumeration can serve as the predeclared eligibility classifier for that interval; without such an attachment, it remains a boundary test specification.

Even when that contact is present, the conclusion remains confined to curated interval eligibility under the frozen start--stop classifier.

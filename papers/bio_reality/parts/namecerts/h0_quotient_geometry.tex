\paragraph{ORF-eligibility boundary.}
The conjectural biological object is open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration.  The packet has no attached reality contacts: no curated ORF interval annotation, no sequence database contact, no transcript evidence, and no experimental row testing an interval in a biological sample.  Therefore the correct local conclusion is negative and bounded.  The finite quotient geometry may be used as a boundary probe saying what kind of external contact would be needed before an ORF-eligibility statement could be made.  It may not itself assert ORF eligibility.

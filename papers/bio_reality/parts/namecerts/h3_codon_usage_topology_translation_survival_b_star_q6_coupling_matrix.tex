\subsection{B-star-Q-six residual coupling against a translation-proxy spectrum}
\label{sec:b-star-q-six-residual-coupling-translation-proxy}

\paragraph{Finite object.}
The record considered here is a finite codon-count experiment, not a statement about a universal biological law.  The codon count space has $61$ sense-codon coordinates, and the BEDC residual side is the nine-coordinate list
$\{\mathrm{Arg\_AGR},\mathrm{Ile\_AUA},\mathrm{K\_AAA},\mathrm{Leu\_CUN\_vs\_UUR},\mathrm{Leu\_UUA\_vs\_UUG},\mathrm{Ser\_UCA\_vs\_UCG},\mathrm{Ser\_UCR\_vs\_AGY},\mathrm{Thr\_ACR\_vs\_ACY},\mathrm{f3\_stress}\}$.
The organism row is the three-element list
$\{\mathrm{Homo\ sapiens},\mathrm{Saccharomyces\ cerevisiae},\mathrm{Escherichia\ coli\ K12}\}$.
The readout available in this finite row is not ribosome occupancy, protein abundance, measured translation efficiency, or a tAI/stAI table.  It is the proxy spectrum described as the tRNA-Leu CUN-versus-UUR log-copy spectrum.  The run identifier is $\mathrm{4a783972e74b}$, and the finite computation was recorded at $\mathrm{2026\text{-}05\text{-}31T20{:}34{:}39{+}00{:}00}$.

\paragraph{Residual projection.}
Let $q_i$ denote one of the nine synonymous residual coordinates after the amino-acid quotient has been imposed.  Let $\ell$ denote the available proxy readout vector, and let $\ell^{\perp}$ denote its synonymous projection against the amino-acid composition component.  The finite coupling entry is read as
$$
\begin{aligned}
A_i &= q_i^{T}\Sigma \ell^{\perp},\\
q_i &\in \{\mathrm{Arg\_AGR},\mathrm{Ile\_AUA},\mathrm{K\_AAA},\mathrm{Leu\_CUN\_vs\_UUR},\mathrm{Leu\_UUA\_vs\_UUG},\\
&\qquad \mathrm{Ser\_UCA\_vs\_UCG},\mathrm{Ser\_UCR\_vs\_AGY},\mathrm{Thr\_ACR\_vs\_ACY},\mathrm{f3\_stress}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
Here $\Sigma$ is the residual count covariance used by the finite-row computation.  The imposed break threshold was $|A_i|<0.01$ for all nine residual coordinates.  That break condition was not met: all nine entries were nonzero under the supplied proxy contact, and the maximum absolute coupling was $1882464.1106377328$.

\paragraph{Observed finite-row outcome.}
The supplied row therefore supports only the following bounded conclusion.  In the three-organism proxy contact, the nine-coordinate residual basis is not numerically silent against the tRNA-Leu CUN-versus-UUR log-copy spectrum.  The finite statement is
$$
\begin{aligned}
\max_i |A_i| &= 1882464.1106377328,\\
\#\{i: A_i\neq 0\} &= 9,\\
\theta_A &= 0.01,\\
\mathrm{break}(A) &= \mathrm{false}.
\end{aligned}
$$
This is a coupling-matrix contact at the codon-usage and tRNA-copy interface.  It is not yet a survival-matrix result for translation readouts, because no finite entries $S_{ij}$ were supplied for ribosome profiling, protein abundance, tAI, or stAI under the full control set.

\paragraph{Survival target distinguished from proxy coupling.}
The intended survival matrix would require entries $S_{ij}=S_{q_i}(r_j\mid Z)$, where $r_j$ ranges over translation-related readouts and $Z$ contains amino-acid composition, organism context, GC3, sequence length, and the simpler $M$-density baseline.  The finite proxy contact computes $A_i$ against one tRNA-copy spectrum; it does not compute partial $R^2$, description-length improvement, $p$-values, null-distribution ranks, or baseline-ablation survival scores.  Thus the correct logical separation is
$$
\begin{aligned}
A_i \neq 0 &\not\Rightarrow S_{ij}>0,\\
\max_i |A_i|>0.01 &\not\Rightarrow \text{ribosome-mediated translation realization},\\
\text{three organisms} &\not\Rightarrow \text{population-level directionality}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The observed nonzero proxy couplings are therefore admissible as a finite contact motivating the survival assay, but they do not certify the survival assay itself.

\paragraph{Local boundary.}
No translation-efficiency conclusion follows from this row unless a separate ribosome-profiling, tAI, stAI, or protein-abundance contact supplies the relevant readout and the full residualized survival estimate.  No folding conclusion, physical-admissibility conclusion, protein-function conclusion, biochemical-mechanism conclusion, or universality claim follows from codon usage and tRNA gene-copy ratios alone.  The signs of the coupling entries are not to be read as stable directions across organisms, and tRNA gene-copy count is not identified here with tRNA abundance, charging state, ribosomal dwell time, or protein output.  The only recorded finite fact is that, after the synonymous residual construction in a $61$-codon count space, the nine listed B-star-Q-six residual coordinates have nonzero coupling to the supplied Leu copy-ratio proxy in the three named organisms, with maximum absolute coupling $1882464.1106377328$ and threshold $0.01$.

\paragraph{Record being audited.}

\paragraph{Boundary conclusion.}
The self-check therefore has a precise conclusion.  The codon and quotient geometry can support reproducible finite enumeration, and the curated interval contact can support a local ORF-eligibility result for the stated RefSeq-derived rows.  However, the self-check row itself has no direct contact attached, and the broader biological interpretation remains bounded.  No translation event follows from the enumeration.  No folded molecular structure follows.  No physical admissibility, cellular mechanism, function, organism-level role, or universal biological law follows.  Such conclusions would require their own contact records and their own tests.  The valid reading is narrower: the finite data audit prevents cross-layer promotion and records that start--stop enumeration, even when checked against a curated interval dataset, remains an ORF-eligibility contact unless further biological evidence is separately supplied.

\paragraph{Local boundary.}
The conclusion of this section is intentionally narrow.  Start and stop codon enumeration can define a finite eligibility test once a biological interval is supplied, and the curated interval run supplies such a contact for the stated RefSeq-derived dataset.  The same data do not show translation realization, folded molecular structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism-level causation, species-wide coverage, or a global law.  Any later promotion to those layers would need a different contact whose measurements are appropriate to that layer.  Without that additional contact, the packet remains a boundary record: it records an admissible test shape and the exact finite rows already checked, but it does not turn coordinate geometry into biology by itself.

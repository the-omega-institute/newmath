\paragraph{Local boundary.}
The conclusion of this chapter is therefore a scoped eligibility statement, not a biological completion statement. The finite codon rows establish enumerated codon structure; the one-window manifest establishes a start--stop classification for one supplied window; the curated interval contact establishes strong finite separation on $168$ curated rows with positive, decoy, and boundary controls. None of these facts licenses any claim of translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanistic causation, or universality unless a further contact of the relevant kind supplies that layer. The self-check row exists precisely to keep that separation explicit: $\mathsf{orf\_eligibility}$ may be reported only where a contact tests an interval, and every higher biological reading remains outside the local claim.

\paragraph{Finite packet.}

\paragraph{Boundary gate.}
The self-check row is explicit: the boundary is a BioReality self-check only, and no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers. The checked gate count is $3$. In this chapter that count is read as a guard on interpretation: the packet may say that a start--stop-window enumeration has been written down, and it may say that the finite rows above passed their internal checks, but it may not say that an ORF has been observed.

The two probe statements enforce this guard. The first probe requires a curated ORF interval annotation, or another sequence or transcript contact of the same biological force, before the packet can leave boundary status. The second probe treats the absence of a sequence database hit, transcript evidence, or curated annotation as a contact gap. Thus the packet is admissible only as a boundary record while its reality-contact list is empty.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
No translation claim follows from the row data. No folded-structure claim follows from the row data. No physical-admissibility claim follows from the row data. No function, mechanism, organismal role, evolutionary universality, or biological realization follows from the row data. Start and stop codons can delimit a formal eligibility window, but without a separate reality contact supplying an observed interval, curated annotation, transcript evidence, or comparable external biological anchor, the window remains only a testable boundary coordinate. In particular, the finite facts $|R|=13$, $|M|=20$, $|\pi_M|=10$, the $M$ face-vector $(20,38,26,8,1)$, the quotient face-vector $(10,14,6,1)$, and the checked gate count $3$ do not by themselves cross the layer boundary into translation realization, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality.

\paragraph{Local conclusion.}
The correct BioReality reading is therefore narrow and complete: this packet records an ORF-eligibility contact gap. Its finite codon enumeration is internally checked, its mismatch lists for the motif union are empty, and its quotient geometry is explicitly finite. Nevertheless, because the contacts list is empty and no curated biological interval is attached, the only permitted claim is that the packet identifies what a later reality contact would have to test. The chapter is ready as a boundary record precisely because it refuses to treat BEDC coordinate data as biological realization.

\paragraph{Boundary retained by the self-claim.} The finite rows justify only an eligibility-layer statement under the attached curated-interval contact. The self-audit prevents a stronger reading: start--stop enumeration, even when exact on the listed codon sets and successful on the curated finite interval dataset, is not evidence that a sequence is translated, that a folded molecular structure exists, that a physical configuration is admissible, that a biological function is present, that a mechanism has been identified, or that a universal biological principle has been proved. Those claims require their own contact data and their own finite audits. The present row therefore certifies a disciplined separation: combinatorial codon geometry may support an externally checked ORF-eligibility test, while every higher biological layer remains unclaimed unless an independent contact supplies it.

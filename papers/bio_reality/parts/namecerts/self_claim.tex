\paragraph{Object and local reading.}
The record $\mathsf{self.claim}$ is a BioReality seed for the layer \texttt{orf\_eligibility}.  Its biological object is open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration, but the finite row deliberately does not assert that an open reading frame has been observed.  The BEDC minimal form is marked as awaiting reality contact: the carrier is awaiting reality contact, the only recorded distinction is awaiting reality contact, the internal structure is none, and the readback is awaiting reality contact.  Thus the chapter records a boundary condition, not a biological realization.  The packet can name what would have to be tested by an external sequence, transcript, or curated interval annotation; it cannot by itself say that any such interval exists in a genome, transcriptome, or translated molecule.

\paragraph{Self-check gate and boundary verdict.}
The self-check row states that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers, and it reports a checked gate count of $3$.  The two boundary probes express the same local discipline in operational form.  The first probe breaks if the pipeline asserts ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, or biological function from start/stop-window enumeration alone.  The second probe breaks if an ORF eligibility claim is recorded while no sequence database, transcript evidence, or curated annotation contact is available to test the proposed interval.  Therefore the admissible verdict is only a boundary seed:
$$
\begin{aligned}
\mathrm{codon\ enumeration}+\mathrm{quotient\ geometry}+\mathrm{self\ check}
&\nRightarrow \mathrm{boundary\ probe},\\
\mathrm{boundary\ probe}
&\nRightarrow \mathrm{no\ ORF\ eligibility\ claim\ without\ contact}.
\end{aligned}
$$
Here the symbol $\nRightarrow$ is part of the local cannot-claim assertion: finite enumeration does not entail biological realization.  No translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality follows unless a separate reality contact supplies the relevant observed interval and the appropriate layer-specific evidence.  In particular, start/stop-window eligibility is not translation realization; translation realization is not folded structure; folded structure is not physical admissibility; and physical admissibility is not biological function.  Each promotion requires its own reality contact and its own audit trail.

\paragraph{Contact gap.}
The contacts list is empty, and the reality-contact reference list is empty.  The required contact named by both probes is a curated ORF interval annotation.  Since no such contact is attached, the record remains at boundary status.  The correct readback is not that the ORF eligibility claim succeeded, but that the finite codon coordinate has exposed the exact point where empirical contact is missing.  The packet is therefore useful as a guardrail: it preserves the verified row facts $|\mathsf R|=13$, $|\mathsf M|=20$, $|\pi_{\mathsf M}|=10$, $f(\mathsf M)=(20,38,26,8,1)$, $f(\pi_{\mathsf M})=(10,14,6,1)$, and the gate count $3$, while preventing those facts from being misread as biological observation.

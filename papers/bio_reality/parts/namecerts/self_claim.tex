\subsection{ORF eligibility seed boundary}\label{subsec:bior reality-orf-eligibility-seed-boundary}
\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Record.}
This BioReality record is a seed packet for the claimed layer $\mathrm{orf\_eligibility}$.  The biological object named by the row is open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration, but the packet deliberately does not assert an observed open reading frame.  Its carrier, distinctions, and readback are all recorded as awaiting reality contact, while the internal structure is recorded as none.  Thus the finite BEDC coordinate is not being used as a biological witness; it is only a boundary object specifying what must not be inferred before an external sequence, transcript, or curated interval annotation is attached.

\paragraph{Self-check gate.}
The self-check row states that no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers.  Its checked gate count is $3$.  In this chapter that count is interpreted only as an internal boundary check: the packet has passed a local refusal to turn a finite coordinate into a biological realization claim.  The evidence basis is the mismatch ledger and the derived probes, not a biological contact.  The two probes require the same missing contact type, namely a curated ORF interval annotation.  One probe breaks if the pipeline asserts ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, or biological function from start/stop-window enumeration alone.  The other breaks if an ORF eligibility claim is recorded while no sequence database, transcript evidence, or curated annotation contact can test the proposed interval.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The local boundary is therefore strict.  From the displayed $13$-element motif set, the $20$-element marked set, the $18$-element saturation row, the $10$-point quotient, the two face vectors, and the $3$ checked self-gates, one may claim only that the packet records a finite boundary probe for ORF eligibility.  No translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, molecular mechanism, taxonomic generality, evolutionary universality, or organism-level relevance follows unless a separate reality contact supplies it.  A later contact may test an actual interval against this seed, but until such a contact is present the row remains a contact-gap boundary record rather than a biological assertion.

\paragraph{Local exclusion boundary.}
The claim made here is exactly bounded. Start--stop-window eligibility may be asserted only where the external curated interval contact supplies the biological interval being tested. Without that contact, the seed remains a gap marker rather than an ORF eligibility assertion. The RefSeq-derived contact remains an independent curated biological input to the experiment and is not absorbed into the finite codon geometry itself.

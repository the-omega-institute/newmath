\subsection{ORF Eligibility Boundary for Start--Stop Codon Enumeration}\label{sec:bioreality-orf-eligibility-boundary}\origin{ai}

\paragraph{Record.}
This BioReality record is a finite-row boundary packet for open-reading-frame eligibility from start/stop codon enumeration.  Its carrier is not an observed transcript, not a curated genomic interval, and not a translated product.  The carrier is the explicit status ``awaiting reality contact.''  The only distinction recorded at this layer is the same contact gap, and the internal structure is empty.  Thus the readback is not an ORF claim; it is the statement that an ORF eligibility claim is not yet licensed by the available data.

\paragraph{Boundary probes.}
Two boundary probes are attached.  The first says that a start/stop-window ORF eligibility seed must remain at boundary status unless an external sequence or transcript contact supplies the observed biological interval.  Its breaking condition is any assertion of ORF eligibility, translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, or biological function from start/stop-window enumeration alone.  The second says that an ORF eligibility seed is admissible only as a boundary probe while it lacks an external biological interval contact.  Its breaking condition is the recording of an ORF eligibility claim while no sequence database, transcript evidence, or curated annotation contact can test the proposed interval.  Both probes require a contact of type $\mathrm{curated.orf.interval.annotation}$, and no such contact is present in this packet.

\paragraph{Self-check verdict.}
The self-check row records the boundary sentence: BioReality self-check only; no codon geometry is promoted across biological layers.  It also records a checked gate count of $3$.  Therefore the local verdict is a boundary verdict, not an ORF-realization verdict.  The finite codon rows may be used to say exactly which codon coordinate has been enumerated, how its saturation row behaves, and what quotient rows were computed.  They may not be used to claim translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, universality, or even ORF eligibility itself unless a separate reality contact supplies the relevant biological interval and licenses that higher-layer interpretation.

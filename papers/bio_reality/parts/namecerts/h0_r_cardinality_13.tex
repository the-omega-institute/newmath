\paragraph{Reality contact and boundary.}
The only reality contact used here is the curated standard genetic-code table, which assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  It can test code-layer readback and local codon-tile label splits.  It cannot test translation realization, folded protein structure, physical admissibility, biological function, or a universal mechanism.  Therefore the supported claim is exactly this: window-six codon coordinates expose a thirteen-row local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table, with the finite values listed above.  No translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality follows unless a separate higher-layer contact supplies that information.

\paragraph{Finite carrier and readback contact.}
This record concerns the standard-code codon table only at the code-read layer.  The carrier is the six-bit codon cube $Q_6$, read as a finite coordinate display for codons, together with the curated standard-code label table as the only external contact.  The contact says that each codon has an amino-acid or stop label in the standard code.  It does not say that the coordinate geometry is a biological mechanism, and it does not observe translation, folding, physical admissibility, biological function, or universality.  The row therefore tests a local codon-tile boundary pattern and nothing above that layer.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
The reality contact for this chapter is only the curated standard-code table, which assigns codons to amino-acid or stop labels.  It can test code readback and the local codon-tile label split.  It cannot test translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, mechanism, or universality.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is therefore not folded protein structure.  A local codon tile in $Q_6$ is not a global biological law.  Any claim crossing from this code-read layer to translation, folding, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate reality contact supplying that higher-layer observation.

\paragraph{Finite object.}
The record concerns the standard-code codon table at the code-read layer.  The finite carrier is the six-coordinate codon cube $Q_6$, read as a binary window presentation of the $64$ codons, together with the curated standard-code assignment of each codon to an amino-acid or stop label. $$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&AAA,AGA,AGG,AUA,CUA,CUC,CUG,CUU,\\
&UAA,UAG,UCA,UGA,UUA\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The direct cardinality check gives $|R|=13$, and the same run reports the expected list exactly.  A separate cell-complex check identifies the same row as a punctured-cube pattern with $f$-vector

Thus the row is not merely a count: it also fixes a local incidence shape, with thirteen vertices, sixteen one-dimensional adjacencies, and four square faces in the induced codon-window geometry.

\paragraph{Local verdict and cannot-claim boundary.}
The ready claim is that the standard-code table, read through the window-six coordinate carrier, contains a verified thirteen-codon local boundary row with punctured-cube incidence, recorded spectral values, nontrivial null-model pressure, and complete module classification.  The claim stops there.  No translation realization, folded structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or global biological law follows from this row unless a separate higher-layer contact supplies that information.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback remains a code-layer observation; it is not a protein-structure observation.

\subsection{Local verdict and boundary}
\label{sec:codon-window-six-local-verdict}

\paragraph{Verdict.}
The finite-row evidence is ready at the code-read layer.  The thirteen-codon row is exactly enumerated, its punctured-cube face vector is recorded as $(13,16,4)$, its spectral calculation has a near-zero cubic residual, its shape is rare under the sampled nulls, and its leave-one recovery and module classification are complete for the recorded protocols.  The curated standard-code table is a sufficient contact for codon-label readback and for testing the local tile label split.

\paragraph{Cannot-claim boundary.}
No higher biological layer follows from this chapter alone.  In particular, the row does not establish translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a mechanism, or universality.  Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded structure.  A local codon tile is not a global biological law.  Crossing any of those boundaries requires a separate reality contact whose observed facts directly test the higher layer.

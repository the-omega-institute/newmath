\subsection{The Thirteen-Codon Boundary Row in the Window-Six Codon Cube}
\label{subsec:h0-r-cardinality-13}

\paragraph{Finite carrier and boundary row.}
The finite carrier is the six-bit codon display $Q_6$, read through the curated standard-code label table.  In this record the distinguished boundary row is the set $R$ of thirteen codons
$$
\begin{aligned}
R=\{&\mathrm{AAA},\mathrm{AGA},\mathrm{AGG},\mathrm{AUA},\mathrm{CUA},\mathrm{CUC},\mathrm{CUG},\mathrm{CUU},\\
&\mathrm{UAA},\mathrm{UAG},\mathrm{UCA},\mathrm{UGA},\mathrm{UUA}\}.
\end{aligned}
$$
The direct finite-row count gives $|R|=13$, and the recorded size field is also $13$.  Thus the row is not being inferred from a continuum approximation or from a biological mechanism; it is a finite subset of the codon cube, checked against the displayed codon labels.  The same row is recorded as a punctured-cube pattern with $f$-vector $(13,16,4)$, so the local object has thirteen vertices, sixteen one-dimensional adjacencies, and four two-dimensional faces in the induced tile complex.

\paragraph{Local geometry and spectral readback.}
The spectral summary supplies a numerical fingerprint for the same local split.  The recorded values are $\lambda_M=0.6752479308830213$, $\lambda_R=0.4758352843197002$, and $\mu=3.051487585298128$.  The radial polynomial check is $-6.224354365258478\times 10^{-12}$, and the cubic polynomial residual has the same value, within the stated numerical tolerance.  The supplied rows therefore support the claim that the thirteen-codon set $R$ participates in a sharply specified local window-six geometry.  They do not by themselves identify translation dynamics, ribosomal realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or a global law of DNA.  The admissible readback is only the code-layer comparison between codon coordinates and curated standard-code labels.

\paragraph{Shape significance against finite null rows.}
The shape-significance row used $N=2000$ finite samples with seed $42$. Within that run there was $1$ exact shape match, giving $p_{\mathrm{exact}}=0.0005$, and $25$ samples with Jaccard score at least $0.9$, giving $p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\ge 0.9}=0.0125$.  A separate amino-acid-class-preserving null row, also with $N=2000$ and seed $42$, records observed $M$-size $20$, exact-match count $0$, lower-tail count $7$, $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ exact}}=0.0$, and $p_{\mathrm{aa\ class\ lower\ tail}}=0.0035$. \paragraph{Reality contact and cannot-claim boundary.}
The only stated reality contact is the curated standard genetic-code table, whose observed fact is that codons are assigned to amino-acid or stop labels.  That contact can test code-read and local codon-tile label split, but it cannot test translation realization, structural order, physical admissibility, function realization, mechanism, or universality.  Consequently the conclusion is deliberately narrow: window-six coordinates expose a thirteen-codon local boundary row with punctured-cube face data, spectral fingerprint, null-model separation, and module-complete readback against curated labels.  No protein realization, folded structure, biochemical admissibility, biological function, global mechanism, or universal biological law follows unless a separate higher-layer contact supplies that evidence.

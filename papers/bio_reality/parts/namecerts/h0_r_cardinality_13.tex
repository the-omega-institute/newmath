\paragraph{Finite coordinate record.}

\begin{aligned}

Thus the finite observation is not merely a count: it isolates a local tile with thirteen codon vertices, sixteen codon-adjacency edges, and four square faces inside the six-bit codon display. The table contact is the curated standard genetic-code assignment from codons to amino-acid or stop labels; it supplies labels for this finite row, not a separate account of molecular dynamics.

\paragraph{Local boundary of the claim.}
The row supports the statement that window-six codon coordinates expose a local boundary-tile structure in the standard-code label table. The curated table is the contact that licenses codon-to-label readback, and the numerical probes describe how the thirteen-codon set behaves under the stated finite comparisons. Nothing in this row by itself establishes translation realization, folded structure, physical admissibility, biological function, universal mechanism, or a global law of living systems. In particular, amino-acid label-sequence readback is still label readback; it is not protein folding evidence. Any assertion about structural order, organism-level mechanism, cross-organism causal interpretation, or functional realization requires an additional contact that observes that layer directly.

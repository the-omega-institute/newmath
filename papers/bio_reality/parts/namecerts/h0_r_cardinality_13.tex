The observed contact is a curated standard genetic-code table assigning codons to amino-acid or stop labels; that contact supplies label readback, not biochemical realization.

$$
\begin{aligned}

$$
\begin{aligned}
p_{\mathrm{Jaccard}\geq 0.9}=0.0125.

\paragraph{Boundary of the claim.}
This row is ready as a BioReality record because it has a concrete carrier, a finite thirteen-codon row, a punctured-cube face count, spectral scalars, null-model probabilities, leave-one recovery statistics, and a complete module classification. The only reality contact used here is the curated standard-code label table. Therefore the chapter cannot claim translation realization, folded protein structure, structural order, physical admissibility, biological function, a universal mechanism, or any necessity principle for DNA. Amino-acid label-sequence readback is not folded structure. A local codon tile is not a global biological law. Any crossing from code readback to translation, structure, physical admissibility, function, mechanism, or universality requires a separate contact supplying that higher-layer fact.

\section*{Layer warning}

This volume intentionally separates four layers:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{Core theory}: clean-room BEDC object-theory specification.
  \item \textbf{Proof target}: theorem statements and proof plans for the finite kernel.
  \item \textbf{Implementation-facing contract}: external guidance for a future proof-assistant encoding.
  \item \textbf{Project governance}: versioning, migration, and maintenance rules.
\end{enumerate}
Terms such as ``model'', ``theorem'', ``table'', ``implementation'', and ``module'' belong to the explanatory metalanguage unless explicitly introduced as BEDC-derived interfaces.

\section*{Current live edge}

The live edge of this master project is not the reconstruction of all traditional mathematics. The live edge is the finite-kernel proof path:
\[
\msame\to \hsame\to \ExtR/\ContR\to \Sig\to \sameSig\to \PkgPol/\GapPol\to \CompGap\to \NameCert.
\]
Large derived interfaces such as \(\RealUp\), \(\CircleUp\), and \(\FoldUp\) remain thin interfaces or future extension modules until the finite kernel is mechanically checked.

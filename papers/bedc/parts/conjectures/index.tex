\chapter{Open Conjectures}
\label{ch:conjectures-index}
\origin{human}

The conjectures listed in this directory are BEDC kernel-formulated
open problems: each is stated entirely in terms of $\BHist$ / $\NameCert$
/ $\hsame$ / $\Cont$ / $\Pkg$ primitives, has an obvious-but-unproved
candidate path, and admits a finite test (verification, weakening, or
counter-example).

The pipeline picks one open conjecture per round (kind =
\texttt{conjecture\_chase}) and attempts: (a) closing it with a proof,
(b) weakening it by adding hypotheses or restricting scope, or (c)
refuting it with a counter-witness drawn from existing concrete
instances. Each attempt updates the conjecture's
\texttt{\textbackslash conjectureStatus\{...\}} row and may move the
chapter between \texttt{open}, \texttt{weakened}, \texttt{closed}, or
\texttt{refuted}.

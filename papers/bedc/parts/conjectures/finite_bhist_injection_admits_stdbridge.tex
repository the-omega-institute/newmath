\section{Finite-BHist-Injection Admits StdBridge}
\label{sec:conjectures-finite-bhist-injection-admits-stdbridge}

\begin{conjecture}[Finite-BHist-Injection Admits StdBridge]
\label{conj:finite-bhist-injection-admits-stdbridge}
For every BEDC chapter $\<X>Up$ whose $\BHistCarrier.\toEventFlow$ image is displayed as a fixed finite set
$$
\{w_0,w_1,\ldots,w_k\}
$$
of $\EventFlow$ values and whose $\fromEventFlow \circ \toEventFlow$ replay is recorded in the chapter packet, the $\StdBridge$ obligation reduces to finite enumeration of the displayed image rows.
\end{conjecture}

\conjectureStatus{weakened}

\paragraph{Status notes.}
The unrestricted regular-language route is outside this conjecture. The active claim is the fixed-image finite test: once the image rows $w_0,w_1,\ldots,w_k$ and the replay equation are displayed in the packet, the bridge check is an enumeration over those rows and their recorded $\NameCert$ obligations.

\paragraph{Weakening route (if not provable).}
The finite-image form is the standing route. A chapter that does not display its image rows and replay record remains outside this test until those rows are made part of its packet.

\paragraph{Refutation route (if false).}
Find a fixed-image chapter whose displayed rows and replay record are present but whose $\StdBridge$ obligation cannot be reduced to finite enumeration because an additional reflection axis is required by its $\NameCert$ surface.

\section{Immediate machine-check order}
The recommended order is:
\[
\text{mark/history sameness}
\to
\text{continuation}
\to
\text{unary transport}
\to
\text{unary right-shift}
\to
\text{package-token reflection}
\to
\text{concrete gap exactness}.
\]
This order avoids depending on completion-like or large derived-object interfaces.

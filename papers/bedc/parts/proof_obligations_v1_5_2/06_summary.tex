
\chapter{v1.5.2 Summary}
\label{ch:v152-summary}

The v1.5.2 increment converts three live risks into smaller proof targets.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Domain membership has a concrete unary instance: \(\mathsf{DomainPolicy}(\mathsf{UnarySource})\).
  \item Concrete package-token sameness is made inductive and reflective through \(\psame_{\Pi}^{\mathrm{sig}}\).
  \item The additive naming boundary is sharpened: \(\mathsf{UnaryContMonoid}^{\uparrow}\) is safe with unit and associativity, while \(\AddUp\) requires unary commutativity.
\end{enumerate}

No new derived-object theory is added. The next development step should be a local verification run for the unary-domain, package-token, and unary-commutativity obligations.

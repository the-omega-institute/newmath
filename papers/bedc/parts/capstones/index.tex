\chapter{Capstone Results}
\label{ch:capstones}

This chapter collects bridge theorems whose conclusions span $\geq 3$
distinct chapters of \texttt{papers/bedc/parts/}. Each capstone formalises
a unification: a single statement that uses concrete BEDC kernel objects
from multiple horizons, witnessed by Lean targets that type-check
together. Capstones are not meta-commentary; they are the unifying
mathematical content.

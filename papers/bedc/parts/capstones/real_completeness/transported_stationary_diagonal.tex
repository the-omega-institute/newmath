\begin{theorem}[Transported stationary rational diagonal]
\label{thm:real-completeness-transported-stationary-diagonal}
Let $d,e:\Hist$ satisfy $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$,
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(e)$, and
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$. Let $X_d$ and $X_e$ be the stationary
regular Cauchy inputs of
\autoref{def:real-completeness-stationary-rational-cauchy}, and let $L_d$ and
$L_e$ be their diagonal representatives from
\autoref{def:real-completeness-diagonal-limit}. Then the transported RatUp
classifier induces the constant StreamNameUp classifier
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e),
$$
the e-one RealUp constant classifier
$$
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d),\Eone(e)),
$$
and the RealUp classifier
$$
\mathsf{RealClassifier}(L_d,L_e).
$$
Moreover these three rows are the same transported stationary rational
diagonal row: every finite RegSeqRat or DyadicRatCore observation window read
from $L_d$ and $L_e$ factors through the constant StreamNameUp classifier and
then through the e-one RealUp constant classifier induced by
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}[Sketch]
First apply
\autoref{thm:real-completeness-stationary-rational-diagonal} to $d$ and to
$e$. The two applications identify the diagonal representatives $L_d$ and
$L_e$ with the constant rational RealUp names
$(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\Eone(d))$ and
$(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e,\Eone(e))$.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:real-completeness-stationary-embedding-classifier-full-correspondence}
to the supplied RatUp classifier. It transports
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$ across the two diagonal agreement rows
and produces $\mathsf{RealClassifier}(L_d,L_e)$.

The finite-window content is the same row read at the StreamNameUp boundary.
Use \autoref{thm:real-completeness-stationary-rational-finite-window-bridge}.
It identifies the RatUp seed classifier with the constant StreamNameUp
classifier and with the e-one RealUp constant classifier for any displayed
unary observation window.

Finally read the RegSeqRat and DyadicRatCore boundary rows through the RealUp
certificate boundary. The RegSeqRat seal consumes the finite StreamNameUp
window together with the dyadic source ledger, and the DyadicRatCore
finite-window transport rows preserve that same ledger under selected
subwindows. Hence every stationary finite observation of $L_d$ and $L_e$
is the transported constant-stream row induced by the original RatUp
classifier, with no additional RealUp comparison datum.
\end{proof}

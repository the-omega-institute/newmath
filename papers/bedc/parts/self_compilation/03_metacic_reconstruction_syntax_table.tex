\subsection{Syntax Constructor Table}\label{subsec:reconstruction-syntax-table}

\begin{tabular}{l l}
\hline
Constructor & Description \\
\hline
\verb|Term.var i| & de Bruijn variable, $i \in \mathbb{N}$ \\
\verb|Term.app f a| & function application $f\ a$ \\
\verb|Term.lam d b| & abstraction $\lambda x:d.\ b$ with domain $d$ and body $b$ \\
\verb|Term.pi d c| & dependent product $\Pi x:d.\ c$ \\
\verb|Term.sort| & single universe sort literal \\
\hline
\end{tabular}

Each constructor is a pure inductive constructor, with no host
metaprogramming.

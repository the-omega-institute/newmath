\subsection{Pure CIC Fragment}\label{subsec:reconstruction-pure-cic-fragment}

\texttt{BEDC.MetaCIC} represents a pure CIC fragment. It does not depend on
any axiom introduced by the Lean standard library, including
\verb|Classical.choice|, \verb|Quot.sound|, and \verb|propext|. This premise
is audited by \verb|bedc_ci.py axiom-purity --strict|, which checks each
theorem's transitive dependencies against the forbidden axiom set.

The PCIC fragment has the following concrete features:

\begin{itemize}
\item no quotient types and no propositional-extensionality strengthening of
propositional equality;
\item no law of excluded middle and no dependent choice;
\item no universe polymorphism;
\item \texttt{pi}, \texttt{lam}, \texttt{app}, \texttt{sort}, and
\texttt{var} as the only represented term constructors.
\end{itemize}

\subsection{Reduction Relations}\label{subsec:reconstruction-reduction-table}

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{l l}
\hline
Relation & Description \\
\hline
\verb|BetaStep t t'| & single-step $\beta$ plus congruence in \texttt{MetaCIC.Beta} \\
\verb|BetaStarStep t t'| & reflexive-transitive closure of \texttt{BetaStep} \\
\verb|BetaParallel t t'| & Tait--Martin-L\"of parallel reduction \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

\verb|BetaStarStep| and \verb|BetaParallel| are separate inductive
propositions, connected by atom-diamond facts and congruence lemmas. There is
no implicit conversion between them.

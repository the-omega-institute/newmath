\subsection{Typing Inference Rules Table}\label{subsec:reconstruction-typing-rules-table}

\begin{center}
\begin{tabular}{l p{0.72\linewidth}}
\hline
Rule & Conclusion \\
\hline
sortRule & $\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{sort} : \mathrm{sort}$ \\
varRule & $\Gamma.\mathrm{lookup}\ i = \mathrm{some}\ A \Rightarrow \Gamma \vdash \mathrm{var}\ i : A$ \\
piRule & (shift-aware) $\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{pi}\ d\ c : \mathrm{sort}$ from $\Gamma \vdash d : \mathrm{sort}$, $(\mathrm{shift}\ 0\ 1\ d) :: \Gamma \vdash c : \mathrm{sort}$ \\
lamRule & (shift-aware) $\Gamma \vdash \mathrm{lam}\ d\ b : \mathrm{pi}\ d\ \mathrm{cod}$ from the same Pi-rule binder slot and body type $\mathrm{cod}$ \\
appRule & $\Gamma \vdash f\ a : \mathrm{substitute}\ 0\ a\ \mathrm{cod}$ from $\Gamma \vdash f : \mathrm{pi}\ d\ \mathrm{cod}$, $\Gamma \vdash a : d$ \\
\hline
\end{tabular}
\end{center}

The shift-aware binder slot appears in the Pi and lambda rules as
$(\mathrm{shift}\ 0\ 1\ d) :: \Gamma$, rather than as $d :: \Gamma$.

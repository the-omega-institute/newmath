\section*{MetaCIC Mechanization Status Dashboard}
\label{sec:metacic-mechanization-status}

The \texttt{BEDC.MetaCIC} formalization is a mathlib-free, zero-axiom,
zero-sorry Lean 4 development over a first-principles represented calculus.
The dashboard below reads that development against a standard CIC
metatheory checklist. It distinguishes proved Lean targets, statement-level
interfaces, finite galleries, and open textbook theorems without upgrading
any open entry into a closure claim.

\subsection*{What is not claimed}
\label{subsec:metacic-mechanization-not-claimed}

The dashboard does not claim universe polymorphism, cumulative universes,
inductive families, large eliminations, proof irrelevance, eta conversion,
quotients, classical choice, or a bridge from represented typing into Lean's
kernel typing judgment. It also does not claim full beta confluence, strong
normalization, decidable type checking for the represented calculus, or a
global representational adequacy theorem. The checked entries are exactly
the Lean targets named above and the already marked MetaCIC targets cited
elsewhere in this chapter.

\begin{remark}[Vision seed status]
\label{rem:public-local-reflective-compilation-vision-seed-status}
This section has vision-seed status: it is a schema horizon and has no
Lean target. It records the structural comparison between public
scientific inquiry, local consciousness-like readback, the compiler-side
audit of observation flows, the MetaCIC-side audit of claim legality, and
the non-internalizable forward-supply position $\mathsf{T}$.

The intended reading is
$$
\begin{gathered}
\mathsf{ScientificInquiry}
=
\mathsf{GroundCompilerLike}(\mathsf{ObservationFlow})
+
\mathsf{MetaCICLike}(\mathsf{ClaimLegality}),\\
\mathsf{Consciousness}
=
\mathsf{LocalSelfCompile}(\mathsf{HistTime})
+
\mathsf{LocalSelfAudit}(\mathsf{Readback}),\\
\mathsf{I}
=
\mathsf{CurrentSelfProxy}(\mathsf{ConsciousRecord}(L_N,F)),\\
\mathsf{T}
=
\mathsf{NonInternalizableForwardSupplyPosition}.
\end{gathered}
$$
These equations are role schemata, not object-level identifications
between the named layers. In particular, the section refuses the
collapses
$$
\mathsf{Science}=\mathsf{Consciousness},
\qquad
\mathsf{Science}=\mathsf{T},
\qquad
\mathsf{Consciousness}=\mathsf{T},
\qquad
\mathsf{I}=\mathsf{T},
\qquad
\mathsf{GroundCompiler}=\mathsf{MetaCIC}.
$$
\end{remark}

\begin{definition}[Twin public audit substrate]
\label{def:twin-public-audit-substrate}
The \emph{twin public audit substrate} of a scientific inquiry context is
the ordered pair
$$
\mathsf{TwinAudit}
:=
\bigl(
\mathsf{CompilerSide},
\mathsf{MetaAuditSide}
\bigr).
$$
The compiler side sends finite observation or event traces through
source separation, encoding, generated recognition, certificate gates,
ledger rows, and cannot-claim rows. This is the role played by the ground
compiler chapters, especially
\autoref{ch:ground-compiler-event-flow},
\autoref{ch:ground-compiler-analysis-pipeline}, and
\autoref{ch:ground-compiler-main-theorems-cannot-claims}.

The meta-audit side checks whether a displayed claim is licensed by its
source, classifier, stability domain, descent data, ledger exposure, and
failure surface. This is the scientific analogue of the MetaCIC
self-study role of \autoref{def:metacic-self-study} and
\autoref{thm:metacic-self-study-instantiates-reflective-architecture}.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Scientific inquiry as public reflective compilation]
\label{def:scientific-inquiry-public-reflective-compilation}
A scientific inquiry packet has \emph{public reflective compilation} when
it factors through the twin public audit substrate:
$$
\mathsf{ScientificInquiry}
=
\mathsf{CompilerSide}(\mathsf{ObservationFlow})
+
\mathsf{MetaAuditSide}(\mathsf{ClaimLegality}).
$$
The first component compiles finite observation flow into source traces,
observed signatures, model-generated signatures, recognizers, certificate
gates, and ledgers. The second component audits what those compiled rows
may legitimately support: fit, lawlike reuse, explanation, prediction,
or only speculative appearance.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Local self-compiler and local self-audit]
\label{def:local-self-compiler-and-audit}
For a focused ledger $(L_N,F)$ with
$$
\mathsf{ConsciousRecord}(L_N,F)
=
(H(F),\Theta(F),S(F),\rho),
$$
the \emph{local self-compiler} is the records-side role
$$
\mathsf{SelfCompiler}(L_N,F):
\mathsf{SelfFlow}
\to
\mathsf{FocusedTrace}
\to
\mathsf{SelectorLedger}
\to
\mathsf{SelfProxy}.
$$
The \emph{local self-audit} is the readback role
$$
\mathsf{SelfMetaAudit}(L_N,F):
\mathsf{SelfProxy}
\to
\mathsf{Readback}
\to
\mathsf{BoundaryRecognition}
\to
\mathsf{GapExposure}.
$$
These roles are local and records-side. They do not assert a public
scientific certificate and do not define a total theory of consciousness.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Current self-proxy]
\label{def:current-self-proxy}
The ``I'' reading of a focused ledger is the current readback center
supported by the focused self-proxy:
$$
\mathsf{I}(L_N,F)
:=
\mathsf{CurrentSelfProxy}(H(F),\Theta(F),S(F),\rho).
$$
Equivalently, on the records side it is the current centered use of
$\rho$ in $\mathsf{ConsciousRecord}(L_N,F)$. On the inscription side it
may be read through
$$
\mathsf{SelfCenter}(C_F,t)
=
\mathsf{InscriptionPoint}(\mathsf{T},C_F,t),
$$
as in \autoref{def:inscription-side-self-center}. This does not identify
the current self-proxy with $\mathsf{T}$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[GroundCompiler and MetaCIC roles do not collapse]
\label{thm:groundcompiler-metacic-roles-do-not-collapse}
The compiler-side role and the MetaCIC-side role are complementary but
not identical:
$$
\mathsf{GroundCompiler}\neq\mathsf{MetaCIC}.
$$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The ground compiler side answers whether an event flow, source channel,
displayed code artifact, generated recognizer, report, or certificate
gate has the status being claimed for it. Its characteristic refusals
include: code is not proof, report is not evidence, metric similarity is
not identity, and hidden host structure is not formal input.

The MetaCIC side answers whether a closed-calculus claim is licensed by
term formation, typing, reduction, substitution, normalization,
confluence, and the open meta-boundary. A ground compiler theorem that a
report cannot discharge a certificate does not prove subject reduction.
A MetaCIC theorem about substitution does not certify that an observation
report has no hidden host input. Therefore the two roles are structurally
linked but cannot be collapsed.
\end{proof}

\begin{corollary}[Self-compilation requires twin audit]
\label{cor:self-compilation-requires-twin-audit}
A BEDC self-compilation story that is meant to be public and
claim-bearing must carry both compiler-side provenance and MetaCIC-side
claim legality.
\end{corollary}

\begin{proof}
Without compiler-side provenance, a displayed artifact may smuggle a
host convention, a report, or a code path into the role of formal input
or proof. Without MetaCIC-side claim legality, a generated artifact may
be well reported while still failing to support the theorem, reduction,
substitution, or boundary claim attached to it. Public self-compilation
therefore needs both sides of \autoref{def:twin-public-audit-substrate}.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Scientific inquiry has the twin audit form]
\label{thm:scientific-inquiry-twin-audit-form}
Scientific inquiry is compiler-like on the source side and MetaCIC-like
on the claim side:
$$
\mathsf{ScientificInquiry}
=
\mathsf{GroundCompilerLike}(\mathsf{ObservationFlow})
+
\mathsf{MetaCICLike}(\mathsf{ClaimLegality}).
$$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
On the source side, scientific inquiry begins with finite observation
flows: instrument outputs, experimental rows, traces, images, simulations,
or public reports. These rows do not become laws merely by being
displayed. They must be separated as source, normalized or encoded where
needed, converted into observed signatures, related to model-generated
signatures, and admitted through classifier and ledger discipline. This
is the ground-compiler-like direction.

On the claim side, the same rows must be audited for what they license.
A finite fit may support a local comparison, a stability certificate may
support reuse in an announced domain, a descent witness may support
transport through a classifier, and a failure surface names how the claim
can be defeated. These are not source-format questions; they are
legality questions about the supported claim. Thus scientific inquiry
has the twin audit form of \autoref{def:twin-public-audit-substrate}.
\end{proof}

\begin{corollary}[Science is not either substrate alone]
\label{cor:science-not-either-substrate-alone}
Scientific inquiry is not identical with the ground compiler and is not
identical with MetaCIC.
\end{corollary}

\begin{proof}
The ground compiler side can produce ledgered artifacts and cannot-claim
rows, but by itself it does not provide the empirical target, model
family, stability domain, descent obligations, or failure surface of a
scientific theory. The MetaCIC side can audit closed-calculus legality,
but by itself it does not generate reality-facing observed signatures.
Scientific inquiry uses both roles analogically and structurally; it is
not either module as an object.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Consciousness-like readback has local twin form]
\label{thm:consciousness-like-readback-local-twin-form}
Within this vision-seed schema, consciousness-like readback has the local
form
$$
\mathsf{Consciousness}
=
\mathsf{LocalSelfCompile}(\mathsf{HistTime})
+
\mathsf{LocalSelfAudit}(\mathsf{Readback}).
$$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
By \autoref{def:records-side-conscious-carrier}, the records-side carrier
of the consciousness reading is
$$
\mathsf{ConsciousRecord}(L_N,F)
=
(H(F),\Theta(F),S(F),\rho).
$$
The focused history $H(F)$ is the current generated endpoint, the trace
$\Theta(F)$ records its local Hist-time route, the selector ledger $S(F)$
records which visible transitions enter the focused chain, and $\rho$
supplies the self-proxy/readback surface. These fields compile a local
Hist-time flow into a self-readable carrier.

The same carrier also supports local boundary recognition: the record can
distinguish present readback, memory, expectation, uncertainty, and
exposed gaps without becoming a public scientific certificate or a total
self-truth predicate. This is a local self-audit role, not a global
MetaCIC closure. Hence consciousness-like readback has local twin form.
\end{proof}

\begin{corollary}[Self-awareness is not a total theory of consciousness]
\label{cor:self-awareness-not-total-theory-consciousness}
Local self-readback does not provide an internal total theory of
consciousness.
\end{corollary}

\begin{proof}
Self-awareness uses the finite carrier
$(H(F),\Theta(F),S(F),\rho)$ at a focused endpoint. A total theory of
consciousness would require the system to close over consciousness as
such, including its own truth and boundary conditions. That would convert
finite readback into a total self-validator and would close the
meta-loop that \autoref{cor:metacic-self-study-structural-not-totalizing}
keeps open.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[The current self-proxy is not subject-substance or T]
\label{thm:current-self-proxy-not-subject-substance-or-t}
The ``I'' reading is neither a subject-substance nor $\mathsf{T}$:
$$
\mathsf{I}\neq\mathsf{SubjectSubstance},
\qquad
\mathsf{I}\neq\mathsf{T}.
$$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
By \autoref{def:current-self-proxy}, the ``I'' reading is the current
self-proxy use of $H(F),\Theta(F),S(F),\rho$. These fields belong to the
focused ledger and add no carrier behind $\Hist$. Thus the current
self-proxy is not a subject-substance.

On the inscription side, the self-center may be read as
$\mathsf{InscriptionPoint}(\mathsf{T},C_F,t)$. This expression still
contains the focused chain $C_F$ and the local stage $t$. It names a local
inscription point of $\mathsf{T}$ on a chain, not the far-end supply
position itself. Identifying the current self-proxy with $\mathsf{T}$
would internalize the non-internalizable boundary position as a recordable
object.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Science, consciousness, and I expose T-sockets without becoming T]
\label{thm:science-consciousness-i-expose-t-sockets-not-t}
Scientific inquiry, consciousness-like readback, and the current
self-proxy may expose $\mathsf{T}$-supply sockets, but none is identical
with $\mathsf{T}$.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Scientific inquiry exposes $\mathsf{T}$-supply sockets when finite
observation records are asked to support future prediction, universal
lawlike reuse, cross-regime extrapolation, or final explanation beyond
the displayed witnesses. Consciousness-like readback exposes such sockets
when finite records are asked to bind self-continuity, expected next
moments, or meaning beyond the current record. The current self-proxy
exposes the same boundary whenever the focused endpoint reads its trace
as continuing beyond what is displayed.

In each case, the socket is a ledgered boundary row. It states the
missing shape, the consumption site, and the audit gate preventing
internalization. By \autoref{prop:scientific-inquiry-not-t} and
\autoref{thm:selfcenter-local-inscription-not-t}, socket exposure does
not supply the far end as an object. Hence these structures expose
$\mathsf{T}$ without becoming $\mathsf{T}$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Public science and local consciousness-like readback]
\label{thm:public-science-local-consciousness-readback}
Scientific inquiry can be the public ledgered mode of
consciousness-like reflection, but it is not consciousness itself:
$$
\mathsf{Science}
=
\mathsf{PublicLedgeredMode}
\bigl(\mathsf{ConsciousnessLikeReflection}(\mathsf{HistTime})\bigr),
\qquad
\mathsf{Science}\neq\mathsf{Consciousness}.
$$
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The first relation is the safe reading established in
\autoref{prop:science-public-consciousness-like-reflection}. A focused
consciousness-like record can supply source readback, and scientific
inquiry can externalize that source through observed signatures,
model-generated signatures, classifiers, approximation towers,
stability, descent, ledgers, failure surfaces, and audit strength.

The inequality follows because the records-side conscious carrier has
only the focused history, local trace, selector ledger, and self-proxy.
Scientific inquiry adds public evidence fields and inter-$\Hist$
transport obligations. A private readback may exist without public
scientific audit, and a public scientific packet is not itself a private
first-person carrier.
\end{proof}

\begin{proof}

\end{proof}

\begin{remark}[Reading]
\label{rem:public-local-reflective-compilation-reading}
Scientific inquiry is compiler-like because it turns finite observation
flows into public evidence packets, and MetaCIC-like because it audits
which claims those packets may legally support. Consciousness-like
readback is the analogous structure turned locally toward a focused
Hist-time trace. The ``I'' is the current self-proxy at the focused
endpoint. The position $\mathsf{T}$ is the boundary far end exposed when
finite records are asked to supply future binding, universal closure,
self-continuity, or meaning beyond their displayed witnesses.
\end{remark}

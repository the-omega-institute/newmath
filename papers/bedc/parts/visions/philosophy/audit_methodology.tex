\section{Audit Methodology and Writing Discipline}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-audit-methodology}

The preceding layers give the structural content of auditable
generativity. The present layer turns that content into writing rules.
Every claim must state its carrier, classifier, ledger, theory-closure
grade, formal-verification grade, gap or refusal surface, and
no-leakage discipline. Theory closure and formal verification are
reported on independent axes and never substitute for one another.

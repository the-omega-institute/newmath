\section{Formal Philosophy Package, Export Interface, and Governance}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-package-governance}

package. This package does not extend the kernel. It organizes how
BEDC structures are read, registered, mapped to traditions and
scientific claims, exported, revised, and refused when a claim exceeds
its certificate surface.

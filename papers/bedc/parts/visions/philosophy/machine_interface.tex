\section{Registry Layer, Manuscript Form, and Machine-Facing Interface}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-machine-interface}

The registry layer is not supplementary material. It is the
export-control surface of the philosophy package. The prose develops
concepts and readings; the registry layer determines which claims may
be exported, which must be downgraded, which are refused, and which can
become future formalization targets.

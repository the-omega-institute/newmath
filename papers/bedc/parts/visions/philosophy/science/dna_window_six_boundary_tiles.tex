\subsection{Local tile dynamics of the genetic code}
\label{subsec:philosophy-dna-window-six-boundary-tiles}

This subsection records a finite, non-axiomatic analysis of the standard
RNA genetic-code table under the two-axis readback
$$
  \mathrm{U}=00,\qquad
  \mathrm{C}=01,\qquad
  \mathrm{A}=10,\qquad
  \mathrm{G}=11.
$$
It does not claim that DNA must obey Zeckendorf rules, that the genetic
code is the window-six fold, or that the biological code is globally equal
to a no-$11$ language. The claims have three layers: a coordinate
definition, a finite enumeration over the standard table, and biological
bridge readings supplied by external genetic-code data.

\section{Research Programme and Implementation Route}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-research-programme}

The philosophical synthesis becomes a research programme only when its
objects are pursued as certificates rather than imported as mature
assumptions. The route is layered, strength-marked, phase-governed,
failure-aware, and audit-facing. It extends BEDC by proving that names
have earned use, not by asserting that mature objects are already
available.

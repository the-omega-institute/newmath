\section{BEDC and Philosophical Traditions}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-traditions}

BEDC is not assigned to an inherited philosophical school. The
comparison discipline is weaker and more precise: a tradition may
identify a question whose structural core lands on a BEDC carrier,
relation, classifier, ledger, certificate, gap, or \(T\)-position.
The comparison is therefore a structural translation, not a doctrinal
identity.

\section{Consciousness, Universe, and Phenomenological Frame}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-phenomenology-framework}

The gap and \(T\)-supply layer shows how a closed observational system
exposes commitments that exceed its finite records. The
phenomenological layer applies this discipline to consciousness,
universe, time, space, identity, causality, freedom, other minds,
mind--matter relation, and self-reference. The result is not a list of
new entities, but a records-side translation of traditional questions
into focused histories, local continuations, inter-history relation
rows, socketed commitments, and inscription events.

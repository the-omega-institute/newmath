\section{Logic, Contradiction, and the Meta Loop}
\label{sec:visions-philosophy-logic-contradiction-meta-loop}

The previous layer identifies objects with licensed names and
knowledge with auditable witnesses. The logical question is therefore
not which rules are attractive in isolation, but which proof patterns
preserve witness, classifier, stability, and ledger discipline. The
central distinction is:
$$
\begin{gathered}
\text{closed ground loop}=\text{constructive formation and local refutation},\\
\text{open meta loop}=\text{no total truth, choice, quotient, or halting closure}.
\end{gathered}
$$

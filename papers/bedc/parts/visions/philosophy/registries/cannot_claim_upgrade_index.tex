\begin{theorem}[Upgrade registry is not completion]
\label{thm:philosophy-upgrade-registry-not-completion}
An upgrade entry states how a target may be promoted; it does not state
that the promotion has occurred.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The entry contains current status as well as next status. Until the
required fields are discharged, the target remains at its current
status.
\end{proof}

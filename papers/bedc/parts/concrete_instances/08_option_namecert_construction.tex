\section{Option composite image classifier normalized present intermediate classifier determinacy}
\label{sec:option-composite-image-classifier-normalized-present-intermediate-classifier-determinacy}

\begin{theorem}[Tagged option composite image classifier normalized present intermediate classifier determinacy]
\label{thm:tagged-option-composite-image-classifier-normalized-present-intermediate-classifier-determinacy}
This theorem fits the per-object template as follows: the traditional target is
classifier-level readback for the intermediate public endpoints in functorial
$\mathsf{OptionUp}$; the BEDC source is $\mathsf{BHist}$ with two composable
$\mathsf{DescentCertificate}$ maps; the carrier is the selected intermediate
$\Eone$ endpoint shape; the classifier is
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{T,\sim_T}$; the stability obligations
are selected visible public factorization, present-payload determinacy,
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionSourceHsameCompatible}(T,\sim_T)$, and the equivalence
fields of $\mathsf{cert}_T$; the ledger records selected intermediate endpoints
and competing visible intermediate payloads; the core theorem is
classifier-level determinacy; exactness says every competing visible
intermediate pair lies in the same $\sim_T$ class; the standard bridge is
some-payload functorial readback; and the status is $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$. Let
the carriers $S,T,U$, classifiers $\sim_S,\sim_T,\sim_U$, descents
$\delta,\epsilon$, preservation fields $\rho_{\delta},\rho_{\epsilon}$,
endpoint-stability field $\eta_{\epsilon}$, certificates
$\mathsf{cert}_S,\mathsf{cert}_T,\mathsf{cert}_U$, compatibility fields,
endpoints $m,m',u,u'$, and selected visible payloads $x,y$ satisfy exactly the
hypotheses of
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-composite-image-classifier-normalized-selected-visible-public-factorization}.
Then there exist $k,k',p,q,a,b:\mathsf{BHist}$ such that
\begin{enumerate}
\item the ten selected visible public factorization clauses of
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-composite-image-classifier-normalized-selected-visible-public-factorization}
hold for $k,k',p,q,a,b$;
\item the selected intermediate endpoints are classified by
$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{T,\sim_T}(k,k');
$$
\item every competing visible intermediate pair lies in the selected
intermediate classifier class:
$$
\forall r,s:\mathsf{BHist}.\;
T(r)\to T(s)\to
\hsame(k,\Eone(r))\to \hsame(k',\Eone(s))\to r\sim_T s.
$$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-composite-image-classifier-normalized-present-intermediate-payload-determinacy}
to the displayed carrier, classifier, descent, preservation, stability,
certificate, compatibility, endpoint, and selected visible payload data. This
supplies $k,k',p,q,a,b$, the ten selected visible public factorization clauses,
and the two present-payload determinacy functions.

The present branch of the ten factorization clauses gives $T(p)$, $T(q)$,
$\hsame(k,\Eone(p))$, $\hsame(k',\Eone(q))$, and $p\sim_T q$. These witnesses
assemble
$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{T,\sim_T}(k,k')
$$
by the present constructor of the tagged option history classifier.

Now fix competing visible intermediate payloads $r,s:\mathsf{BHist}$ with
$T(r)$, $T(s)$, $\hsame(k,\Eone(r))$, and $\hsame(k',\Eone(s))$. The two
determinacy functions yield $\hsame(p,r)$ and $\hsame(q,s)$.

Target hsame compatibility converts these to $p\sim_T r$ and $q\sim_T s$.
Using the symmetry field of $\mathsf{cert}_T$ on $p\sim_T r$ gives
$r\sim_T p$; two applications of the transitivity field of $\mathsf{cert}_T$
compose $r\sim_T p$, $p\sim_T q$, and $q\sim_T s$ into $r\sim_T s$.
\end{proof}

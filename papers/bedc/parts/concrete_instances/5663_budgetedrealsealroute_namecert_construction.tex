$$

\item $T$ is the $\RegularCauchyTailMeetUp$ threshold row that must be displayed before the completion route may expose a seal;
\item $O$ is the $\RealObservationBudgetUp$ entrance row tying the selected budget to the Real-facing observation surface;
\item $L$ is the $\CauchyLimitSealUp$ route row that carries the sealed Cauchy-limit handoff;

The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,T,O,L,R,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for quotient streams, host real equality, countable choice, an external limit selector, or an ambient completeness theorem.

$$

\begin{enumerate}

\item $T$ is the $\FiniteTraceEvaluatorUp$ observation row, recording the finite checker trace, bounded inputs, accepted checkpoint reads, and validation boundary.

\begin{theorem}[Finite observation fold NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:finite-observation-fold-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted packet $O=(K,T,R,N,S,G,H,C,P,L)$. The carrier definition exposes exactly the critical-path, trace, endpoint, seal, fold, refusal, transport, replay, provenance, and naming rows.

The finite observation rows are read before any fold is public. The trace row $T$ records bounded checker output, while $R$ and $N$ record the typed endpoint and consistency seal available for that bounded output.

The fold row $S$ aggregates only those displayed observations, and the refusal row $G$ blocks export as a stronger global decision procedure. Since the excluded unbounded and global claims are absent from the carrier, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CauchyRateNormalForm NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-rate-normal-form-obligations}

\begin{proof}

The raw rate row $\rho$ is not consumed directly by the downstream Real seal. The replay row $C$ first routes the precision request $q$ through $\rho$ and then through the monotone row $\nu$.

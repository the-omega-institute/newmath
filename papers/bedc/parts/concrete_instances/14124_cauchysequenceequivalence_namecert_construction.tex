$$

with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $X$ and $Y$ are the two $\RegSeqRatUp$ regular rational Cauchy name rows;
\item $S$ and $T$ are the paired $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window rows through which $X$ and $Y$ are observed;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance ledger attached to the paired windows;
\item $E$ is the local finite equivalence classifier recording that every scheduled paired observation is within the displayed dyadic tolerance;
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ seal-comparison row that may be consumed only after $X,Y,S,T,D,E$ have been displayed;
\item $H$ is the componentwise $\hsame$ provenance for the two regular names, the paired stream windows, the dyadic ledger, the classifier, and the seal-comparison row;

\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and the local naming row;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CauchySequenceEquivalenceUp}$ row.

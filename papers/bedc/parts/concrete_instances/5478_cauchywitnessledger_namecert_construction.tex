\begin{theorem}[Cauchy witness ledger NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The route rows in $C$ replay exactly that order, while $H$ preserves the same coordinates under componentwise transport and $P,N$ provide provenance and local naming. No hidden row remains from which a completion-facing consumer could bypass one of the four analytic rows.

\begin{definition}[Cauchy witness ledger carrier]
\label{def:cauchy-witness-ledger-carrier}
A $\CauchyWitnessLedgerUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $Q$ is the $\CauchyDiagonalBudgetUp$ row exposing the finite precision, mesh, comparison, and selected diagonal budget for the completion-facing request;
\item $B$ is the $\RealObservationBudgetUp$ row repackaging the same budget into finite observation windows, dyadic readbacks, $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff rows, and $\RealUp$ seal rows;
\item $S$ is the $\CauchySealBudgetSynchronizerUp$ row tying the selected diagonal budget, observation budget, shared-threshold route, and seal row into one synchronized read;
\item $K$ is the $\RealCompletionSealClassifierUp$ row classifying the completion-facing seal only after the synchronized budget and seal rows have been consumed;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $Q,B,S,K,C,P,N$;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes from the diagonal budget to the observation budget, from the observation budget to the synchronizer, and from the synchronizer to the completion-seal classifier;

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CauchyWitnessLedgerUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for quotient stream equality, host real equality, selected external limits, countable choice, or ambient completeness.
\end{definition}

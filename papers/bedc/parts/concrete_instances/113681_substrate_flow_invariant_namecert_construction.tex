\begin{theorem}[Substrate-flow-invariant NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:substrate-flow-invariant-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:substrate-flow-invariant-carrier}. The substrate row $U$, orbit row $O$, invariant ledger $F$, audit route $A$, and stream window row $W$ are the only data rows available before transport and replay.

Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming are then exactly the rows $H,C,P,N$, so the packet cannot substitute an infinite orbit, internal induction principle, semantic conservation theorem, hidden classifier repair, or external trust source for the displayed rows.
\end{proof}

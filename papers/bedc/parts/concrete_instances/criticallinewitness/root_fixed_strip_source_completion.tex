\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness fixed-strip source totality]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-source-totality}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet.
Every fixed critical-strip source read of $W$ is total on the displayed
source rows, zero-strip/source modulus rows, finite $\BHist$ observation
windows, refusal ledger, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming
surface
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The read may expose the supplied $\ZetaZerosUp$ zero row, the supplied
$\CritStripUp$ strip row, the $\NatUp$ depth row, the $\RealUp$ real-part row,
the $\RatUp$ rational comparison row, the displayed $\hsame$ transports,
$\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and the local
$\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ handoff. It exposes no zero-producing
search, analytic-continuation theorem, host equality, quotient
representative, ambient completeness witness, or proof of $\mathsf{RH}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted packet through the carrier of
\autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. The visible coordinates are
exactly the supplied zero row $Z$, strip row $S$, modulus row $M$, real-part
row $R$, rational ledger $Q$, transport row $H$, continuation route $C$,
provenance row $P$, and local naming row $N$.

Next use the fixed-strip tuple exhaustion of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-row-exhaustion} together with
the root finite-budget source totality of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-finite-budget-source-totality}. These
rows identify the source and modulus faces as the same finite observation
window rather than as separate analytic inputs.

Finally read the refusal side through
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-zero-route-nonescape}. Since no other
carrier coordinate is available, the displayed rows exhaust the fixed
critical-strip source and the excluded analytic, host, quotient, completeness,
and $\mathsf{RH}$ outputs have no route into the read.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness modulus refusal ledger exhaustion]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-refusal-ledger-exhaustion}
For an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet
$W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$, every zero-strip/source modulus request is exhausted
by the finite refusal ledger over
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The ledger records the $\BHist$ observation window, $\hsame$ transport,
$\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ handoff for the
source and modulus rows. It cannot be extended by a hidden modulus source,
analytic-continuation evidence, zero-free-strip evidence, a global zero
classifier, a host numeric oracle, quotient equality, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the source totality of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-source-totality}. It fixes the
only readable source and modulus coordinates at $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$.

Now apply the modulus refusal exhaustion row of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-refusal-exhaustion}. A
zero-strip modulus request is routed through the displayed $\BHist$,
$\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ surface, so the request remains a
finite ledger entry rather than an analytic continuation claim.

It remains to rule out alternate sources. The finite-budget ledger stability
of \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-finite-budget-ledger-stability}
keeps the comparison row $Q$ on the same transported route, while
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-boundary-nonescape} removes
zero-free-strip evidence, global classifiers, host numeric oracles, quotient
equality, ambient completeness, and $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data from the packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness RH-boundary NameCert handoff]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-boundary-namecert-handoff}
Every downstream $\mathsf{RH}$-boundary consumer of an accepted
$\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet receives only the local
$\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ handoff for the fixed-strip source and
modulus refusal ledger
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The handoff may cite finite source rows, finite observation windows,
componentwise $\hsame$ transports, displayed $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$
provenance, and the refusal ledger. It does not deliver $\mathsf{RH}$,
analytic continuation, zero production, host zero classification, quotient
presentation, host equality, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First read the local handoff through
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. The five obligation
rows admit the carrier, bind zero-strip compatibility, discipline the
modulus, enforce non-escape, and exhaust the ledger through
$Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$.

Next combine fixed-strip source totality with modulus refusal ledger
exhaustion. The source face and the modulus face are the same finite packet
as seen by the downstream consumer, and their transport, replay, provenance,
and naming rows are already displayed.

Finally use the RH consumer boundary
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-consumer-boundary}. The downstream
consumer can receive only the finite BEDC witness boundary, so the NameCert
handoff cannot become analytic continuation, zero production, host
classification, quotient presentation, host equality, ambient completeness,
or a proof of $\mathsf{RH}$.
\end{proof}

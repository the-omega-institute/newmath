\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness source modulus classifier lock]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-source-modulus-classifier-lock}

Consequently a root consumer may classify the supplied zero-strip source only by reading those displayed rows. It receives no $\mathsf{RH}$ proof object, global zero classifier, analytic-continuation certificate, quotient representative, host equality, or ambient completeness coordinate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Use the root comparison ledger of \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-comparison-ledger}. The ledger places every rational comparison through $Q,M,R,H,C,P,N$ and refuses analytic or quotient replacement rows.

Finally apply the zero-strip carrier totality and RH-refusal non-escape theorems. They identify the accepted packet as exactly $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ and exclude an $\mathsf{RH}$ proof object, global zero classifier, analytic-continuation certificate, quotient representative, host equality, and ambient completeness.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:critical-line-witness-zero-strip-readback-totality}

\begin{proof}

\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness root finite route totality]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-root-finite-route-totality}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet.
Every root-facing consumer of $W$ reads only the supplied zero row, the
critical-strip row, the modulus row, the real-part row, the rational
comparison ledger, and the structural $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$,
and $\NameCert$ rows displayed by
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The consumer exports no proof of $\mathsf{RH}$, global zero classifier,
analytic-continuation certificate, quotient representative, host equality, or
ambient completeness datum.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with carrier route exactness and the root-source triad,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-carrier-route-exactness} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-source-triad}. They expose the
supplied zero row, critical-strip row, and source-facing rational comparison
row as finite $\BHist$ data before any root-facing consumer reads the packet.

Next apply the source-normalization interface and the modulus-ledger
interface,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-source-normalization-interface} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-interface}. These rows place
$M,R,Q$ downstream of the already exposed $Z,S$ source route and attach the
read to the same $\hsame/\Cont/\Pkg/\NameCert$ surface.

Finally use the RH-refusal ledger completeness row
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-refusal-ledger-completeness}. It removes
proof of $\mathsf{RH}$, global zero classification, analytic continuation,
quotient representatives, host equality, and ambient completeness from every
root-facing export, so the route is exactly the displayed finite packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness root modulus source exactness]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-root-modulus-source-exactness}
For an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet
$W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$, every root-facing modulus or rational-comparison read
is sourced by $M,R,Q$ only after the supplied $Z,S$ source rows have been
exposed. No modulus request may be completed from a hidden analytic estimate,
host equality, quotient representative, ambient completeness row, external
zero search, or proof of $\mathsf{RH}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First expose the source side by
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-source-normalization-interface} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-source-triad}. These results place the
zero row and critical-strip row in the accepted finite packet before any
modulus or rational-comparison read is available to the root boundary.

Now apply the modulus-ledger interface
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-interface}. It routes every
modulus, real-part, and rational-comparison request through $M,R,Q$ together
with the displayed transport, continuation, package, and naming rows.

The exclusion of alternate sources follows from
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-modulus-ledger-scope} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-source-modulus-refusal-totality}. Failed or
off-route requests land in the finite refusal ledger rather than in analytic
estimates, host equality, quotient representatives, ambient completeness,
external zero search, or an $\mathsf{RH}$ proof.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness root refusal ledger non-escape]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-root-refusal-ledger-nonescape}
Every downstream $\mathsf{RH}$-facing read of an accepted
$\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet remains attached to the displayed refusal
ledger over
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The ledger cannot be bypassed by host equality, quotient representatives, zero
search, analytic continuation, ambient completeness, a standard bridge, or an
off-packet modulus source.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the finite route totality above. It identifies the root-facing
source, modulus, comparison, transport, continuation, package, and naming
surface as the entire packet available to downstream consumers.

Next apply the root refusal-boundary totality
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-refusal-boundary-totality} and the
downstream RH boundary nonescape
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-rh-boundary-nonescape}. These
rows keep $\mathsf{RH}$-facing reads on the finite refusal side of the same
displayed packet.

Finally use
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-refusal-ledger-completeness} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-refusal-downstream-gate}. The refusal
ledger has no escape coordinate for host equality, quotient representatives,
zero search, analytic continuation, ambient completeness, a standard bridge,
or an off-packet modulus source, so every downstream read remains attached to
the displayed ledger.
\end{proof}

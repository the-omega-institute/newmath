\begin{theorem}[CriticalLineWitness fixed-strip NameCert source lock]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-namecert-source-lock}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted fixed-strip
$\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet. Every accepted $\NameCert$ source read of
$W$ is locked to the supplied $\ZetaZerosUp$ row $Z$, the supplied
$\CritStripUp$ row $S$, the fixed-strip source and refusal-budget packet,
and the displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local
$\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ rows carried by $H,C,P,N$. The read
has no coordinate for zero search, $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data, analytic
continuation, host equality, quotient representatives, or ambient
completeness.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.CriticalLineWitnessUp.CriticalLineWitnessCarrier\_fixed\_strip\_namecert\_source\_lock}
\begin{proof}
Start with the carrier of \autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. It
lists the accepted witness as the finite packet $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$, so a
NameCert source read begins on the displayed zero row, strip row, support
rows, and local naming row.

Apply the NameCert obligations of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. These obligations
bind the local naming row to the same $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and
$\Pkg$ surface and leave no independent host or quotient naming coordinate.

Now attach the fixed-strip refusal-budget packet
\autoref{def:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-refusal-budget-packet}. Its
row inventory adds the fixed-strip source, line-modulus, comparison, budget,
transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows without adding an
analytic or ambient completion row.

Finally use the fixed-strip budget route determinacy theorem
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-budget-route-determinacy}.
It forces every budget-facing source read through that finite packet, so
zero search, $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data, analytic continuation, host equality,
quotient representatives, and ambient completeness are refused rather than
read.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CriticalLineWitness fixed-strip classifier stability]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-classifier-stability}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted fixed-strip
$\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet. The fixed-strip classifier determined by
$W$ is stable under componentwise $\hsame$ transport and displayed $\Cont$
replay on the same rows $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$. Transport or replay may move
the accepted read along the displayed packet route, but it cannot replace
the classifier by zero search, $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data, analytic
continuation, host equality, quotient representatives, or ambient
completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with classifier exactness,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-classifier-exactness}. It identifies the
root classifier with the supplied zero row $Z$, critical-strip row $S$, and
displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local $\NameCert$ surface.

Use componentwise StdBridge transport,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-componentwise-stdbridge-transport}. The
transport row preserves the displayed packet components, and the replay row
keeps the read on the same $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ carrier.

It remains to exclude alternate classifier sources after transport. Apply
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-namecert-source-lock}. Since
the NameCert source itself is locked to the fixed-strip packet and refuses
zero search, $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data, analytic continuation, host equality,
quotient representatives, and ambient completeness, the transported
classifier is stable on exactly the displayed fixed-strip rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CriticalLineWitness fixed-strip ledger exactness]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-ledger-exactness}
Every downstream zeta or $\mathsf{RH}$-boundary consumer of an accepted
fixed-strip $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet receives exactly the fixed-strip
ledger rows $Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ and no stronger analytic coordinate. The
consumer may read the supplied zero row, critical-strip row, modulus row,
real-part row, rational comparison and refusal-budget ledger, transport
row, replay row, provenance row, and local naming row; it does not receive
zero search, $\mathsf{RH}$ proof data, analytic continuation, host equality,
quotient representatives, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply ledger route stability,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-ledger-route-stability}. It keeps the
modulus and comparison ledger on the displayed $M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ route and
prevents branching to host comparison, quotient representatives, analytic
continuation, or ambient completeness.

Next use the fixed-strip zeta consumer boundary,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-zeta-consumer-boundary}. It
places every downstream zeta-facing read on the refusal budget and rational
comparison ledger of the same finite packet.

Finally apply fixed-strip classifier stability,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-classifier-stability}. Since
transport and replay preserve the classifier only on
$Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$, a downstream zeta or $\mathsf{RH}$-boundary consumer
receives exactly those ledger rows and no stronger analytic coordinate.
\end{proof}

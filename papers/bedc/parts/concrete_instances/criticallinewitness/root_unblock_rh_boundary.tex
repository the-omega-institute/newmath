\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness supplied zero-strip route]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-supplied-zero-strip-route}
For an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$, every root-facing read of the supplied $\ZetaZerosUp$ row and the $\CritStripUp$ row factors through the displayed finite route
$$
Z,S,H,C,P,N.
$$
The route is supplied by the packet carrier and local $\NameCert$ obligations, and it cannot be replaced by zero search, host complex equality, a global zero-set classifier, quotient representatives, analytic continuation, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Read the carrier in \autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. The rows $Z$ and $S$ are displayed coordinates of the accepted finite packet, and $H,C,P,N$ are the transport, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows that carry them.

Apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations} and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-supplied-input-carrier-row}. These results make the supplied zero and strip rows the entry point for zero-facing reads before any modulus or comparison route is consumed.

Use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-strip-zero-compatibility}, \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-strip-zero-route-exactness}, and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-supplied-zero-readback}. They keep the zero-strip read on the same displayed $\BHist$ surface and through the same $\hsame$ and $\Cont$ rows.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-nonescape-boundary}. Since the excluded analytic and host-level coordinates are absent from the packet, the supplied zero-strip route is exactly the finite route through $Z,S,H,C,P,N$.
\end{proof}

\end{theorem}

The non-escape clauses of \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-nonescape-boundary} and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-nonescape} then remove host equality, hidden numeric oracles, quotient representatives, analytic continuation, and ambient completeness from the ledger entry.

\label{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-nonescape-package}

Next apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-consumer-boundary-package} and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-source-obligation}. These results bind every downstream RH-facing read to the displayed supplied-input packet and its finite modulus ledger.

Use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-nonclassification-boundary}, \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-nonproof-boundary}, and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-refusal-package}. \begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness root modulus-window totality]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-root-modulus-window-totality}
Every root modulus-window read of an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet
$W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ factors through
$$
M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
The modulus row, real-part row, rational comparison ledger, componentwise
transport, displayed continuation, package provenance, and local naming row
are therefore the whole finite route available to a root-facing consumer. No
hidden numeric oracle, host real equality, quotient representative, analytic
continuation row, or ambient completeness input can answer such a read.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the modulus ledger entry,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-entry}. It identifies
$M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ as the finite ledger used by Real and rational-comparison
reads.

Next apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-row},
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-depth-routing}, and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-request-factorization}. These
results force every depth request through the displayed modulus row, real-part
row, rational comparison row, transport row, and continuation route before
$M(n)$ is public.

The rational side is supplied by
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rat-comparison-ledger-exactness} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-comparison-route-totality}. They
make the comparison row $Q$ the only finite rational surface attached to the
modulus window.

Finally use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-nonescape} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-nonescape-boundary}. The excluded host and
analytic coordinates are absent, so $M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ exhaust every root
modulus-window read.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness root strip-comparison classifier]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-root-strip-comparison-classifier}
The supplied zero-strip row $Z,S,H,C,P,N$ and the rational comparison row
$M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ of an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet share the same
finite classifier route through the displayed carrier
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N.
$$
A root-facing classifier may compare the zero-strip evidence and the
half-line rational comparison only by these rows and their componentwise
$\hsame$ and $\Cont$ routes.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the supplied zero-strip route,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-supplied-zero-strip-route}. It locks every
zero-facing and strip-facing read to $Z,S,H,C,P,N$.

Then apply the modulus ledger entry and root modulus-window totality,
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-modulus-ledger-entry} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-modulus-window-totality}. Together
they put every rational comparison read on $M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$.

Use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-supplied-input-carrier-row} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. These results bind
the supplied rows and the modulus-comparison rows to one accepted carrier and
one local naming surface.

The classifier route is therefore componentwise: $H$ transports the displayed
rows, $C$ replays their reads, $P$ records their provenance, and $N$ names the
local packet. The non-escape boundary excludes host equality, zero search,
global zero classification, quotient representatives, analytic continuation,
and ambient completeness from the comparison.
\end{proof}

\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-strip-comparison-classifier} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-source-obligation}. The
consumer source read is then forced through the displayed supplied rows,
modulus rows, rational comparison ledger, and structural rows.

Apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-consumer-boundary-package},
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-nonclassification-boundary}, and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-nonproof-boundary}. These boundaries

search, and any proof of $\mathsf{RH}$.

Finally use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-refusal-package} and
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-rh-refusal-ledger-completeness}. Their
refusal rows remove quotient representatives, host equality, analytic
continuation, and ambient completeness, so the downstream gate exports exactly
the finite refusal package.
\end{proof}

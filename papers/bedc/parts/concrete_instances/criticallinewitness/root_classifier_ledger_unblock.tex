\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness classifier exactness]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-classifier-exactness}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet.
The classifier available at the root is determined only by the supplied zero
row $Z$, the critical-strip row $S$, and the displayed support rows
$H,C,P,N$. It cannot be replaced by host equality, zero search, analytic
continuation, quotient representatives, or ambient completeness; any
accepted classifier read must remain on the finite $\BHist$, $\hsame$,
$\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ surface displayed by
$W$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. Its
source side displays exactly the supplied zero row $Z$, the supplied strip row
$S$, and the support rows $H,C,P,N$ before any public critical-line read is
available.

Apply the NameCert obligations of
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. The zero-strip
compatibility row binds $Z$ and $S$ through displayed $\hsame$ transport, while
carrier admission and non-escape keep the classifier read inside the same
finite packet.

Finally use the root source triad
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-source-triad} and the root unblock
refusal boundary
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-unblock-refusal-boundary}. They expose
the finite source rows and refuse host equality, zero search, analytic
continuation, quotient representatives, and ambient completeness as alternate
classifier sources. Thus the classifier is exact only through
$Z,S,H,C,P,N$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness ledger route stability]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-ledger-route-stability}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet.
The modulus and comparison ledger route through $M,R,Q$ is stable under the
displayed $\hsame$ transports, $\Cont$ replay rows, $\Pkg$ provenance, and
local $\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ row. Every accepted comparison read
therefore uses the same packet-local route $M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ and does not branch
to a host comparison, quotient representative, analytic continuation row, or
ambient completeness witness.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.CriticalLineWitnessUp.CriticalLineWitnessCarrier\_ledger\_route\_stability}
\begin{proof}
Read the modulus and comparison coordinates from
\autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. The packet lists $M$ as the
depth-to-line modulus row, $R$ as the consumed real-part row, and $Q$ as the
rational comparison ledger.

Use \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. Its modulus
discipline and ledger-exactness rows attach $M,R,Q$ to the same displayed
$H,C,P,N$ support, so comparison reads are transported, replayed, recorded,
and named inside the accepted packet.

It remains to rule out alternate ledger routes. The root source triad
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-source-triad} keeps the source side
finite, and the root unblock refusal boundary
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-unblock-refusal-boundary} refuses the
host, quotient, analytic-continuation, and completeness replacements. Hence
the $M,R,Q$ ledger route is stable exactly under the displayed
$\hsame/\Cont/\Pkg/\NameCert$ rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Critical-line witness downstream consumer unblock]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-downstream-consumer-unblock}
Every downstream $\mathsf{RH}$-boundary consumer of an accepted
$\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet may cite only the finite surface
$$
Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N .
$$
The consumer may use this surface as the supplied zero row, supplied strip row,
modulus row, real-part row, rational comparison ledger, transport row,
continuation row, provenance row, and local naming row; it may not cite an
$\mathsf{RH}$ proof, a global zero classifier, a zero-producing search, host
equality, analytic continuation, quotient representatives, or ambient
completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the carrier definition
\autoref{def:critical-line-witness-carrier}. It makes
$Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ the visible packet and withholds every global analytic or
host-level coordinate from the carrier.

Next apply \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-namecert-obligations}. Carrier
admission, non-escape, and ledger exactness say that accepted public reads are
exhausted by the displayed packet rows and their local $\NameCert$ support.

Finally combine the root source triad
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-source-triad} with the root unblock
refusal boundary
\autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-unblock-refusal-boundary}. The triad
exposes the finite zero-strip and comparison source before downstream use, and
the refusal boundary removes every off-packet $\mathsf{RH}$, zero-search,
host-equality, analytic-continuation, quotient, and completeness demand.
Therefore downstream consumers are unblocked precisely by the finite
$Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ surface.
\end{proof}

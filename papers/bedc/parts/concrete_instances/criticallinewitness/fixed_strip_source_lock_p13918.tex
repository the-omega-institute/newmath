\subsection{Fixed-strip source lock}

\begin{theorem}[CriticalLineWitness fixed-strip source lock]
\label{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-source-lock}
Let $W=(Z,S,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CriticalLineWitnessUp$ packet whose fixed-strip read is consumed by an $\mathsf{RH}$-facing route. The read is locked to the finite source tuple consisting of the $\CritStripUp$ strip row $S$, the $\ZetaContinuationWitnessUp$ continuation row read through $Z$, the $\ZetaZerosUp$ zero ledger $Z$, the $\RealUp$ and $\RegSeqRatUp$ finite-window rows $R,Q$, the modulus row $M$, the displayed $\BHist$ carrier data, componentwise $\hsame$ transport $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance $P$, and local $\NameCert_{\CriticalLineWitnessUp}$ support $N$. No accepted consumer may replace this tuple by an $\mathsf{RH}$ proof, a nontrivial-zero classification, host complex equality, ambient analytic continuation, quotient equality, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the root zeta, real, and rational source-readiness package of \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-zeta-real-rat-source-readiness}. It exposes $Z,M,R,Q,H,C,P,N$ before any downstream critical-line, zeta, $\RealUp$, or $\RegSeqRatUp$ consumer can read $W$.

Attach the fixed-strip side through the fixed-strip source totality and source classifier rows, \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-source-totality} and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-fixed-strip-carrier-source-totality}. These place the $\CritStripUp$ strip row, the $\ZetaZerosUp$ ledger, and the $\ZetaContinuationWitnessUp$ source read on the same displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local $\NameCert$ route. The real and rational finite-window side is the finite-window surface supplied by \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}.

Finally apply the root gap refusal completeness and downstream unblock package, \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-gap-refusal-completeness} and \autoref{thm:critical-line-witness-root-downstream-unblock-package}. They leave the consumer with exactly the finite source tuple stated above and refuse every request for $\mathsf{RH}$ truth, nontrivial-zero classification, host complex equality, ambient analytic continuation, quotient equality, or ambient completeness outside that tuple.
\end{proof}

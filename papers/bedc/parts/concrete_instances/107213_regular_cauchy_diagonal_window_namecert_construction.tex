\label{thm:regular-cauchy-diagonal-window-obligations}

\item diagonal-window visibility, requiring each consumer-facing diagonal request to read a displayed finite window before any Real seal is exposed;

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The Real seal is downstream of that finite route. Componentwise $\hsame$ transport in $H$ preserves the displayed rows, and $\Cont$ replay in $C$ repeats the same ordered route before $E$ can be inspected.

Finally, provenance and naming are exhausted by $P$ and $N$. Since the carrier has no coordinate for quotient streams, selected limits, countable choice, trichotomy, host real equality, or ambient Real completeness, no such obligation can be exported by the local certificate.

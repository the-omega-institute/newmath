\item $F$ is the fold-source row listing the finite source entries whose target fibers are inspected;
\item $L$ is the target-fiber ledger, with each retained source entry attached to a displayed target address in $W$;
\item $M$ is the collision-kernel matrix row whose entries are finite ledger reads comparing pairs of target fibers inside $W$;
\item $I$ is the moment-index row naming the finite family of moment requests consumed by downstream readers;
\item $S$ is the Hankel-shadow row obtained by reading the visible moment-index rows against the finite matrix row;
\item $H$ stores the componentwise $\hsame$ transports for window, fold-source, target-fiber ledger, matrix, moment-index, and Hankel-shadow rows;

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CollisionKernelUp}$ row.

The displayed transport, continuation, package, and naming surface is
$$

$$

The carrier has no coordinate for a host collision-counting operation, an ambient spectrum, an external measure, an infinite Hankel matrix, or quotient fibers.

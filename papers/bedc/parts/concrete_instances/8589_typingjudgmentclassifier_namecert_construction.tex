\begin{definition}[TypingJudgmentClassifier carrier]
\label{def:typing-judgment-classifier-carrier}
A $\TypingJudgmentClassifierUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
T=(J,M,D,S,C,H,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $J$ is the typing-judgment row whose membership in the accepted classifier is being read;
\item $M$ is the displayed $\Ext$ membership row certifying that $J$ is accepted by the local type-checking classifier;
\item $D$ is the finite derivation-route row that explains how the classifier reached $M$;
\item $S$ is the $\SigRel$ readback row naming the accepted typing judgment as the public reading of the membership row along $D$;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ replay routes for reading the judgment row, membership row, derivation route, readback row, provenance row, and local naming row through the same finite packet;
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $J,M,D,S,C,P,N$;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over \autoref{ch:visions-type-checking-as-classifier-membership}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typeclassifiermembership-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-classifiertyping-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-subjectreduction-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typecheckingderivationtrace-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-classifiermorphism-namecert}, $\BHist$, $\Ext$, $\SigRel$, $\Cont$, $\hsame$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\TypingJudgmentClassifierUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for host type semantics, hidden conversion, quotient syntax equality, unrecorded derivation search, closed subject reduction, or an ambient classifier morphism.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[TypingJudgmentClassifier NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:typing-judgment-classifier-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:typing-judgment-classifier-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\TypingJudgmentClassifierUp}$ surface consists exactly of five finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission exposes precisely $T=(J,M,D,S,C,H,P,N)$;
\item membership exactness reads the accepted typing-judgment decision only from the displayed $\Ext$ row $M$;
\item derivation-route replay reads the route only through $D$ and the displayed $\Cont$ rows $C$;
\item public readback exposes the accepted judgment only through the $\SigRel$ row $S$ transported by $H$;
\item provenance and non-escape bind every consumer read to $P,N$ and exclude host type semantics, hidden conversion, quotient syntax equality, unrecorded derivation search, closed subject reduction, and ambient classifier morphisms.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from the finite tuple in \autoref{def:typing-judgment-classifier-carrier}. The judgment, membership, derivation route, readback, replay, transport, provenance, and naming rows are visible coordinates.

Membership exactness follows because $M$ is the only $\Ext$ membership coordinate. The derivation route $D$ and continuation row $C$ can replay how the decision is read, but they cannot replace $M$ by a hidden checker.

The public classifier read is exhausted by $S,H,P,N$. These rows name, transport, package, and locally certify the same displayed membership event, so the excluded host and quotient-side objects have no coordinate from which a consumer could recover them.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[TypingJudgmentClassifier derivation route]
\label{thm:typing-judgment-classifier-derivation-route}
Every accepted $\TypingJudgmentClassifierUp$ carrier routes the typing judgment through displayed classifier membership before any public readback is available:
$$
J \to M \to D \to S.
$$
The replay and transport rows $C,H$ preserve that order for downstream consumers, and the package and local name rows $P,N$ record the finite provenance of the same route.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Inspect the carrier inventory. The judgment row $J$ is not an accepted classifier decision by itself; the accepted decision is the displayed membership row $M$.

The derivation route $D$ records how the membership row is reached, and the $\SigRel$ row $S$ records the public reading of that accepted judgment. The continuation and transport rows replay and preserve these coordinates componentwise.

Since $P,N$ only package and name the same rows, every consumer route factors through the displayed chain from judgment to membership, derivation, and readback.
\end{proof}

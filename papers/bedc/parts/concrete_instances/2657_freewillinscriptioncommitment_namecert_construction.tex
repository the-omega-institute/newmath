\begin{theorem}[FreeWillInscriptionCommitment NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:free-will-inscription-commitment-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier exposure is projection from the finite tuple $W=(B,I,G,T,R,H,C,P,N)$. Every consumer-facing coordinate is one of the displayed rows.

Prior-boundary accountability reads $B$, while inscription-event accountability reads $I$ together with the GAP provenance row $G$. These two obligations keep the event located at the finite boundary named by the packet.

Classifier stability is componentwise through $T,H,C,P$. The transport rows compare and replay the visible boundary, event, provenance, and ledger data; they do not add a hidden decision procedure or an unrecorded source of novelty.

The non-escape obligation is the inventory boundary. The carrier has no coordinate for a hidden faculty, selected novelty source, deterministic reducer, host psychology predicate, or ambient agency oracle, so the local NameCert row cannot export one.
\end{proof}

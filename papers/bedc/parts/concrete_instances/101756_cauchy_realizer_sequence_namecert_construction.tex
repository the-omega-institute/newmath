\item $W$ is the finite family of $\CauchyRealizerUp$ rows, one row for each displayed sequence window in $S$.

\begin{theorem}[Cauchy realizer sequence NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-realizer-sequence-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\CauchyRealizerSequenceUp$ carrier
$$
Z=(S,W,R,D,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\CauchyRealizerSequenceUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier exposure for the finite $\StreamNameUp$ schedule $S$, the $\CauchyRealizerUp$ family $W$, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback family $R$, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ ledger family $D$, the terminal $\RealUp$ seal family $E$, and the structural rows $H,C,P,N$;
\item schedule exactness, requiring each displayed sequence index and precision request to enter through $S$ before any realizer row is read;
\item rowwise realizer exposure, requiring every member of $W$ to be an exposed $\CauchyRealizerUp$ row attached to the corresponding finite window of $S$;
\item regular-readback exactness, requiring $R$ to consume only the displayed schedule and realizer rows $S,W$;

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:cauchy-realizer-sequence-carrier}. The finite schedule $S$ is the only source of displayed sequence indices and precision requests, while the family $W$ is the only source of CauchyRealizer rows attached to that schedule.

For each displayed window, the readback and ledger rows are then forced. The row $R$ records exactly the regular rational observations obtained from $S$ and $W$, and $D$ records exactly the dyadic tolerances and comparison evidence used to certify those observations.

The terminal seal rows are downstream. The family $E$ has no entrance other than the matching $S,W,R,D$ rows, while $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite route. Since the excluded objects do not occur as carrier coordinates, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

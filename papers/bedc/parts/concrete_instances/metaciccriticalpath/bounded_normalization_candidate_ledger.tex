\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC bounded normalization candidate ledger]
\label{thm:metacic-critical-path-bounded-normalization-candidate-ledger}
Any bounded normalization candidate consumed by the current Phase Real and Completion route for an accepted $\MetaCICCriticalPathUp$ packet must display the finite packet rows
$$
S,N,O,U,D,H,C,P,L
$$
together with the four L10 faces $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\RealUp$. The ledger may be read only as candidate-mediated normalization evidence feeding confluence-facing and bounded decidability-facing consumers through the open-phase source budget; it exports no closed subject reduction, full consistency, quotient term equality, global beta-conversion decidability, selected limit, or $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the candidate-mediated SN finite socket normal form, \autoref{thm:metacic-critical-path-candidate-sn-finite-socket-normal-form}. It identifies the consumable normalization candidate as the socket-aware rows $S,N,O,U,D,H,C,P,L$ together with separate local face records for $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\RealUp$.

Next apply confluence candidate mediation, \autoref{thm:metacic-critical-path-confluence-candidate-mediation}. Confluence-facing reads remain mediated by the handoff row $U$, the obstruction and socket rows $O,D$, and the structural rows $H,C,P,L$; the candidate route does not become a closed Church--Rosser or subject-reduction theorem.

Apply the decidability fragment route, \autoref{thm:metacic-critical-path-decidability-fragment-route}. Bounded checker evidence is confined to the same packet rows and four L10 faces, so it cannot be promoted to global beta-conversion decidability or quotient term equality.

Finally use the real-completion source budget, \autoref{thm:metacic-critical-path-confluence-decidability-source-budget}. The current Phase Real and Completion route reads $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\RealUp$ before any candidate-mediated handoff is consumed. Since the cited routes contain no row for selected limits, full consistency, closed subject reduction, quotient equality, global beta-conversion decidability, or $\RealUp$ completeness, the displayed rows form the whole bounded normalization candidate ledger.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[TopGroupUp obligation carrier-classifier surface]
\label{thm:topgroup-obligation-carrier-classifier-surface}
The obligation surface for the $\TopGroupUp$ carrier and classifier consists of
one finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ packet carrying the displayed
$\NameCert_{\GroupUp}$ row, the displayed $\NameCert_{\TopologyUp}$ row, the
shared carrier endpoints, the componentwise $\hsame$ classifier rows, and the
single $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance row. These rows are the carrier-classifier
obligations visible to a public consumer, and no independent group carrier,
topology carrier, or host topological-group classifier is part of the surface.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.TopGroupUp.TopGroupRootThresholdPackage\_obligation\_carrier\_classifier\_surface}
\begin{proof}
Start from \autoref{def:topgroup-root-shared-source-packet}. Its displayed
coordinates are the $\GroupUp$ source, the $\TopologyUp$ source, the shared
$\mathsf{BHist}$ carrier endpoints, the componentwise classifier rows, and the
provenance packet.

Apply \autoref{thm:topgroup-root-shared-carrier-obligation}. It binds those
coordinates to one accepted packet and rules out a split into two independent
source packets.

Finally use \autoref{thm:topgroup-carrier-classifier-obligation}. The public
carrier-classifier obligation is exactly componentwise $\hsame$ over the two
displayed source rows together with the same $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance row.
\end{proof}

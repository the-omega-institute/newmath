\begin{lemma}[Metric L10 sibling dependency route]
\label{thm:metric-l10-sibling-dependency-route}
Every $\MetricUp$ consumer in the L10 route reads distance rows through the finite sibling dependency surface already named by the metric packet: $\RealUp$ distance endpoints from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, $\RealAlgOrderUp$ comparison rows or apartness rows when positive distance is inspected, $\SOneUp$ rows where circle distances are consumed through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-s1-namecert}, and $\ContinuousMapUp$ rows where modulus transport is needed through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-continuousmap-namecert}. The route is a finite displayed dependency route, not a host metric space, completed distance function, quotient real equality, or ambient continuity theorem.
\end{lemma}

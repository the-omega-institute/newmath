\begin{theorem}[Located compact interval selection NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:located-compact-interval-selection-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Read the interval rows first. The located row $I$ supplies endpoint windows and rational containment, while the closed bounded row $B$ supplies the compact interval source used by the selection route.

It extends the Bishop real source of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-bishopreal-namecert}, the comparison surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-bishoprealcomparison-namecert}, the apartness witness of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-cauchy-real-apartness-namecert}, and the L10 readback route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert} without importing trichotomy, quotient reals, selected limits, countable choice, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[Bishop real comparison modulus carrier]
\label{def:bishop-real-comparison-modulus-carrier}
A $\BishopRealComparisonModulusUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
Q=(B,A,R,S,D,M,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $B$ is the accepted $\BishopRealUp$ row exposing the regular-Cauchy real names and their finite comparison-facing source surface.
\item $A$ is the $\CauchyRealApartnessUp$ witness row supplying positive apartness data whenever the comparison request has a separated outcome.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row used to inspect the rational values of the requested finite windows.
\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ schedule row naming exactly the finite windows read by the comparison request.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance row storing the dyadic precision, slack, and positive-gap ledger used by the modulus.
\item $M$ is the comparison modulus row; it maps each displayed comparison request to the finite window and dyadic tolerance budget consumed by $R,S,D$.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $B,A,R,S,D,M$ and for the structural certificate rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a comparison request to the window, readback, tolerance, apartness, and modulus rows.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BishopRealUp$, $\CauchyRealApartnessUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BishopRealComparisonModulusUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted carriers only through the rows $B,A,R,S,D,M,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for quotient real equality, total order trichotomy, a selected limit, countable choice, or an ambient completeness principle for $\RealUp$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Bishop real comparison modulus NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:bishop-real-comparison-modulus-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\BishopRealComparisonModulusUp$ carrier
$$
Q=(B,A,R,S,D,M,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\BishopRealComparisonModulusUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the Bishop-real source, apartness witness, regular readback, stream schedule, dyadic tolerance, comparison modulus, transport, replay, provenance, and local-name rows;
\item modulus readback, requiring each comparison request to expose through $M$ the finite $S$ windows, $R$ rational observations, and $D$ dyadic tolerance budget it consumes;
\item apartness handoff, requiring every positive separated outcome to pass through the displayed $\CauchyRealApartnessUp$ row $A$ before it is offered to a real-facing consumer;
\item regular-sequence compatibility, requiring the rational observations in $R$ to be read from the same Bishop-real source surface named by $B$;
\item finite stream-window compatibility, requiring $S$ to name only the windows requested by $M$ and replayed by $C$;
\item dyadic tolerance exactness, requiring $D$ to store precisely the comparison precision, slack, and positive-gap data used by $M$ and $A$;
\item componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $B,A,R,S,D,M,C,P,N$ through $H$;
\item displayed $\Cont$ replay of each comparison request through the window, readback, tolerance, apartness, and modulus rows;
\item $\Pkg$ provenance for exactly the displayed packet;
\item ledger non-escape, requiring every consumer to remain inside $B,A,R,S,D,M,H,C,P,N$.
\end{enumerate}
Consequently the packet exports a finite comparison modulus interface for Bishop real names, not trichotomy, quotient real equality, selected limits, countable choice, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:bishop-real-comparison-modulus-carrier}. The projection exposes exactly the Bishop source, apartness witness, regular readback, stream schedule, dyadic tolerance, comparison modulus, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

The modulus obligation is read from $M$ together with $S,R,D$. A comparison request first names its finite stream windows, then reads the corresponding regular rational observations, and finally displays the dyadic precision and slack ledger used by the comparison.

The positive separated branch is forced through $A$. Since $A$ is the only apartness witness row, the carrier cannot turn a modulus read into trichotomy, quotient equality, or a selected real representative.

The remaining rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite route. They do not add any coordinate outside $B,A,R,S,D,M$, so the listed obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CauchyControlSequence NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-control-sequence-namecert-obligations}
For an accepted $\CauchyControlSequenceUp$ carrier $(R,W,D,M,E,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\CauchyControlSequenceUp}$ surface consists of five finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier formation for the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row, $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window schedule, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance row, displayed control/modulus row, $\RealUp$ seal handoff, transports, continuations, provenance, and local naming data;
\item classifier stability, stating that replacement of the displayed rows through $H$ and replay through $C$ preserves the same accepted control-sequence packet;
\item control-window coverage, stating that the control row $M$ covers exactly the finite windows $W$ at the dyadic tolerances recorded by $D$;
\item $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff, stating that the regular rational readback $R$ is the only source row inspected by the control/modulus surface;
\item Real seal non-escape, stating that the handoff $E$ reaches $\RealUp$ only through $R,W,D,M,H,C,P,N$ and not through quotient stream equality, a chosen limit, trichotomy, or ambient completeness.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier formation is the tuple of \autoref{def:cauchy-control-sequence-carrier}. It exposes exactly one regular rational readback row, one finite-window schedule, one dyadic tolerance row, one control/modulus row, one Real seal handoff, and the local transport, continuation, package, and naming rows.

Classifier stability is componentwise over $H$ and $C$. The replacement rows may transport the displayed coordinates and replay their consumers, but they do not introduce a second source row, a second tolerance ledger, or an external equality principle.

Control-window coverage is the binding role of $M$. The control row is accepted only as a displayed modulus for the windows $W$ and tolerances $D$, with the regular readback $R$ providing the rational values inspected inside those windows.

The $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff and the Real seal non-escape are then immediate from the carrier inventory. Since $E$ appears after $R,W,D,M$ and the provenance rows contain no quotient, chosen limit, trichotomy, or completeness coordinate, every seal-facing read is forced through the finite control route.
\end{proof}

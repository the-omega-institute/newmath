\begin{proposition}[IntUp Nat Add Rat Dyadic dependency spine]
\label{prop:int-nat-add-rat-dyadic-dependency-spine}
The $\IntUp$ signed-pair rows consume the unary arithmetic discipline of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-nat-namecert} and the finite additive comparison rows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-add-namecert}. Those signed rows then feed the denominator surfaces of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-rat-namecert}, and the same denominator and mantissa coordinates are read by the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ routes of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert}. Thus $\IntUp$ is the finite arithmetic spine from NatUp and AddUp into RatUp and DyadicRatCore, not an isolated hub or a host-integer equality principle.
\end{proposition}

\begin{proposition}[Int Nat Add Rat dependency surface]
\label{prop:int-nat-add-rat-sibling-dependency-surface}
The $\IntUp$ signed-pair carrier depends on the unary magnitude rows of $\NatUp$ from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-nat-namecert} and on the additive transport rows of $\AddUp$ from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-add-namecert} before the $\RatUp$ denominator consumer of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-rat-namecert} can use the integer surface. Thus the Rat-facing denominator route reads an already balanced signed-pair ledger rather than treating $\IntUp$ as a private rational subcarrier.
\end{proposition}

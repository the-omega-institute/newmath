\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

That theorem consumes exactly the fused $\StreamNameUp$ packet, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ endpoint and radius ledger, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ finite-request section, and the local $\RealUp$ seal over the displayed support rows.

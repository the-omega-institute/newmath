\label{thm:dyadicratcore-finite-tail-window-factorization}
Every finite-tail $\RealUp$ L10 window read whose $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ face is obtained from a displayed DyadicRatCore packet by a finite positive-unary exponent widening factors through the scale-window packet of \autoref{def:dyadicratcore-scale-window-packet}. The visible route is exactly the four-face finite-tail packet
$$
\DyadicRatCoreUp \to \StreamNameUp \to \RegSeqRatUp \to \RealUp
$$
with the displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local $\NameCert$ rows. It exports no host rational order, quotient equality, selected limit, ambient completeness row, or $\RealUp$ completeness principle.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the displayed finite positive-unary widening on the DyadicRatCore face. By scale-window cofinality, \autoref{thm:dyadicratcore-scale-window-cofinality}, the widened observation remains on the same cofinal dyadic comparison route carried by the scale-window packet.

Next apply terminal fusion, \autoref{thm:dyadicratcore-scale-window-terminal-fusion}. It identifies the terminal widened comparison packet as one fused $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window source, using only displayed scale transports, componentwise $\hsame$ rows, displayed $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert_{\DyadicRatCoreUp}$ data.

Now use RegSeqRat bridge handoff exactness, \autoref{thm:dyadicratcore-regseqrat-bridge-handoff-exactness}. The fused terminal packet is read by $\RegSeqRatUp$ as the same public DyadicRatCore envelope, so the finite request section does not add an ambient order coordinate or a quotient-equality coordinate.

Finally pass to the Real L10 finite-window determinacy surface, \autoref{thm:real-l10-finite-window-witness-determinacy}. That theorem consumes exactly the fused $\StreamNameUp$ packet, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ endpoint and radius ledger, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ finite-request section, and the local $\RealUp$ seal over the displayed support rows.

The exclusion clauses of the cited DyadicRatCore, RegSeqRat, and Real L10 theorems are stable under this composition. Hence the finite-tail read has the stated four-face factorization and cannot read host rational order, quotient equality, selected limits, ambient completeness, or Real completeness.
\end{proof}

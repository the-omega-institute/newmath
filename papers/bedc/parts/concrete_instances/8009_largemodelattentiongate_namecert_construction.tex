$$

\item $A$ is the $\LargeModelActivationOrbitUp$ row naming the finite activation-window segment selected for the read;
\item $C$ is the context-window row, carrying the bounded prompt and response boundary consumed by the gate;
\item $K$ is the $\LargeModelCorpusSupplyUp$ row that records the corpus-supply and filter boundary available to this local selection;

\begin{theorem}[LargeModelAttentionGate NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:large-model-attention-gate-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:large-model-attention-gate-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\LargeModelAttentionGateUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for $G=(T,A,C,K,U,V,H,R,P,N)$;
\item activation-window readback through the displayed activation-orbit row $A$;
\item corpus boundary exposure through the corpus-supply row $K$;
\item audit-channel routing through $U$;
\item verified-output handoff through $V$;
\item componentwise transport through $H$;
\item replay through the displayed $\Cont$ row $R$;
\item provenance through $P$;
\item local naming through $N$.
\end{enumerate}
Every accepted attention-gate consumer must display these rows before it can read the output-facing gate as a certified large-model packet.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:large-model-attention-gate-carrier}. The packet lists exactly $T,A,C,K,U,V,H,R,P,N$, so carrier admission supplies no additional hidden state.

The activation and context rows are the entry surface. The trace row $T$ is only readable through the activation-window row $A$ and the bounded context row $C$, while $K$ keeps the corpus-supply boundary attached to the same local read.

The output-facing rows are downstream. The audit-channel row $U$ and verified-output row $V$ are reached only after the displayed activation, context, and corpus rows have been consumed.

Finally $H,R,P,N$ provide transport, replay, provenance, and local naming for the same packet. Since the carrier has no hidden attention stream, context-recovery oracle, corpus-truth predicate, unchecked acceptance row, or model-side semantic proof coordinate, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:large-model-attention-gate-finite-window-soundness}

$$

By \autoref{thm:diagonal-limit-uniform-criterion-terminal-uniqueness-pullback}, the terminal consumer and the uniqueness-seal consumer collapse to one finite pullback route rather than two endpoint sources.

Finally combine this pullback with the non-escape clauses on both sides.

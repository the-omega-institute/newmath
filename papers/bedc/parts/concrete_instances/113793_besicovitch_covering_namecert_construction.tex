\item $R$ is the positive-radius ledger, read through $\RatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, or $\RealUp$ rows before any ball is admitted.

\begin{theorem}[Besicovitch covering NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:besicovitch-covering-namecert-obligations}

$$

No obligation is stored outside $X,M,R,Q,S,O,V,F,D,H,C,P,N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:besicovitch-covering-carrier}. The source rows $X$ and $M$ provide the metric points, distances, and ball-membership reads, while $R$ supplies the positive-radius data used by every candidate ball.

The sibling handoffs are then exhausted rowwise. The row $V$ exposes only the Vitali disjoint-selection route, $F$ exposes only finite shadow coverage, and $D$ exposes only the doubling-metric finite-growth route.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same route. Since the carrier contains none of the excluded host-cover, choice, measure-completion, quotient, compactness, differentiation, or ambient-completeness coordinates, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

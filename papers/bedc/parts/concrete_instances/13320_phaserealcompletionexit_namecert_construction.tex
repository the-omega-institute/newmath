\begin{definition}[Phase real-completion exit carrier]
\label{def:phase-real-completion-exit-carrier}
A $\PhaseRealCompletionExitUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ face recording the finite dyadic endpoint, tolerance, comparison, and arithmetic ledger used by the exit;
\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ face recording the finite observation windows through which the exit is read;
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ face recording the regular rational sequence handoff generated from the dyadic and stream rows;
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ face recording the real seal reached only after $D,S,Q$ have been exposed;
\item $B$ is the $\RealCompletionExactBoundaryUp$ face recording that the same route supplies exact boundary information without exporting host completeness, quotient stream equality, or a selected limit outside the packet;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the dyadic, stream, regular, real, exact-boundary, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes for reading $D$, then $S$, then $Q$, then $R$, and finally the boundary row $B$;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\PhaseRealCompletionExitUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\RealCompletionExactBoundaryUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\PhaseRealCompletionExitUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier accepts exactly packets whose visible exit surface is $D,S,Q,R,B,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for an ambient Cauchy completion, quotient-stream equality, selected real limit, host completeness proof, or a fused bridge token identifying the four local faces.

$$

\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row exposing regular rational values on those windows.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance ledger naming the dyadic request, halving data, and comparison cells.

\label{thm:effective-cauchy-sequence-space-regular-readback-route}

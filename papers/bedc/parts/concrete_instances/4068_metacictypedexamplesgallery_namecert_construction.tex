\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp$}

The $\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp$ packet realizes the typed-example audit map of \autoref{ch:metacic-typedexamples-audit-map} as a finite NameCert gallery. It sits beside the confluence audit surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacic-confluence-audit-namecert} and the closed-term substitution boundary of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-closedtermsubstitutionboundary-namecert}: those chapters constrain global metatheorem reads, while this chapter exposes the worked syntax and typing examples as finite rows.

\begin{definition}[MetaCIC typed-examples gallery carrier]
\label{def:metacic-typedexamples-gallery-carrier}
A $\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
G=(B,N,L,S,E,I,A,R,K,H,C,P,M)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $B$ records the Church Bool example rows, including the displayed true, false, and eliminator typing reads;
\item $N$ records the Church Nat example rows, including zero, successor, and numeral typing reads;
\item $L$ records the Church List example rows, including nil, cons, and eliminator typing reads;
\item $S$ records the Church Sum example rows, including left injection, right injection, and case-analysis typing reads;
\item $E$ records equality example rows, including reflexivity and transport-shaped reads that stay inside the displayed typing ledger;
\item $I$ records identity example rows, including polymorphic identity and dependent identity examples;
\item $A$ records application example rows, including function application and argument instantiation reads;
\item $R$ records beta-redex example rows and their displayed reduction endpoints;
\item $K$ records checker-acceptance rows for the example families named by $B,N,L,S,E,I,A,R$;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for every gallery row, route row, provenance row, and local naming row;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes for syntax readback, typing readback, beta-redex replay, checker-acceptance replay, and consumer exposure;
\item $P$ records $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, $\NameCert$, the MetaCIC typed-example rows, and the checker rows named by \autoref{ch:metacic-typedexamples-audit-map};
\item $M$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only by these displayed rows. It has no coordinate for arbitrary closed-term normalization, confluence, subject reduction, conversion decidability, or a complete algebraic theory of the Church encodings.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC typed-examples gallery NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metacic-typedexamples-gallery-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp$ carrier
$$
G=(B,N,L,S,E,I,A,R,K,H,C,P,M),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier exposure for the finite gallery, representative theorem inventory for the Church Bool, Nat, List, Sum, equality, identity, application, and beta-redex rows, regression exactness for the displayed example endpoints, checker handoff through $K$, non-escape from global metatheorems, and ledger provenance through $P,M$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:metacic-typedexamples-gallery-carrier}. The rows $B,N,L,S,E,I,A,R$ are the complete representative theorem inventory exposed by this gallery packet.

The checker handoff is the row $K$, and every read of $K$ is replayed through the displayed route row $C$. Componentwise transport through $H$ preserves the same example rows and does not add a new theorem coordinate.

Finally read provenance through $P$ and local naming through $M$. Since the carrier has no coordinate for subject reduction, confluence, normalization, or conversion decidability, the gallery obligations are exhausted by the finite example rows and their checker-acceptance routes.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC typed-examples gallery regression surface]
\label{thm:metacic-typedexamples-gallery-regression-surface}
An accepted $\MetaCICTypedExamplesGalleryUp$ packet is a finite regression surface for represented syntax and typing. Consumers may inspect the Church Bool, Nat, List, Sum, equality, identity, application, beta-redex, checker-acceptance, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local $\NameCert$ rows, but they cannot read the packet as a subject-reduction theorem, a confluence theorem, a normalization theorem, or a decidability theorem.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the visible coordinates of $G$. They are example rows and routes for syntax, typing, beta-redex replay, checker acceptance, transport, provenance, and local naming.

Each consumer read factors through the finite route row $C$ and the checker row $K$. These rows can replay the represented gallery examples, but they have no endpoint for a global preservation or conversion principle.

Therefore the accepted packet is exactly a finite regression surface for the displayed examples. The excluded metatheorems remain outside the carrier because no row of $G$ can be projected as such a theorem.
\end{proof}

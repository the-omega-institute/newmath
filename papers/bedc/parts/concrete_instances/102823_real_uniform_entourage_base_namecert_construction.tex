\begin{theorem}[Real uniform entourage-base NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:real-uniform-entourage-base-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\RealUniformEntourageBaseUp$ carrier
$$
E_{\Real}=(R,U,B,F,D,S,Q,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\RealUniformEntourageBaseUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed Real uniform source, induced UniformSpace row, Real-induced EntourageBase row, CauchyFilter handoff, DyadicRatCore radius ledger, StreamName windows, RegSeqRat readback, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming row;
\item Real metric-ball basis containment, requiring every basis entourage in $B$ to be generated from the Real metric-ball route of $R$ and to carry a displayed containment witness into $U$;
\item finite-intersection refinement, requiring every accepted pair of Real-induced basis rows to refine through a displayed row of $B$ before any Cauchy-filter consumer can inspect it;
\item Cauchy-filter handoff, requiring every completion-facing read in $F$ to consume the displayed Real-induced basis row $B$ before reading $S$ or $Q$;
\item classifier transport, requiring $H$ and $C$ to preserve the displayed route $R,U,B,F,D,S,Q$ componentwise under $\hsame$ and replay it under $\Cont$;
\item ledger non-escape, requiring $D$, $P$, and $N$ to export no quotient completion, selected limit, open-cover compactness, countable-choice selector, trichotomy, or ambient Real completeness principle.
\end{enumerate}
No local NameCert obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is the finite projection from \autoref{def:real-uniform-entourage-base-carrier}. The projection exposes exactly the Real uniform source, the induced UniformSpace row, the Real-induced EntourageBase row, the CauchyFilter handoff, the DyadicRatCore ledger, finite windows, readback, and the structural rows $H,C,P,N$.

Real metric-ball basis containment is forced by the source row. A basis request first reads the Real metric-ball route in $R$, then records the induced entourage in $B$, and finally displays its containment witness in $U$.

Finite-intersection refinement is internal to $B$. The accepted pair of Real-induced basis rows is not refined by a completion object; it is refined by a displayed basis row whose radius comparison is recorded in $D$.

The Cauchy-filter handoff is downstream of that basis row. A completion-facing consumer reaches $F$ only after $B$ has been replayed, and the finite observations $S$ and $Q$ are read after the handoff.

Transport and replay do not add additional mathematical primitives. The row $H$ preserves each displayed coordinate componentwise, while $C$ repeats the route $R,U,B,F,D,S,Q$ for every accepted request.

Finally, provenance is exhausted by $D,P,N$. Since quotient completion, selected limits, open-cover compactness, countable choice, trichotomy, and ambient Real completeness are not rows of $E_{\Real}$, the local NameCert surface cannot export them.
\end{proof}

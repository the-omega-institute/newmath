The $\CertificateMachineBranchUp$ packet realizes the branch-separation side of \autoref{subsec:philosophy-certificate-machine-allegory-branch} as finite BEDC certificate data. It sits downstream of the finite certificate machine surface in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-certificate-machine-namecert}, but it records a narrower packet: a kernel spine, an admission gate, a derivation-strength record, a cannot-claim ledger boundary, and explicit branch-separation rows for allegory and biological bridge candidates. The packet is not a plain Turing-machine identity claim, not a biological semantics claim, not a semantic truth oracle, and not an unbounded execution trace.

\begin{definition}[CertificateMachineBranch carrier]\label{def:certificate-machine-branch-carrier}
A $\CertificateMachineBranchUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
B=(K,A,D,L,G,R,S,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $K$ is the finite kernel-spine row, naming the constructors, candidate rows, acceptance stamps, and refusal slots inherited from the BEDC certificate kernel.
\item $A$ is the admission-gate row. It records the finite evidence by which a branch candidate is accepted, refused, or kept outside the public certificate surface.
\item $D$ is the derivation-strength record, naming the declared strength at which the accepted branch may be read.
\item $L$ is the cannot-claim boundary ledger, excluding semantic truth, plain Turing-machine identity, unbounded execution, biological identity, and external proof authority.
\item $G$ is the allegory-branch row, recording candidate symbolic patterns only as pre-admission branch material.
\item $R$ is the biological bridge row, recording DNA-facing or external natural branches only as bridge candidates, not as kernel primitives.
\item $S$ is the branch-separation row saying that allegory, DNA, and certificate-kernel reads remain distinct unless the admission gate names a finite accepted route.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $K,A,D,L,G,R,S,C,P,N$.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from kernel spine through admission gate, derivation-strength record, cannot-claim boundary, and branch-separation rows.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\CertificateMachineBranchUp$, $\CertificateMachineUp$, finite certificate kernels, admission gates, derivation-strength records, cannot-claim ledgers, allegory branches, biological bridge branches, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CertificateMachineBranchUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted branch packets only through $K,A,D,L,G,R,S,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for host machine equality, semantic truth, biological identity, unbounded execution logs, unlicensed allegory admission, or external proof authority.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[CertificateMachineBranch NameCert obligations]\label{thm:certificate-machine-branch-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\CertificateMachineBranchUp$ carrier
$$
B=(K,A,D,L,G,R,S,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\CertificateMachineBranchUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item kernel-spine admission, requiring every branch candidate, constructor, acceptance stamp, and refusal slot to pass through $K$;
\item admission-gate exactness, requiring every accepted branch read to cite the finite row $A$;
\item derivation-strength exactness, requiring the public strength of the branch read to be the displayed row $D$;
\item cannot-claim boundary non-escape, requiring $L$ to exclude semantic truth, plain Turing-machine identity, unbounded execution, biological identity, and external proof authority;
\item allegory and biological branch separation, requiring $G,R,S$ to keep symbolic-pattern and DNA-facing candidates distinct from kernel primitives unless admitted by $A$;
\item transport, continuation, provenance, and local naming stability through $H,C,P,N$.
\end{enumerate}
Lean TasteGate formalization must include explicit $\mathsf{Nontrivial}(\CertificateMachineBranchUp)$ and $\mathsf{FieldFaithful}(\CertificateMachineBranchUp)$ instances before the packet may be consumed as a nondegenerate field-facing branch certificate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:certificate-machine-branch-carrier}. The branch candidate first meets the kernel-spine row $K$ and the admission-gate row $A$, so no public branch read can arise from an unlicensed symbolic or biological source.

The derivation-strength row $D$ and cannot-claim ledger $L$ then determine the public reading strength and the forbidden exits. These rows keep certificate acceptance separate from semantic truth, host machine equality, unbounded execution, and biological identity.

The rows $G,R,S$ provide the branch inventory. They distinguish allegory candidates from DNA-facing bridge candidates and from kernel primitives, and they expose no equality between those sources unless the admission gate records the finite route.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same packet. Since no other coordinate occurs in the carrier, the listed obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CertificateMachineBranch separation non-escape]\label{thm:certificate-machine-branch-separation-nonescape}
Every accepted public read of a $\CertificateMachineBranchUp$ packet factors through
$$
K,A,D,L,G,R,S,H,C,P,N .
$$
The read first traverses the finite kernel spine $K$, the admission gate $A$, the derivation-strength record $D$, and the cannot-claim boundary $L$; only then may it inspect the allegory row $G$, biological bridge row $R$, and branch-separation row $S$. It exports no plain Turing-machine identity, biological semantics, host execution trace, unlicensed branch acceptance, or semantic truth predicate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the accepted carrier $B$. The only kernel-facing row is $K$, and the only acceptance row is $A$, so a public read cannot skip directly to an allegory or DNA-facing branch.

The row $D$ fixes the derivation strength carried by the accepted branch. The row $L$ remains visible during the replay, so the consumer receives the stated boundary rather than a stronger semantic or biological claim.

The rows $G,R,S$ are read after that boundary. They record branch inventory and separation, not an identification of allegory, biology, and kernel primitives.

The remaining rows $H,C,P,N$ preserve the same finite route by transport, continuation replay, provenance, and local naming. Hence every accepted read stays inside the displayed packet.
\end{proof}

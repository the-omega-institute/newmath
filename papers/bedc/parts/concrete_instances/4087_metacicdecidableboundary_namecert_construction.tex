\label{def:metacic-decidable-boundary-carrier}

$$

with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}

\item $S$ is the $\mathsf{checkSameTerm}$ structural-equality row used by the checker;
\item $B$ is the bounded normal-equality row for $\mathsf{normalizeBounded}$ and $\mathsf{decideClosedBetaConv}$;
\item $F$ is the finished-normal-form side-condition row consumed by the bounded comparison soundness theorems;
\item $R$ records the refusal rows for a standalone $\mathsf{ClosedAt}$ decision theorem and for global closed beta-conversion decidability;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport across $T,S,B,F,R,P,N$;

It sits beside the typed endpoint packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typedreductionnormalform-namecert}, the finite Zeckendorf normal-form packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-zeckendorf-normal-namecert}, and the terminal continuation packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-continuationtracenormalform-namecert}. The packet records terminal normality, bounded fuel replay, and non-escape from total host evidence without exporting a divergence oracle, host conversion row, or global normalization procedure.

\falsifiablePrediction{Every accepted ZNormalUp packet exposes a finite terminal-normality row and admits no consumer route to a divergence oracle or host conversion row.}

\independenceWitness{Nearest siblings: typedreductionnormalform, zeckendorf-normal, zcarry, continuationtracenormalform, and normalformconsistencyseal.

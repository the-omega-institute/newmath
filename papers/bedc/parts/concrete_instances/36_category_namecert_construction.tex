\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for \(\CategoryUp\)}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-category-namecert}

The category interface \(\CategoryUp\) is licensed by an object carrier together with, for each ordered pair of objects, a morphism carrier, identity morphisms at each object, a binary composition operation respecting source and target, and the associativity and identity laws expressed as morphism-classifier sameness. The size discipline is BEDC-internal: object and morphism carriers are individually licensed by their own NameCerts, avoiding any global "set of all objects" assumption. It is not a primitive structure; it is licensed only as a multi-sorted carrier with relational composition.

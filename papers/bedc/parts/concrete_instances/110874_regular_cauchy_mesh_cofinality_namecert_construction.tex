\begin{theorem}[Regular Cauchy mesh cofinality NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:regular-cauchy-mesh-cofinality-namecert-obligations}

$$

The shared row $B$ aligns these sources. It is the only place where the precision request, cofinal selector depth, and diagonal inspected indices meet, so the window row $S$ and regular readback row $A$ inherit one finite route.

The seal and structural rows $E,H,C,P,N$ preserve that route. Since the carrier has no coordinate for the excluded completion objects, the listed rows exhaust the local certificate surface.

tail-envelope certificates for displayed real-name routes before Real and
Completion consumers can read them. This interface consumes the DyadicRatCore
tolerance ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert},
the RegSeqRat regular-sequence handoff of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, and the RealUp terminal
seal of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}.

\begin{definition}[UniformCompletionFunctor carrier]
\label{def:uniform-completion-functor-carrier}

$$

\item $U$ is the $\UniformCompletionUp$ source row, supplying the displayed Cauchy-filter, Cauchy-net, universal-extension, classifier, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming data read by the functor.
\item $F$ is the $\CauchyCompletionFunctorUp$ handoff row, recording the source-to-completion unit route, completion-facing bind route, and functorial classifier surface accepted for the same source.
\item $E$ is the $\CompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ universal-extension row, naming the finite extension and uniqueness ledger consumed after $U$ and $F$ have been projected.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regular rational readback row used when the completion consumer asks for a regular source name or a regular output name.

\item $S$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal row reached only after the uniform-completion source, functorial handoff, universal-extension ledger, regular readback, stream windows, and dyadic tolerance rows have been replayed.

The classifier compares accepted functor packets only through the displayed rows $U,F,E,R,W,D,S,H,C,P,N$. There is no coordinate for quotient completion equality, selected limits, host complete uniform spaces, open-cover compactness, or choice.

\begin{theorem}[UniformCompletionFunctor NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:uniform-completion-functor-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\UniformCompletionFunctorUp$ carrier
$$
\mathcal{F}=(U,F,E,R,W,D,S,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\UniformCompletionFunctorUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed uniform-completion source, CauchyCompletionFunctor handoff, universal-extension row, regular readback row, stream-window row, dyadic tolerance row, Real seal row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming;
\item source compatibility, requiring every completion-facing read to project the $\UniformCompletionUp$ source row $U$ before any functorial handoff is available;
\item handoff exactness, requiring $F$ to expose the unit, bind, and classifier rows consumed by completion functor readers over the same source row;
\item universal-extension ordering, requiring $E$ to be read after $U$ and $F$ and before any terminal $\RealUp$ seal is exposed;
\item readback and tolerance exactness, requiring $R,W,D$ to carry all regular rational, finite-window, and dyadic precision requests used by the handoff;
\item ledger non-escape, requiring $H,C,P,N$ to export no quotient completion, selected limit, host complete uniform space, open-cover compactness proof, or choice principle.
\end{enumerate}
No local obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:uniform-completion-functor-carrier}. The accepted packet displays exactly the source, handoff, extension, readback, window, tolerance, seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

Source compatibility and handoff exactness are read from $U$ and $F$. A completion consumer first reads the $\UniformCompletionUp$ source row and then the $\CauchyCompletionFunctorUp$ handoff row, so the packet has no separate entry point for a quotient completion or selected limit.

Universal-extension ordering is read from $E$, while readback and tolerance exactness are read from $R,W,D$. The seal row $S$ is downstream of those rows, and $H,C,P,N$ only transport, replay, source, and name the same displayed route. Since the excluded principles have no carrier coordinate, no replay edge, and no provenance row, the local NameCert surface is exactly the listed obligation surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[UniformCompletionFunctor universal handoff]
\label{thm:uniform-completion-functor-universal-handoff}

$$

$$
Thus the consumer receives the uniform-completion source row, then the CauchyCompletionFunctor handoff, then the universal-extension ledger, and only then the regular rational, StreamName, DyadicRatCore, Real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with an accepted carrier $\mathcal{F}=(U,F,E,R,W,D,S,H,C,P,N)$. The only uniform-completion source coordinate is $U$, so every completion-facing request begins by projecting the displayed $\UniformCompletionUp$ data.

The functorial handoff is then read through $F$. Its unit, bind, and classifier rows are attached to the same source row, and the universal-extension row $E$ records the extension and uniqueness ledger available to the consumer after that handoff.

The readback rows are downstream of the extension ledger. Regular rational readback uses $R$, finite observation uses $W$, dyadic precision uses $D$, and the terminal real-name seal is exposed only through $S$ after those requests have been replayed.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name the same finite route. The carrier definition contains no coordinate for quotient completion equality, selected limits, host complete uniform spaces, open-cover compactness, or choice, so no completion-facing consumer can read such data from the handoff.
\end{proof}

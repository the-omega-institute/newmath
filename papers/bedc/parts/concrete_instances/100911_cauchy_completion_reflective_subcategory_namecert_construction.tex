$$

\item $Q$ is the counit and idempotence row, recording return along already completed reads and reuse of the same completed-object surface after a second completion pass.

The carrier exposes only the finite reflective-subcategory boundary. It contains no quotient completion, no selected limit, no host equality for completed objects, no countable-choice selector, no ambient completeness theorem, and no full completeness theorem for $\RealUp$.

\item reflective inclusion exactness, requiring every completed-object read to pass through $J$ after the completed-object surface $M$ has been displayed;

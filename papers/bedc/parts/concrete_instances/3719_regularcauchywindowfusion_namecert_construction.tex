\begin{definition}[Regular Cauchy window fusion carrier]
\label{def:regular-cauchy-window-fusion-carrier}
A $\RegularCauchyWindowFusionUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
F=(R,W,S,D,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $R$ is the RatUp seed classifier row for the stationary rational source;
\item $W$ is the finite $\StreamNameUp$ constant-window classifier read over the displayed observation window;
\item $S$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ seal-consumption row obtained from the same finite window;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation ledger carried by that window;
\item $E$ is the $\RealUp$ finite-window classifier and seal row reached from $W,S,D$;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $R,W,S,D,E$ and for the package and local naming rows;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes by which consumers first read the seed row, then the shared window, then the dyadic and regular-sequence rows, and finally the real seal row;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over RatUp, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RegularCauchyWindowFusionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for a selected limit, quotient stream equality, real trichotomy, host completeness, or an unrecorded RealUp comparison row.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Regular Cauchy window fusion NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:regular-cauchy-window-fusion-namecert-obligations}
The local $\NameCert_{\RegularCauchyWindowFusionUp}$ surface for the carrier of \autoref{def:regular-cauchy-window-fusion-carrier} consists of carrier admission for
$$
F=(R,W,S,D,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
seed-to-window exposure, $\RegSeqRatUp$ seal readback, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation-ledger exposure, $\RealUp$ seal handoff, componentwise $\hsame$ stability, displayed $\Cont$ locality, $\Pkg$ provenance, and non-escape from selected limits, quotient streams, trichotomy, ambient completeness, and hidden RealUp comparison data.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:regular-cauchy-window-fusion-carrier}. The rows $R,W,S,D,E,H,C,P,N$ are the only rows admitted to the packet.

The observation obligations are read in order. The RatUp seed row $R$ feeds the finite $\StreamNameUp$ window $W$; the same window supplies the $\RegSeqRatUp$ seal row $S$ and the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ ledger $D$.

The Real-facing obligation is the row $E$. It records the finite-window $\RealUp$ seal reached from $W,S,D$, while $H$ and $C$ preserve and route that same displayed row without adding an analytic carrier.

Finally $P$ and $N$ bind the packet to its package provenance and local naming row. Since no carrier coordinate names selected limits, quotient streams, trichotomy, ambient completeness, or a hidden RealUp comparison, none can be exported by the certificate.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:cauchy-equivalence-setoid-quotient-boundary-completion-route}

The structural rows then transport, replay, source, and name the same route. Thus the terminal Real readback is downstream of quotient-boundary refusal and completion readiness, not a replacement for them.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Context-free pumping lemma carrier]
\label{def:context-free-pumping-lemma-namecert-carrier}
A $\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
P=(G,W,D,A,E,I,Y,H,C,R,Q,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ is the finite $\ContextFreeGrammarUp$ grammar packet, including terminal alphabet, nonterminal carrier, start row, and production ledger.
\item $W$ is the terminal $\ListUp$ word row whose length comparison is read through $\NatUp$.
\item $D$ is the finite parse or derivation ledger yielding $W$ from $G$.
\item $A$ is the repeated-nonterminal row, recording the two derivation-prefix positions and the common nonterminal exposed inside the finite window.
\item $E$ is the decomposition row, displaying the word factorization $W=u v x y z$ with the repeated-nonterminal window attached to $v,x,y$.
\item $I$ is the pump-index row, read as a $\NatUp$ input for the requested finite replay.
\item $Y$ is the yielded-word row, displaying the pumped terminal word $u v^I x y^I z$ as a $\ListUp$ row.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for grammar rows, derivation prefixes, repeated-nonterminal evidence, decomposition rows, pump indices, and yielded words.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a derivation prefix and pump index through the finite pump window.
\item $R$ is the regular-language or finite-prefix consumer boundary, used only to compare the yielded word with sibling language-recognition packets.
\item $Q$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp$, $\ContextFreeGrammarUp$, $\RegularLanguageUp$, $\FinitePrefixAutomatonUp$, $\ListUp$, $\NatUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted carriers only through $G,W,D,A,E,I,Y,H,C,R,Q,N$. It contains no coordinate for host language equality, infinite derivation trees, grammar quotient semantics, countable choice over derivations, or semantic membership in an ambient language set.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Context-free pumping lemma NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:context-free-pumping-lemma-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp$ carrier $P=(G,W,D,A,E,I,Y,H,C,R,Q,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp}$ surface consists exactly of finite grammar admission through $G$; terminal-word and length admission through $W$; derivation-ledger admission through $D$; repeated-nonterminal exposure through $A$; decomposition through $E$; pump-index reading through $I$; yielded-word display through $Y$; componentwise transport through $H$; replay through $C$; sibling language-recognition boundary through $R$; provenance through $Q$; and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:context-free-pumping-lemma-namecert-carrier}. The finite packet displays the grammar, word, derivation ledger, repeated nonterminal, decomposition, pump index, yielded word, transport, replay, consumer boundary, provenance, and local name.

The positive route begins at the finite derivation ledger $D$. The repeated nonterminal row $A$ is read inside that ledger before the decomposition row $E$ may expose the factors $u,v,x,y,z$.

The pump index $I$ is then replayed through $C$ to produce the yielded word row $Y$. The structural rows $H,Q,N$ preserve and name that same finite route, while $R$ only offers a sibling boundary for later regular-language or finite-prefix consumers.

Since the classifier has no coordinate for host language equality, infinite derivation trees, grammar quotient semantics, or semantic language membership, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Context-free pumping window boundary]
\label{thm:contextfreegrammar-pumping-window-boundary}
Let $P=(G,W,D,A,E,I,Y,H,C,R,Q,N)$ be an accepted $\ContextFreePumpingLemmaUp$ carrier. Every pumpable-yield read of $P$ factors through the finite derivation-prefix rows $D,A,E,I,Y$ before reaching transport, replay, provenance, local naming, or a sibling language-recognition boundary. No accepted read factors through host language equality, infinite derivation trees, grammar quotient semantics, or semantic membership in an ambient language set.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:context-free-pumping-lemma-namecert-carrier}. The only rows capable of producing a pumped yield are the finite derivation ledger $D$, the repeated-nonterminal row $A$, the decomposition row $E$, the pump-index row $I$, and the yielded-word row $Y$.

Transport and replay preserve this displayed window. The $\hsame$ row $H$ changes no grammar or word coordinate, and the $\Cont$ row $C$ replays the same derivation prefix at the requested index.

The provenance and local naming rows close the packet, while $R$ only places the yielded word at a regular-language or finite-prefix boundary. Because the absent host and quotient semantics are not carrier coordinates, they cannot supply another pumpable-yield route.
\end{proof}

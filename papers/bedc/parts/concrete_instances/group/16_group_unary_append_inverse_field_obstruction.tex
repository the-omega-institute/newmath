\begin{theorem}[Unary append inverse field collapses to singleton]
\label{thm:unary-append-inverse-field-collapses-to-singleton}
For every operation $i:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{BHist}$, the right-inverse
field on the full unary-history append carrier,
$$
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(h,i(h)),\emp),
$$
implies the singleton-unit collapse
$$
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp).
$$
The analogous left-inverse field,
$$
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(i(h),h),\emp),
$$
implies the same singleton-unit collapse.

The ten-field fit is: traditional target obstruction to making the full unary
append monoid into a group; BEDC source $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$,
$\mathsf{append}$, and
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}$; carrier $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$;
classifier $\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})$;
stability from unit-product factor exactness; ledger the proposed inverse
field; core theorem this block; exactness inverse-law collapse to the
singleton unit class; downstream consumer the concrete $\GroupUp$ carrier
choice; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume the right-inverse field and fix a unary history $h$. Specializing the
field at $h$ gives the classifier statement that
$\mathsf{append}(h,i(h))$ is classified as $\emp$.

Apply \autoref{thm:unary-append-unit-product-factor-exactness} to this append
product. The forward direction decomposes the unit-product classifier into the
two factor classifiers, and the left factor is exactly
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp)$.

For the left-inverse field, fix the same unary history $h$ and specialize the
field at $h$. Apply
\autoref{thm:unary-append-unit-product-factor-exactness} to
$\mathsf{append}(i(h),h)$. The forward direction now gives the two factor
classifiers with $h$ as the right factor, so the right factor is the displayed
singleton-unit conclusion.
\end{proof}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.GroupUp.MonoidHistoryClassifier\_unary\_append\_inverse\_field\_collapse}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.GroupUp.unary\_append\_inverse\_field\_collapses\_to\_singleton}

\begin{theorem}[Unary append inverse field iff singleton collapse]
\label{thm:unary-append-inverse-field-iff-singleton-collapse}
For every operation $i:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{BHist}$ satisfying unary
preservation
$$
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(i(h)),
$$
the two-sided inverse-field laws under
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})$ are equivalent to
singleton-unit collapse:
$$
\begin{array}{c}
\left(
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(h,i(h)),\emp)
\right)\\
\land\\
\left(
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(i(h),h),\emp)
\right)
\end{array}
\Longleftrightarrow
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp).
$$
The ten-field fit is: traditional target exact obstruction to a full unary
append group; BEDC source $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$, $\mathsf{append}$, and
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}$; carrier $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$;
classifier $\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})$;
stability from unit-product factor exactness and unary preservation; ledger
the inverse candidate; core theorem this block; exactness the displayed iff;
downstream consumer $\GroupUp$ and $\QuotientGroupUp$; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the forward direction, assume the displayed conjunction of inverse-field
laws and fix a unary history $h$. The right-inverse conjunct gives
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(h,i(h)),\emp)$.

Apply \autoref{thm:unary-append-unit-product-factor-exactness} to this
classifier. The forward direction decomposes it into the two factor
classifiers, and the left factor is exactly the displayed singleton-unit
conclusion for $h$.

For the reverse direction, assume singleton-unit collapse and fix a unary
history $h$. The collapse hypothesis gives
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp)$. Unary
preservation gives $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(i(h))$, and applying the same
collapse hypothesis to $i(h)$ gives
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(i(h),\emp)$.

Apply the reverse direction of
\autoref{thm:unary-append-unit-product-factor-exactness} to these two factor
classifiers. This produces the right inverse-field classifier for
$\mathsf{append}(h,i(h))$.

The left inverse-field classifier is obtained from the same two factor
classifiers in the opposite order. Applying the reverse direction of
\autoref{thm:unary-append-unit-product-factor-exactness} to the pair
$i(h)$, $h$ classifies $\mathsf{append}(i(h),h)$ as $\emp$, completing the
two-sided inverse-field laws.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Unary append singleton collapse obstruction]
\label{thm:unary-append-singleton-collapse-obstruction}
The full unary-history append classifier cannot classify every unary history
as the singleton unit:
$$
\left(
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp)
\right)
\Rightarrow \mathsf{False}.
$$
The ten-field fit is: traditional target obstruction to collapsing the full
unary append carrier to the unit; BEDC source $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$; carrier
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$; classifier
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})$; stability from the
concrete classifier fields; ledger the visible unary endpoint $\Eone(\emp)$;
exactness the contradiction; downstream consumer the inverse-field obstruction;
status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume the displayed singleton-unit collapse. The concrete history
$\Eone(\emp)$ is unary, so the collapse hypothesis classifies
$$
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(\Eone(\emp),\emp).
$$
Unfolding the classifier exposes the internal witness
$\Eone(\emp)\hsame\emp$, contradicting the BEDC history no-confusion theorem.
\end{proof}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.MonoidUp.MonoidHistoryClassifier\_unary\_append\_singleton\_collapse\_obstruction}

\begin{theorem}[Full unary append left-inverse obstruction]
\label{thm:full-unary-append-left-inverse-obstruction}
For every operation $\mathsf{inv}:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{BHist}$, if the
full unary-history append carrier carries the left-inverse field
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow{}\\
\qquad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\mathsf{inv}(h))\land{}\\
\qquad
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\mathsf{append}(\mathsf{inv}(h),h),\emp),
\end{array}
$$
then $\mathsf{False}$.

The ten-field fit is: traditional target obstruction to the full unary append
group; BEDC source $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$, $\mathsf{append}$, and
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}$; carrier full unary histories; classifier
$\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})$; stability from
unit-product factor exactness and singleton collapse; ledger the proposed left
inverse operation; core theorem this block; exactness impossibility of the
left-inverse field; downstream consumer the concrete $\GroupUp$ carrier choice
and $\QuotientGroupUp$; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume such an operation $\mathsf{inv}$ and its displayed left-inverse field.
The left-inverse half of
\autoref{thm:unary-append-inverse-field-iff-singleton-collapse} applies to the
classifier component of that field and gives singleton-unit collapse:
$$
\forall h:\mathsf{BHist},\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(h)\Rightarrow
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})(h,\emp).
$$

Instantiate the collapse at $h=\Eone(\emp)$, using that $\Eone(\emp)$ is a
concrete unary history. This gives
$$
\mathsf{MonoidHistoryClassifier}(\mathsf{UnaryHistory})
(\Eone(\emp),\emp).
$$

The extra $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\mathsf{inv}(h))$ component supplied by the
displayed field is retained by the ledger but is not needed for the
contradiction.

Unfold the classifier from \autoref{def:concrete-history-monoid-instance}. The
displayed classifier contains the internal history-sameness witness
$\Eone(\emp)\hsame\emp$. This contradicts
\autoref{thm:history-no-confusion}, because an $\Eone$-history and $\emp$ have
different outer constructors. Hence the left-inverse field yields
$\mathsf{False}$.
\end{proof}

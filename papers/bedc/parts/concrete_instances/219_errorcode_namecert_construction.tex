\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\ErrorCodeUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-errorcode-namecert}

The error-correcting-code interface $\ErrorCodeUp$ packages a subset of $\mathbb{F}_q^n$ at minimum Hamming distance $d$ together with encoder, syndrome, and decoder data correcting up to $\lfloor (d-1)/2 \rfloor$ errors. It depends on $\NameCert_{\FieldUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-field-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\VecSpaceUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-vecspace-namecert}).

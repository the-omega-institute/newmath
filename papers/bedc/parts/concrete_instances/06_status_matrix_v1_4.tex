\begin{remark}[Meaning of statuses]
\(
\textsf{PaperProof}
\) means the present volume gives a proof at the theory-specification level. \(
\textsf{PolicyConditional}
\) means the theorem is conditional on a named policy. \(
\textsf{ConcretePaperProof}
\) means a concrete instance is constructed in the paper theory. \(
\textsf{CertificateConstructed}
\) means a naming certificate object is supplied in paper form. None of these statuses implies machine verification.
\end{remark}

\section{Carrier and classifier}

\begin{definition}[MetaCIC theory-identity carrier]
\label{def:metacic-theory-identity-carrier}
A $\MetaCICTheoryIdentityUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
I=(G,E,R,P,A,H,C,Q,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ is the closed-generator row, naming the inductive constructors, recursor rules, substitution rows, beta-step rows, and finite beta-chain rows admitted by the BEDC.MetaCIC substrate;
\item $E$ is the literal AST equality row, recording that object comparison is syntactic equality of displayed MetaCIC abstract-syntax rows and not semantic equality inherited from another calculus;
\item $R$ is the structural-recursion row, recording that admissible computation is generated by the displayed recursors on the closed syntax and history carriers;
\item $P$ is the strict-purity row, recording the absence of additional axioms, classical choice, quotient soundness, proposition extensionality, and host semantic conversion in the identity packet;
\item $A$ is the apophatic-boundary row, recording the finite list of absent exports: closed subject reduction, unrestricted conversion decidability, global normalization, confluence outside the named fragments, and kernel soundness;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for the generator, equality, recursor, purity, apophatic boundary, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes by which audit chapters read the five commitment rows without adding a semantic oracle;
\item $Q$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over \autoref{ch:bedc-is-its-own-theory}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacicclosuretrace-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-kernelacceptancetrace-namecert}, BEDC.MetaCIC generator rows, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICTheoryIdentityUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no comparison embedding into classical CIC, no quotient of syntax by a host semantics, no erased proof-identity principle, and no imported kernel-correctness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC theory-identity NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metacic-theory-identity-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MetaCICTheoryIdentityUp$ carrier
$$
I=(G,E,R,P,A,H,C,Q,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICTheoryIdentityUp}$ surface consists of five finite positive commitments:
\begin{enumerate}
\item closed-generator exposure through $G$;
\item literal AST equality exposure through $E$;
\item structural-recursion exposure through $R$;
\item strict-purity exposure through $P$;
\item apophatic-boundary exposure through $A$.
\end{enumerate}
The rows $H,C,Q,N$ transport, route, package, and name only those five commitments, so the packet records BEDC.MetaCIC identity as finite BEDC data rather than as a comparison claim about another calculus.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the displayed packet $I$. The five positive coordinates $G,E,R,P,A$ are the only theory-identity coordinates before transport, route replay, provenance, or local naming is considered.

Read $H$ and $C$ componentwise. They preserve the five displayed coordinates and provide only the consumer routes named by the carrier, so no route can add an external semantic equality, quotient, or host conversion rule.

Finally inspect $Q$ and $N$. Provenance ties the packet to the source identity principle and to the sibling closure and kernel traces, while local naming packages the same finite surface. Since no further coordinate occurs in $I$, the listed rows exhaust the NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC theory-identity closed-generator scope]
\label{thm:metacic-theory-identity-closed-generator-scope}
Every generator read from an accepted $\MetaCICTheoryIdentityUp$ carrier factors through the closed-generator row $G$ and the structural-recursion row $R$. The read may cite the closure-trace and kernel-acceptance siblings through $Q$, but it cannot introduce an unlisted constructor, host reduction rule, or semantic generator outside the displayed BEDC.MetaCIC substrate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the carrier projection. The generator coordinate is $G$, and the computation coordinate that may replay it is $R$.

The provenance row $Q$ can point to accepted closure and kernel traces, but those traces are read as finite BEDC rows. They do not add a second generator surface to $I$.

Thus every generator read remains inside $G,R,Q,N$, and any unlisted constructor or semantic rule would require a coordinate absent from the carrier.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:metacic-theory-identity-literal-equality-boundary}

It exports syntactic comparison of displayed MetaCIC rows and no quotient equality, proof irrelevance, host semantic equivalence, or classical CIC identity transport.

Therefore the equality boundary is literal and local: the packet exposes AST equality and excludes the stronger equality principles listed in the statement.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC theory-identity purity boundary]
\label{thm:metacic-theory-identity-purity-boundary}

The packet records a first-principles BEDC.MetaCIC surface with no additional axiom coordinate, no classical-choice coordinate, no quotient-soundness coordinate, no proposition-extensionality coordinate, and no host semantic conversion coordinate.

\end{proof}

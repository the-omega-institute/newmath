\begin{definition}[Explanation-complexity information carrier]
\label{def:explanation-complexity-information-carrier}
An $\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
E=(S,B,D,K,V,Q,G,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the explanation score row, recording the public weight profile and the finite source, feature, circuit, classifier, stability, intervention, ledger, seal, semantic, and verification cost coordinates.
\item $B$ is the benefit row, recording coverage gain, prediction gain, transfer gain, intervention gain, verification gain, and unification gain inside the declared explanation scope.
\item $D$ is the explanation-debt row, separating NDNA debt, ledger debt, semantic debt, black-box debt, shortcut debt, safety debt, generalization debt, and verification debt.
\item $K$ is the bounded description-cost row, read through the displayed program and code discipline of $\KolmogorovComplexityUp$ without importing an uncomputable minimum selector.
\item $V$ is the classifier-novelty row, read through $\ClassifierNoveltyWitnessUp$ only when two explanation states display a common source and a changed classifier judgment.
\item $Q$ is the certified-quality row, read through $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$ when the score, debt, audit, and quality-gate rows jointly certify a model-quality comparison.
\item $G$ is the net information row, displaying the accepted arithmetic comparison between benefit and the score-plus-debt surface over the same scope.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for score, benefit, debt, description-cost, classifier-novelty, certified-quality, net-information, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from scope inspection through score, benefit, debt, description-cost, classifier novelty, certified quality, net information, provenance, and local naming.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp$, $\KolmogorovComplexityUp$, $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$, $\ClassifierNoveltyWitnessUp$, $\EffectiveReplacementLedgerUp$, $\AuditSystemUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,B,D,K,V,Q,G,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for explanation brevity alone, private evaluator preference, hidden semantic truth, unbounded Kolmogorov minimization, raw model score, or classifier novelty without the displayed changed-judgment row.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Explanation-complexity information NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:explanation-complexity-information-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp$ carrier $E=(S,B,D,K,V,Q,G,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp}$ surface consists exactly of scope-coherent score exposure, benefit exposure, explanation-debt exposure, bounded description-cost handoff through $\KolmogorovComplexityUp$, classifier-novelty handoff through $\ClassifierNoveltyWitnessUp$, certified-quality handoff through $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$, net-information comparison, componentwise transport, displayed replay, provenance, and local naming.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:explanation-complexity-information-carrier}. The analytic rows are $S,B,D,K,V,Q,G$, and the structural rows are $H,C,P,N$.

The score, benefit, and debt rows share one explanation scope. Thus the net row $G$ may compare benefit against score and debt only after $S,B,D$ have been displayed for that same scope.

Read the sibling rows next. The description-cost row $K$ uses \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-kolmogorov-complexity-namecert} only as a bounded displayed-program cost surface; the novelty row $V$ uses \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-classifier-novelty-witness-namecert} only when a changed classifier judgment is visible; and the quality row $Q$ uses \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-certified-model-quality-namecert} only after its debt and audit gates are exposed.

Finally, $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same rows. Since none of the excluded objects appears in the carrier, the listed obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Explanation-complexity information net-score route]
\label{thm:explanation-complexity-information-net-score-route}
An accepted $\ExplanationComplexityInformationUp$ packet may export positive net explanation information only after the score row $S$, benefit row $B$, debt row $D$, classifier-novelty row $V$, and certified-quality row $Q$ have all been read through the same scope. A lower score, a shorter description, a quality-gate pass, or a classifier-novelty witness alone is therefore insufficient for the exported net-information row $G$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the net-information row $G$. By carrier projection, $G$ is downstream of the public score row $S$, benefit row $B$, debt row $D$, classifier-novelty row $V$, and certified-quality row $Q$.

The score and description rows do not determine the export alone. The row $S$ records weighted cost, while $K$ records bounded description-cost evidence through $\KolmogorovComplexityUp$; neither row contains the benefit and debt comparison required by $G$.

The novelty and quality rows are also insufficient in isolation. The row $V$ certifies a changed classifier judgment, and $Q$ certifies debt-aware model quality, but $G$ must still read the shared score, benefit, and debt surface before the packet exports positive net information.
\end{proof}

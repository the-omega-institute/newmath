\begin{definition}[Observer licensed comparison carrier]
\label{def:observer-licensed-comparison-carrier}
An $\mathsf{ObserverLicensedComparison}^{\uparrow}$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
L=(H_0,H_1,A,R,D,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $H_0$ and $H_1$ are the observer-history rows being compared;
\item $A$ is the licensed observation ledger, listing exactly the displayed mark, $\Cont$, $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$, $\mathsf{SigRel}$, and comparison reads allowed to affect the public result;
\item $R$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the histories, licensed observations, classifier-output row, route row, package row, and local naming row;
\item $D$ is the finite classifier-output row, read only after the licensed ledger and transport rows are displayed;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes by which a consumer reads $H_0,H_1,A,R,D,P,N$;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$, $\mathsf{SigRel}$, $\ObserverHistIdentityUp$, $\ObserverHistoryIdentityUp$, $\ObserverHsameClassifierUp$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\mathsf{ObserverLicensedComparison}^{\uparrow}}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier accepts only comparisons whose public result factors through $A,R,D,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for unlicensed observation, observer-substance, host equality, quotient history identity, or hidden classifier state.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Observer licensed comparison NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:observer-licensed-comparison-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:observer-licensed-comparison-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\mathsf{ObserverLicensedComparison}^{\uparrow}}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for $L=(H_0,H_1,A,R,D,C,P,N)$, licensed-observation exactness for $A$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $R$, classifier-output confinement through $D$, displayed consumer routes through $C$, package provenance through $P$, local naming through $N$, and non-escape from unlicensed observation, observer-substance, host equality, quotient identity, and hidden classifier state.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier. The displayed rows are exactly $H_0,H_1,A,R,D,C,P,N$, so carrier admission and local naming are finite $\BHist$ reads rather than host-side equality claims.

Licensed-observation exactness is the row $A$. A consumer may use a mark, continuation, probe bundle, signature relation, or prior comparison read only when that datum appears in the displayed licensed ledger and is then transported through $R$.

Classifier-output confinement is the row $D$ read through $C,P,N$. The same route also records provenance through $\ObserverHistIdentityUp$, $\ObserverHistoryIdentityUp$, and the sibling classifier surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-observerhsameclassifier-namecert}.

The excluded objects have no carrier coordinate. Thus no accepted comparison can expose unlicensed observation, observer-substance, host equality, quotient identity, or hidden classifier state.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Observer licensed comparison non-escape]
\label{thm:observer-licensed-comparison-nonescape}
Every public consumer of an accepted $\mathsf{ObserverLicensedComparison}^{\uparrow}$ packet reads only the licensed observation ledger $A$, the componentwise $\hsame$ transports $R$, the classifier-output row $D$, the displayed $\Cont$ routes $C$, package provenance $P$, and local name row $N$. Changing any unlicensed observer-substance datum leaves the public comparison read unchanged.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the accepted packet $L=(H_0,H_1,A,R,D,C,P,N)$. The consumer-facing comparison rows are $A,R,D,C,P,N$; the two history rows are sources for the licensed ledger, not observer-substance fields.

Apply the NameCert obligations of \autoref{thm:observer-licensed-comparison-namecert-obligations}. They force every public comparison read through the displayed licensed observations, $\hsame$ transports, classifier output, route, provenance, and local name.

No unlicensed observer-substance row is present in the carrier. A change confined to such a row changes none of $A,R,D,C,P,N$, so every public comparison read remains unchanged.
\end{proof}

\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\MirrorSymmetryUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-mirrorsymmetry-namecert}

The mirror-symmetry interface $\MirrorSymmetryUp$ packages, for a Calabi-Yau pair $(X, X^\vee)$, the conjectural equivalence of the A-model on one side with the B-model on the other (Hodge numbers, Fukaya category vs derived category). It depends on $\NameCert_{\SymplecticUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-symplectic-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\DerivedCatUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-derivedcat-namecert}), and bridges symplectic and complex-algebraic certificates through a common categorical classifier.

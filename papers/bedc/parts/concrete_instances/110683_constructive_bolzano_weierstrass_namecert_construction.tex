\item $B$ is the $\BolzanoWeierstrassUp$ carrier row, exposing the interval-net and regular-subsequence route without adding a separate host compactness object.

The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,B,I,T,R,Q,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for open-cover compactness, quotient real equality, a selected least cluster point, countable choice, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

The $\CookFrontierWitnessUp$ packet realizes the compile-frontier engineering path of \autoref{ch:visions-cook-compile-frontier-engineering-path} as a finite NameCert witness surface. It sits beside the substrate-facing cellular packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-cellularautomaton-namecert} and the halting-facing diagonal packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-haltingdiagonal-namecert}. Its role is not to prove Cook universality again, decide a halting problem, or identify compiler stages by host equality; it records the finite stage, target, replay, obstruction, transport, continuation, package, and naming rows a consumer may inspect.

\begin{definition}[Cook frontier witness carrier]
\label{def:cook-frontier-witness-carrier}
A $\CookFrontierWitnessUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
W=(F,M,Y,R,T,A,O,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $F$ is the frontier-coordinate row, recording the accepted production count, tape-symbol count, Rule~110 step budget, and the reported coordinate relative to the halted-TM target of \autoref{def:cook-frontier-engineering-coordinate};
\item $M$ is the TM-to-tag compile-stage row, naming the finite Turing-machine input, tag-system output, and stage-local audit route;
\item $Y$ is the tag-to-cyclic compile-stage row, naming the finite tag-system input, cyclic-tag output, and stage-local audit route;
\item $R$ is the cyclic-to-Rule-110 compile-stage row, naming the finite cyclic-tag input, Rule~110 initial row, evaluator budget, and decode route;
\item $T$ is the halted-target row, recording the halted-machine target coordinate and the expected halt-state readback;
\item $A$ is the replay-audit row, replaying the round trip from the Rule~110 trace back through $R$, $Y$, and $M$ to the target read in $T$;
\item $O$ is the obstruction-ledger row, recording memory scaling, encoding constants, verification cost, and any displayed failure boundary for the accepted coordinate;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $F,M,Y,R,T,A,O,P,N$;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes for each compile stage, the replay audit, the obstruction read, and the local naming row;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\CookFrontierWitnessUp$, \autoref{ch:visions-cook-compile-frontier-engineering-path}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-cellularautomaton-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-haltingdiagonal-namecert}, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CookFrontierWitnessUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for a total halting decision, a semantic oracle, a host equality between compiler stages, an unbounded substrate trace, or an unstaged Cook universality proof.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Cook frontier witness obligation surface]
\label{thm:cook-frontier-witness-obligation-surface}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:cook-frontier-witness-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\CookFrontierWitnessUp}$ surface consists exactly of these finite rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item frontier-coordinate exposure through $F$;
\item three compile-stage exposures through the TM-to-tag row $M$, the tag-to-cyclic row $Y$, and the cyclic-to-Rule-110 row $R$;
\item halted-target exposure through $T$;
\item replay-audit exposure through $A$, requiring the finite route back through $R,Y,M$;
\item obstruction-ledger exposure through $O$;
\item componentwise transport through $H$;
\item continuation replay through $C$;
\item package provenance through $P$;
\item local naming through $N$.
\end{enumerate}
Thus an accepted $\CookFrontierWitnessUp$ consumer may report a Cook frontier coordinate only through $F,M,Y,R,T,A,O,H,C,P,N$, and not through a bare substrate run, host compiler equality, selected halting conclusion, unstaged replay, or ambient universality theorem.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier of \autoref{def:cook-frontier-witness-carrier}. The displayed packet contains exactly the frontier row, three compile-stage rows, halted-target row, replay-audit row, obstruction ledger, and structural rows $H,C,P,N$.

The compile route is staged. The row $M$ supplies the TM-to-tag read, $Y$ supplies the tag-to-cyclic read, and $R$ supplies the cyclic-to-Rule-110 read. The replay audit $A$ can therefore be checked only by following the finite route back through $R,Y,M$ to the halted target $T$.

The obstruction row $O$ records where the accepted coordinate stops and why a larger coordinate is not claimed by this packet. The transport, continuation, provenance, and naming rows then bind those finite reads into one NameCert surface.

Since no coordinate remains for a total halting decision, host compiler equality, semantic oracle, unbounded trace, or unstaged universality theorem, the listed rows exhaust the public obligation surface.
\end{proof}

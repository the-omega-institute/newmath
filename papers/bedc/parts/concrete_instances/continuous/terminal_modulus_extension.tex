\begin{theorem}[Continuous function carrier terminal modulus extension]
\label{thm:continuous-function-carrier-terminal-modulus-extension}
For histories
$source,map,target,modulus,cert,extra,modulus',cert'$, a continuous-function
carrier remains a continuous-function carrier after the terminal certificate
is extended by one more modulus witness and the modulus ledger is composed:
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}(source,map,target,modulus,cert)
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(cert,extra,cert')
\Longrightarrow
\ContR(modulus,extra,modulus')
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}(source,map,target,modulus',cert').
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unpack the given carrier. This exposes the graph ledger
$\ContR(source,map,target)$ and the terminal modulus-certificate ledger
$\ContR(target,modulus,cert)$, together with the unary carrier data for the
displayed histories.

Unpack the additional modulus witness
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(cert,extra,cert')$. It supplies
$\ContR(cert,extra,cert')$, so the two ledgers
$\ContR(target,modulus,cert)$ and $\ContR(cert,extra,cert')$ assemble the chain
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusChain}(target,modulus,extra,cert')$ with middle
history $cert$.

Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:continuous-function-carrier-modulus-chain-replacement}
to the original carrier, this chain, and the displayed composition ledger
$\ContR(modulus,extra,modulus')$. The replacement theorem repacks the preserved
graph data with the composite terminal modulus certificate, giving the displayed
carrier conclusion.

The ten-field certificate fit is the following. The traditional target is
terminal modulus extension for continuous maps. The BEDC source is
$\mathsf{BHist}$ with $\ContR$. The carrier is
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}$ and the classifier is $\hsame$. The
stability obligation is preservation under one terminal modulus extension. The
ledger is source, map, target, original modulus and certificate, extra modulus,
composite modulus, and terminal certificate. The core theorem is this closure,
exactness is the displayed carrier conclusion, the standard bridge is deferred,
and the status is $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{proof}

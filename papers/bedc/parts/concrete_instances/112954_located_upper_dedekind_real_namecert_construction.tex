It sits beside the $\RealUp$ completion surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, the rounded cut rows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dedekindcut-namecert}, the one-sided upper ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-upperreal-namecert}, the lower comparison ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-lowerreal-namecert}, and the located real interface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-locatedreal-namecert}. \begin{definition}[Located upper Dedekind real carrier]
\label{def:located-upper-dedekind-real-carrier}
A $\LocatedUpperDedekindRealUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
U=(D,A,L,O,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the $\DedekindCutUp$ row, read only as rounded lower-and-upper rational observation data;
\item $A$ is the $\UpperRealUp$ row, recording the upper-side rational or dyadic observations that the consumer may inspect;
\item $L$ is the $\LowerRealUp$ comparison row, used only to display lower-upper separation when a locatedness request asks for it;
\item $O$ is the locatedness row: for each displayed rational interval $a<b$, it records either a lower-side observation below $b$ or an upper-side observation above $a$ inside the same finite history;
\item $E$ is the $\RealUp$ seal row reached only after $D,A,L,O$ have been displayed;
\item $H$ is componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the cut row, upper row, lower comparison row, locatedness row, Real seal, and structural rows;
\item $C$ is the displayed $\Cont$ replay route for rereading the same cut-facing upper observations;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\LocatedUpperDedekindRealUp$, $\DedekindCutUp$, $\UpperRealUp$, $\LowerRealUp$, $\LocatedRealUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\LocatedUpperDedekindRealUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier exports no quotient class of cuts, no selected representative, no trichotomy branch, no countable-choice selector, no Dedekind completeness theorem, and no completeness claim for $\RealUp$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Located upper Dedekind real NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:located-upper-dedekind-real-namecert-obligations}
For an accepted $\LocatedUpperDedekindRealUp$ carrier $U=(D,A,L,O,E,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\LocatedUpperDedekindRealUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed $\DedekindCutUp$ row, $\UpperRealUp$ row, $\LowerRealUp$ comparison row, locatedness row, $\RealUp$ seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item upper cut observation, stating that every upper-side read factors through $A$ together with the upper side of $D$;
\item located comparison, stating that every requested rational interval $a<b$ is answered by the displayed row $O$ together with the comparison row $L$ and the upper row $A$;
\item Real-seal boundary, stating that a $\RealUp$-facing consumer reaches $E$ only after the cut, upper, lower, and locatedness rows have been inspected;
\item representation restraint, stating that Cauchy-Dedekind bridge or equivalence consumers may cite this packet only as a finite cut-facing located upper surface.
\end{enumerate}
No obligation introduces quotient stream equality, quotient cut equality, selected representatives, trichotomy, countable choice, Dedekind completeness, or $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:located-upper-dedekind-real-carrier}. The displayed packet contains the cut row, upper row, lower comparison row, locatedness row, Real seal, and structural rows as finite $\BHist$ data.

The upper cut observation obligation reads $D$ and $A$ together. The cut row supplies rounded lower-and-upper rational observations, while the upper row exposes only the upper-side candidates and their finite comparison budgets.

The located comparison obligation is the role of $O$. For a displayed interval $a<b$, the packet answers through the same finite lower and upper rows, so a consumer receives located evidence without a trichotomy branch or an external order oracle.

The Real-seal boundary is $E$. Since $E$ is downstream of $D,A,L,O$ and is transported only by $H$ and replayed only by $C$, a $\RealUp$-facing read cannot bypass the finite cut-facing route.

Finally the provenance row $P$ names $\LocatedRealUp$ and the Cauchy-Dedekind comparison siblings only as dependency anchors. The packet therefore supports later bridge comparison while adding no quotient equality, selected representative, countable-choice selector, Dedekind completeness theorem, or completeness claim for $\RealUp$.
\end{proof}

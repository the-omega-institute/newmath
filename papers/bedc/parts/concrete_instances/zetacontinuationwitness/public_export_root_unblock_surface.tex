\begin{theorem}[ZetaContinuationWitness public export route]
\label{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-public-export-route}
For an accepted $\ZetaContinuationWitnessUp$ packet $W=(B,E,A,P,F,T,G,H,C,R,N)$, every public root-facing export factors through the finite contour, Gamma, critical-strip, provenance, and local naming route
$$
B,E,A,P,F,T,G,H,C,R,N.
$$
The route exposes the source row, eta-continuation row, analytic-continuation row, pole ledger, functional-equation route, trivial-zero ledger, Gamma boundary, componentwise $\hsame$ transports, displayed $\Cont$ routes, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ row, and it exports no RH predicate, non-trivial-zero locator, host complex equality, off-packet analytic branch, or completed Gamma certificate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-public-export}. It assembles the carrier row, contour route, Gamma ledger, critical-strip scope, provenance rows, and local $\NameCert$ rows into one public finite witness export.

Use \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-public-export-factorization} and \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-public-root-export}. These results force every public root-facing read through the displayed carrier coordinates and the same $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ surface.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-critical-strip-public-refusal} and \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-gamma-boundary-public-export}. Critical-strip and Gamma-facing consumers receive only the finite route above, while RH data, zero-location data, host complex equality, completed Gamma data, and off-packet analytic branches remain outside the export.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-consumer-packet-totality}

$$

and their $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ structure. No accepted consumer route remains outside the source, contour, pole, trivial-zero, Gamma, transport, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows of the same packet.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First project the consumer input package by \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-zero-route-input-package}. It identifies the rows received by zero-facing and critical-strip consumers as the displayed packet coordinates with their BEDC structure.

Next use \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-critical-strip-consumer-scope} and \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-public-export-route}. The critical-strip route and the public root-facing export route are the same finite packet surface, not two independent consumer packets.

The NameCert obligation surface, contour route, carrier row, provenance row, and local name exhaust the consumer-visible packet, so there is no admitted route left to classify outside the displayed rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[ZetaContinuationWitness analytic boundary non-escape]
\label{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-analytic-boundary-nonescape}
An accepted $\ZetaContinuationWitnessUp$ public export keeps RH, non-trivial-zero location data, analytic continuation as an external analytic premise, and host complex equality outside the consumer packet. The only analytic boundary visible to the packet is the finite route through $B,E,A,P,F,T,G,H,C,R,N$ and the displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the analytic and Gamma boundaries. The contour route of \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-contour-route} and the Gamma boundary public ledger of \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-gamma-boundary-public-ledger} restrict analytic-facing reads to the displayed continuation, pole, functional-equation, Gamma, transport, provenance, and naming rows.

Use \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-no-rh-nonescape} and \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-critical-strip-consumer-nonescape}. These theorems exclude RH predicates, non-trivial-zero locators, global zero classifiers, and completed Gamma certificates from the packet inventory.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:zeta-continuation-witness-consumer-packet-totality}. Since every accepted consumer route has already been exhausted by the displayed finite packet, analytic continuation outside the packet and host complex equality have no consumer-visible coordinate from which to enter.
\end{proof}

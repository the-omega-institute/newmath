\begin{theorem}[TheoremDnaScore NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:theorem-dna-score-namecert-obligations}

$$

\item role completeness, requiring $G$ to display source, feature, circuit, classifier, stability, intervention, ledger, and seal loci before $W$ may score the packet;

\begin{proof}

Classifier faithfulness is read from $B,F,U,K$. The classifier row is tied to the displayed behavior and explanatory rows, so the stability and intervention rows can only support the declared boundary.

The remaining obligations are read from $A,I,L,E,W,H,C,P,N$. Stability and intervention evidence is ledgered before the seal and score are consumed, and the structural rows only transport, replay, source, and name the same route. Since the carrier has no coordinate for hidden model knowledge, unobserved causality, unanchored language, unlabeled debt, or unscoped taste, the listed rows exhaust the NameCert surface.

\begin{theorem}[Singleton linear-map continuation append-pair classifier]
\label{thm:linearmap-singleton-classifier-continuation-append-pair-classifier-iff}
For histories $p,q,r,s,h,t:\mathsf{BHist}$, if
$\ContR(\mathsf{append}(p,q),\mathsf{append}(r,s),h)$, then
$$
\lambda_{\emp}(h,t)
\Longleftrightarrow
L_{\emp}(p)\wedge L_{\emp}(q)\wedge L_{\emp}(r)\wedge
L_{\emp}(s)\wedge L_{\emp}(t).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The continuation displays $h$ as the append of the two appended endpoints.
The forward direction reads the left carrier field of the classifier and
splits that empty append once for the two displayed endpoints, then once more
inside each endpoint to expose the four source carrier witnesses; the target
carrier field supplies $t$. Conversely, the four source carrier witnesses
rebuild both displayed endpoints as empty histories, then rebuild their
continuation result as empty, and symmetry on the target carrier completes the
singleton classifier.
\end{proof}

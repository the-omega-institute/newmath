\begin{theorem}[CompleteMetricNestedBalls NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:complete-metric-nested-balls-namecert-obligations}

\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:complete-metric-nested-balls-carrier}. The displayed rows are exactly $K,M,D,I,S,Q,E,H,C,P,N$, and every local obligation must be attached to one of them.

The complete-metric and metric-space rows are read first. They supply the admissible completion service and the distance surface on which centers, ball membership, and containment can be checked.

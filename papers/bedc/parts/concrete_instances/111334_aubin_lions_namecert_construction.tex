\label{thm:aubin-lions-namecert-obligations}

$$

\item source discipline, requiring every compactness request to enter through the $\SobolevUp$ row $S$ before temporal or spatial compactness data are read;
\item time-translation exactness, requiring $T$ to display the derivative bounds, translation windows, and temporal equicontinuity budget used by the compactness route;

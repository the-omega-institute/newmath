\label{def:hard-problem-closure-carrier}

$$

$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $R$ is the generated-record row, recording the finite histories, focused records, universe-slice records, observer-identity records, and local relation records available to the question;
\item $L$ is the ledgered-local-choice row, recording the finite choices, selectors, focus windows, and refusal entries used by the read;

\begin{theorem}[Hard-problem closure NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:hard-problem-closure-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}

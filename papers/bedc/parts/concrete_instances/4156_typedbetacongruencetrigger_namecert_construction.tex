\begin{theorem}[Typed beta congruence trigger NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:typed-beta-congruence-trigger-namecert-obligations}
The local $\NameCert_{\TypedBetaCongruenceTriggerUp}$ surface for \autoref{def:typed-beta-congruence-trigger-carrier} has five finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item trigger admission, requiring $T=(k,r,s,j,b,d,H,C,P,N)$ to display a concrete $\mathrm{BetaStep}$ congruence constructor and its source term row;
\item congruence-constructor classification, requiring $k$ to identify whether the step is application-function, application-argument, lambda-body, lambda-domain, Pi-codomain, or Pi-domain;
\item typing-boundary preservation handoff, requiring the judgement boundary $j$ and audit row $b$ to point to the sibling substitution or subject-reduction discharge row $d$ rather than to an implicit typing rule;
\item route non-escape, requiring every consumer to pass through the displayed $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local $\NameCert$ rows before it can read the discharge surface;
\item ledger exactness, requiring the exported rows to be exactly $k,r,s,j,b,d,H,C,P,N$ and no hidden conversion, closed subject-reduction, or normalisation ledger.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from the finite tuple in \autoref{def:typed-beta-congruence-trigger-carrier}. The constructor tag, beta-step row, source term, judgement boundary, audit row, discharge row, transport, route, provenance, and local name are all displayed coordinates.

The congruence classification is rowwise. The beta audit map separates the direct beta constructor from the congruence constructors, and the carrier records only the congruence side through $k$ and $r$.

The preservation handoff is therefore a route fact. The row $j$ is read together with $b$ and then handed to $d$, so the packet cites the substitution and subject-reduction discharge siblings instead of asserting a new typing-preservation rule.

Finally inspect $H,C,P,N$. These rows provide the only transport, continuation, provenance, and naming paths. Since no coordinate contains conversion typing, full closed subject reduction, fuel-erased normalisation, or quotient equality, the exported ledger is exactly the displayed finite packet.
\end{proof}

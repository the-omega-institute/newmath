\begin{theorem}[Category hom carrier composition right-factor endpoint determinacy]
\label{thm:category-comp-right-factor-endpoint-deterministic}
For the unary-continuation category, suppose $f$ is a hom-carrier from $a$ to
$b$, $fg$ is displayed both as $\ContR(f,g,fg)$ and $\ContR(f,g',fg)$, the
composite $fg$ is a hom-carrier from $a$ to $c$, and $g'$ is a hom-carrier
from $b'$ to $c$. Then the recovered and displayed right-factor endpoints and
right-factor histories agree:
$$
\HSame(b,b')\wedge\HSame(g,g').
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Right-factor recovery turns the displayed composite and $\ContR(f,g,fg)$ into
a concrete hom-carrier from $b$ to $c$ for $g$. Left cancellation of
$\ContR(f,g,fg)$ against $\ContR(f,g',fg)$ gives $\HSame(g,g')$. Transporting
the recovered hom-carrier across this morphism sameness gives a hom-carrier
from $b$ to $c$ for $g'$, and source determinacy against the displayed
$b'\to c$ carrier gives $\HSame(b,b')$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Category hom carrier tail-comm continuation readback]
\label{thm:category-hom-carrier-tail-comm-cont-readback}

$b$, $g$ is a hom-carrier from $b$ to $c$, and $fg$ and $gf$ are the two

$$
\ContR(f,g,z)\wedge\ContR(g,f,z).

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Tail-comm public readback compares the displayed carrier $z:a\to c$ with the
two closed tail carriers. From the forward closed carrier it gives

ledger from the displayed carrier itself: since $f:a\to b$, $g:b\to c$, and
$z:a\to c$ is displayed, the exact characterization converts that carrier
into $\ContR(f,g,z)$.

For the reverse order, transport the given continuation ledger
$\ContR(g,f,gf)$ along $\HSame(gf,z)$ using finite-kernel history-same
transport for continuation results. This yields $\ContR(g,f,z)$, and the two
recovered ledgers form the required conjunction.
\end{proof}

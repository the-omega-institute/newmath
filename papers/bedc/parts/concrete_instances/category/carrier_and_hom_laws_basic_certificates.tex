\begin{theorem}[Category hom carrier identity semantic name certificate]
\label{thm:category-hom-carrier-identity-semantic-namecert}
For a unary object history $a$, the identity hom-carrier predicate
$f\mapsto\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(a,a,f)$ carries a semantic naming
certificate whose source, pattern, and ledger predicates are this hom-carrier
predicate and whose classifier is $\hsame$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The carrier witness is the empty history, using the empty identity hom-carrier
at $a$. The classifier laws are the reflexive, symmetric, and transitive laws
for $\hsame$. Carrier transport follows by transporting the concrete
hom-carrier witness across history sameness.
\end{proof}

Carrier transport keeps endpoints fixed at $(a',c')$, transports the displayed
hom carrier along the classifier proof, and composes the inverse classifier

identities because all three semantic predicates coincide.

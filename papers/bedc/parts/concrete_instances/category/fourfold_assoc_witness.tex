\begin{theorem}[Category hom carrier fourfold associativity witness]
\label{thm:category-hom-carrier-fourfold-assoc-witness}
For the unary-continuation category, let $f$ be a hom-carrier from $a$ to $b$, let $g$ be a hom-carrier from $b$ to $c$, let $h$ be a hom-carrier from $c$ to $d$, and let $i$ be a hom-carrier from $d$ to $e$. If $fg$, $fgh$, $hi$, $ghi$, $left$, and $right$ satisfy
$$
\ContR(f,g,fg),\quad \ContR(fg,h,fgh),\quad \ContR(h,i,hi),\quad \ContR(g,hi,ghi),\quad \ContR(fgh,i,left),\quad \ContR(f,ghi,right),
$$
then $left$ and $right$ are both hom-carriers from $a$ to $e$, and $\HSame(left,right)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First build the left fourfold carrier. Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:category-hom-carrier-comp-closed} to $f:a\to b$ and $g:b\to c$ with $\ContR(f,g,fg)$, obtaining $fg:a\to c$. Apply the same theorem to $fg:a\to c$ and $h:c\to d$ with $\ContR(fg,h,fgh)$, obtaining $fgh:a\to d$. Apply it once more to $fgh:a\to d$ and $i:d\to e$ with $\ContR(fgh,i,left)$, obtaining $left:a\to e$.

Next build the right fourfold carrier. Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:category-hom-carrier-comp-closed} to $h:c\to d$ and $i:d\to e$ with $\ContR(h,i,hi)$, obtaining $hi:c\to e$. Apply the same theorem to $g:b\to c$ and $hi:c\to e$ with $\ContR(g,hi,ghi)$, obtaining $ghi:b\to e$. Apply it once more to $f:a\to b$ and $ghi:b\to e$ with $\ContR(f,ghi,right)$, obtaining $right:a\to e$.

Finally apply the kernel fourfold continuation associativity witness, $cont\_assoc\_four$, to the six displayed continuation relations. The two fourfold composition ledgers therefore generate the same composite history classifier, namely $\HSame(left,right)$.
\end{proof}

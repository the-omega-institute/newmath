\label{def:smuggling-pattern-classifier-carrier}

$$

\begin{enumerate}
\item $E$ is the external-supply row, recording whether the requested supply is philosophical, mathematical, or host-provided;
\item $I$ is the internal-object request row, recording the BEDC-internal reading the consumer attempts to make from that supply;
\item $G$ is the refusal-or-gate row, recording the accepted certificate gate, the typed gap route, or the refusal boundary for the request;
\item $Q$ is the sibling-provenance row, recording the relevant host-leakage, quotient-soundness, refutation-boundary, cannot-claim, and logic/meta-loop surfaces;

\begin{proof}

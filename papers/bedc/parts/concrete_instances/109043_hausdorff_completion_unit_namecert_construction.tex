\label{thm:hausdorff-completion-unit-namecert-obligations}

$$

$$
No obligation may be replaced by quotient completion equality, selected representatives, countable choice, host equality of completion points, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:hausdorff-completion-unit-carrier}. The numerical side is downstream. The $\StreamNameUp$ windows and $\RegSeqRatUp$ readbacks expose finite regular Cauchy data before the $\RealUp$ seal is consumed. Rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name that same finite route.

Since the packet contains no quotient equality, selected representative, choice schedule, host completion-point equality, or ambient-completeness coordinate, the displayed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.

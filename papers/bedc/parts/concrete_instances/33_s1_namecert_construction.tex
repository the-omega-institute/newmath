\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for \(\SOneUp\)}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-s1-namecert}

The circle interface $\SOneUp$ is a certificate-obligation schema over the product carrier $\RealUp \times \RealUp$ from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}. Its carrier is the unit-circle subspace: pairs $(x,y)$ equipped with a certified equation witness that $x^2 + y^2$ is classified as the real unit. The interface is not a primitive topological space; it is admitted only when the product real witnesses, equation witness, componentwise classifier, and equation transport data are supplied.

$$

\item $F$ is the $\ContinuousMapUp$ family row, exposing only the displayed finite family observations needed by the compact-net argument.

\begin{theorem}[CompactUniformEquicontinuityStability handoff]\label{thm:compact-uniform-equicontinuity-stability-handoff}

$$
K,F,A,M,T \longmapsto Q \longmapsto R,U \longmapsto H,C,P,N .
$$

After $Q$ is displayed, $R$ seals the finite metric reads and $U$ exports the uniform-modulus handoff. The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same route, so the excluded open-cover, selector, choice, quotient, host-equality, and ambient-completeness data have no row from which to be consumed.
\end{proof}

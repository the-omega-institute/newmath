\begin{theorem}[Abel theorem NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:abel-theorem-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\AbelTheoremUp$ carrier
$$
L=(A,H,C,D,W,R,E,T,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\AbelTheoremUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Abel summation admission through $A$, requiring every radial polynomial-window read to pass through the displayed $\AbelSummationUp$ packet;
\item Cesaro-source admission through $H$, requiring the Cesaro-facing mean or convergence row to be visible before the Abel route is read as a radial limit certificate;
\item dyadic radial admission through $D$ and $W$, requiring radial parameters and finite observation windows to be read from $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ and $\StreamNameUp$;
\item regular rational readback through $R$, requiring the radial-window observations to be repacked as a $\RegSeqRatUp$ row before the Real seal is visible;
\item terminal Real-seal admission through $E$, requiring every Real-facing consumer to pass through $A,H,D,W,R$ before reading the displayed $\RealUp$ boundary;
\item componentwise transport, replay, provenance, and local naming through $C,T,P,N$, with no extra host limit, quotient stream equality, countable choice, trichotomy, host equality, or ambient completeness row.
\end{enumerate}
No Abel theorem naming obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:abel-theorem-carrier}. The mathematical source rows are $A,H,D,W,R,E$, while $C,T,P,N$ only transport, replay, source, and name that displayed route.

The Abel summation row supplies the radial polynomial-window data. The Cesaro-facing row supplies the mean or convergence surface already displayed by the sibling chapters, so the route consumes a finite summation certificate rather than a host series limit.

The dyadic radial ledger $D$ and the stream window $W$ determine the visible precision request. The readback row $R$ repacks exactly that finite observation as regular rational data, and the Real seal $E$ is downstream of the same route.

Since the carrier has no coordinate for a selected radial limit, quotient stream equality, choice, trichotomy, host equality, or ambient completeness, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Sequentially complete uniform-space NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:sequentially-complete-uniform-space-namecert-obligations}

$$
U,S_b \longmapsto B \longmapsto Q,R \longmapsto E
$$

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:sequentially-complete-uniform-space-carrier}. The accepted packet exposes exactly the entourage row, sequence-window row, Cauchy-basis request, regular readback, Real seal, complete-uniform-space handoff, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

The sequence side is read first. The row $U$ supplies the finite entourage request, and $S_b$ supplies the finite tail windows inspected at that request. The row $B$ then converts those displayed sequence windows into Cauchy-basis obligations.

The readback side is forced through $Q$ and $R$. Regular rational observations and the Real seal are therefore downstream of the same sequence windows and entourage basis, not detached host data.

Finally $E$ records the complete-uniform-space handoff, and $H,C,P,N$ preserve the same route by transport, replay, provenance, and local naming.

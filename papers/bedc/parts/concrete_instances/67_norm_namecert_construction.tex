\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\NormUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-norm-namecert}

The norm interface $\NormUp$ packages a vector-space carrier with a constructive non-negative real-valued norm satisfying positive definiteness, absolute homogeneity, and the triangle inequality. It depends on $\NameCert_{\VecSpaceUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-vecspace-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\RealUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}), and is the immediate prerequisite for $\NameCert_{\BanachUp}$.

\section{Carrier definition}
\label{sec:norm-carrier}

\begin{definition}[Concrete singleton empty-history norm instance]
\label{def:singleton-empty-history-norm-instance}
Use the concrete singleton empty-history vector space of
\autoref{def:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-instance}. Thus the vector
carrier is $A_{\emp}$, the vector classifier is $\rho_{\emp}$, the scalar
source is the singleton $\VecSpaceUp$ scalar field, and the vector zero is
$\emp$.

Let $z_{\mathsf{Rat}}$ be the RatUp zero endpoint supplied to the constant-rational
$\RealAlgOrder$ boundary of
\autoref{thm:realalgorder-constant-rational-order-boundary}. Write
$$
0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}:=\Eone(z_{\mathsf{Rat}})
$$
for its e-one RealUp constant seal. The norm map is the constant endpoint
$$
\nu_{\emp}(m):=0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}
$$
on every displayed vector history $m:\mathsf{BHist}$, and the norm classifier
is $\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}$. The resulting $\NormUp$ seed is
the tuple
$$
(A_{\emp},\rho_{\emp},\nu_{\emp},
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier},
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}).
$$
Its ledger records the singleton vector-space source, the RatUp zero witness,
and the e-one constant-real seal.
\end{definition}
\leandef{BEDC.Derived.NormUp.NormSingletonNorm}

\begin{theorem}[Singleton empty-history norm carrier and classifier]
\label{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-carrier-classifier}
For the concrete norm instance of
\autoref{def:singleton-empty-history-norm-instance}, every carried vector
$m:\mathsf{BHist}$ has a carried constant-zero norm:
$$
A_{\emp}(m)\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\nu_{\emp}(m)).
$$
Moreover, if $A_{\emp}(m)$ and $A_{\emp}(n)$, then
$$
\rho_{\emp}(m,n)
\quad\text{and}\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\nu_{\emp}(m),\nu_{\emp}(n)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.NormUp.NormSingletonEmptyHistory\_carrier\_classifier}
\begin{proof}
The vector carrier and classifier clauses are those of the singleton
empty-history vector space. Apply
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-endpoint-collapse} to the two
carried endpoints. It gives $\rho_{\emp}(m,n)$ and identifies each carried
vector endpoint with $\emp$ in the singleton classifier.

For the norm value, unfold $\nu_{\emp}$. It is the displayed endpoint
$0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}=\Eone(z_{\mathsf{Rat}})$. The constant-rational boundary of
\autoref{thm:realalgorder-constant-rational-order-boundary} transports the
RatUp zero carrier to
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})$.

The same boundary transports the RatUp zero self-classifier to
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c},0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})$. Since
both norm endpoints are definitionally $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$, this is exactly the
displayed norm-classifier conclusion.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Singleton empty-history norm laws]
\label{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-laws}
The concrete instance of
\autoref{def:singleton-empty-history-norm-instance} satisfies the $\NormUp$
law package over the singleton empty-history vector space:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item nonnegativity:
  $\mathsf{RealAlgNonnegativeRow}(\nu_{\emp}(m))$ for every carried
  $m:A_{\emp}$;
  \item positive definiteness on the singleton carrier:
  $\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\nu_{\emp}(m),0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})$
  holds exactly on vectors classified with the singleton zero vector $\emp$;
  \item absolute homogeneity:
  $\nu_{\emp}(\mathsf{smul}(r,m))$ is
  $\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}$-classified with
  $\mathsf{RealAlgMul}(\mathsf{RealAlgAbs}(r),\nu_{\emp}(m))$ whenever the
  scalar endpoint is read through the singleton scalar field and the
  constant-rational RealAlgOrder boundary;
  \item triangle inequality:
  $\nu_{\emp}(\mathsf{add}(m,n))$ is bounded by the RealAlgOrder sum of
  $\nu_{\emp}(m)$ and $\nu_{\emp}(n)$ for every pair of carried singleton
  vectors.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The vector-space obligations are supplied by
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-laws}. Its endpoint-collapse
form, \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-endpoint-collapse}, says
that carried vectors, their sum, their negation, the zero vector, and every
scalar action endpoint all reduce to the singleton endpoint under
$\rho_{\emp}$.

The real-valued obligations are supplied by the constant-rational boundary of
\autoref{thm:realalgorder-constant-rational-order-boundary}. Apply it to the
RatUp zero witness and then seal by e-one. This gives the constant RealUp
carrier, the constant classifier at zero, the nonnegative row for zero, the
absolute-value row at zero, the multiplication row with zero output, and the
addition row with zero output.

For nonnegativity, unfold $\nu_{\emp}(m)$. The value is $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$, so
the transported RatUp zero nonnegative row gives the required RealAlgOrder
row.

For positive definiteness, the forward direction reads the norm classifier as
the constant-real zero classifier and uses
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-endpoint-collapse} to identify
the carried vector endpoint with $\emp$. Conversely, if the vector is
classified with $\emp$, the norm definition is still the constant endpoint
$0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$, and the RealUp constant classifier readback gives
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c},0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})$.

For absolute homogeneity and the triangle inequality, every displayed vector
argument has norm $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$. The singleton scalar action and singleton
addition endpoints collapse by
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-endpoint-collapse}. The
constant-rational RealAlgOrder rows then read
$|r|\cdot 0=0$ and $0+0=0$ at the e-one RealUp boundary, and classifier
transport repacks both conclusions under
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}$.
\end{proof}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.NormUp.NormSingletonEmptyHistory\_laws}

\begin{theorem}[Singleton empty-history norm zero exactness]
\label{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-zero-exactness}
For every carried vector endpoint $m:\mathsf{BHist}$ in the concrete norm
instance of \autoref{def:singleton-empty-history-norm-instance},
$$
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\nu_{\emp}(m),0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})
\Longleftrightarrow
\rho_{\emp}(m,\emp).
$$
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.NormUp.NormSingletonEmptyHistory\_zero\_exactness}
\begin{proof}
In the forward direction, the premise says that the constant norm endpoint is
classified with real zero. The carried-vector assumption and
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-endpoint-collapse} identify
$m$ with $\emp$ in the singleton vector classifier, giving
$\rho_{\emp}(m,\emp)$.

In the reverse direction, unfold the norm definition. Both sides of the real
classifier are the same e-one seal $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$. The RealUp constant
classifier readback, together with the RatUp zero self-classifier transported
by \autoref{thm:realalgorder-constant-rational-order-boundary}, gives
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c},0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c})$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Singleton empty-history NormUp scoped certificate]
\label{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-scoped-certificate}

the scoped $\NormUp$ certificate over $\mathsf{BHist}$. Its vector carrier is
$A_{\emp}$, its vector classifier is $\rho_{\emp}$, its norm endpoint carrier

$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}$. Stability is supplied by
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-carrier-classifier} and
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-laws}; exactness is supplied by
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-zero-exactness}. The ledger consists

e-one $\RealUp$ seal.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the singleton vector-space source. The carrier $A_{\emp}$, the
classifier $\rho_{\emp}$, the zero vector $\emp$, and the singleton scalar
action are exactly the data of
\autoref{def:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-instance}, with the vector-space
laws supplied by \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-vecspace-laws}.

Attach the norm endpoint boundary. The RatUp zero witness is sealed by e-one
as $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$, and
\autoref{thm:realalgorder-constant-rational-order-boundary} gives the
constant RealUp carrier, classifier, and order rows used by the constant norm
map $\nu_{\emp}$.

Now compose the checked $\NormUp$ rows. The carrier and classifier stability
come from \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-carrier-classifier}; the
nonnegativity, positive-definiteness, absolute-homogeneity, and triangle rows
come from \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-laws}; and the exact
zero row comes from \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-zero-exactness}.

These clauses exhaust the scoped certificate. The ledger contains no data
outside the singleton $\VecSpaceUp$ source, the RatUp zero witness, and the
e-one $\RealUp$ seal, so the certificate is scoped to the displayed
$\mathsf{BHist}$ construction and asserts no standard bridge.
\end{proof}

\end{corollary}
\begin{proof}

constant norm endpoint $\nu_{\emp}$, the Real constant carrier, and the Real

\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-laws}. Its nonnegativity,

\begin{theorem}[Singleton empty-history NormUp standard bridge boundary]
\label{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-standard-bridge-boundary}
The singleton BEDC $\NormUp$ tuple bridges to the one-point standard normed
vector-space presentation. Carrier readback sends every carried $A_{\emp}$
endpoint to the sole standard point and sends that standard point back to
$\emp$ under $\rho_{\emp}$. Norm readback sends every carried endpoint to
standard zero via $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$. Positive-definite exactness is
exactly \autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-zero-exactness}. The ledger
introduces no hidden quotient, host equality, or non-singleton norm data.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The public surface is the ten-field NameCert surface of
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-semantic-namecert-surface}. The
scoped certificate assembled in
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-scoped-certificate} identifies the
singleton vector-space source, the classifier $\rho_{\emp}$, the constant norm
map $\nu_{\emp}$, the constant RealUp carrier, and the exact zero row as the
complete certificate data.

The boundary statement of
\autoref{cor:singleton-empty-history-norm-scoped-certificate-boundary} says
that these rows exhaust the scoped $\NameCert_{\NormUp}$ boundary. Hence every
carried $A_{\emp}$ endpoint has the singleton readback to the one-point
standard carrier, and the return leg lands at $\emp$ under $\rho_{\emp}$.

For norm readback, the same scoped certificate pins every displayed norm
endpoint to the e-one seal $0_{\mathsf{Real}}^{c}$ of the RatUp zero witness.
Thus the standard norm interpretation sends each carried endpoint to standard
zero in the one-point normed vector space.

Exactness is precisely
\autoref{thm:singleton-empty-history-norm-zero-exactness}: the real
constant-zero classifier for $\nu_{\emp}(m)$ is equivalent to
$\rho_{\emp}(m,\emp)$ on carried singleton vectors.

Finally, the scoped certificate boundary leaves no extra ledger slot: no
quotient, host equality, or non-singleton norm datum is added beyond the public
surface cited above. The standard carrier and standard zero norm therefore
appear only as bridge readbacks.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Observer-locality pullback carrier]
\label{def:observer-locality-pullback-carrier}
An $\ObserverLocalityPullbackUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
U=(O,D,M,T,J,H,Q,C,P,N)
$$

\begin{enumerate}
\item $O$ is the $\ObserverLocalityCellUp$ row supplying the two-observer locality cell;
\item $D$ is the displayed finite family of $\CrossHistCausalUp$ rows over the same observer-locality cell;
\item $M$ is the $\MaxCausalRateUp$ row read only over the causal family $D$;
\item $T$ is the $\ObserverLocalityTransportUp$ row transporting $O$ through $D$ before $M$ is consumed;
\item $J$ is the pullback compatibility row asserting that $O,D,M,T$ are one finite packet rather than four detached sibling reads;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $O,D,M,T,J$ and the local naming row;
\item $Q$ records $\psame$ stability for the probe reads used by the observer-locality cell, causal rows, maximum-rate row, and transport row;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes for reading $O$, replaying each row of $D$, reading $M$, transporting through $T$, checking $J$, and auditing the local name;

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ObserverLocalityPullbackUp}$ row.

The carrier exports only the displayed locality cell, causal rows, maximum-rate row, transport row, compatibility row, structural rows, provenance, and local naming data.

\begin{theorem}[Observer-locality pullback NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:observer-locality-pullback-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:observer-locality-pullback-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\ObserverLocalityPullbackUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for
$$
U=(O,D,M,T,J,H,Q,C,P,N),
$$
observer-locality source exactness through $O$, causal-family exactness through $D$, maximum-rate readback through $M$, transport exactness through $T$, simultaneous pullback compatibility through $J$, componentwise transport through $H$, probe stability through $Q$, displayed route replay through $C$, package provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:observer-locality-pullback-carrier}. The rows $O,D,M,T,J,H,Q,C,P,N$ are the only coordinates available to a local $\NameCert_{\ObserverLocalityPullbackUp}$ read.

Read the four sibling surfaces in packet order. The observer-locality cell supplies $O$, the displayed causal family supplies $D$, the maximum-rate row $M$ is attached to that family, and the transport row $T$ carries the locality-cell read through the causal and rate rows.

The compatibility row $J$ then records that these four surfaces are simultaneous. The structural rows $H,Q,C,P,N$ transport, stabilize, replay, package, and name the same finite packet, so no detached rate row or host frame can replace the pullback carrier.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Observer-locality pullback non-escape]
\label{thm:observer-locality-pullback-nonescape}
Every rate-facing consumer of an accepted $\ObserverLocalityPullbackUp$ packet factors through
$$
O,D,M,T,J,H,Q,C,P,N.
$$
In particular, a consumer cannot read the $\MaxCausalRateUp$ row $M$ unless the displayed $\CrossHistCausalUp$ family $D$, the $\ObserverLocalityCellUp$ row $O$, and the $\ObserverLocalityTransportUp$ row $T$ are present in the same pullback packet. The packet exports no global frame, synchronized clock, metric tensor, Lorentz geometry, host real-valued speed, or detached cross-Hist causal-rate row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the accepted carrier. The maximum-rate coordinate is $M$, and its only admissible sources in the carrier are the displayed locality cell $O$, causal family $D$, transport row $T$, and compatibility row $J$.

Apply \autoref{thm:observer-locality-pullback-namecert-obligations}. It forces every rate-facing read through the same finite packet and then through $H,Q,C,P,N$ for transport, probe stability, route replay, provenance, and local naming.

The excluded objects have no coordinate in $U$. Therefore a request for a global frame, synchronized clock, metric tensor, Lorentz geometry, host speed, or detached causal-rate row leaves the accepted pullback packet rather than projecting from it.
\end{proof}

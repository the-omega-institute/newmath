\begin{theorem}[CyclotomicUp unary exponent source closure]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-unary-exponent-source-closure}
For an accepted $\CyclotomicUp$ carrier
$$
\mathcal{C}_{\zeta}=(\mathcal{N},n,p,\mathcal{S},\zeta,\alpha,\lambda,\rho)
$$
of \autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-carrier}, every displayed positive exponent
row used by the cyclotomic root carrier is the unary-history
$\mathsf{BHist}$ row $n$. This row is stable under the classifier of
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-rational-polynomial-classifier} and under the
$\Cont$ source ledger recorded by $\alpha$ and $\lambda$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the cyclotomic carrier. Its exponent coordinate is the single positive
row $n$, and the polynomial row $p$ is read as the ledger for $X^n-1$.

Apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-positive-exponent-row-coverage}. It identifies
$n$ as the only exponent datum exported by the certificate and ties every read
of $X^n-1$ to the same $\mathsf{BHist}$ row, polynomial ledger, continuation
entry, and provenance row.

Finally use the classifier clauses for exponent and source comparison. They
transport $n$ through $\hsame$ and keep the same $\Cont$ source ledger attached
to the accepted carrier, so every displayed exponent row remains a unary
$\mathsf{BHist}$ source row.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CyclotomicUp root-action package provenance]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-root-action-pkg-provenance}
For accepted $\CyclotomicUp$ carriers related by the classifier of
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-rational-polynomial-classifier}, every Galois-action or
splitting-field consumer row for the primitive endpoint $\zeta$ factors through
the same $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance row $\rho$ as the root carrier, polynomial
classifier, and splitting ledger. No action row receives an independent
provenance package outside the carried $\NameCert_{\CyclotomicUp}$ packet.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.CyclotomicUp.CyclotomicRootCarrier\_root\_action\_pkg\_provenance}
\begin{proof}
Begin with \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-splittingfield-provenance-conservation}. It
places the splitting-field consumer surface at the polynomial row $p$, the
primitive endpoint $\zeta$, the factorization rows of $\mathcal S$, the
$\Cont$ acceptance row, the transported $\hsame$ comparisons, and the package
row $\rho$.

Use \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-galois-action-namecert-boundary} to read the
Galois-facing action surface from the same endpoint and factorization
transport. The action row is therefore attached to the transported carrier
surface rather than to a host-field equality package.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-source-provenance-confluence}. The
root route and splitting-polynomial route share the same $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\Cont$, and $\mathsf{Pkg}$ row family, so each action or splitting consumer
factors through $\rho$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CyclotomicUp downstream NameCert obligation packet]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-downstream-namecert-obligation-packet}
The $\PadicUp$, $\IwasawaUp$, $\ClassFieldUp$, $\GaloisGroupUp$,
$\NumFieldUp$, and $\SplittingFieldUp$ consumers of an accepted
$\CyclotomicUp$ carrier receive exactly the $\mathsf{BHist}$ root carrier, the
unary exponent row, the polynomial classifier, the root-action provenance, and
the ledger exactness rows listed in this chapter. They receive no valuation,
completion, class group, reciprocity map, ambient automorphism quotient,
minimal polynomial, or host-field equality from $\NameCert_{\CyclotomicUp}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Use \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-unary-exponent-source-closure} to fix the exponent
input as the single unary $\mathsf{BHist}$ row $n$. Use
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-polynomial-ledger-exactness} and
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-factorization-ledger-exactness} to identify the
polynomial and splitting ledgers attached to that row.

Apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-action-pkg-provenance} for the
Galois-action and splitting-field provenance surface. Apply
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-classfield-repack-exactness} for the class-field
consumer and \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-padic-tower-consumer-exactness} for the
local tower consumers.

The remaining exclusions are supplied by
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-minimal-polynomial-nonexport} and
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-local-symbol-exclusion}. Thus each listed downstream
$\NameCert$ consumes only the displayed root, exponent, polynomial,
factorization, action-provenance, $\Cont$, and $\mathsf{Pkg}$ rows.
\end{proof}

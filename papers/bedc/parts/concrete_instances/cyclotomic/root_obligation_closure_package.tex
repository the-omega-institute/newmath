\begin{definition}[Cyclotomic root obligation packet]
\label{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet}
The $\CyclotomicUp$ root obligation packet is the finite row family
$$
(\mathcal{N},n,p,\mathcal{S},\zeta,\alpha,\lambda,\rho),
$$
where $\mathcal{N}$ is the $\NameCert_{\NumFieldUp}$ source row, $n$ is the
positive unary $\mathsf{BHist}$ exponent, $p$ is the rational polynomial ledger
for $X^n-1$, $\mathcal{S}$ is the $\NameCert_{\SplittingFieldUp}$ source over
$p$, $\zeta$ is the primitive-root endpoint, $\alpha$ is the finite $\Cont$
acceptance and factorization ledger, $\lambda$ is the transported $\hsame$
comparison between source base rows, and $\rho$ is the $\mathsf{Pkg}$
provenance for the paired source certificates.

The packet is a row package for downstream consumers. It is not a host
cyclotomic field, not a quotient of roots, and not a class-field, valuation, or
completion object.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Cyclotomic root source row exhaustion]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-root-source-row-exhaustion}
Every source row of the $\CyclotomicUp$ root boundary is exhausted by the root
obligation packet of \autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet}. Conversely,
the displayed packet repacks into the root carrier, classifier transport,
polynomial ledger, primitive endpoint, and threshold surface of the accepted
cyclotomic root interface.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the carrier of \autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-carrier}. Its visible
coordinates are exactly the NumFieldUp source, exponent, polynomial,
SplittingFieldUp source, primitive endpoint, acceptance, comparison, and
provenance rows displayed in
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet}.

Apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-layer-classifier-transport}. It transports
those carrier rows componentwise through $\hsame$, while keeping the $\Cont$
acceptance and $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance attached to the same finite packet.

Use \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-polynomial-ledger-exactness} for the polynomial
row and \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-primitive-root-endpoint-transport} for the
primitive endpoint. These theorems account for the source ledger and endpoint
readback without adding a quotient root or ambient field equality.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-obligation-threshold}. It repacks
the carrier, classifier transport, exact ledger, endpoint, $\Cont$, $\hsame$,
and $\mathsf{Pkg}$ rows as the threshold surface. Since no other source
coordinate occurs in the carrier or threshold theorem, the packet is exhaustive.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Cyclotomic primitive-root ledger transport]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-primitive-root-ledger-transport}
For accepted $\CyclotomicUp$ carriers related by the rational-polynomial
classifier, the primitive-root endpoint, its polynomial ledger for $X^n-1$, the
SplittingFieldUp factorization rows, the $\Cont$ acceptance row, and the
$\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance are transported as the same root obligation packet.
No transported row becomes a host-field equality, quotient of primitive roots,
or independent minimal-order datum.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the related carriers through
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-rational-polynomial-classifier}. The classifier exposes
only the displayed source, exponent, polynomial, splitting-field, endpoint,
acceptance, comparison, and provenance rows.

Use \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-layer-classifier-transport} to move those rows
componentwise through the available $\hsame$ comparisons. The finite $\Cont$
and $\mathsf{Pkg}$ rows remain attached to the transported endpoint and source
certificates.

Apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-polynomial-ledger-exactness} and
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-primitive-root-endpoint-transport}. The polynomial
ledger and primitive endpoint are therefore the transported rows of the same
packet, not new algebraic objects.

Repack the transported family by
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet} and the threshold theorem
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-obligation-threshold}. The exclusions follow from
the carrier definition and the threshold surface, both of which name no host
field equality, root quotient, or separate order witness.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Cyclotomic SplittingField consumer boundary]
\label{thm:cyclotomic-splittingfield-consumer-boundary}
A downstream $\SplittingFieldUp$ consumer of $\NameCert_{\CyclotomicUp}$ may
read exactly the splitting-field source row $\mathcal{S}$, the polynomial row
$p$, the primitive endpoint $\zeta$, the factorization and acceptance row
$\alpha$, the transported $\hsame$ comparisons, and the $\mathsf{Pkg}$
provenance contained in the root obligation packet. It receives no extra
splitting field object, host equality of fields, valuation row, class-field
operation, or completion datum from $\CyclotomicUp$ alone.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the SplittingField-facing coordinates of
\autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet}: $\mathcal{S}$, $p$, $\zeta$,
$\alpha$, $\lambda$, and $\rho$. These are the only rows of the packet addressed
to a splitting-field consumer.

Apply \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-splitting-polynomial-classifier-transport}. It
transports the polynomial and splitting-field source rows through the displayed
classifier support. Then use
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-factorization-ledger-exactness} to identify the
factorization rows as the ledger covered by $\mathcal{S}$.

The primitive endpoint is controlled by
\autoref{thm:cyclotomic-primitive-root-endpoint-transport}, and the root
threshold is fixed by \autoref{thm:cyclotomic-root-obligation-threshold}. These
results repack exactly the displayed splitting-field consumer rows.

No remaining coordinate of \autoref{def:cyclotomic-root-obligation-packet}
names a second splitting field, ambient field equality, valuation, class-field
operation, or completion object. Therefore a SplittingFieldUp consumer is
bounded by the listed packet rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Helly convex-geometry dependency lattice]
\label{thm:helly-theorem-convex-geometry-dependency-lattice}
Every accepted $\HellyTheoremUp$ finite-intersection read consumes the convex-geometry lattice
$$
\ConvexSetUp,\ \PolytopeUp,\ \CaratheodoryConvexHullUp
$$
before it exposes its local intersection row. The $\ConvexSetUp$ surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-convexset-namecert} supplies the membership and convex-family rows, the $\PolytopeUp$ surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-polytope-namecert} supplies the finite witness rows, and the $\CaratheodoryConvexHullUp$ surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-caratheodory-convex-hull-namecert} supplies the bounded-support handoff consumed by convex-hull readers. A Helly packet that omits one of these rows is outside the accepted sibling route.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier in \autoref{def:helly-theorem-carrier}. Its convex-family coordinate is $F$, its finite witness coordinate is $W$, and its subfamily and full-family intersection coordinates are $S,I$.

The row $F$ is read through the $\ConvexSetUp$ classifier, so no finite subfamily can be inspected before the convex-family membership rows have been displayed. The row $W$ is the finite $\PolytopeUp$ witness ledger, so every inspected subfamily intersection is backed by the polytope witness surface before $S$ is replayed.

The convex-hull consumer then reads the same finite subfamily ledger through $\CaratheodoryConvexHullUp$. Since the carrier has no alternate row for a host separation theorem, selected intersection point, quotient convex-set equality, or ambient standard-model convexity, the three named sibling surfaces form the whole dependency lattice for the Helly read.
\end{proof}

Consequently $\HellyTheoremUp$ is not an isolated hub: its public convex-geometry use is routed through $\ConvexSetUp$ membership rows, $\PolytopeUp$ finite witness rows, and $\CaratheodoryConvexHullUp$ bounded-support rows before any local $\NameCert_{\HellyTheoremUp}$ intersection certificate is consumed.

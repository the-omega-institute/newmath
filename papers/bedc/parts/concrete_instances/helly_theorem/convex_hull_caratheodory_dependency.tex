\paragraph{Convex-hull and Caratheodory dependency.}
The $\HellyTheoremUp$ packet consumes its finite convex-family carrier through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-convex-hull-namecert} and its affine-combination reduction through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-caratheodory-namecert} before it exports an intersection certificate.

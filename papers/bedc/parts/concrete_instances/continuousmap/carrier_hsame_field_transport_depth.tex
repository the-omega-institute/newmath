\begin{theorem}[ContinuousMap carrier hsame-field transport with depth ledger]
\label{thm:continuousmap-carrier-hsame-field-transport-depth}
For histories $source,source',map,map',target,target',modulus,modulus',
cert,cert',distance,distance':\mathsf{BHist}$, suppose
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(source,map,target,modulus,cert,distance)
$$
and
$$
\hsame(source,source')\land
\hsame(map,map')\land
\hsame(target,target')\land
\hsame(modulus,modulus')\land
\hsame(cert,cert')\land
\hsame(distance,distance')
$$
hold. Then the transported histories again form a continuous-map carrier, and
the transported distance exposes the endpoint continuation and depth ledger:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(source',map',target',modulus',cert',distance')
\\
\land\ \ContR(source',target',distance')
\\
\land\
\mathsf{MetricDistanceDepth}(distance')=
\mathsf{MetricDistanceDepth}(source')+\mathsf{MetricDistanceDepth}(target').
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unpack the displayed $\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}$ into its
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}$ half and its
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}$ half. Apply
\autoref{thm:continuous-function-carrier-hsame-transport} to the five function
fields $source,map,target,modulus,cert$ and their displayed $\hsame$ images.
This gives the transported continuous-function half.

For the metric half, apply
\autoref{thm:metric-distance-witness-hsame-fields-transport} to
$source,target,distance$ and $source',target',distance'$. The resulting unary
fields and transported continuation repack as
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(source',target',distance')$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:metric-distance-witness-hsame-transport-cont-depth-add} to the
same three $\hsame$ witnesses and the original metric witness. It supplies the
displayed $\ContR(source',target',distance')$ and the displayed depth equation.
Pairing the transported continuous-function half with the transported metric
witness gives the carrier component; pairing this with the continuation and
depth ledger gives the stated conjunction.

The ten-field fit is: traditional target stable uniformly continuous map under
classifier transport; BEDC source $\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}$,
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}$, and $\hsame$; carrier
$\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}$; classifier $\hsame$; stability obligation
six-field transport plus transported metric depth ledger; ledger hidden
distance history; core theorem this block; exactness transported carrier and
distance-depth equation; standard bridge deferred; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{proof}

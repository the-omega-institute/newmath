\begin{theorem}[ContinuousMap composition category-metric package]
\label{thm:continuousmap-composition-category-metric-package}
For histories
$source,mid,target,mapF,mapG,mapFG,modF,modG,modFG,certF,certG,certFG,distF,distG,displayed:\mathsf{BHist}$,
assume the two concrete continuous-map carriers
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(source,mapF,mid,modF,certF,distF),\\
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(mid,mapG,target,modG,certG,distG),
\end{array}
$$
and assume the continuation ledgers
$$
\ContR(mapF,mapG,mapFG),\qquad
\ContR(modF,modG,modFG),\qquad
\ContR(target,modFG,certFG).
$$
Then the composite exposes a category-metric package:
\begin{enumerate}
\item the canonical composite continuous-map carrier,
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}
(source,mapFG,target,modFG,certFG,\mathsf{append}(source,target));
$$

\item the composite categorical hom row,
$$
\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(source,target,mapFG);
$$

\item the composite target-side modulus row,
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(target,modFG,certFG);
$$

\item the canonical metric distance row,
$$
\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(source,target,\mathsf{append}(source,target));
$$

\item every displayed composite carrier with the same visible fields has the
canonical distance ledger,
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(source,mapFG,target,modFG,certFG,displayed)
\Rightarrow \hsame(displayed,\mathsf{append}(source,target)).
$$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the continuous-function halves from the two
$\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}$ hypotheses. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuous-function-carrier-comp-closed} to those halves and
the three continuation ledgers, obtaining the composite
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}(source,mapFG,target,modFG,certFG)$.

Package the canonical source-target distance by applying
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-carrier-canonical-distance-iff} from right to
left to this composite continuous-function carrier. This gives the first
package row,
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}
(source,mapFG,target,modFG,certFG,\mathsf{append}(source,target)).
$$

Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-carrier-category-metric-decomposition} to the
canonical composite carrier. Its three projections are exactly the displayed
$\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}$ row, the
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}$ row, and the canonical
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}$ row.

For the final determinacy clause, compare any displayed composite carrier
with the canonical composite carrier by
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-target-cert-distance-deterministic}. The two
carriers have the same source, map, target, modulus, and certificate histories,
so the distance component of the determinacy conclusion is precisely
$\hsame(displayed,\mathsf{append}(source,target))$.
\end{proof}

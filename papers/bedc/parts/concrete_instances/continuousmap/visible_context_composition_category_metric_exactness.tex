\begin{theorem}[ContinuousMap visible-context composition category-metric exactness]
\label{thm:continuousmap-visible-context-composition-category-metric-exactness}
For histories
$p,q,source,middle,target,f,g,fg,modF,modG,modFG,certF,certG,cert,distF,distG,displayed:\mathsf{BHist}$,
assume the two visible-context carriers
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(p\cdot source,f,p\cdot middle,modF\cdot q,
(p\cdot certF)\cdot q,p\cdot distF),\\
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(p\cdot middle,g,p\cdot target,modG\cdot q,
(p\cdot certG)\cdot q,p\cdot distG),
\end{array}
$$
and assume the graph, modulus, and target-certificate ledgers
$$
\ContR(f,g,fg),\qquad
\ContR(modF,modG,modFG),\qquad
\ContR(target,modFG,cert).
$$
Then visible-context composition exposes the full category-metric package:
\begin{enumerate}
\item the composite categorical hom row,
$$
\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(source,target,fg);
$$

\item the composite target-side modulus row,
$$
\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(target,modFG,cert);
$$

\item the canonical source-target metric distance row,
$$
\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(source,target,\mathsf{append}(source,target));
$$

\item the visible composite carrier has exactly the canonical distance
classifier,
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}(p\cdot source,fg,p\cdot target,modFG\cdot q,
(p\cdot cert)\cdot q,p\cdot displayed)
\\[2pt]
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\hsame(displayed,\mathsf{append}(source,target)).
\end{array}
$$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-carrier-visible-context-category-metric-decomposition}
to the first visible carrier. This recovers unary evidence for $p$ and $q$,
the central categorical row
$\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(source,middle,f)$, the central modulus row
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(middle,modF,certF)$, and the metric row
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(source,middle,distF)$.

Apply the same decomposition theorem to the second visible carrier. The result
has the same visible contexts and exposes
$\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(middle,target,g)$,
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(target,modG,certG)$, and
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(middle,target,distG)$.

The two category and modulus rows repack as the two central
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}$ hypotheses. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuous-function-carrier-comp-closed} with the three
displayed continuation ledgers. The composite central carrier
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}(source,fg,target,modFG,cert)$ follows.

Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuous-function-carrier-graph-modulus-cont-readback} to
this composite central carrier. Its graph projection is exactly
$\mathsf{CategoryHomCarrier}(source,target,fg)$, and its target-modulus
projection is exactly
$\mathsf{ContinuousModulusWitness}(target,modFG,cert)$.

Use Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-carrier-canonical-distance-iff} from right
to left on the composite central carrier. Decomposing the resulting canonical
$\mathsf{ContinuousMapCarrier}$ by
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-carrier-category-metric-decomposition} gives
the canonical row
$\mathsf{MetricDistanceWitness}(source,target,\mathsf{append}(source,target))$.

It remains to classify visible displayed distances. The unary witnesses for
$p$ and $q$ are already available, and the composite central
$\mathsf{ContinuousFunctionCarrier}$ has been constructed. Therefore
Theorem~\ref{thm:continuousmap-visible-context-canonical-distance-exactness}
specializes the displayed visible carrier exactly to
$\hsame(displayed,\mathsf{append}(source,target))$.
\end{proof}

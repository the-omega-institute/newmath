\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\AtiyahSingerUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-atiyahsinger-namecert}

The Atiyah-Singer-index interface $\AtiyahSingerUp$ packages the equality between the analytic index of an elliptic operator on a closed manifold and a topological index defined via Chern characters and Todd classes. It depends on $\NameCert_{\SpectralTheoremUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-spectraltheorem-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\ChernWeilUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-chernweil-namecert}), and bridges spectral and topological certificates through the same index pairing.

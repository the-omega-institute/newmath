\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-metric-completion-universal-property-namecert}
\origin{human}

Following Bishop and Bridges, \emph{Constructive Analysis}, the metric-completion universal property is read here as a finite extension-and-uniqueness packet over displayed completion data. The packet refines the metric-completion surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metriccompletion-namecert} by binding it to the universal-extension ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-completionuniversalproperty-namecert}, the regular Cauchy universal route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regular-cauchy-completion-universal-namecert}, the finite stream windows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}, the regular rational readback route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, the real-name seal of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, and the continuous-map interface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-continuousmap-namecert}. It supplies later complete-metric and continuous-extension chapters with an L10 completion route that contains no quotient stream, selected host limit, countable-choice schedule, host function equality, or ambient completeness theorem.

\begin{definition}[MetricCompletionUniversalProperty carrier]
\label{def:metric-completion-universal-property-carrier}
A $\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
U=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following visible rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $M$ is the $\MetricCompletionUp$ row naming the completion-facing source packet;
\item $V$ is the $\CompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ row that supplies extension existence and uniqueness ledgers over displayed completion data;
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regular rational source row used for dense metric observations and finite readback;
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window schedule through which the dense source is inspected;
\item $A$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal read after $R$ and $W$ have displayed the requested approximation data;
\item $F$ is the $\ContinuousMapUp$ dense-source map row, including the graph and modulus data consumed by the extension route;
\item $E$ is the extension ledger from $F,R,W,A$ into a completion-facing $\ContinuousMapUp$ row over $M$;
\item $L$ is the uniqueness ledger comparing two extension rows when they agree on the same $M,V,R,W,A,F,E$ coordinates;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the completion row, universal-property row, dense source, window schedule, real seal, continuous-map row, extension ledger, uniqueness ledger, package row, and local naming row;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay of the route
$$
M,V,R,W,A,F\longmapsto E\longmapsto L ;
$$
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MetricCompletionUp$, $\CompletionUniversalPropertyUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RealUp$, $\ContinuousMapUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no quotient stream, no selected host limit, no countable-choice schedule, no host function equality, and no ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[MetricCompletionUniversalProperty classifier]
\label{def:metric-completion-universal-property-classifier}
Two accepted $\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ carriers
$$
U=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N)
$$
and
$$
U'=(M',V',R',W',A',F',E',L',H',C',P',N')
$$
classify together exactly when the classifier compares the displayed coordinates componentwise by $\hsame$, and when the displayed $\Cont$ replay has the same completion, dense-source, extension, and uniqueness order on both packets. Thus the classifier is the finite route
$$
M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N ,
$$
not equality of quotient completion classes, not host function extensionality, and not a selected-limit principle.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MetricCompletionUniversalProperty extension route]
\label{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-extension-route}
Let $U=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ carrier. Every completion-facing extension exported by $U$ factors through the source completion row $M$, the universal-property row $V$, the dense regular rational row $R$, the finite stream-window schedule $W$, the terminal real-name seal $A$, the dense $\ContinuousMapUp$ row $F$, and only then the extension ledger $E$.

The exported route is therefore
$$
M,V,R,W,A,F\longmapsto E ,
$$
with the structural rows $H,C,P,N$ recording transport, replay, provenance, and local naming. It is not a quotient-stream construction, not a selected host limit, not an appeal to countable choice, and not an ambient completeness theorem.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier of \autoref{def:metric-completion-universal-property-carrier}. The completion-facing source is $M$, and the universal-extension surface is $V$.

The dense source is read through $R$ and $W$. The row $R$ supplies regular rational readback, while $W$ supplies the finite windows through which the requested observations are displayed.

The target real-facing boundary is $A$, and the dense map data are $F$. The extension ledger $E$ is the only coordinate that repacks those rows as a completion-facing continuous-map read.

The remaining rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name the same finite route. Since the carrier has no coordinate for quotient streams, selected limits, countable choice, host function equality, or ambient completeness, none of those principles can be exported by the extension route.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetricCompletionUniversalProperty uniqueness ledger]
\label{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-uniqueness-ledger}
Let $U=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N)$ and $U'=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E',L',H',C',P,N')$ be accepted $\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp$ carriers over the same completion row, universal-property row, dense source, stream-window schedule, real seal, dense continuous-map row, and provenance. If $E$ and $E'$ agree on every displayed finite read from $R$ and $W$ after the real-seal handoff through $A$, then the two exported extension rows are compared by the displayed uniqueness ledgers $L$ and $L'$.

The uniqueness conclusion is internal to $\MetricCompletionUp$, $\CompletionUniversalPropertyUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RealUp$, $\ContinuousMapUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$. It is not host equality of maps and not equality of quotient completion representatives.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Read the common coordinates from the two accepted packets. The rows $M,V,R,W,A,F,P$ fix the completion source, universal-property surface, dense observations, real seal, dense map, and provenance shared by both extension ledgers.

The hypothesis compares $E$ and $E'$ on every finite read displayed by $R$ and $W$. Transport those reads through $H,H'$ and replay them through $C,C'$ until both extension rows are presented over the same real-seal coordinate $A$.

The uniqueness coordinates are exactly $L$ and $L'$. Applying them gives the required displayed $\hsame$ comparison of extension rows.

No carrier coordinate names host function extensionality, quotient equality, a selected limit, countable choice, or ambient completeness. The uniqueness claim therefore remains a ledger comparison over the visible BEDC rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetricCompletionUniversalProperty NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:metric-completion-universal-property-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\MetricCompletionUniversalPropertyUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier display for the packet $U=(M,V,R,W,A,F,E,L,H,C,P,N)$;
\item classifier stability by componentwise $\hsame$ transport and displayed $\Cont$ replay as in \autoref{def:metric-completion-universal-property-classifier};
\item extension existence through the finite route of \autoref{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-extension-route};
\item uniqueness through the ledger comparison of \autoref{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-uniqueness-ledger};
\item non-escape, requiring every consumer read to remain inside $\MetricCompletionUp$, $\CompletionUniversalPropertyUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RealUp$, $\ContinuousMapUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\end{enumerate}
No obligation is stored in a quotient stream, selected host limit, countable-choice schedule, host function equality row, or ambient completeness theorem.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier display is projection from \autoref{def:metric-completion-universal-property-carrier}. The accepted packet lists exactly the completion row, universal-property row, dense source, window schedule, real seal, continuous-map row, extension ledger, uniqueness ledger, and structural support rows.

Classifier stability follows from \autoref{def:metric-completion-universal-property-classifier}. The comparison is componentwise on those displayed coordinates, and the replay data only preserve the same route order.

Extension existence is the obligation of \autoref{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-extension-route}. It forces every exported extension to pass through $M,V,R,W,A,F$ before the ledger $E$ is read.

Uniqueness is the obligation of \autoref{thm:metric-completion-universal-property-uniqueness-ledger}. It compares candidate extension rows through $L$ and $L'$ over the same finite dense-source and real-seal reads.

The non-escape row is obtained by inspecting the carrier inventory. Since every exported object is one of the listed BEDC rows or a displayed route between them, the excluded host-side principles have no coordinate from which to enter the certificate.
\end{proof}

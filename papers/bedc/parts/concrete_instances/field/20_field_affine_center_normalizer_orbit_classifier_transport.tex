\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp affine centralizer normalizer orbit classifier transport]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-orbit-classifier-transport}
For Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, fix a certified
affine automorphism $A:\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ in the concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$
exit object. Let $C_F(A)$ be the affine centralizer carrier, let
$N_F(C_F(A))$ be its normalizer carrier, and let $\sim_{N_F(A)}$ be the orbit
relation of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-orbit}. If
$X,Y,X',Y':\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ satisfy $C_F(A)(X)$, $C_F(A)(Y)$,
$C_F(A)(X')$, $C_F(A)(Y')$, $X\sim_{N_F(A)}Y$,
$X\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}X'$, and $Y\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'$, then
$$
X'\sim_{N_F(A)}Y'.
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Choose the normalizer witness $T:\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ from
$X\sim_{N_F(A)}Y$. Thus $N_F(C_F(A))(T)$ and
$\mathrm{Act}_T(X)\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-action-classifier-congruence}
with the same acting element in both acting slots. Using classifier reflexivity
for $T$ and the endpoint comparison $X\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}X'$, this gives
$$
\mathrm{Act}_T(X)\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}\mathrm{Act}_T(X').
$$

By classifier symmetry, read the last display from $\mathrm{Act}_T(X')$ back
to $\mathrm{Act}_T(X)$. Compose it by classifier transitivity with the
original orbit endpoint comparison
$\mathrm{Act}_T(X)\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y$ and then with
$Y\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'$. The result is
$$
\mathrm{Act}_T(X')\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'.
$$

The same witness $T$ therefore satisfies the defining clause of
$X'\sim_{N_F(A)}Y'$.
\end{proof}

$\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}$; stability endpoint classifier transport; ledger
source endpoint, target endpoint, transported endpoints, and normalizer
witness; exactness orbit reachability stable under affine classifier.

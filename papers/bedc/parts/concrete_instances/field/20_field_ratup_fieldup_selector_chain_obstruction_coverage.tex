\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp adjacent selector-chain obstruction coverage]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-adjacent-selector-chain-obstruction-coverage}
For $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$,
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let $E_q(h,k,\ell)$ be the bracketing endpoint selector
of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} for each
$q:\mathsf{BMark}$. If $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the same
concrete $\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$ contains all of the
following row families:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the complete selector exit obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(s,t,u,v)$;
  \item the complete selector exit obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(u,v,w,z)$;
  \item the selector-chain final boundary rows of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} for $(s,t)$,
  $(u,v)$, and $(w,z)$, including the chained denominator rows
  $$
  R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  R_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell))
  $$
  and the chained endpoint classifier rows
  $$
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} to the
displayed denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness,
selectors $s,t,u,v$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This gives the complete
selector exit obstruction certificate for the first adjacent link
$(s,t,u,v)$.

Apply the same theorem again with selectors $u,v,w,z$ and the same denominator
tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness, and contexts. This gives
the complete selector exit obstruction certificate for the terminal adjacent link
$(u,v,w,z)$.

Apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} to the six
selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z$ over the same denominator tuple, histories, carrier
witnesses, support witness, and contexts. This supplies the final boundary rows
for $(s,t)$, $(u,v)$, and $(w,z)$, including the chained $R_D^u$ rows and the
chained $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ endpoint rows.

Finally cite \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} only to
keep all three row families inside the same concrete
$\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$. Conjunction introduction
packages the two adjacent complete obstruction certificates and the
selector-chain final boundary as one concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ coverage
certificate.
\end{proof}

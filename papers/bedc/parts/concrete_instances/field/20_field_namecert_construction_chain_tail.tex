\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp algebra hierarchy chain-stable exit]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-algebra-hierarchy-chain-stable-exit}
For $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, the concrete denominator tuple
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let $E_q(h,k,\ell)$ be the bracketing endpoint selector
of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} for each
$q:\mathsf{BMark}$. If $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then one
concrete $\mathsf{BHist}$ package contains all of the following rows:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item every row of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-algebra-hierarchy-exit-witness};
  \item every shared certificate and operation-table row of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-assembled-certificate-operation-table-coverage};
  \item the assembled obstruction certificate for $(s,t,u,v)$ from
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate};
  \item the selector-chain final boundary rows of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} for $(s,t)$,
  $(u,v)$, and $(w,z)$, including the chained $R_D^u$ rows and the chained
  $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ endpoint rows.
\end{enumerate}
Equivalently, the concrete FieldUp over RatUp source determines a
chain-stable Phase Algebra Hierarchy exit certificate whose carrier,
classifier, denominator, operation-table, obstruction, and selector-chain
boundary rows are all borne by the same concrete $\mathsf{BHist}$ package.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-algebra-hierarchy-exit-witness} at
$\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$. This supplies the hierarchy rows over the common
carrier $\mathsf{BHist}$, with the RatUp classifier and denominator rows.

Next instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-assembled-certificate-operation-table-coverage} at
the concrete operation table of
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-operation-table}. This supplies the
shared certificate rows and operation-table rows for the same source tuple.

Then instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-final-obstruction-chain-boundary-package}
with the displayed denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, selectors
$s,t,u,v,w,z$, contexts $L,R,r$, and support witness
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$. This supplies the assembled obstruction
certificate and the selector-chain boundary rows.

Finally cite \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} only to
keep all row families inside the same concrete exit object. Conjunction
introduction packages the hierarchy, operation-table, obstruction, and
chain-boundary rows as one chain-stable Phase Algebra Hierarchy exit
certificate.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target chain-stable $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit;
BEDC source $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ over $\mathsf{BHist}$; carrier and
classifier the RatUp and denominator rows; stability obligations the hierarchy,
operation-table, obstruction, and chain-boundary rows; ledger selector sextuple
$s,t,u,v,w,z$ plus contexts $L,R,r$; exactness one chain-stable Phase Algebra
Hierarchy exit certificate; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp concrete two-step obstruction chain package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-two-step-obstruction-chain-package}
For $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$,
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let $E_q(h,k,\ell)$ be the bracketing endpoint selector
of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} for each
$q:\mathsf{BMark}$. If $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the same
concrete $\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$ contains the following
three row families:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the full assembled obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(s,t,u,v)$;
  \item the full assembled obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(u,v,w,z)$;
  \item the selector-chain final boundary rows of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} for $(s,t)$,
  $(u,v)$, and $(w,z)$, including the chained denominator rows
  $$
  R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  R_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell))
  $$
  and the chained endpoint classifier rows
  $$
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-final-obstruction-boundary-package} with
the displayed source, concrete exit object, denominator tuple, histories,
carrier witnesses, support witness, selectors $s,t,u,v$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This supplies the assembled obstruction certificate for
the adjacent selector replacement from $(s,t)$ to $(u,v)$.

Next instantiate the same theorem with the same source, concrete exit object,
denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness, selectors
$u,v,w,z$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This supplies the assembled
obstruction certificate for the adjacent selector replacement from $(u,v)$ to
$(w,z)$.

Then instantiate \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} with
the six selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z$ over the same denominator tuple, histories,
carrier witnesses, support witness, and contexts. This contributes the three
selector-pair boundary rows together with the chained $R_D^u$ rows and chained
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ rows from the first endpoint pair to the final
endpoint pair.

Finally cite \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} only to
keep both adjacent obstruction certificates and the chained final boundary rows
inside the same concrete $\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$.
Conjunction introduction packages the two adjacent obstruction certificates and
the selector-chain final boundary as one concrete
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit-object certificate.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target two-step selector obstruction chain for
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\mathsf{BMark}$, and
$\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$; carrier rows $C_D^u$ and the concrete
selector-obstruction carriers; classifier rows $R_D^u$ and
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$; ledger selector sextuple $s,t,u,v,w,z$ plus
contexts $L,R,r$; stability obligations the two adjacent complete selector exit
obstruction certificates and the selector-chain final boundary; exactness one
two-step obstruction-chain package; downstream consumer Phase Algebra Hierarchy
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit certificate; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

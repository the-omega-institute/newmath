\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp crossed ten-field exit certificate]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-crossed-ten-field-exit-certificate}
For the concrete source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, and histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$, let
$x:=\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k),\ell)$ and
$y:=\mu_D^u(h,\mu_D^u(k,\ell))$. If
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the concrete ten-field paper
certificate for $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ over $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ is the
composition of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-ten-field-exit-certificate},
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-product-classifier-and-nonzero-exit-package},
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-five-factor-nonzero-factor-ledger},
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-independent-threefold-contextual-boundary},
and
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-independent-cross-endpoint-classifier-boundary}.
Its conclusion contains all of the following concrete rows, with no schematic
$\mathsf{mul}$, $\mathsf{add}$, or $\mathsf{neg}$ operator parameter:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the same-bracketing endpoint rows $C_D^u(x)$, $C_D^u(y)$,
  $R_D^u(x,y)$, and, for all $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$, the contextual
  singleton-classifier exclusions
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,x),\mathsf{append}(R,x))
  \to\bot
  $$
  and
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,y),\mathsf{append}(R,y))
  \to\bot;
  $$
  \item the crossed contextual singleton-classifier exclusions, for all
  $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$,
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,x),\mathsf{append}(R,y))
  \to\bot
  $$
  and
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,y),\mathsf{append}(R,x))
  \to\bot;
  $$
  \item the two-sided product classifier and nonzero equivalences, for every
  $a,b,u,v:\mathsf{BHist}$ with certified outer witnesses
  $p_a:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a)$ and $p_b:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(b)$,
  $$
  \bigl(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,u),b)
  \hsame
  \mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,v),b)\bigr)
  \Longleftrightarrow
  u\hsame v
  $$
  and
  $$
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,u),b))
  \Longleftrightarrow
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(u);
  $$
  \item the five-factor nonzero ledger, for every
  $a,b,c,d,e:\mathsf{BHist}$,
  $$
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(
  \mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d),e))
  \Longleftrightarrow
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(a)\wedge\mathsf{NonZero}_F(b)\wedge
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(c)\wedge\mathsf{NonZero}_F(d)\wedge
  \mathsf{NonZero}_F(e).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-ten-field-exit-certificate}. It fixes the
paper certificate site, the $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ source, the
$\mathsf{BHist}$ carrier, the contextual action, and the denominator rows as one
concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ package.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-independent-threefold-contextual-boundary}.
It supplies the same-bracketing endpoint rows $C_D^u(x)$, $C_D^u(y)$, and
$R_D^u(x,y)$, together with the $x$--$x$ and $y$--$y$ contextual
singleton-classifier exclusions for arbitrary $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$.

Then apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-independent-cross-endpoint-classifier-boundary}.
It supplies the two crossed contextual singleton-classifier exclusions, again
for arbitrary $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$, so the certificate consumes both the
$x$--$y$ and $y$--$x$ classifier boundary rows.

Finally use
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-product-classifier-and-nonzero-exit-package} for the
two-sided product classifier and nonzero equivalences, and
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-five-factor-nonzero-factor-ledger} for the five
nonzero factor witnesses. These clauses share the same concrete source,
carrier, classifier, and ledger vocabulary, so they assemble as one ten-field
exit certificate rather than as separate paper endpoints.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp threefold support singleton-classifier noncollapse]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-threefold-support-singleton-classifier-noncollapse}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and
histories $h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and
$C_D^u(\ell)$, let $x:=\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k),\ell)$. If
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then for all $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$,
$$
\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,x),\mathsf{append}(R,x))
\to\bot.
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-threefold-support-non-singleton-exactness} with the
same concrete denominator tuple, carried histories, and left-nested endpoint.
The threefold support witness gives $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(x)$.

Package this carrier witness on both sides, together with reflexivity of
$\hsame$, as $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x,x)$.

Now fix arbitrary $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-singleton-empty-classifier-append-rat-classifier-absurd}.
It sends any displayed singleton-classifier witness to contradiction.
\end{proof}

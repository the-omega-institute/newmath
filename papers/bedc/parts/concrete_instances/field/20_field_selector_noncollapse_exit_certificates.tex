\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp final certificate two-selector noncollapse boundary]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-two-selector-noncollapse-boundary}
For Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let
$E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $E_t(h,k,\ell)$ be the corresponding bracketing endpoint
selectors of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector}. If
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the same concrete
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit boundary contains every row of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-selector-noncollapse-boundary}
together with the following right-selector rows:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_t(h,k,\ell)))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(E_t(h,k,\ell),r))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_t(h,k,\ell)));
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(E_t(h,k,\ell),r)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-selector-noncollapse-boundary} to
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the denominator tuple,
the histories, the three carrier witnesses, the selectors $s,t$, the contexts
$L,R,r$, and the $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ witness. This fixes the
concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit boundary and supplies every row of the
final selector noncollapse boundary.

Next apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-independence-package}
with the same selector pair $s,t$ and the same context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. The
right endpoint rows of that package give the two singleton-carrier exclusions
and the two $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}$ witnesses for
$E_t(h,k,\ell)$, so the boundary consumes both selected endpoints under the same
concrete certificate.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target final two-selector noncollapse boundary
inside $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\mathsf{BMark}$, $\mathsf{RatUp}$, and
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}; denominator tuple
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$; selected endpoints
$E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $E_t(h,k,\ell)$; carrier rows those of the final selector
boundary plus the right-selector singleton-carrier exclusions; classifier rows
those of the final selector boundary and the selector-independence package;
nonzero rows the left-selector witnesses of the final selector boundary plus
the two right-selector witnesses; stability obligations final selector
noncollapse boundary and bracketing selector independence; ledger selector pair
plus contexts $L,R,r$; exactness predicate one concrete FieldUp exit boundary
with both endpoint contexts; downstream consumer final certificate bracketing
endpoint consumption; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp complete selector exit obstruction certificate]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate}
For Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t,u,v:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let
$E_q(h,k,\ell)$ be the bracketing endpoint selector of
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} for each
$q:\mathsf{BMark}$. If $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the
concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit package of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} contains, as one
assembled certificate, all of the following rows:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item every selector-pair strict row of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-pair-strict-exit-determinacy} for the
  selector comparison $(s,t)$ against $(u,v)$ and contexts $L,R$;
  \item every $(s,t)$ obstruction row of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-complete-obstruction-package},
  with $a=E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $b=E_t(h,k,\ell)$;
  \item every row of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-two-selector-noncollapse-boundary}
  for the selector pair $(s,t)$, including the right-selector rows
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_t(h,k,\ell)))\to\bot,
  $$
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(E_t(h,k,\ell),r))\to\bot,
  $$
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_t(h,k,\ell))),
  $$
  and
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(E_t(h,k,\ell),r)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-pair-strict-exit-determinacy} with the
given denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness,
selectors $s,t,u,v$, and contexts $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$. This supplies the two
componentwise selector comparisons and the strict exit rows for the two selected
pairs.

Next instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-complete-obstruction-package}
with the same denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness,
selector pair $(s,t)$, and contexts $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$. The resulting rows
are exactly the concrete obstruction rows for
$a=E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $b=E_t(h,k,\ell)$.

Now instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-two-selector-noncollapse-boundary}
with the same concrete exit object, denominator tuple, histories, carrier
witnesses, support witness, selector pair $(s,t)$, contexts $L,R$, and right
context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This contributes the final boundary rows and the
four displayed right-selector noncollapse rows for $E_t(h,k,\ell)$.

Finally use \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} only to
identify these rows as rows of the concrete
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}. Conjunction
introduction then repacks the three row families into one concrete
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit certificate.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target complete selector exit obstruction
certificate inside $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\mathsf{BMark}$, $\mathsf{RatUp}$, and
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}; denominator tuple
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$; selected endpoints
$E_s(h,k,\ell)$, $E_t(h,k,\ell)$, $E_u(h,k,\ell)$, and $E_v(h,k,\ell)$;
carrier rows selector strict exit carriers, contextual obstruction carrier
exclusions, and right-selector noncollapse carrier exclusions; classifier rows
componentwise denominator comparisons, $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ rows,
and contextual singleton-classifier rejection; nonzero rows strict endpoint
nonzero witnesses, contextual endpoint witnesses, and final right-selector
witnesses; stability obligations selector-pair strict exit determinacy,
bracketing selector complete obstruction, and final certificate two-selector
noncollapse; ledger selector comparison $(s,t)$ against $(u,v)$ plus contexts
$L,R,r$; exactness predicate one concrete selector exit obstruction certificate;
downstream consumer Phase Algebra Hierarchy $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit criterion;
status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[Field apart-zero tail-factor continuation hsame closure]
\label{thm:field-apart-tail-factor-continuation-hsame-closure}
For histories $w,w',x,x',q,z:\mathsf{BHist}$, if
$\mathsf{FieldApartTailFactor}(w,x)$, $w\hsame w'$, $x\hsame x'$, and
$\ContR(w',q,z)$, then $\mathsf{FieldApartTailFactor}(z,x')$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Transport the carried tail-factor endpoints along the two $\hsame$
comparisons. The continuation witness then extends the generated spine by the
carried-tail constructor, yielding the displayed endpoint and carried factor.
\end{proof}

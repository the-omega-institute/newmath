\begin{theorem}[Rat denominator empty-unit strict-support FieldUp subcertificate]
\label{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-strict-support-field-subcertificate}
For the tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ of
\autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-continuation-monoid-instance} and
the strict support predicate $N_D^u$ of
\autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-strict-product-support}, the
FieldUp denominator side over $\mathsf{BHist}$ carries the following concrete
strict-support subcertificate.
\begin{enumerate}
\item The empty-unit commutative monoid laws hold for
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u)$, including commutativity up to $R_D^u$.

\item For all carried histories $h,k:\mathsf{BHist}$, classifier transport
preserves and reflects strict support:
$$
R_D^u(h,k)\to (N_D^u(h)\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(k)).
$$

\item For all carried histories $h,k:\mathsf{BHist}$, strict support is exact
for the product:
$$
N_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k))\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(h)\lor N_D^u(k).
$$

\item For every carried history $u:\mathsf{BHist}$, if $u$ satisfies both
one-sided unit laws against all carried histories, then
$$
R_D^u(u,e_D^u).
$$

\item For all carried histories $h,k,t:\mathsf{BHist}$, classifier equality is
cancellative for a common carried factor on the right and on the left:
$$
R_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,t),\mu_D^u(k,t))\to R_D^u(h,k),
$$
and
$$
R_D^u(\mu_D^u(t,h),\mu_D^u(t,k))\to R_D^u(h,k).
$$

\item For histories $p,q,p',q',h,k:\mathsf{BHist}$, empty endpoint contexts
preserve and reflect the classifier:
if $p\hsame\emp$, $q\hsame\emp$, $p'\hsame\emp$, and $q'\hsame\emp$, then
$$
R_D^u(\mathsf{append}(p,\mathsf{append}(h,q)),
\mathsf{append}(p',\mathsf{append}(k,q')))
\Longleftrightarrow
R_D^u(h,k).
$$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The first field is exactly the concrete commutative monoid boundary of
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-commutative-monoid-laws}. It
anchors the carrier, classifier, multiplication, and empty unit in the
$\mathsf{BHist}$ tuple rather than in an abstract monoid variable.

For support transport, unfold $R_D^u$ as
$\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier}$ and $N_D^u$ as
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$. A classifier witness supplies the endpoint
$\hsame$ comparison between $h$ and $k$; applying
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier\_hsame\_transport}$ in both directions gives
$N_D^u(h)\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(k)$.

The product support field is supplied by
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-strict-product-support-exactness}.
Its predicate is the $N_D^u$ introduced in
\autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-strict-product-support}, so the
empty endpoint remains carried while the genuine Rat denominator endpoint is the
only strict support source.

The unit field is
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-identity-unique}. It takes an
arbitrary carried two-sided unit witness $u$ and classifies it against the
distinguished endpoint $e_D^u$.

The cancellation field is the two-sided classifier cancellation package of
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-cancellative-commutative-monoid-package}.

The empty-context field is exactly the preservation-and-reflection theorem
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-unit-classifier-empty-context-exactness}.
It uses the same classifier $R_D^u=\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier}$ and the
same append operation as the tuple.

Together these clauses compose the carrier and classifier laws, strict-support
transport, product support exactness, unit uniqueness, cancellation, and
empty-context classifier exactness into the concrete denominator boundary
required by the $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit criterion.
\end{proof}

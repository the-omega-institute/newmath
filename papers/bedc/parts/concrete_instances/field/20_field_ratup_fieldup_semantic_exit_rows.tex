\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp transported nondegenerate exit certificate]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-exit-transported-support-nondegenerate}
Let $d_1:=\Eone(\emp)$. For
Definition~\ref{def:concrete-ratup-fieldup-certificate-instance}, under the
hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-support-neutrality-row}, the same
concrete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit certificate contains the source
nondegeneracy and the transported support-neutrality conclusions:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d_1)$;
  \item $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(d_1)\to\bot$;
  \item the transported nested action carrier conclusion
  $$
  C_D^u(\Gamma_{p',q',s',t'}(\Gamma_{p,q,s,t}(h)))
  $$
  \item the transported strict-support neutrality conclusion
  $$
  N_D^u(\Gamma_{p',q',s',t'}(\Gamma_{p,q,s,t}(h)))
  \Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(h).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First fix the certificate site. Theorem~\ref{thm:ratup-fieldup-semantic-certificate-assembly}
reads Definition~\ref{def:concrete-ratup-fieldup-certificate-instance} as the
concrete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ certificate, and
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-certificate-rows-source-anchored} pins its rows to
the same $\mathsf{BHist}$ source tuple.

The endpoint part is supplied by
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-source-non-singleton-witness}. At
$d_1=\Eone(\emp)$ it gives both
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d_1)$ and
$\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(d_1)\to\bot$ over that source.

It remains to read the transported action row. Under the stated hypotheses,
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-support-neutrality-row} supplies the
carried transported nested action and the displayed strict-support equivalence.
Because the certificate assembly and source-anchoring theorems identify these
rows as entries of the same concrete certificate instance, the four displayed
conclusions coexist in the single exit certificate.
\end{proof}

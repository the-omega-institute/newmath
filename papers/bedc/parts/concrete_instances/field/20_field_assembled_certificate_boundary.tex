\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp denominator action envelope certificate component]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-certificate-component}
For the concrete denominator tuple
$(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},\mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$
and the contextual action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, the
denominator-action component required by
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations} is supplied by the envelope closure
of \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-closure}, the
normalized row exhaustion of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion},
and the context-pair action certificate of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}.
Consequently the assembled $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ boundary may treat the
multiplicative denominator coverage, the contextual boundary, the normalized
row coverage, and the context-pair product and action rows as one concrete
BHist-anchored certificate component.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-closure}. It fixes the
multiplicative coverage and contextual multiplicative boundary over the RatUp
denominator carrier, classifier, append operation, empty endpoint, strict-support
predicate, and contextual action $\Gamma$.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion}.
It accounts for every normalized denominator action row by the same concrete
BHist carrier data, the empty external contexts, and the pair product $\star$.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}. It
supplies the pair classifier, pair product, unit-pair action, product-action
composition, support readback, and iterated action rows for the same
$(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},\mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$
tuple and the same action $\Gamma$.

All three cited results use identical carrier, classifier, append, empty unit,
strict-support predicate, and contextual-action data. Their conclusions therefore
occupy one denominator-action component of
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}, with no transport between separate
FieldUp sources.
\end{proof}

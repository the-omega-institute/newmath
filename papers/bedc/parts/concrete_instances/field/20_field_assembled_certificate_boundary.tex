\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp denominator action envelope certificate component]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-certificate-component}
For the concrete denominator tuple
$(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},\mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$
and the contextual action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, the
denominator-action component required by
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations} is supplied by the envelope closure
of \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-closure}, the
normalized row exhaustion of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion},
and the context-pair action certificate of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}.
Consequently the assembled $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ boundary may treat the
multiplicative denominator coverage, the contextual boundary, the normalized
row coverage, and the context-pair product and action rows as one concrete
BHist-anchored certificate component.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-closure}. It fixes the
multiplicative coverage and contextual multiplicative boundary over the RatUp
denominator carrier, classifier, append operation, empty endpoint, strict-support
predicate, and contextual action $\Gamma$.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion}.
It accounts for every normalized denominator action row by the same concrete
BHist carrier data, the empty external contexts, and the pair product $\star$.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}. It
supplies the pair classifier, pair product, unit-pair action, product-action
composition, support readback, and iterated action rows for the same
$(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},\mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$
tuple and the same action $\Gamma$.

All three cited results use identical carrier, classifier, append, empty unit,
strict-support predicate, and contextual-action data. Their conclusions therefore
occupy one denominator-action component of
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}, with no transport between separate
FieldUp sources.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp assembled certificate boundary]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-assembled-certificate-boundary}
The named source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-source-tuple} satisfies
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}. Its carrier is
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$, its classifier is
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$, its denominator source is the concrete
RatUp denominator tuple, its contextual source is $\Gamma$, its context-pair
source is the monoid of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-monoid}, and its affine
source is $\mathsf{AffAut}_F$. The jointly registered rows are:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the commutative-ring rows inherited from $\CommRingUp$ on the same
  history carrier and classifier;
  \item the inverse and nonzero rows supplied by
  \autoref{thm:field-inverse-classifier-exact-from-apartness},
  \autoref{thm:field-inverse-nonzero-domain-automorphism},
  \autoref{thm:field-inverse-product-classifier-exact-from-apartness}, and
  \autoref{thm:field-product-factor-map-classifier-exact-from-apartness};
  \item the multiplicative denominator rows supplied by
  \autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-certificate-record}
  and \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly};
  \item the denominator-action component consumed by
  \autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}, supplied by
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-certificate-component}
  from envelope closure, normalized row exhaustion, and context-pair action
  certification;
  \item the contextual denominator rows supplied by
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-row-closure-package},
  \autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-normalized-contextual-action-package},
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-laws},
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}, and
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion};
  \item the affine automorphism closure supplied by
  \autoref{thm:field-affine-automorphism-group-certificate}.
\end{enumerate}
Consequently $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ supplies the Phase Algebra
Hierarchy paper-certificate object for the concrete BHist-anchored
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ certificate. The machine-checked phase exit is the
canonical Lean certificate site for that same object.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unpack \autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-source-tuple}. The carrier and
classifier fields are the concrete RatUp history predicates
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$ and $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$, so every row
below is read over the same $\mathsf{BHist}$ source rather than over detached
parameters.

The denominator coordinate is
$(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},
\mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$, the contextual-action
coordinate is $\Gamma$, and the context-pair coordinate is the carried pair
monoid of \autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-monoid}. The
affine coordinate is $\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ with rows read from
\autoref{thm:field-affine-automorphism-group-certificate}.

The algebraic spine is the inherited $\CommRingUp$ structure together with the
inverse block. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-inverse-classifier-exact-from-apartness},
\autoref{thm:field-inverse-nonzero-domain-automorphism},
\autoref{thm:field-inverse-product-classifier-exact-from-apartness}, and
\autoref{thm:field-product-factor-map-classifier-exact-from-apartness}. These
rows give classifier exactness for inverse formation, nonzero transport through
the inverse domain, product inverse exactness, and certified multiplication by
nonzero factors.

The denominator subcertificate is then read from
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-certificate-record}
and \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly}. The first
theorem records the multiplicative denominator rows, and the second identifies
them as the denominator component consumed by the FieldUp certificate
obligations.

Now apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-action-envelope-certificate-component}.
It combines the envelope closure, normalized row exhaustion, and context-pair
action certificate into the single denominator-action component required by
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}.

The contextual extension first uses
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-row-closure-package} and
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-normalized-contextual-action-package}. These
rows add one-step and iterated contextual actions, normalized action transport,
classifier exactness, strict-support readback, and bilateral cancellation for
the same denominator carrier.

Next apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-laws},
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-action-certificate}, and
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-context-pair-normalized-row-exhaustion}.
The pair-action block supplies pair composition, unit action, componentwise
product laws, certified contextual action rows, and normalized row coverage
across the concrete BHist context pairs.

Finally, apply \autoref{thm:field-affine-automorphism-group-certificate}. Its
identity, composition, inverse, associativity, classifier-congruence, and
witness-choice descent rows close the affine automorphism part of the FieldUp
interface. Combining these disjoint rows gives exactly the carrier,
classifier, stability, exactness, and ledger obligations listed in
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}.
\end{proof}

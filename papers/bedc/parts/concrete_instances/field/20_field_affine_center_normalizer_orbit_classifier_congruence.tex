\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp affine centralizer normalizer orbit classifier congruence]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-orbit-classifier-congruence}
For Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, fix
$A:\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ in the concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit object. Let
$C_F(A)$ be the affine centralizer carrier, let $N_F(C_F(A))$ be its normalizer
carrier, define $\mathrm{Act}_T(X)$ as in
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-action}, and let
$\sim_{N_F(A)}$ be the reachability relation of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-orbit}. If
$C_F(A)(X)$, $C_F(A)(X')$, $C_F(A)(Y)$, $C_F(A)(Y')$,
$X\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}X'$, $Y\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'$, and
$X\sim_{N_F(A)}Y$, then
$$
X'\sim_{N_F(A)}Y'.
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Choose the same normalizer witness from $X\sim_{N_F(A)}Y$. Thus there is
$T:\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ with $N_F(C_F(A))(T)$ and
$\mathrm{Act}_T(X)\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-action-classifier-congruence}
with the acting coordinate $T$ classifier-equal to itself and with the acted
coordinate transported from $X'$ to $X$ by classifier symmetry. This gives
$$
\mathrm{Act}_T(X')\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}\mathrm{Act}_T(X).
$$

Compose this comparison with the witness endpoint
$\mathrm{Act}_T(X)\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y$, and then with the supplied endpoint
comparison $Y\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'$. Affine classifier transitivity yields
$$
\mathrm{Act}_T(X')\equiv_{\mathrm{aff}}Y'.
$$

The same $T$ still lies in $N_F(C_F(A))$, so the displayed endpoint comparison
is precisely the data required by
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-affine-centralizer-normalizer-orbit} to witness
$X'\sim_{N_F(A)}Y'$.
\end{proof}

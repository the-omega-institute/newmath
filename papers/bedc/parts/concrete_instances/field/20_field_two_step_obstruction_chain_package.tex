\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp concrete two-step obstruction chain package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-two-step-obstruction-chain-package}
For $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$,
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let $E_q(h,k,\ell)$ be the bracketing endpoint selector
of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} for each
$q:\mathsf{BMark}$. If $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then the same
concrete $\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$ contains the following
three row families:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the full assembled obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(s,t,u,v)$;
  \item the full assembled obstruction certificate of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-complete-selector-exit-obstruction-certificate} for
  $(u,v,w,z)$;
  \item the selector-chain final boundary rows of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} for $(s,t)$,
  $(u,v)$, and $(w,z)$, including the chained denominator rows
  $$
  R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  R_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell))
  $$
  and the chained endpoint classifier rows
  $$
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_w(h,k,\ell))
  \land
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_z(h,k,\ell)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First instantiate
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-final-obstruction-boundary-package} with
the displayed source, concrete exit object, denominator tuple, histories,
carrier witnesses, support witness, selectors $s,t,u,v$, and contexts
$L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This supplies the assembled obstruction certificate for
the adjacent selector replacement from $(s,t)$ to $(u,v)$.

Next instantiate the same theorem with the same source, concrete exit object,
denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness, selectors
$u,v,w,z$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This supplies the assembled
obstruction certificate for the adjacent selector replacement from $(u,v)$ to
$(w,z)$.

Then instantiate \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-chain-final-boundary} with
the six selectors $s,t,u,v,w,z$ over the same denominator tuple, histories,
carrier witnesses, support witness, and contexts. This contributes the three
selector-pair boundary rows together with the chained $R_D^u$ rows and chained
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ rows from the first endpoint pair to the final
endpoint pair.

Finally cite \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object-complete} only to
keep both adjacent obstruction certificates and the chained final boundary rows
inside the same concrete $\mathsf{RatUpFieldExitObject}_F(\mathcal E_F)$.
Conjunction introduction packages the two adjacent obstruction certificates and
the selector-chain final boundary as one concrete
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit-object certificate.
\end{proof}

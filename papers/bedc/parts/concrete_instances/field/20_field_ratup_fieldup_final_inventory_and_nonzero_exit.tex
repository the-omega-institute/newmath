\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp Rat-classified factors exclude empty product]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-rat-classified-factors-exclude-empty-product}
For a BEDC history-level $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ candidate satisfying the hypotheses
of \autoref{thm:field-nonzero-factors-exclude-zero-product}, specialize the
nonzero index to
$$
\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(h):=(h\hsame0\to\bot).
$$
Assume the RatUp concrete exit object identifies the field-zero endpoint with
the empty history, and includes classifier transport for
$\mathsf{ApartZero}_F$ together with contradiction of
$\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(0)$. Then for all histories
$a,a',b,b':\mathsf{BHist}$,
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(a,a')
\land
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(b,b')
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
(\mathsf{mul}(a,b)\hsame0\to\bot)
\land
(\mathsf{mul}(a',b')\hsame0\to\bot).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unpack the two classifier witnesses. Apply
\autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-endpoints-not-empty} to
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(a,a')$ and to
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(b,b')$. The four endpoint exclusions, transported
across the concrete identification of the field-zero endpoint with $\emp$,
give witnesses
$p_a:\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(a)$, $p_{a'}:\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(a')$,
$p_b:\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(b)$, and $p_{b'}:\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(b')$.

The stability field for $\mathsf{ApartZero}_F$ is transport along $\hsame$:
if $h\hsame k$ and $h\hsame0\to\bot$, then any $k\hsame0$ composes back to
$h\hsame0$ and gives contradiction. The zero contradiction clause is immediate
because $\mathsf{ApartZero}_F(0)$ applied to reflexivity of $0\hsame0$ yields
$\bot$.

Instantiate \autoref{thm:field-nonzero-factors-exclude-zero-product} first at
$(a,b)$ with witnesses $p_a$ and $p_b$, obtaining
$\mathsf{mul}(a,b)\hsame0\to\bot$. Instantiate the same theorem at
$(a',b')$ with witnesses $p_{a'}$ and $p_{b'}$, obtaining
$\mathsf{mul}(a',b')\hsame0\to\bot$. Conjunction introduction packages the two
Rat-classified product exclusions.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target Rat-classified nonzero product in
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$, and the FieldUp apart-zero certificate;
carrier RatHistoryClassifier endpoints; classifier $\hsame$; stability Rat
endpoint nonemptiness plus FieldUp zero-product exclusion; ledger the two
classifier witnesses; exactness predicate no empty product for classified Rat
endpoints; downstream consumer Phase Algebra Hierarchy $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit
certificate; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[Rat denominator carried continuation unit obstruction]
\label{thm:rat-denominator-carried-continuation-unit-obstruction}
For every history $u:\mathsf{BHist}$, a carried Rat denominator cannot itself be
the continuation left unit for all carried denominators. Concretely,
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(u)
\to
\left(
\forall d,r:\mathsf{BHist}.\;
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)\to
\Cont(u,d,r)\to
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(r,d)
\right)
\to
\mathsf{False}.
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume a carrier witness $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(u)$ and the displayed
left-unit classifier law. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-continuation-left-unit-endpoint-iff-empty}
to the law; the forward implication yields $u\hsame\emp$.

Now apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-carrier-not-empty} to the same carrier
witness and the endpoint comparison $u\hsame\emp$. This contradicts the
nonempty Rat denominator condition, giving $\mathsf{False}$.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target carried-unit obstruction for the
Rat-denominator side of $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\Cont$, and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$; carrier
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$; classifier $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$;
stability obligation left-unit endpoint exactness; ledger external unit versus
carried denominator; core theorem
\autoref{thm:rat-denominator-carried-continuation-unit-obstruction}; exactness
predicate no carried denominator is the continuation unit; downstream consumer
the concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit certificate; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(a,a')
\land
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(b,b'),
$$

  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-concrete-operation-table-laws};
  \item the final row inventory of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-row-inventory-coverage};
  \item the two Rat-classified product nonzero exclusions
  $(\mathsf{mul}(a,b)\hsame0\to\bot)$ and
  $(\mathsf{mul}(a',b')\hsame0\to\bot)$ from
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-rat-classified-factors-exclude-empty-product};
  \item the obstruction saying that $q$ cannot be the continuation left unit for

  \autoref{thm:rat-denominator-carried-continuation-unit-obstruction}.

\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-rat-classified-factors-exclude-empty-product} to the

$(\mathsf{mul}(a,b)\hsame0\to\bot)$ and
$(\mathsf{mul}(a',b')\hsame0\to\bot)$ as concrete endpoint rows for the same
history-level $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ package.

carried denominators.

All rows are sourced from $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, the operation table of
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-operation-table}, and the exit object
of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}. Conjunction

\begin{theorem}[Field inverse empty-apartness hsame transport]
\label{thm:field-inverse-apart-empty-hsame-transport}
For a BEDC history-level $\FieldUp$ candidate over $\mathsf{BHist}$ with
multiplication $\mathsf{mul}$, unit $1$, proof-indexed inverse
$\mathsf{inv}(a,p)$, multiplicative associativity, left and right unit laws,
multiplication congruence under $\hsame$, and left and right inverse laws, if
$a\hsame b$, then apartness of the indexed inverse at $a$ from $\emp$
transports to the indexed inverse at $b$:
$$
(\mathsf{inv}(a,p_a)\hsame\emp\to\bot)
\Longrightarrow
(\mathsf{inv}(b,p_b)\hsame\emp\to\bot).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply inverse congruence from apartness transport to $a\hsame b$, obtaining
$\mathsf{inv}(a,p_a)\hsame\mathsf{inv}(b,p_b)$. Any collapse
$\mathsf{inv}(b,p_b)\hsame\emp$ composes with that equality, producing a
collapse of $\mathsf{inv}(a,p_a)$ to $\emp$, contradicting the supplied
apartness predicate.
\end{proof}

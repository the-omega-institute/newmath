\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp transported exit row full noncollapse package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-full-noncollapse-package}
For the concrete source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, the denominator
tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, and the contextual action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, let
$x:=\Gamma_{p',q',s',t'}(\Gamma_{p,q,s,t}(h))$. Under the hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-support-neutrality-row}, suppose also
that $N_D^u(h)$ holds. Then the transported strict-support endpoint $x$
carries one package containing:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the exit-row closure of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-nondegenerate-closure};
  \item $C_D^u(x)$, $N_D^u(x)$, $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(x)$,
  $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(x)\to\bot$, and $x\hsame\emp\to\bot$;
  \item for every $r:\mathsf{BHist}$, both contextual carrier exclusions on
  $\mathsf{append}(r,x)$ and $\mathsf{append}(x,r)$;
  \item for every $r:\mathsf{BHist}$, both inverse-domain witnesses
  $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,x))$ and
  $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(x,r))$;
  \item for all $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$, the singleton-classifier obstruction on
  $\mathsf{append}(L,x)$ and $\mathsf{append}(R,x)$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-nondegenerate-closure} with the
same source tuple, denominator tuple, contextual action, endpoint, and witness.
This supplies the exit-row closure clause.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-strict-support-separation-package} at
the same endpoint. The resulting separation package gives $C_D^u(x)$,
$N_D^u(x)$, $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(x)$, the singleton carrier rejection,
and the direct empty-history separation.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-strict-support-contextual-singleton-exclusion}
for an arbitrary context history $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. Its two conclusions are
the carrier exclusions for $\mathsf{append}(r,x)$ and $\mathsf{append}(x,r)$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-strict-support-contextual-nonzero-package}
with the same endpoint and support witness. This gives the two contextual
inverse-domain witnesses for every $r:\mathsf{BHist}$.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-singleton-classifier-noncollapse}.
For arbitrary $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$ it supplies the classifier obstruction on the
two endpoints. Collecting these clauses gives the full noncollapse package.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp denominator complete contextual row package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-complete-contextual-row-package}
The concrete denominator row
$(C_D^\dagger,R_D^\dagger,\mu_D^\dagger,e_D^\dagger,N_D^\dagger)$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row} and the contextual
action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance} jointly
supply the $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ denominator package consumed by the
FieldUp certificate. The package fields are the concrete BHist data
$$
C_D^\dagger=C_D^u=\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\qquad
R_D^\dagger=R_D^u=\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},
$$
$$
\mu_D^\dagger=\mu_D^u=\mathsf{append},\qquad
e_D^\dagger=e_D^u=\emp,\qquad
N_D^\dagger=N_D^u=\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}.
$$
Its row component satisfies all denominator row laws of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row-laws}; its contextual
component satisfies all bilateral contextual classifier and strict-support laws
of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-certificate}; and the
combined carrier, classifier, append, unit, and strict-support data are the same
concrete data used by the assembled denominator subcertificate of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The two displayed definitions give the field identifications directly. Both
rows use $\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier}$ as carrier,
$\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier}$ as classifier, native BHist append as the
binary operation, $\emp$ as the unit, and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$ as the
strict-support predicate.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row-laws} to the row
$(C_D^\dagger,R_D^\dagger,\mu_D^\dagger,e_D^\dagger,N_D^\dagger)$. This
supplies the envelope, closure, unit, append, congruence, support, product,
cancellation, contextual fixed-factor, external-unit, and absent carried-unit
clauses for the denominator row.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-certificate} to the
same concrete carrier, classifier, append, unit, and support fields, now with
the bilateral action $\Gamma$. This supplies the contextual classifier law and
the two contextual strict-support laws for carried left and right denominator
contexts under empty external endpoints.

Finally,
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly} is the denominator
subcertificate consumed by $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$. Since its carrier,
classifier, multiplication, unit, and strict-support entries are the displayed
BHist data above, the row laws and contextual action laws assemble into the
announced concrete denominator package.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp denominator package closure]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-package-closure}
For the concrete denominator row
$(C_D^\dagger,R_D^\dagger,\mu_D^\dagger,e_D^\dagger,N_D^\dagger)$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row} and the contextual
action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, every
denominator-side row required by \autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations} at
the $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ boundary is covered by the row laws of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row-laws}, the contextual
action certificate of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-certificate}, the
obligation map of \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-obligation-map}, and
the row exhaustion theorem
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-row-exhaustion}. The concrete carrier,
classifier, multiplication, unit, and strict-support data are exactly the data
assembled in \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unfold the concrete denominator row and the contextual action instance. The
definitions identify the row fields as
$\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier}$, $\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier}$,
$\mathsf{append}$, $\emp$, and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$, and they identify
the action as the bilateral context action $\Gamma$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-complete-contextual-row-package}. This
packages the row component and the contextual component over the same concrete
BHist data, with the row laws coming from
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-row-laws} and the action laws
coming from
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-certificate}.

Next apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-obligation-map}. It aligns
those concrete row and contextual fields with the denominator rows named by
\autoref{def:field-certificate-obligations}, so the package is read as the
denominator-side interface of the FieldUp certificate rather than as a detached
list of laws.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-row-exhaustion}. It states
that no denominator-side row used by the assembled
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ certificate lies outside the multiplicative and
contextual sources already named above. Together with
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly}, this proves that
the concrete denominator-side FieldUp package is closed under exactly the listed
components.
\end{proof}

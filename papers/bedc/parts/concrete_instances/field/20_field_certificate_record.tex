\begin{definition}[$\FieldUp$ certificate obligations]
\label{def:field-certificate-obligations}
The carrier, source, pattern, classifier, stability, and ledger data listed above are the obligations required for the interface name $\FieldUp$.
\end{definition}

\paragraph{Per-object certificate record.}
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Traditional target: constructive field.
  \item BEDC source: $\RatUp$ plus $\CommRingUp$ data.
  \item Carrier choice: the $\RatUp$ carrier with $\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a)$ derived from apartness from $0$.
  \item Classifier choice: the rational classifier plus $\mathsf{NonZero}_F$ transport.
  \item Stability obligations: commutative-ring laws, contradiction of $\mathsf{NonZero}_F(0)$, nonzero transport, inverse closure, and inverse laws indexed by nonzero witnesses.
  \item Ledger: ring expression tree plus the $\mathsf{NonZero}_F$ witness for each inverse.
  \item Core theorem list: inverse closure, left inverse, right inverse, nonzero stability, inverse congruence from apartness transport, inverse involution under explicit nonzero evidence, right-multiplication equation solving, right-multiplication equation exactness, left-multiplication equation solving, left-multiplication equation exactness, middle multiplication equation solving, middle multiplication equation exactness, two-sided multiplication exactness, two-sided zero-product exactness, zero-product exclusion from two nonzero witnesses, signed-factor apartness exclusivity, affine left equation exactness, affine right equation exactness, two-sided affine equation exactness, one-sided affine map classifier exactness, two-sided affine map classifier exactness, affine automorphism package, product-witness independence, denominator row laws, denominator contextual action certificate, \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-complete-contextual-row-package}, \autoref{thm:field-affine-automorphism-group-certificate}, and distributivity inherited from $\CommRingUp$.
  \item Exactness predicate: coverage, soundness, completeness, exact zero classification, denominator row exactness, contextual denominator action exactness, nonzero-product contradiction inside certified two-sided multiplication for admitted field packages, and the concrete affine automorphism $\GroupUp$ certificate.
  \item Standard bridge: deferred conservative bridge.
  \item Status: ProofTarget.
\end{enumerate}

\paragraph{Primitive boundary.}
$\FieldUp$ is not a BEDC primitive. It is admitted only when those obligations are supplied.

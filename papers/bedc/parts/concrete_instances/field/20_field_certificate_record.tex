\begin{definition}[Field ledger policy]
\label{def:field-ledger-policy}
Record the ring ledger and the $\mathsf{NonZero}_F$ witness used for each inverse.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[$\FieldUp$ certificate obligations]
\label{def:field-certificate-obligations}
For the concrete target $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$, a certificate must provide the
following rows as a single obligation record.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item Source and carrier: the rational-history source on
  $\mathsf{BHist}$ with carrier $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$.
  \item Classifier and pattern: $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ and the
  continuation pattern $\Cont$ over $\mathsf{BHist}$.
  \item Ring base: the $\CommRingUp$ additive, multiplicative,
  commutativity, distributivity, operation-congruence, and ring-ledger rows.
  \item Apartness and inverse data: $\mathsf{NonZero}_F$ apartness from zero,
  transport of nonzero evidence, inverse closure, inverse classifier
  exactness, and left and right inverse laws indexed by nonzero witnesses.
  \item Product data: the two-sided zero-product rows and the certified
  nonzero-product contradiction rows.
  \item Denominator tuple:
  $(\mathsf{RatDenomUnitCarrier},\mathsf{RatDenomUnitClassifier},
  \mathsf{append},\emp,\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier})$ with its multiplicative,
  unit, closure, classifier, and ledger rows.
  \item Contextual denominator data: the action $\Gamma$, its envelope and
  normalized-support rows, and the context-pair monoid rows for pair product,
  unit-pair action, composition, and strict-support readback.
  \item Affine source: $\mathsf{AffAut}_F$ with the packaged affine
  $\GroupUp$ rows used by the field certificate.
  \item Ledger and exactness data: ring expression ledger rows, inverse
  witness ledger rows, denominator ledger rows, contextual-pair ledger rows,
  affine coefficient ledger rows, coverage, soundness, completeness, exact
  zero classification, denominator exactness, contextual-action exactness,
  context-pair exactness, and affine classifier exactness.
  \item Status: the record status field, instantiated here as
  $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}

\paragraph{Primitive boundary.}
$\FieldUp$ is not a BEDC primitive. It is admitted only when those obligations are supplied.

\begin{remark}[Zero-axiom encoding note]
\label{rem:field-zero-axiom-note}
Explicit apartness witnesses support inverse formation without LEM-based
$a\ne0$ reasoning.
\end{remark}

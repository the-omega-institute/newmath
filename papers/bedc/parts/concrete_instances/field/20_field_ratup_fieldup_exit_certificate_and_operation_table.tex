\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp five-factor nonzero factor ledger]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-five-factor-nonzero-factor-ledger}
For a BEDC history-level $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ candidate over
$\mathsf{BHist}$ satisfying the hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:field-four-factor-product-nonzero-iff-factors} and
\autoref{thm:field-product-nonzero-of-nonzero-factors}, together with the
zero-factor exclusion hypotheses used by
\autoref{thm:field-product-nonzero-excludes-zero-factors}, assume the
apartness-to-nonzero bridge
$$
\alpha_h:\bigl(h\hsame0\to\bot\bigr)\to\mathsf{NonZero}_F(h)
$$
for each history term occurring below. Then every
$a,b,c,d,e:\mathsf{BHist}$ satisfies
$$
\mathsf{NonZero}_F(
\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d),e))
\Longleftrightarrow
\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a)\wedge\mathsf{NonZero}_F(b)\wedge
\mathsf{NonZero}_F(c)\wedge\mathsf{NonZero}_F(d)\wedge
\mathsf{NonZero}_F(e).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the forward direction, assume
$p_{abcde}:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(
\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d),e))$.
Apply \autoref{thm:field-product-nonzero-excludes-zero-factors} to the outer
binary product with left factor
$\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d)$ and right factor $e$. This
gives apartness of the fourfold prefix from $0$ and apartness of $e$ from $0$.

Convert those two apartness witnesses through $\alpha_h$. The left conversion
gives
$p_{abcd}:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d))$,
and the right conversion gives $\mathsf{NonZero}_F(e)$.

Apply \autoref{thm:field-four-factor-product-nonzero-iff-factors} to
$p_{abcd}$. It supplies the four factor witnesses for $a$, $b$, $c$, and $d$.
Pair these four witnesses with the converted witness for $e$ in the displayed
order.

For the reverse direction, assume witnesses
$p_a:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a)$, $p_b:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(b)$,
$p_c:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(c)$, $p_d:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(d)$, and
$p_e:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(e)$. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-four-factor-product-nonzero-iff-factors} in the reverse
direction to obtain
$p_{abcd}:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(\mathsf{mul}(a,b),c),d))$.

Apply \autoref{thm:field-product-nonzero-of-nonzero-factors} to $p_{abcd}$ and
$p_e$. The resulting witness is exactly the nonzero certificate for the
left-nested fivefold product, completing the equivalence.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target RatUp FieldUp five-factor nonzero
factor ledger; BEDC source $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ over $\mathsf{BHist}$
with product nonzero exclusion and product nonzero closure; carrier
$\mathsf{BHist}$; classifier $\hsame$ with $\mathsf{NonZero}_F$; stability
obligations four-factor exactness, binary product closure, zero-factor
exclusion, and apartness-to-nonzero transport; ledger factor witnesses
$p_a,p_b,p_c,p_d,p_e$, prefix witness $p_{abcd}$, and fivefold product witness
$p_{abcde}$; core theorem
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-five-factor-nonzero-factor-ledger}; exactness
predicate nonzero of the left-nested fivefold product equivalent to the five
factor nonzero witnesses; standard bridge deferred; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

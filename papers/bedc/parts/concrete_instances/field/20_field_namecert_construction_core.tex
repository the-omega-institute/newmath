\begin{theorem}[Rat denominator unit contextual action strict-support endpoint empty contradiction]
\label{thm:rat-denom-unit-contextual-action-strict-support-endpoint-empty-absurd}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and the
contextual action $\Gamma$, assume the four external endpoints are empty and
the five denominator contexts $h,l,r,l',r'$ are carried. If the core has strict
RatUp support, then the nested contextual action cannot be empty:
$$
N_D^u(h)\to
\Gamma_{p',q',l',r'}(\Gamma_{p,q,l,r}(h))\hsame\emp\to\bot.
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The contextual composition support law reads the nested support predicate as
the disjunction of the two outer supports, the two inner supports, and the core
support. Insert $N_D^u(h)$ into the core disjunct. This yields RatUp support for
the nested action, contradicting its asserted empty endpoint.
\end{proof}

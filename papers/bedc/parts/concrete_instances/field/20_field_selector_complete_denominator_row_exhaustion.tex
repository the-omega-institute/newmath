\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp selector-complete denominator row exhaustion]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-row-exhaustion}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and
histories $h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and
$C_D^u(\ell)$, let $E_s(h,k,\ell)$ be the endpoint selector of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector}. Fix contexts
$L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$ and assume $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$. Set
$x:=\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k),\ell)$ and
$y:=\mu_D^u(h,\mu_D^u(k,\ell))$. Then the selected denominator row of
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-instance} is
exhausted by exactly the following four ordered endpoint rows:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $(x,x)$, with carrier rows $C_D^u(x)$ and $C_D^u(x)$, classifier row
  $R_D^u(x,x)$, and obstruction
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L,x),\mathsf{append}(R,x))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item $(x,y)$, with carrier rows $C_D^u(x)$ and $C_D^u(y)$, classifier row
  $R_D^u(x,y)$, and obstruction
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L,x),\mathsf{append}(R,y))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item $(y,x)$, with carrier rows $C_D^u(y)$ and $C_D^u(x)$, classifier row
  $R_D^u(y,x)$, and obstruction
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L,y),\mathsf{append}(R,x))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item $(y,y)$, with carrier rows $C_D^u(y)$ and $C_D^u(y)$, classifier row
  $R_D^u(y,y)$, and obstruction
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L,y),\mathsf{append}(R,y))\to\bot.
  $$
\end{enumerate}
For every selector pair $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$, the row selected by
$(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$ is one of these four rows, and its two carrier
rows, $R_D^u$ classifier row, and singleton-empty contextual obstruction are
the corresponding rows supplied by the selector-complete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$
exit package. Conversely, the four displayed ordered endpoint rows are realized
by the selector pairs $(\mathsf{BMark.b0},\mathsf{BMark.b0})$,
$(\mathsf{BMark.b0},\mathsf{BMark.b1})$,
$(\mathsf{BMark.b1},\mathsf{BMark.b0})$, and
$(\mathsf{BMark.b1},\mathsf{BMark.b1})$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply Lemma~\ref{lem:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector-exhaustion} to
the arbitrary selectors $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$. It gives the four generated
selector branches and identifies their selected endpoint pairs as $(x,x)$,
$(x,y)$, $(y,x)$, and $(y,y)$.

Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-well-formed-exit-slice}
to the concrete denominator tuple, the three carrier hypotheses, the support
witness $\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$, the selected pair $s,t$, and the
contexts $L,R$. This supplies the two carrier rows, the $R_D^u$ classifier row,
and the singleton-empty contextual obstruction for the selected denominator
row.

Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-exit-package} to the
same data. The classifier row and the contextual obstruction are recorded as
concrete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit-package rows over
$\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, so each of the four cases is an admissible row
of the concrete exit package.

It remains to account for the converse realization. Specialize
Lemma~\ref{lem:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector-exhaustion} to the
four selector pairs
$(\mathsf{BMark.b0},\mathsf{BMark.b0})$,
$(\mathsf{BMark.b0},\mathsf{BMark.b1})$,
$(\mathsf{BMark.b1},\mathsf{BMark.b0})$, and
$(\mathsf{BMark.b1},\mathsf{BMark.b1})$. Unfolding
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} gives respectively
the endpoint rows $(x,x)$, $(x,y)$, $(y,x)$, and $(y,y)$, and the preceding
well-formedness and exit-package applications attach the corresponding four
rows displayed in the statement.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp selector-complete row stability]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-row-stability}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and two
denominator triples $h,k,\ell,h',k',\ell':\mathsf{BHist}$, assume
$C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, $C_D^u(\ell)$, $C_D^u(h')$, $C_D^u(k')$, and
$C_D^u(\ell')$. Let $E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $E_s(h',k',\ell')$ be the endpoint
selector of \autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector} on the
two triples. If
$$
\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)
\quad\hbox{and}\quad
\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h',k',\ell')
$$
hold, and if
$$
R_D^u(h,h'),\quad R_D^u(k,k'),\quad R_D^u(\ell,\ell')
$$
hold, then for every selector pair $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$ and all contexts
$L,R,L',R':\mathsf{BHist}$ with $\hsame(L,L')$ and $\hsame(R,R')$, the
selected endpoints are denominator-classifier stable:
$$
R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_s(h',k',\ell'))
\quad\hbox{and}\quad
R_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell),E_t(h',k',\ell')).
$$
The primed selected row also supplies the same three-field row package as
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-well-formed-exit-slice}:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $C_D^u(E_s(h',k',\ell'))$ and $C_D^u(E_t(h',k',\ell'))$;
  \item $R_D^u(E_s(h',k',\ell'),E_t(h',k',\ell'))$;
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L',E_s(h',k',\ell')),
   \mathsf{append}(R',E_t(h',k',\ell')))\to\bot.
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Use the generated case split on $s:\mathsf{BMark}$. In the
$\mathsf{BMark.b0}$ branch, the selected endpoint is the left-nested
denominator $\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k),\ell)$, and the primed endpoint is
$\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h',k'),\ell')$. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-continuation-binary-classifier-congruence}
first to the pair $h,h'$ and $k,k'$, and then to the resulting left-nested
pair and $\ell,\ell'$. This gives the displayed classifier relation for the
$s$-selected endpoints.

In the $\mathsf{BMark.b1}$ branch, the selected endpoint is the right-nested
denominator $\mu_D^u(h,\mu_D^u(k,\ell))$, and the primed endpoint is
$\mu_D^u(h',\mu_D^u(k',\ell'))$. Apply the same concrete binary classifier
congruence first to $k,k'$ and $\ell,\ell'$, and then to $h,h'$ and the
resulting right-nested pair. This gives the second generated endpoint
comparison. Repeating the same two-branch argument for $t$ gives the
$t$-selected endpoint comparison.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-well-formed-exit-slice}
to the primed triple, its three carrier hypotheses, the support witness
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h',k',\ell')$, the selectors $s,t$, and the contexts
$L',R'$. It supplies the primed selected endpoint carrier rows, the primed
selected denominator-classifier row, and the displayed contextual
singleton-classifier noncollapse. The assumptions $\hsame(L,L')$ and
$\hsame(R,R')$ identify these contextual row slots with the same left and right
context classes as the unprimed selected row.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp selector-complete hierarchy exit witness]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-hierarchy-exit-witness}
For the concrete source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ and the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, let
$h,k,\ell,h',k',\ell':\mathsf{BHist}$ carry the six denominator carrier
hypotheses
$C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, $C_D^u(\ell)$, $C_D^u(h')$, $C_D^u(k')$, and
$C_D^u(\ell')$. Let $E_s(h,k,\ell)$ be the endpoint selector of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector}, and set
$x:=\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(h,k),\ell)$ and
$y:=\mu_D^u(h,\mu_D^u(k,\ell))$. If
$$
\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)
\quad\hbox{and}\quad
\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h',k',\ell')
$$
hold, and if
$$
R_D^u(h,h'),\quad R_D^u(k,k'),\quad R_D^u(\ell,\ell')
$$
hold, then the concrete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ paper certificate contains
the selector-complete denominator row family as a stable concrete component:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the algebra-hierarchy exit package over $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$;
  \item the selector-complete exit package for every $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$ and
  $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$;
  \item the strict selector exit package for the same selector and context data;
  \item the four ordered endpoint rows $(x,x)$, $(x,y)$, $(y,x)$, and $(y,y)$,
  each with its two $C_D^u$ carrier rows, its $R_D^u$ classifier row, and its
  singleton-empty contextual obstruction;
  \item the row-stability transport for every $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$ and
  $L,R,L',R':\mathsf{BHist}$ with $\hsame(L,L')$ and $\hsame(R,R')$.
\end{enumerate}
Thus the concrete hierarchy exit witness records both the selector-complete row
coverage and its transport under $R_D^u$-related denominator triples and
$\hsame$-related contexts.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-algebra-hierarchy-exit-witness}. This
fixes the ambient $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ algebra-hierarchy package over
$\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ and supplies the paper-side certificate into which
the denominator rows are placed.

Next apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-exit-package} and
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-strict-exit-package} to the same
concrete denominator tuple, carrier hypotheses, support witness, selectors, and
contexts. These two sites supply the selector-indexed carrier rows, denominator
classifier row, contextual singleton-empty obstruction, and strict endpoint
witnesses.

Then apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-denominator-row-exhaustion}. It
identifies the selector-indexed rows with the four ordered endpoint rows
$(x,x)$, $(x,y)$, $(y,x)$, and $(y,y)$, and records the converse realization by
the four selector pairs over $\mathsf{BMark}$.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-complete-row-stability} to the
two denominator triples, the $R_D^u$ comparisons, and the
$\hsame$-related contexts. It transports selected endpoints and the primed row
package through the same concrete denominator classifier, so the selector rows
remain stable inside the hierarchy exit certificate.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp normalized contextual separation package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-normalized-contextual-separation-package}
For the concrete source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, and the contextual
action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, suppose
$C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, $C_D^u(l)$, $C_D^u(r)$, $C_D^u(l')$, and
$C_D^u(r')$. Suppose also that $p\hsame\emp$, $q\hsame\emp$, $p'\hsame\emp$,
$q'\hsame\emp$, $\neg N_D^u(l)$, $\neg N_D^u(r)$, $\neg N_D^u(l')$,
$\neg N_D^u(r')$, and $N_D^u(h)$. Let
$x:=\Gamma_{p',q',l',r'}(\Gamma_{p,q,l,r}(h))$ and let
$y:=\Gamma_{p',q',\mu_D^u(l',l),\mu_D^u(r,r')}(h)$. Then the concrete
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit certificate simultaneously contains:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the assembled certificate boundary of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-assembled-certificate-boundary};
  \item the carried nested endpoint $C_D^u(x)$;
  \item the normalized classifier row $R_D^u(x,y)$;
  \item the strict-support equivalence $N_D^u(x)\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(h)$;
  \item the exit-row closure recorded by
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-nondegenerate-closure};
  \item the direct endpoint separation $x\hsame\emp\to\bot$;
  \item for every context history $r:\mathsf{BHist}$, both singleton-context
  rejection maps
  $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,x))\to\bot$ and
  $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(x,r))\to\bot$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-assembled-certificate-boundary}. This
fixes the single concrete source tuple $\mathsf{RatUpFieldSource}_F$ and the
certificate boundary in which the carrier, classifier, denominator source,
contextual source, context-pair source, and affine source are jointly registered.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-total-normalization-support-collapse-row}
to the displayed carried endpoints, empty numerator endpoints, and
empty-support denominator contexts. Its nested row gives $C_D^u(x)$, its
collected row gives $R_D^u(x,y)$, and its support row gives
$N_D^u(x)\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(h)$.

The assumed witness $N_D^u(h)$ and the displayed equivalence recover the
strict-support hypothesis at the transported endpoint $x$. Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-exit-row-contextual-separation-package}
with that endpoint and the same contextual action. It supplies the
nondegenerate exit-row closure, the direct empty separation
$x\hsame\emp\to\bot$, and the two singleton-context rejection maps for each
$r:\mathsf{BHist}$.

Collecting the boundary, normalized row, support transport, and contextual
separation clauses gives the stated concrete normalized
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit certificate.
\end{proof}

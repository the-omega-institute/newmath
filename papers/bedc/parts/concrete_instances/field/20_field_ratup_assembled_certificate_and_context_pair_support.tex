\begin{theorem}[Rat denominator context-pair product strict support commutation]
\label{thm:field-rat-denominator-context-pair-product-strict-support-commutes}
For carried denominator context pairs $(l,r)$ and $(l',r')$, strict support is
unchanged by swapping the two pair factors:
$$
N_D^u(\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(l',l),\mu_D^u(r,r')))
\Longleftrightarrow
N_D^u(\mu_D^u(\mu_D^u(l,l'),\mu_D^u(r',r))).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the forward direction, apply
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-context-pair-product-strict-support} to the
left displayed product. This gives the finite disjunction
$N_D^u(l')\lor N_D^u(l)\lor N_D^u(r)\lor N_D^u(r')$.

Apply the same strict-support theorem to the swapped product in the reverse
direction. Constructive case analysis on the four left disjuncts reorders them
as $N_D^u(l)\lor N_D^u(l')\lor N_D^u(r')\lor N_D^u(r)$, which is exactly the
support disjunction required for the swapped product.

The reverse direction is identical with the two displayed products exchanged.
The swapped product first yields
$N_D^u(l)\lor N_D^u(l')\lor N_D^u(r')\lor N_D^u(r)$, and constructive case
analysis reorders it back to
$N_D^u(l')\lor N_D^u(l)\lor N_D^u(r)\lor N_D^u(r')$. Closing
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-context-pair-product-strict-support} in the
reverse direction gives strict support of the original product.
\end{proof}

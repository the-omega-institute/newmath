\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp four context-pair adjacent-swap closure]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-four-context-pair-adjacent-swap-closure}
For the concrete denominator tuple
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and the contextual action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, let
$h,l_0,r_0,l_1,r_1,l_2,r_2,l_3,r_3:\mathsf{BHist}$. Suppose $C_D^u(h)$ and
suppose that $(l_0,r_0)$, $(l_1,r_1)$, $(l_2,r_2)$, and $(l_3,r_3)$ are
carried denominator context pairs. Let
$$
H_{0123}:=
\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_3,r_3}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_2,r_2}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_1,r_1}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_0,r_0}(h)))).
$$
Let
$$
H_{0213}:=
\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_3,r_3}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_1,r_1}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_2,r_2}
(\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_0,r_0}(h)))).
$$
Then the middle-swap histories are denominator-classified:
$$
R_D^u(H_{0123},H_{0213}).
$$
Their strict-support readbacks agree:
$$
N_D^u(H_{0123})
\Longleftrightarrow
N_D^u(H_{0213}).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First carry the action of $(l_0,r_0)$ on $h$. Apply
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-contextual-action-composition-support-laws}
with empty endpoints and the carried pair data to keep
$\Gamma_{\emp,\emp,l_0,r_0}(h)$ inside $C_D^u$.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-context-pair-adjacent-swap-exit-row} over that
carried base, using $(l_1,r_1)$ and $(l_2,r_2)$ as the adjacent pair to be
exchanged and $(l_3,r_3)$ as the common outer pair. This gives the displayed
classifier between $H_{0123}$ and $H_{0213}$.

The contextual action pair transport rows carry the same classifier through the
common outer context and preserve the strict-support predicate on transported
denominator histories. Applying the support part of the adjacent-swap row, then
reading it through the common outer transport, gives the displayed support
equivalence.
\end{proof}

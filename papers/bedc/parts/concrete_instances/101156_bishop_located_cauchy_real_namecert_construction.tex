\begin{definition}[Bishop located Cauchy real carrier]
\label{def:bishop-located-cauchy-real-carrier}

$$

\item $B$ is the $\BishopRegularCauchyCompletionUp$ boundary row, carrying the regular Cauchy completion-facing route through dyadic endpoints, stream windows, regular rational readback, and the terminal Real seal.

\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window row opened by the Cauchy name before any locatedness request reads the terminal seal.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regular rational readback row attached to the same stream windows and dyadic tolerance ledger.
\item $A$ is the $\RealRationalApproximationUp$ row, displaying the rational approximation window used to compare the Cauchy readback with the located interval endpoints.
\item $W$ is the located-Cauchy witness ledger, recording that the regular Cauchy tail route and the located interval route use the same dyadic tolerance request before the terminal $\RealUp$ seal is read.

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BishopLocatedCauchyRealUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $I,B,D,S,R,A,W,H,C,P,N$. The carrier has no coordinate for $\RealUp$ completeness, quotient stream equality, selected limits, countable choice, host real equality, trichotomy, compactness, or bridge checking.

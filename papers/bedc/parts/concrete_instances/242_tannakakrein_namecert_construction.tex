\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\TannakaKreinUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-tannakakrein-namecert}

The Tannaka-Krein interface $\TannakaKreinUp$ packages the reconstruction of a compact group from its tensor category of finite-dimensional representations together with a fibre functor to vector spaces. It depends on $\NameCert_{\LieGroupUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-liegroup-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\MonoidalCatUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-monoidalcat-namecert}), and bridges representation theory and category theory through the same fibre-functor classifier.

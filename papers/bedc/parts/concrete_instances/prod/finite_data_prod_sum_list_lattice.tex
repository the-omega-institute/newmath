\begin{proposition}[Product, sum, and list finite-data lattice]\label{prop:prod-sum-list-finite-data-lattice}
$\ProdUp$ consumes the same finite-data constructor discipline as \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-sum-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-list-namecert}. Its componentwise carrier supplies paired projections, $\SumUp$ supplies branch separation, and $\ListUp$ supplies spine iteration; therefore any public finite-data read that routes through all three surfaces must preserve the product component ledgers before it may branch or iterate.
\end{proposition}

\begin{theorem}[ComplexDiffUp seed boundary]
\label{thm:complex-differentiability-seed-boundary}
The seed boundary for $\ComplexDiffUp$ consists exactly of the finite
$\mathsf{BHist}$ rows for $\mathsf{CplxDiffAt}$ and
$\mathsf{CplxDiffQuot}$, the explicit unary moduli, $\hsame$ transport,
uniqueness, closure laws, and the displayed semantic $\NameCert$ theorem. No
host complex equality, quotient witness, or non-constructive case split is part
of the public surface.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Collect the carrier and quotient rows from
\autoref{thm:cplx-diff-carrier-obligation-surface} and
\autoref{thm:cplx-diff-quotient-pattern-obligation-surface}. These rows expose
$\mathsf{CplxDiffAt}$, $\mathsf{CplxDiffQuot}$, visible quotient steps, and the
explicit unary modulus data as finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ entries.

Next collect the transport rows from
\autoref{lem:cplx-diff-at-full-hsame-transport},
\autoref{lem:cplx-diff-quot-hsame}, and
\autoref{thm:cplx-diff-transport-ledger-obligation-surface}. They keep the
public surface inside $\hsame$ transport and finite ledger rows.

Finally use \autoref{thm:complexdiff-finite-obligation-inventory-complete}. It
identifies the displayed semantic $\NameCert$ surface with exactly these
carrier, quotient, transport, uniqueness, closure, and ledger rows, while
excluding host complex equality, quotient witnesses, and non-constructive case
analysis from the public boundary.
\end{proof}

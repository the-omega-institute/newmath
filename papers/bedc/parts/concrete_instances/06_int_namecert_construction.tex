$\IntUp$ is the L7 signed-unary arithmetic surface built from the unary
magnitude rows of $\NatUp$ in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-nat-namecert}
and the balancing and continuation rows of $\AddUp$ in
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-add-namecert}. $\RatUp$ in
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-rat-namecert} consumes this surface as signed
numerator data, while the DyadicRatCore, StreamName, RegSeqRat, and Real
route is downstream consumer context rather than the source that defines
$\IntUp$.

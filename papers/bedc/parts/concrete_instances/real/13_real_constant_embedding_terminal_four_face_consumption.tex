\begin{theorem}[Real constant embedding terminal four-face consumption]
\label{thm:real-constant-embedding-terminal-four-face-consumption}
The concrete constant rational embedding
$$
d\longmapsto (\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\Eone(d))
$$
from \autoref{thm:real-constant-rational-embedding-certificate} is accepted
as a $\RealUp$ public seal read only through the four Phase Real and Completion
faces
$$
\DyadicRatCoreUp,\quad \StreamNameUp,\quad \RegSeqRatUp,\quad \RealUp.
$$
Its visible support rows are precisely the $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$,
$\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows carried by those faces. The consumption route
therefore exports no quotient stream equality, selected host limit, ambient
completeness row, metric-completeness datum, countable choice, or standard
bridge row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with \autoref{thm:real-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}. It
packages the hidden rational seed $d$, the constant stream
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d$, and the e-one seal $\Eone(d)$ as one concrete
$\BHist$-anchored rational-to-real name.

Apply \autoref{thm:regseqrat-constant-real-seal-handoff}. The same constant
source reaches the local $\RealUp$ seal only after the constant
$\StreamNameUp$ schedule, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ endpoint and radius rows, and
the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regularity and classifier ledger have been read through
their displayed support rows.

Next apply \autoref{thm:streamname-realup-constant-observation-handoff}. This
keeps the constant observation surface at the pointwise StreamName boundary
and leaves regularity, dyadic seal data, and the Real seal classifier as
downstream obligations rather than additional source coordinates.

Finally use \autoref{thm:real-open-phase-four-object-terminal-route}. That
terminality theorem reads every accepted public seal or completion-facing
route through exactly the four displayed faces with $\BHist$, $\hsame$,
$\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ support. Hence the constant rational
embedding is consumed by the same terminal four-face boundary, and the excluded
quotient, selected-limit, completeness, choice, metric-completeness, and
standard-bridge rows cannot replace any face of the route.
\end{proof}

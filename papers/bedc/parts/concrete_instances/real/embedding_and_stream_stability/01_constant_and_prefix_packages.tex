\begin{theorem}[Independent reindexed constant rational embedding certificate]
\label{thm:real-independent-reindexed-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}

$$
d\longmapsto (\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,\Eone(d)),
\qquad
e\longmapsto (\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q,\Eone(e))
$$

RealUp. The first endpoint has carrier exactness
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d)),
$$
and the second endpoint has carrier exactness
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(e)).

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

to $d$ with observation map $r$. This gives the first carrier row, identifying
the RatUp carrier of $d$, the StreamNameUp carrier of
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r$, and the RealUp constant carrier of
$\Eone(d)$.

Apply the same theorem separately to $e$ with observation map $q$. This gives
the second carrier row, identifying the RatUp carrier of $e$, the StreamNameUp
carrier of $\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q$, and the RealUp constant carrier
of $\Eone(e)$.

For classifier exactness, apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-independent-reindexed-stream-bridge}. Its three

\begin{theorem}[Real reindexed constant finite-window package]
\label{thm:real-reindexed-constant-finite-window-package}

\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n).

constant bridge package:

  \item the finite StreamName observation surface
  $$
  \mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}
  (\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
   \mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q,\mathcal{P});
  $$
  \item the carrier rows for $d$ and $e$ displayed in
  \autoref{thm:real-independent-reindexed-constant-rational-embedding-certificate};
  \item the classifier bridge from $\rho$ through the independently reindexed
  StreamName row, the RealUp unary-observation stream row, and the RealUp
  constant-history classifier row of $\Eone(d)$ and $\Eone(e)$;
  \item the RegSeqRat-facing ledger whose entries are exactly the
  $\mathsf{InBundle}$ rows of $\mathcal{P}$ together with their
  $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$ witnesses and the pointwise RatUp classifier copied
  from $\rho$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First build the finite observation row. Apply
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-finite-window-exactness} to the constant

$\mathcal{P}$. The reverse direction of that exactness theorem consumes
$\rho$ and yields the finite-window classifier for
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d$ and $\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e$.

The displayed streams are independently reindexed, but constant streams ignore
their observation argument. Thus at every bundled certified unary observation
$n$, both composites evaluate as
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d(r(n))=d$ and
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e(q(n))=e$. The same pointwise classifier $\rho$
therefore supplies the required
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}
(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q,\mathcal{P})$ row.

Next attach the global bridge. Apply
\autoref{thm:real-independent-reindexed-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}
to $d,e,r,q$. Its two carrier rows give the RatUp--StreamName--RealUp carrier
identifications for the two endpoints, and its classifier rows transport
$\rho$ through the independently reindexed StreamName classifier, the RealUp
unary-observation stream classifier, and the RealUp constant-history classifier
of the two e-one seals.

Finally repack the finite-window row with those carrier and classifier rows.
The ledger consumed downstream by $\RegSeqRat$ is not a new carrier: it is the
visible $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ spine, each $\mathsf{InBundle}$ membership row,
the corresponding $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$ witness, and the copied RatUp point
classifier at that observation. This is precisely the finite observation
surface associated to the checked global constant embedding.
\end{proof}

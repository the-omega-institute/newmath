\begin{theorem}[Rat StreamName unary-context e-one tail coverage package]
\label{thm:rat-streamname-unary-context-e1-tail-coverage-package}
For observation maps $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$, a selected observation $n:\Hist$,
prefix and tail histories $p_s,p_t,q_s,q_t:\Hist$, middle histories
$m_s,m_t:\Hist$, output histories $o_s,o_t:\Hist$, and zero-head tests
$z_s,z_t:\Hist$, assume
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(p_s),\quad
\hsame(p_s,p_t),\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(q_s),\quad
\hsame(q_s,q_t),\quad
\mathsf{Cont}(p_s,s(n),m_s),\quad
\mathsf{Cont}(m_s,q_s,o_s),\\
\mathsf{Cont}(p_t,t(n),m_t),\quad
\mathsf{Cont}(m_t,q_t,o_t).
\end{array}
$$
Then the local unary-context hypotheses construct displayed one-extension tails
for the contextual outputs and carry the full RatUp endpoint package:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\exists a,b:\Hist,\ 
\hsame(o_s,\Eone(a))\land
\hsame(o_t,\Eone(b))\land\\
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(o_s,o_t)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_s)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_t)\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(o_s)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(o_t)\land
(\hsame(o_s,\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(o_t,\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(o_s,\Ezero(z_s))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(o_t,\Ezero(z_t))\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
\hsame(a,b).
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the point classifier from
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ at the certified unary observation $n$.
This gives $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(n),t(n))$.

Apply
\autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-cont-unary-context-positive-denominators}
to that point classifier, the two matching unary contexts, and the four
continuation rows. This supplies
$\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_s)$ and
$\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_t)$.

Unfold the two positive unary denominator witnesses. Choose tails $a$ and $b$
together with the displayed one-extension rows
$\hsame(o_s,\Eone(a))$ and $\hsame(o_t,\Eone(b))$.

With those displayed tails, apply
\autoref{thm:rat-streamname-unary-context-full-endpoint-package} to the original
StreamNameUp classifier, the certified observation, the matching context rows,
the four continuation rows, and the two selected one-extension displays. The
returned classifier, denominator, unary, exclusion, and tail readback rows
combine with the two displays under $\exists a,b$.
\end{proof}

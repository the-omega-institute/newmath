\begin{definition}[Real name seal carrier]
\label{def:real-name-seal-carrier}
A $\RealUp$ name-seal carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
R=(D,S,Q,L,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation row recording the endpoint, radius, and finite tolerance data available to the read;
\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window schedule that exposes the requested observations;
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regularity row that records the pointwise regular-rational readback;
\item $L$ is the finite ledger tying the selected windows, dyadic observations, radius rows, and regularity checks to one request surface;
\item $E$ is the local $\RealUp$ seal row exposed only after $D,S,Q,L$ have been displayed;
\item $H$ records the componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $D,S,Q,L,E,C,P,N$;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes from the finite-window schedule through the dyadic and regularity rows to the seal row;
\item $P$ records $\Pkg$ provenance over $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RealUp}$ row for this name-seal packet.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for a quotient stream, a selected host limit, host real equality, host sequence equality, or an ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Real name seal source factorization]
\label{thm:real-name-seal-source-factorization}
Every $\RealUp$ seal read accepted through the carrier of \autoref{def:real-name-seal-carrier} factors through the displayed source route
$$
S,D,Q,L \longmapsto E
$$

Equivalently, the seal may be read only after the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window schedule, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation row, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regularity row, and the finite ledger have all been consumed through the same packet.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.RealUp.RealNameSeal\_source\_factorization}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted packet through \autoref{def:real-name-seal-carrier}. The only seal coordinate is $E$, and the only source rows that lead to it are $S,D,Q,L$ with the transport, continuation, package, and naming rows $H,C,P,N$.

The route is the local RealUp form of \autoref{thm:real-regseqrat-streamname-lattice-route}. That theorem places the Phase Real and Completion seal path behind the StreamName, RegSeqRat, and DyadicRatCore sibling surfaces before the local seal is public.

The RegularCauchyName seal results provide the readback discipline used by the same boundary. Apply \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-real-seal-consumer-scope}, \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-real-seal-ledger-exhaustion}, and \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-seal-readback-exhaustion}: a real-seal consumer is downstream of the displayed finite window, dyadic observation, regularity ledger, package provenance, and local NameCert rows. Repacking those rows as $D,S,Q,L,E,H,C,P,N$ gives the stated factorization.
\end{proof}

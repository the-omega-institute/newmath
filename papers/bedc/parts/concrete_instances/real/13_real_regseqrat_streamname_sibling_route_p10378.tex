\label{thm:real-regseqrat-streamname-sibling-route}

\end{theorem}

It routes any seal-facing read with a StreamName source and a RegSeqRat readback through the visible StreamName finite-window ledger and the RegSeqRat pointwise handoff before the local real classifier is consumed.

Next use \autoref{thm:real-streamname-regseqrat-source-lattice}. This places the StreamName and RegSeqRat rows inside a common source lattice and attaches the DyadicRatCore tolerance ledger before the Real seal becomes public.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:real-dyadic-stream-regseq-route}. The ordered route
$$
\DyadicRatCoreUp \to \StreamNameUp \to \RegSeqRatUp \to \RealUp

Since the cited packets expose only the displayed finite carrier, classifier, transport, packaging, and certificate rows, the public RealUp name has no coordinate for quotient-real equality, selected host limits, ambient completeness, hidden dyadic samples, or a direct seal bypass.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real unary StreamNameUp finite-window selected classifier appended-tail package]
\label{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-classifier-appended-tail-package}
For observation maps $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$ and histories
$\alpha,\omega,k,u,v,z_s,z_t,d_s,d_t:\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(t),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t),
$$
$$
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\alpha),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\omega),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,\omega),
$$
and the displayed selected window endpoints
$$
\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(u)),
\quad
\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(v)).
$$
Then the selected endpoints carry the selected-shape endpoint package and the
appended e-zero-tail exclusions:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(u),\Eone(v))\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)\land\hsame(u,v)\land\\
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
(\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Ezero(z_s))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Ezero(z_t))\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),d_s\cdot\Ezero(z_s))\to
\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),d_t\cdot\Ezero(z_t))\to
\mathsf{False}).
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-shape-package}
to the two carrier rows, the StreamNameUp classifier, the unary anchor and
bound, the selected offset, and the displayed one-headed endpoint comparisons.
This gives every selected endpoint row through the zero-head exclusions,
including the two selected positive-denominator witnesses.

For the left endpoint, suppose
$\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),d_s\cdot\Ezero(z_s))$. Transport the
positive denominator witness for $s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k))$ across this
displayed sameness by
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-unary-denominator-hsame-transport}. The transported
witness is a positive unary denominator witness for $d_s\cdot\Ezero(z_s)$,
contradicting
\autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-append-e0-tail-absurd}.

The right endpoint is identical. A display
$\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),d_t\cdot\Ezero(z_t))$ transports the
positive denominator witness for $t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k))$ to
$d_t\cdot\Ezero(z_t)$, and the same appended e-zero-tail absurdity theorem
contradicts that transported witness.

Repack the original selected-shape rows with the two appended-tail exclusions.
This is exactly the displayed selected classifier appended-tail package.
\end{proof}

\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-classifier-appended-tail-package}.

e-zero-tail exclusions for the chosen witnesses $u$ and $v$.

Package $u$ and $v$ as the existential witnesses. Prepending their two selected
endpoint displays to the returned appended-tail package gives exactly the
displayed coverage package.

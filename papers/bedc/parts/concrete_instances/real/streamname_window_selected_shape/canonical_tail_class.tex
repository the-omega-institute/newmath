\begin{theorem}[Real unary StreamNameUp finite-window selected e-one tail pairwise coherence]
\label{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-e1-tail-pairwise-coherence}
For observation maps $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$ and histories
$\alpha,\omega,k,a,a',b,b':\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(t),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t),
$$
$$
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\alpha),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\omega),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,\omega),
$$
and the four displayed selected e-one endpoint comparisons
$$
\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(a)),\quad
\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(a')),
$$
$$
\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(b)),\quad
\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(b')).
$$
Then the displayed tails form one pairwise selected-tail class:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$.
\item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a')$.
\item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)$.
\item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b')$.
\item $\hsame(a,a')$.
\item $\hsame(b,b')$.
\item $\hsame(a,b)$.
\item $\hsame(a',b')$.
\item $\hsame(a,b')$.
\item $\hsame(a',b)$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-shape-package} to the
first left and right endpoint displays, together with the StreamNameUp classifier,
the unary anchor and bound, and the selected offset. This gives
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)$, and $\hsame(a,b)$.

Apply the same selected shape package to the primed left and right endpoint
displays. This gives $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a')$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b')$,
and $\hsame(a',b')$.

For the left selected endpoint, compose the symmetry of
$\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(a))$ with
$\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(a'))$. The kernel transitivity theorem
$\mathsf{BEDC.FKernel.Hist.hsame\_trans}$ gives
$\hsame(\Eone(a),\Eone(a'))$, and
$\mathsf{BEDC.FKernel.Hist.hsame\_e1\_iff}$ inverts the common e-one head to give
$\hsame(a,a')$.

The right selected endpoint is identical. Compose the symmetry of the displayed
comparison with $\Eone(b)$ and the displayed comparison with $\Eone(b')$, then
invert the common e-one head by
$\mathsf{BEDC.FKernel.Hist.hsame\_e1\_iff}$ to get $\hsame(b,b')$.

It remains to fill the two off-diagonal rows. Transitivity sends
$\hsame(a,a')$ and $\hsame(a',b')$ to $\hsame(a,b')$, while symmetry of
$\hsame(a,a')$ followed by $\hsame(a,b)$ gives $\hsame(a',b)$. Together with the
four unary rows and the two direct cross rows, these are exactly the ten displayed
claims.
\end{proof}

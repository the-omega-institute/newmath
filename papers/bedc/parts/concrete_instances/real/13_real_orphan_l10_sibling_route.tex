The $\RealUp$ seal is a downstream consumer of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert}. The $\StreamNameUp$ surface supplies finite observation windows, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ surface supplies the regular rational source row, and the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ surface supplies the dyadic tolerance and readback ledger; only after those three sibling surfaces are in scope does the local $\RealUp$ seal present its public certificate boundary.

\begin{definition}[Real stream classifiers]
\label{def:real-stream-classifiers}
For rational-observation streams $x,y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, the stream classifier is pointwise rational-history classification. The finite prefix classifier records the same data recursively through a selected observation index.
\end{definition}
\leandef{BEDC.Derived.RealUp.RealStreamClassifier}
\leandef{BEDC.Derived.RealUp.RealStreamPrefixClassifier}

\begin{definition}[Real unary-observation stream classifier]
\label{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier}
For rational-observation maps $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$, the unary-observation
stream classifier is pointwise rational-history classification over certified
unary BEDC observation histories:
$$
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(n),t(n)).
$$
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Constant rational streams bridge StreamNameUp and RealUp]
\label{thm:streamname-real-constant-prefix-bridge}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$, the RatUp point classifier, the StreamNameUp
constant-stream classifier, the RealUp unary-observation stream classifier, and
the constant real-history classifier at e-one seals are equivalent:
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e).
$$
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e).
$$
$$
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d),\Eone(e)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the first equivalence, the forward direction is exactly the constant
classifier part of \autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness}. Conversely,
unfold Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-classifier} and read the pointwise
classifier at the empty unary observation history $\emp$ using its unary
witness; the two constant stream values are $d$ and $e$.

For the second equivalence, unfold
Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-classifier} and
Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier}. Both predicates
are the same pointwise requirement over certified unary observation histories,
so each direction is identity transport of the displayed witness.

For the last equivalence, unfold
Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier} and
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}$. Reading at the empty unary observation gives
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$, while a rational point classifier supplies
the same pointwise witness at every unary observation.

Apply \autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-e1-iff-rat} to identify that
rational point classifier with
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d),\Eone(e))$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Reindexed constant rational streams bridge StreamNameUp and RealUp]
\label{thm:real-constant-reindexed-stream-bridge}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$ and any observation map $r:\Hist\to\Hist$, the
RatUp point classifier, the StreamNameUp classifier of reindexed constant
streams, the RealUp unary-observation stream classifier of the same reindexed
streams, and the constant real-history classifier at e-one seals are
equivalent:
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ r).
$$
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ r)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ r).
$$
$$
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ r)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d),\Eone(e)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the first equivalence, unfold $\mathsf{RatConstStream}$ and
Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-classifier}. The forward direction reuses
the supplied $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$ at every certified unary
observation; the observation map is ignored because both reindexed constant
streams still evaluate to their hidden seeds $d$ and $e$. Conversely, read the
stream-name classifier at the empty unary observation history $\emp$.

For the second equivalence, unfold
Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-classifier} and
Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier}. After expanding
the reindexed constant streams, both predicates ask for the same pointwise
RatUp classifier over certified unary observations, so each direction transports
the displayed pointwise witness unchanged.

For the last equivalence, the forward direction again reads the unary stream
classifier at $\emp$ to recover $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$, then
applies \autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-e1-iff-rat}. In the
reverse direction, the same theorem unseals the e-one classifier as the RatUp
point classifier, and the constant observation maps reuse it at every certified
unary observation.
\end{proof}

The reindexed constant-stream certificate fit has ten fields: traditional target
constant rational real names under observation reindexing; BEDC source RatUp
plus StreamNameUp plus RealUp; carrier the reindexed constant rational
observation maps and e-one real seals; classifier the three displayed bridge
rows; stability from ignoring the observation map in constant streams; ledger
hidden rational seed plus observation map; core theorem
\autoref{thm:real-constant-reindexed-stream-bridge}; exactness the three
equivalences; standard bridge target constant rational real names under
reindexed observation; downstream use by the RealUp dyadic embedding theorem.

\begin{theorem}[Constant rational stream carriers bridge StreamNameUp and RealUp]
\label{thm:streamname-real-constant-carrier-bridge}
For every history $d:\Hist$, the RatUp point carrier, the StreamNameUp
constant-stream carrier, and the RealUp constant real-history carrier at the
e-one seal are equivalent:
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d).
$$
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The first equivalence is the carrier row of
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-point-exactness}, read symmetrically:
the StreamNameUp constant carrier is exactly the RatUp carrier of its hidden
constant seed.

For the second equivalence, compose that carrier row with
\autoref{thm:real-constant-history-carrier-e1-iff-rat}. A StreamNameUp
constant carrier first recovers $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$, then the
e-one theorem seals it as
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d))$; the reverse direction unseals
the RealUp constant carrier to $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ and repacks it
as the StreamNameUp constant carrier.
\end{proof}

The carrier certificate fit has ten fields: traditional target constant
rational real carrier embedding; BEDC source RatUp histories plus StreamNameUp
observations; carrier the displayed constant stream and e-one real seal;
classifier inherited from \autoref{thm:streamname-real-constant-prefix-bridge};
stability from RatUp carrier transport and RealConstant carrier transport;
ledger the hidden rational seed and observation witness; core theorem
\autoref{thm:streamname-real-constant-carrier-bridge}; exactness the two
displayed equivalence rows; standard bridge constant rational real name; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[Constant rational embedding certificate]
\label{thm:real-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}
The concrete map
$$
d\longmapsto (\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\Eone(d))
$$
from a RatUp history seed $d:\Hist$ supplies the constant rational embedding
into RealUp. Its carrier exactness is the chain
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d)).
$$
Its classifier exactness, for histories $d,e:\Hist$, is the chain
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d),\Eone(e)).
$$
The certificate therefore packages the hidden rational seed, the constant
StreamNameUp observation map, and the e-one RealUp seal as one concrete
BHist-anchored rational-to-real name.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For carrier exactness, use
\autoref{thm:streamname-real-constant-carrier-bridge}. Its two displayed rows
identify the RatUp carrier with the StreamNameUp constant-stream carrier, and
then identify that carrier with the RealUp constant history carrier at the
e-one seal.

For classifier exactness, use
\autoref{thm:streamname-real-constant-prefix-bridge}. Its three displayed rows
identify the RatUp point classifier, the StreamNameUp constant-stream
classifier, the RealUp unary stream classifier, and the RealUp constant
history classifier at the paired e-one seals.

The endpoint part of the last row is the Real constant classifier equivalence
and transport mechanism: \autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-e1-iff-rat}
turns an e-one sealed RealUp classifier into the underlying RatUp classifier
and back, while
\autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-endpoint-transport} preserves the
same classifier under endpoint-equivalent real histories. These facts repack
the two chains as one certificate for the displayed map.
\end{proof}

The constant rational embedding certificate has ten fields: traditional target
constant rational embedding; BEDC source RatUp plus StreamNameUp plus RealUp;
carrier the constant stream and e-one seal; classifier the displayed equivalence
chain; stability inherited from RatUp classifier fields and Real constant
endpoint transport; ledger hidden rational seed plus unary observation; core
theorem \autoref{thm:real-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}; exactness
the carrier and classifier chains; standard bridge ordinary rational constants;
status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

concrete map
$$

$$

$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d)).

\autoref{thm:real-constant-reindexed-stream-bridge}.

\begin{proof}

Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-carrier}. The forward direction reuses the
carrier of $d$ at every observation. Conversely, read the stream-name carrier at
$\emp$ to recover the hidden seed carrier.

\autoref{thm:real-constant-history-carrier-e1-iff-rat}. It transports
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ to
$\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryCarrier}(\Eone(d))$ and back, so it composes with
the reindexed StreamNameUp carrier row to give the displayed carrier chain.

For classifier exactness, apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-reindexed-stream-bridge}. Its three rows identify the
RatUp classifier, the StreamNameUp classifier of the reindexed constant streams,
the RealUp unary-observation stream classifier of those streams, and the RealUp
constant-history classifier at the paired e-one seals.
\end{proof}

The certificate fit has ten fields: traditional target constant rational real

constant rational observation maps; classifier the displayed bridge; stability
inherited from RatUp classifier fields; ledger the constant rational history and

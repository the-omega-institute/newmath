\begin{theorem}[Real unary StreamNameUp finite-window selected shape package]
\label{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-shape-package}
For observation maps $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$ and histories
$\alpha,\omega,k,u,v,z_s,z_t:\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(t),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t),
$$
$$
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\alpha),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\omega),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,\omega),
$$
and the displayed selected window endpoints
$$
\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(u)),
\quad
\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Eone(v)).
$$
Then the selected endpoints carry the finite-window RatUp readback package:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(u),\Eone(v))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)\land
\hsame(u,v)\land\\
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))\land\\
(\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Ezero(z_s))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Ezero(z_t))\to\mathsf{False}).
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-coverage} to the two
carrier rows, the StreamNameUp classifier, and the unary anchor and bound. This
gives $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamWindowClassifier}(s,t,\alpha,\omega)$.

Unfold Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-stream-window-classifier} at the selected
offset $k$. The offset witness $\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,\omega)$ projects the
window classifier to
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(
s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k))).
$$
This is the first row of the selected shape package.

Transport this selected RatUp classifier across the two displayed $\Eone$
endpoint comparisons by \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-hsame-transport}.
The transported classifier is
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(u),\Eone(v))$.

Apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-e1-tail-unary-iff} in the forward
direction to the transported $\Eone$ classifier. This reads back
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)$, and $\hsame(u,v)$.

Apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-positive-denominators} to the
selected classifier before the $\Eone$ transport. Then apply
\autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-unary-and-nonempty} separately to the
two positive-denominator rows. These projections supply the positive
denominators, unary endpoint rows, and nonempty endpoint exclusions.

For a displayed zero head on the left endpoint, transport
$\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)))$ along
$\hsame(s(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k)),\Ezero(z_s))$ by
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-unary-denominator-hsame-transport}. This contradicts
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-denominator-e0-absurd}.

For the right endpoint, repeat the same transport from
$t(\mathsf{append}(\alpha,k))$ to $\Ezero(z_t)$. The zero-head absurdity theorem
gives the second exclusion, and the collected rows are exactly the displayed
finite-window selected shape package.
\end{proof}

Fit. Traditional target: selected finite-window rational readback for RealUp
StreamNameUp classifiers. BEDC source: $\StreamNameUp+\RealUp+\RatUp$ over
$\mathsf{BHist}$. Carrier: $\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}$. Classifier:
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$ through
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamWindowClassifier}$. Stability: $\hsame$ transport of
selected endpoints and positive-denominator transport. Ledger: anchor, bound,
offset, selected endpoint displays, and zero-head tests. Core theorem:
\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-selected-shape-package}.
Exactness: selected source RatUp classifier, displayed RatUp one-tail classifier,
denominator, unary, nonempty, and zero-headed exclusion rows. Standard bridge:
finite rational observation windows. Status: $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

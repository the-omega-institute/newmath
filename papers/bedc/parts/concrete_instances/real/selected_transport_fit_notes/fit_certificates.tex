\begin{theorem}[Real selected transport fit certificate]
\label{thm:real-selected-transport-fit-certificate}
For rational-observation streams
$x,x',y,y',p_X,p_Y,t_X,t_Y,m_X,m_Y,o_X,o_Y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$,
indices $m,n$, and histories $a,a',b,b',z_X,z_Y:\Hist$, assume the pointwise
transport rows and the two source classifier rows
$$
\forall i,\ \hsame(x(i),x'(i)),\quad
\forall i,\ \hsame(y(i),y'(i)),\quad
\mathsf{RealStreamClassifier}(x,y),\quad
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,m+n).
$$
Assume also the selected transported context rows
$$
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(p_X(n)),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(t_X(n)),\quad
\hsame(p_X(n),p_Y(n)),\quad
\hsame(t_X(n),t_Y(n)),
$$
the selected continuation rows
$$
\mathsf{Cont}(p_X(n),x'(n),m_X(n)),\quad
\mathsf{Cont}(m_X(n),t_X(n),o_X(n)),
\quad\mathsf{Cont}(p_Y(n),y'(n),m_Y(n)),\quad
\mathsf{Cont}(m_Y(n),t_Y(n),o_Y(n)),
$$
and the selected e-one displays
$$
\hsame(o_X(n),\Eone(a)),\quad
\hsame(o_X(n),\Eone(a')),\quad
\hsame(o_Y(n),\Eone(b)),\quad
\hsame(o_Y(n),\Eone(b')),\quad
\hsame(x'(n),\Eone(a)),\quad
\hsame(y'(n),\Eone(b)).
$$
Then the selected-transport fit carries the displayed package
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\Bigl(
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(o_X(n),o_Y(n))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_X(n))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(o_Y(n))\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(o_X(n))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(o_Y(n))\land
(\hsame(o_X(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(o_Y(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(o_X(n),\Ezero(z_X))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(o_Y(n),\Ezero(z_Y))\to\mathsf{False})\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
\hsame(a,b)
\Bigr)\\
{}\land
\Bigl(
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a')\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b')\land\\
\hsame(a,a')\land
\hsame(b,b')\land
\hsame(a,b)\land
\hsame(a',b')
\Bigr)\\
{}\land
\Bigl(
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(a),\Eone(b))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
\hsame(a,b)\land\\
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(x'(n))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(y'(n))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(x'(n))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(y'(n))\land\\
(\hsame(x'(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(y'(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(x'(n),\Ezero(z_X))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(y'(n),\Ezero(z_Y))\to\mathsf{False})
\Bigr).
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
We build the three displayed conjuncts separately and then conjoin them.

For the first conjunct, apply
\autoref{thm:real-stream-classifier-transported-contextual-full-endpoint-package}
to the two pointwise transport rows, the stream classifier row, the displayed
context rows, the four continuation rows, and the endpoint displays
$\hsame(o_X(n),\Eone(a))$ and $\hsame(o_Y(n),\Eone(b))$. Its six enumerated
rows project to the classifier row, the two positive-denominator rows, the two
endpoint-unary rows, the two empty exclusions, the two zero-headed exclusions
using $z_X$ and $z_Y$, and the tail rows
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)$, and $\hsame(a,b)$.

For the second conjunct, apply
\autoref{thm:real-stream-classifier-transported-contextual-e1-tail-determinacy}
to the same transport, classifier, context, and continuation rows, now using
all four displayed e-one endpoints. The eight displayed rows of that theorem
project directly to the four unary tail rows and the four hsame tail rows.

For the third conjunct, apply
\autoref{thm:real-stream-prefix-transported-selected-full-shape-readback-package}
to the prefix classifier row, the two pointwise transport rows, and the selected
endpoint displays $\hsame(x'(n),\Eone(a))$ and
$\hsame(y'(n),\Eone(b))$. Its displayed readback package projects to the
e-one classifier, tail, positive-denominator, endpoint-unary, empty-exclusion,
and zero-headed exclusion rows for $x'(n)$ and $y'(n)$.

The three row packages just obtained are exactly the three displayed
conjuncts, and their conjunction is the stated selected-transport fit
certificate.
\end{proof}

\autoref{thm:real-selected-transport-fit-certificate} with the displayed

finite-prefix selected endpoint-shape package used below.

Next invoke
\autoref{thm:real-stream-classifier-transported-contextual-displayed-e1-appended-tail-exclusion-package}

\autoref{thm:rat-positive-unary-denominator-hsame-transport}; the resulting
positive denominator for $e_X\cdot\Ezero(w_X)$ contradicts
\autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-append-e0-tail-absurd}.

For $y'(n)$, the same argument applied to
$\hsame(y'(n),e_Y\cdot\Ezero(w_Y))$ transports the positive-denominator row to
$e_Y\cdot\Ezero(w_Y)$ and contradicts the appended e-zero-tail obstruction.
Combining the three fit conjuncts with these four exclusions gives the displayed
certificate.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Constant rational real finite-window exactness]
\label{thm:real-constant-rational-finite-window-exactness}
For histories $d,e,a,w:\Hist$ with $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$ and
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(w)$,
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)
\Longleftrightarrow
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamWindowClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e,a,w).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the forward direction, assume
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$. The constant-stream witness
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness} supplies
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)$.

Read this StreamNameUp classifier as the corresponding RealUp unary-observation
stream classifier by \autoref{thm:streamname-real-constant-prefix-bridge}. With
the displayed unary anchor and bound witnesses, apply
\autoref{thm:real-unary-stream-window-coverage} to obtain the finite-window
classifier.

For the reverse direction, assume
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamWindowClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e,a,w)$. The empty history is a certified unary offset
below $w$: use the unary witness for $w$, the unary witness for $\emp$, and the
continuation row $\ContR(\emp,w,w)$.

Apply
\autoref{lem:real-unary-stream-window-endpoint-projection} at the offset
$\emp$. This yields
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d(\mathsf{append}(a,\emp)),
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e(\mathsf{append}(a,\emp))).
$$

Unfold $\mathsf{RatConstStream}$. Both constant streams ignore the observation
history $\mathsf{append}(a,\emp)$, so the projected endpoint classifier is
exactly $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$.
\end{proof}

The ten-field fit is: traditional target constant rational observations inside
RealUp finite windows; BEDC source RatUp plus StreamNameUp plus RealUp; carrier
constant rational observation streams; classifier the displayed equivalence;
stability inherited from constant stream classification and finite-window
endpoint projection; ledger hidden rational seed, anchor, bound, and empty
offset; core theorem
\autoref{thm:real-constant-rational-finite-window-exactness}; exactness the
displayed equivalence; standard bridge constant rational finite observation
windows; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

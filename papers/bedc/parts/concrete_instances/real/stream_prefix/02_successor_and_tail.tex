\begin{lemma}[Real stream prefix successor inversion]
\label{lem:real-stream-prefix-successor-inversion}
For rational-observation streams $x,y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$ and every prefix
length $n$:
$$
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+1)
\Longleftrightarrow
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n)\land
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+1),y(n+1)).
$$
Ten-field fit: traditional target finite prefix successor inversion; BEDC source
RealUp prefix classifier over RatUp point classifiers; carrier
rational-observation streams; classifier $\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}$;
stability inherited from hsame transport and endpoint projection; ledger prefix
length and endpoint observation; core theorem this lemma; exactness the
displayed iff; standard bridge finite prefix recursion; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{lemma}
\begin{proof}
Unfold the recursive definition of
$\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+1)$. In the forward direction, the
successor prefix witness is a pair; project its left component to obtain
$\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n)$ and its right component to obtain
the endpoint classifier
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+1),y(n+1))$.

Conversely, assume the previous prefix witness and the endpoint rational-history
classifier. Pair these two witnesses by the successor constructor of the
recursive prefix display, obtaining
$\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+1)$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real stream prefix classifier tail extension]
\label{thm:real-stream-prefix-classifier-tail-extension}
For rational-observation streams $x,y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$ and indices $n,m$,
an already classified prefix through $n$ extends across any finite tail whose
endpoints are classified:
$$
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n)\land
\bigl(\forall k,\ 1\le k\le m\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+k),y(n+k))\bigr)
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+m).
$$
Ten-field fit: traditional target finite prefix continuation from certified
endpoints; BEDC source RealUp stream prefixes over RatUp history classifiers;
carrier rational-observation streams; classifier
$\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}$; stability inherited from each endpoint
classifier and recursive prefix pairing; ledger initial prefix length, tail
length, and endpoint offsets; core theorem this tail continuation principle;
exactness the displayed implication; standard bridge finite prefix recursion;
status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Induct on $m$. At $m=0$, the target prefix is the given prefix through $n$,
since $n+0=n$, and no tail endpoint is required.

For the successor step, assume the principle for tail length $m$ and suppose
the prefix through $n$ is classified together with endpoint classifiers for
all offsets $1\le k\le m+1$. Restrict the endpoint assumption to offsets
$1\le k\le m$ and apply the induction hypothesis. This gives
$\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+m)$.

It remains to add the final endpoint. Taking $k=m+1$ in the endpoint assumption
gives $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+m+1),y(n+m+1))$ after the usual
successor arithmetic. Apply the reverse direction of
\autoref{lem:real-stream-prefix-successor-inversion} to the classified prefix
through $n+m$ and this endpoint classifier, obtaining the classified prefix
through $n+m+1$, which is the prefix through $n+(m+1)$.
\end{proof}

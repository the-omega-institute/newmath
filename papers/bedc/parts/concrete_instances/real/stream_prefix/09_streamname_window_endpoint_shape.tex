\begin{theorem}[Real unary StreamNameUp finite-window endpoint shape package]
\label{thm:real-unary-streamname-window-endpoint-shape-package}
For observation maps $s,t,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$, histories
$a,w,k,u,v,z_s,z_t:\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t),
$$
$$
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(w),\quad
\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,w),
$$
the pointwise transport rows
$$
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(s(n),s'(n)),
\quad
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(t(n),t'(n)),
$$
and the displayed finite-window endpoints
$$
\hsame(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\Eone(u)),
\quad
\hsame(t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\Eone(v)).
$$
Then the selected transported endpoints carry the bounded RealUp readback
package:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}
(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)))\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)))\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)))\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)))\land\\
(\hsame(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\Ezero(z_s))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\Ezero(z_t))\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)\land
\hsame(u,v).
\end{array}
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First convert the StreamNameUp classifier into the RealUp unary stream
classifier by the reverse direction of
\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-classifier-exactness}. The carrier rows for
$s$ and $t$ are the two fields projected from
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$, so the classifier row becomes
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$.

Apply \autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-finite-window-coverage} to the
projected carrier rows, the same StreamNameUp classifier, the anchor $a$, and
the bound $w$. This gives
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamWindowClassifier}(s,t,a,w)$. Project the selected
offset with \autoref{lem:real-unary-stream-window-endpoint-projection}, using
$\mathsf{UnaryOffsetLe}(k,w)$, to obtain
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}
(s(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),t(\mathsf{append}(a,k))).
$$

Unfolding the offset bound supplies $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(k)$, and unary append
closure with $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$ gives
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(\mathsf{append}(a,k))$. Evaluate the two pointwise
transport rows at this appended observation. Transport the projected RatUp
classifier along those two $\hsame$ rows by
\autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-hsame-transport}; the result is
$$
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}
(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k))).
$$

Apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-positive-denominators} to the
transported classifier. Then apply
\autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-unary-and-nonempty} separately to the
two positive-denominator rows. These projections give the positive denominator
witnesses, the unary endpoint rows, and the two exclusions against $\emp$.

For a displayed zero head on the left endpoint, transport the left positive
denominator along
$\hsame(s'(\mathsf{append}(a,k)),\Ezero(z_s))$ by
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-unary-denominator-hsame-transport}. This contradicts
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-denominator-e0-absurd}. The right endpoint uses the
same argument with $t'(\mathsf{append}(a,k))$ and $z_t$.

Finally, transport the selected RatUp classifier along the two displayed
$\Eone$ endpoint comparisons and apply
\autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-e1-tail-unary-iff}. This reads back
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)$, and $\hsame(u,v)$, and
the collected rows are exactly the stated package.
\end{proof}

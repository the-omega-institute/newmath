\begin{theorem}[Real stream prefix finite-tail transported endpoint denominator package]
\label{thm:real-stream-prefix-finite-tail-transported-endpoint-denominator-package}
For rational-observation streams $x,y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, indices $n,m$, and
transported endpoint histories $u,v:\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n),
$$
$$
\forall k<m,\ \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+k+1),y(n+k+1)),
$$
$$
\forall i,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(x(i)),
$$
and
$$
\hsame(x(n+m),u),\quad \hsame(y(n+m),v).
$$
Then the transported finite-tail boundary carries the endpoint denominator
package:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(u,v)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(u)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(v)\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)\land
(\hsame(u,\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(v,\emp)\to\mathsf{False}).
\end{array}
$$
Ten-field fit: traditional target finite-tail transported RealUp boundary
package; BEDC source RealUp stream prefixes with RatUp finite tail classifiers;
carrier rational-observation streams and transported endpoint histories;
classifier $\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}$ followed by
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$; stability by tail extension with unary anchor,
endpoint projection, and endpoint $\hsame$ transport; ledger base prefix length,
tail length, selected boundary, and two endpoint transport rows; core theorem
this finite-tail boundary package; exactness classifier, positive denominator,
unary, and nonempty rows for $u$ and $v$; standard bridge RegSeqRat finite
boundary observations; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First extend the classified prefix through the displayed finite tail. Apply
\autoref{thm:real-stream-prefix-tail-extension-with-unary-anchor} to the prefix
through $n$, the tail classifier row, and the pointwise unary anchor on $x$.
This supplies $\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n+m)$, together with the
left unary boundary row kept in the construction.

Next apply \autoref{thm:real-stream-prefix-classifier-endpoint} at the boundary
index $n+m$. The selected endpoint classifier is
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+m),y(n+m))$.

Transport this RatUp classifier along the two displayed $\hsame$ rows by
\autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-hsame-transport}. The transported classifier
is $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(u,v)$.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-positive-denominators} to the
transported classifier, and then apply
\autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-unary-and-nonempty} separately to the
two positive-denominator witnesses. These readback rows provide the two
positive denominator witnesses, the two unary endpoint witnesses, and the two
nonempty exclusions, so the displayed package repacks directly.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real stream prefix finite-tail transported full endpoint package]
\label{thm:real-stream-prefix-finite-tail-transported-full-endpoint-package}
For rational-observation streams $x,y:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, indices $n,m$,
transported endpoint histories $u,v:\Hist$, and histories
$a,b,z_U,z_V:\Hist$, assume
$$
\mathsf{RealStreamPrefixClassifier}(x,y,n),
$$
$$
\forall k<m,\ \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(x(n+k+1),y(n+k+1)),
$$
$$
\forall i,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(x(i)),
$$
and
$$
\hsame(x(n+m),u),\quad \hsame(y(n+m),v),\quad
\hsame(u,\Eone(a)),\quad \hsame(v,\Eone(b)).
$$
Then the transported finite-tail boundary carries the full endpoint package:
$$
\begin{array}{l}
\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(u,v)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(u)\land
\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(v)\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(u)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(v)\land
(\hsame(u,\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(v,\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
(\hsame(u,\Ezero(z_U))\to\mathsf{False})\land
(\hsame(v,\Ezero(z_V))\to\mathsf{False})\land\\
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
\hsame(a,b).
\end{array}
$$
Ten-field fit: traditional target finite transported rational observation
boundary; BEDC source RealUp prefix classifier plus RatUp endpoint classifier
and $\mathsf{BHist}$ transports; carrier transported endpoint histories;
classifier $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$; stability endpoint $\hsame$
transport; ledger base prefix, finite tail, transported endpoints, and endpoint
display tests; core theorem this block; exactness the displayed full package;
standard bridge RegSeqRat finite boundary readback; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply
\autoref{thm:real-stream-prefix-finite-tail-transported-endpoint-denominator-package}
to the prefix classifier, finite-tail RatUp classifier row, pointwise unary
anchor, and two transported endpoint rows. This gives the transported
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(u,v)$, both positive denominator witnesses, both
unary endpoint rows, and both nonempty exclusions.

Next derive the zero-headed exclusions. If $u$ is displayed as
$\Ezero(z_U)$, transport the positive denominator witness for $u$ across that
display and apply RatUp positive-denominator zero-headed absurdity. The right
endpoint uses the same argument with $v$ and $z_V$.

Finally transport the transported classifier across the two displayed
$\Eone$ comparisons. RatUp e-one tail classifier exactness then reads back the
two unary tails and their internal sameness, namely
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)$, and $\hsame(a,b)$.
Together with the denominator package and the two zero-headed exclusions, these
rows repack as the displayed full endpoint package.
\end{proof}

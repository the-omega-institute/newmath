\begin{theorem}[RegSeqRat stream classifier stability spine]
\label{thm:regseqrat-stream-classifier-stability-spine}
Let $s,t,u,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$ be rational-observation maps. Suppose
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(t),\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(u),
$$
$$
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t),\quad
\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(t,u),
$$
and suppose the displayed endpoints transport pointwise over unary
observations:
$$
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(s(n),s'(n)),
\quad
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(t(n),t'(n)).
$$
Then the $\RegSeqRat$ stream certificate spine supplies
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,s)$,
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(t,s)$,
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,u)$, the endpoint rows
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(s(n))$ and
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(t(n))$ at every certified unary observation $n$,
and the transported stream-name classifier
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unfold Definition~\ref{def:regseqrat-stream-certificate-spine},
Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-carrier},
Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-classifier}, and
Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier}. The spine is
therefore the existing BEDC unary stream row together with the regularity
ledger; no separate classifier is being postulated.

Apply \autoref{thm:real-unary-stream-classifier-stability-package} to the
three carried streams, the two displayed RealUp unary stream classifiers, and
the two pointwise $\hsame$ transport rows. Its reflexivity, symmetry, and
transitivity clauses give the three
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}$ conclusions, and its endpoint clause gives
the pointwise $\RatUp$ carrier rows.

The same package returns
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ and
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s',t')$. Combining the transported point
classifier with RatUp classifier $\hsame$ transport and the transported
endpoint carrier rows repacks exactly
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RegSeqRat seal supplies RealUp observation classifier]
\label{thm:regseqrat-realup-seal-consumes-stream-spine}
Let $X$ and $Y$ be sealed $\RealUp$ candidates exposing rational-observation
streams $s,t:\Hist\to\Hist$. If the exposed streams satisfy the
$\RegSeqRat$ stream certificate spine and the seal records
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ as its stream comparison row, then
the sealed candidates export exactly the
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ row required by
\autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the exposed stream data from the two $\RealUp$ seals. The
$\RegSeqRat$ spine gives the $\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}$ rows, the
regularity ledger, and the pointwise RatUp classifier row over every certified
unary observation.

Now read the seal's stored stream comparison row. By
Definition~\ref{def:real-unary-observation-stream-classifier}, this is exactly
the pointwise rational-history classifier over the same certified unary
observations.

Repack that row under the RealUp seal. The result is the classifier row named
by \autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}; no quotient or completion
principle is used.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RegSeqRat stream spine scoped package]
\label{thm:regseqrat-stream-spine-scoped-package}
Let $s,t,u,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$ be rational-observation maps carrying
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s)$, $\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(t)$, and
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(u)$. Suppose the stream comparison rows
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ and
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(t,u)$ are recorded, and suppose the endpoint
transports are pointwise over unary observations:
$$
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(s(n),s'(n)),
\quad
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(t(n),t'(n)).
$$
Then the $\RegSeqRat$ stream certificate spine supplies the following scoped
package.
\begin{enumerate}
  \item For any $d:\Hist$ with $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$, the constant
  stream $\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d$ inhabits the $\RegSeqRat$ stream spine.
  \item The classifier rows
  $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,s)$,
  $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(t,s)$, and
  $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,u)$ are available.
  \item At every certified unary observation $n$, the endpoint carrier rows
  $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(s(n))$ and
  $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(t(n))$ are available.
  \item The transported stream-name classifier
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$ is available.
  \item For sealed $\RealUp$ candidates exposing $s$ and $t$, whose seal records
  $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ as the stream comparison row, the
  RealUp seal exports the row required by
  \autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The constant-stream clause is exactly
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-constant-stream-spine}. It turns any
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ into the carried constant stream, its reflexive
unary stream classifier, and the constant regularity ledger.

For the stability clauses, apply
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-stream-classifier-stability-spine} to $s,t,u,s',t'$,
the three carried-stream rows, the two displayed classifier rows, and the two
pointwise $\hsame$ transports. This gives the reflexive, symmetric, and
transitive $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}$ rows, the endpoint
$\RatUp$ carrier rows at every certified unary observation, and the transported
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$ row.

Finally, apply
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-realup-seal-consumes-stream-spine} to any sealed
$\RealUp$ candidates exposing $s$ and $t$ with the recorded stream comparison.
The seal consumer repacks that same comparison as the RealUp classifier export.

All rows are obtained by projection, pointwise $\hsame$ transport, and repacking
of the listed certificate data. No quotient, completion, or host equality
principle is used.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RegSeqRat spine pointwise hsame transport]
\label{thm:regseqrat-spine-pointwise-hsame-transport}
Let $s,t,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$ be rational-observation maps. Suppose $s$ and
$t$ carry $\RegSeqRat$ stream certificate spines, and suppose the stream
comparison row $\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ is recorded. If the
endpoint transports are pointwise over certified unary observations,
$$
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(s(n),s'(n)),
\quad
\forall n,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n)\to\hsame(t(n),t'(n)),
$$
then the transported streams $s'$ and $t'$ carry $\RegSeqRat$ stream
certificate spines and export the transported
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$. Moreover, for sealed $\RealUp$
candidates exposing $s'$ and $t'$, whose seal records the transported stream
comparison row, the seal exports the corresponding RealUp observation
classifier required by \autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First read the endpoint data from the original spine. The scoped package in
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-stream-spine-scoped-package} supplies the carrier rows,
the reflexive, symmetric, and transitive classifier rows, and the dyadic ledger
entries over certified unary observations.

Next transport the stream-name comparison. Apply
\autoref{thm:real-unary-streamname-transported-classifier} to the recorded
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ row and to the two pointwise
$\hsame$ transport families. It returns the transported
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$, including the transported endpoint
carrier rows for $s'$ and $t'$.

These transported carrier rows, together with the original dyadic requests read
at the same certified unary observations, repack as the $\RegSeqRat$ stream
certificate spines for $s'$ and $t'$. The construction only changes the
endpoint histories by the displayed $\hsame$ witnesses; the unary observation
indices and tolerance ledger are unchanged.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-realup-seal-consumes-stream-spine} to sealed $\RealUp$
candidates exposing $s'$ and $t'$ with the transported stream comparison row.
The seal consumer exports exactly the RealUp observation classifier named by
\autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RegSeqRat seal finite-window transport package]
\label{thm:regseqrat-seal-finite-window-transport-package}
Let $s,t,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$ be certified rational-observation maps, let
$\mathcal P$ be a $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ finite window, let $n$ be a bundled
unary observation in $\mathcal P$, and let the selected transported endpoints
be displayed as $\Eone(a)$ and $\Eone(b)$. Suppose $s$ and $t$ carry
$\RegSeqRat$ stream certificate spines, the row
$\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(s,t)$ is recorded, and the bundled
pointwise transport rows
$$
\forall m\in\mathcal P,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(m)\to\hsame(s(m),s'(m)),
\quad
\forall m\in\mathcal P,\ \mathsf{UnaryHistory}(m)\to\hsame(t(m),t'(m))
$$
carry the selected source endpoints to the displayed endpoints
$s'(n)=\Eone(a)$ and $t'(n)=\Eone(b)$. Then the $\RealUp$ seal over $s'$ and
$t'$ exports the finite-window public readback package consisting of the
transported
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s',t',\mathcal P)$ row, the
selected $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s'(n),t'(n))$ row, the positive unary
denominator rows for the displayed endpoints, the endpoint-shape exclusions,
one-tail agreement, and the RealUp observation classifier row required by
\autoref{def:real-classifier-specification}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:regseqrat-spine-pointwise-hsame-transport} to the
$\RegSeqRat$ spines, the recorded stream comparison, and the two bundled
pointwise $\hsame$ transport families. This gives the transported stream
certificate spines for $s'$ and $t'$ and the transported stream-name classifier.

Restrict that transported stream classifier to the bundle $\mathcal P$ using
the StreamName finite-window stability fields. The selected observation $n$ is
already bundled, so the restriction exposes the finite-window classifier row and
the selected endpoint histories at $s'(n)$ and $t'(n)$.

Apply the transported selected full endpoint package to the displayed endpoints
$\Eone(a)$ and $\Eone(b)$. It supplies the selected
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ row, the positive unary denominator rows, the
endpoint-shape exclusions, and one-tail agreement for the same selected
observation.

Finally repack these rows through the RealUp seal consumer. The consumer keeps
the finite-window StreamName row, the selected endpoint ledger, and the
transported stream classifier as the public readback surface, and exports the
RealUp observation classifier row without adding quotient, completion, or host
equality data.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant-tail carrier and classifier obligations]
\label{thm:real-constant-tail-obligation-carrier-classifier}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E,z_D,z_E:\Hist$ and observation maps
$r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, set
$$
D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)
\qquad\text{and}\qquad
E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E).
$$
The appended-tail histories $D$ and $E$ provide the RealUp constant-tail
carrier obligation, and the four bridge rows
$$
A=\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E),
\qquad
B=\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
$$
$$
U=\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
\qquad
C=\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(D),\Eone(E))
$$
provide the classifier obligation through RatUp, StreamNameUp, unary RealUp,
and constant-history RealUp.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The carrier is the concrete BEDC history carrier already used by the
constant-tail readback package: $D$ and $E$ are obtained by BEDC append from
the visible denominator heads $d,e$ and their displayed e-one tails.

For the classifier obligation, apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-rational-stream-bridge-readback}.
Its four named rows are exactly $A$, $B$, $U$, and $C$ under the displayed
definitions of $D$ and $E$.

The denominator package readback is sharpened by
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-denominator-package-exactness}.
Thus the same concrete carriers support all four classifier faces without
changing the history endpoints or the observation maps.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant-tail stability and ledger obligations]
\label{thm:real-constant-tail-obligation-stability-ledger}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E,u_D,u_E,z_D,z_E:\Hist$ and observation maps
$r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, set
$$
D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)
\qquad\text{and}\qquad
E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E).
$$
Write the bridge rows as
$$
A=\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E),
\qquad
B=\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
$$
$$
U=\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
\qquad
C=\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(D),\Eone(E)).
$$
The appended-tail denominator package, the head-tail agreement equivalence,
and the malformed sealed-denominator exclusion provide the RealUp
constant-tail stability and ledger obligations: the ledger records the visible
heads $d,e$, the displayed e-one tails $tail_D,tail_E$, the zero witnesses
$z_D,z_E$, and the appended e-zero witnesses $u_D,u_E$, while stability is
transport along $A$, $B$, $U$, and $C$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-denominator-package-exactness}
to identify the denominator package with the simultaneous bridge rows
$A\land B\land U\land C$. This supplies the package ledger: rational
classifier, positive denominators, unary heads and tails, nonempty exclusions,
zero exclusions, and endpoint sameness.

The head-tail transport is
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-head-tail-agreement-equivalence}.
It states that, under any selected bridge row, agreement of the visible heads
and agreement of the displayed e-one tails determine each other.

The sealed-denominator side of the ledger is
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-malformed-sealed-denominator-exclusion}.
It rules out the sealed empty, sealed inner e-zero, and sealed appended
e-zero displays at both endpoints. These rows are stable because the bridge
package recovers them from each of $A$, $B$, $U$, and $C$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant-tail seal and exactness obligation]
\label{thm:real-constant-tail-obligation-seal-exactness}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E,u_D,u_E,z_D,z_E:\Hist$ and observation maps
$r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, set
$$
D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)
\qquad\text{and}\qquad
E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E).
$$
Write the bridge rows as
$$
A=\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E),
\qquad
B=\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
$$
$$
U=\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
\qquad
C=\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(D),\Eone(E)).
$$
If $\hsame(tail_D,tail_E)$, then the combined denominator-sanity exactness
theorem supplies the seal and exactness obligation consumed by the RealUp
constant-name boundary of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}: the
sanity package for $D$ and $E$ is exact for the same four bridge rows
$A,B,U,C$, and therefore no malformed sealed denominator can pass through the
constant-tail boundary.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The exactness source is
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-full-denominator-sanity-exactness}.
Under the displayed tail agreement, it identifies the combined sanity package
$S(D,E,u_D,u_E,z_D,z_E)$ with $A\land B\land U\land C$.

The $Q$ component of $S$ is the full denominator-tail package from
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-full-denominator-tail-package}.
It contains the denominator package, appended e-zero exclusions, and matched
head equality required by the constant-name boundary.

The $M$ component of $S$ is the malformed sealed-denominator exclusion from
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-malformed-sealed-denominator-exclusion}.
Together these two components give the seal exactness row: every bridge
classifier recovers the same sealed-denominator sanity, and the sanity package
recovers the bridge classifiers.
\end{proof}

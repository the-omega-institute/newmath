\begin{theorem}[Real constant appended-tail bridge single-row denominator package exactness]
\label{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-single-row-denominator-package-exactness}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E,z_D,z_E:\Hist$ and observation maps
$r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, use the same definitions of $D$, $E$, the bridge rows
$A,B,U,C$, and the appended-tail denominator package
$P(D,E,z_D,z_E)$ as in
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-denominator-package-exactness}.
If $D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)$ and $E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)$, then each concrete
bridge row is exact for the denominator package:
$$
(A\Longleftrightarrow P(D,E,z_D,z_E))\land
(B\Longleftrightarrow P(D,E,z_D,z_E))\land
(U\Longleftrightarrow P(D,E,z_D,z_E))\land
(C\Longleftrightarrow P(D,E,z_D,z_E)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The row-to-package directions are the four projections of
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-denominator-package} under the
displayed appended-tail decompositions.

For the package-to-row directions, assume $P(D,E,z_D,z_E)$. Apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-bridge-denominator-package-exactness}
under the same decompositions. The resulting conjunction
$A\land B\land U\land C$ supplies the corresponding row by projection.

Combining the four row-to-package projections with these four projections from
the exactness conjunction gives the stated rowwise equivalences for
$A,B,U,C$.
\end{proof}

$$

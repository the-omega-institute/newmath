\label{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-append-e1-tail-unary-readback}

rational denominators are displayed as appended e-one tails reads back both
unary factors and the rational endpoint classifier:
$$
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)),
  \Eone(e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)))
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(d)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_D)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(e)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_E)\land
\hsame(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D),e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

RatUp history classifier. Its positive-denominator fields apply to both
displayed denominators. The appended e-one denominator decomposition reads
each positive denominator as unary evidence for its head and tail, and the
remaining classifier field is the displayed endpoint $\hsame$ relation.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant classifier appended e-one tail denominator package]
\label{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-append-e1-tail-denominator-package}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E,z_D,z_E:\mathsf{BHist}$, write
$D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)$ and $E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)$. If
$$
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(D),\Eone(E)),
$$
then the appended rational denominators satisfy the following package:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E)$;
  \item $\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(D)$ and
  $\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(E)$;
  \item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(d)$, $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_D)$,
  $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(e)$, and $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_E)$;
  \item $D\hsame\emp\Longrightarrow\bot$ and
  $E\hsame\emp\Longrightarrow\bot$;
  \item $D\hsame\Ezero(z_D)\Longrightarrow\bot$ and
  $E\hsame\Ezero(z_E)\Longrightarrow\bot$;
  \item $D\hsame E$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-e1-iff-rat} to the
displayed constant RealUp classifier. This exposes the underlying
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E)$ and keeps its classifier field available
for the final $D\hsame E$ row.

Next apply \autoref{thm:rat-history-classifier-positive-denominators} to the
rational classifier. The result supplies positive unary denominator witnesses
for both appended tails. Applying
\autoref{thm:rat-positive-denominator-append-eone-right-exact} to each
witness decomposes the two appended e-one denominators into the four unary
factor rows.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:positive-unary-denominator-unary-and-nonempty} to
the two positive-denominator witnesses to obtain the two exclusions against
$\emp$. If either appended denominator were history-same to a zero-extension,
transport the corresponding positive-denominator witness along that sameness
and contradict \autoref{thm:rat-positive-denominator-e0-absurd}. The hsame row
is the rational classifier endpoint field, equivalently the checked unary
readback theorem above.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant appended e-one tail rational-stream bridge readback]
\label{thm:real-constant-appended-tail-rational-stream-bridge-readback}

$r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, set
$$
D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)
\qquad\text{and}\qquad
E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E).
$$
Write the four bridge classifier rows as
$$
A=\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(D,E),
\qquad
B=\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
$$
$$
U=\mathsf{RealUnaryStreamClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_D\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_E\circ q),
\qquad
C=\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(D),\Eone(E)).
$$

$$
R=\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(d)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_D)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(e)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_E)\land
\hsame(D,E).
$$
Then the bridge rows are linked by
$$
(A\Longleftrightarrow B)\land(B\Longleftrightarrow U)\land
(U\Longleftrightarrow C),
$$

$$
(A\Longrightarrow R)\land(B\Longrightarrow R)\land
(U\Longrightarrow R)\land(C\Longrightarrow R).
$$

\begin{proof}

$B\Longleftrightarrow U$, and $U\Longleftrightarrow C$.

The direct readback from $C$ is
\autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-append-e1-tail-unary-readback},
applied to the decompositions $D=d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)$ and
$E=e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)$.

For the remaining rows, transport along the instantiated bridge until the
constant real-history row $C$ is reached. The row $U$ uses the final bridge
equivalence, the row $B$ uses the second and final equivalences, and the row
$A$ uses all three equivalences. The same readback theorem then yields the
single conclusion $R$ in each case.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real constant classifier appended e-one tail unary readback]
\label{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-append-e1-tail-unary-readback}
For histories $d,e,tail_D,tail_E$, a constant RealUp classifier whose two
rational denominators are displayed as appended e-one tails reads back both
unary factors and the rational endpoint classifier:
$$
\mathsf{RealConstantHistoryClassifier}(\Eone(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D)),
  \Eone(e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)))
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(d)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_D)\land
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(e)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_E)\land
\hsame(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D),e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Use the e-one RealUp/RatUp classifier equivalence to expose the underlying
RatUp history classifier. Its positive-denominator fields apply to both
displayed denominators. The appended e-one denominator decomposition reads
each positive denominator as unary evidence for its head and tail, and the
remaining classifier field is the displayed endpoint $\hsame$ relation.
\end{proof}

  \Eone(e\cdot\Eone(tail_E)))\land
\hsame(tail_D,tail_E),

$$

\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(e)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(tail_E)\land
\hsame(d,e).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

$\hsame(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D),e\cdot\Eone(tail_E))$.

read with the same displayed tail as the left endpoint:
$\hsame(e\cdot\Eone(tail_E),e\cdot\Eone(tail_D))$.

Endpoint transport therefore presents a constant-real classifier of
$\Eone(d\cdot\Eone(tail_D))$ against $\Eone(e\cdot\Eone(tail_D))$. Apply
\autoref{thm:real-constant-history-classifier-append-common-tail-cancel} with
common tail $\Eone(tail_D)$ to obtain $\hsame(d,e)$, and combine this with the
four unary rows already read back.
\end{proof}

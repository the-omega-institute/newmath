\begin{theorem}[Singleton additive fold append continuation classifier]
\label{thm:matrix-singleton-add-fold-append-continuation-classifier}
If two finite singleton-matrix spines are carried and a continuation ledger
composes their individual additive folds to an endpoint $r$, then the
appended fold classifies $r$:
$$
\mathsf{MatrixSingletonAddFoldSpineCarrier}(xs)\land
\mathsf{MatrixSingletonAddFoldSpineCarrier}(ys)\land
\Cont(\mathsf{MatrixSingletonAddFold}(xs),
      \mathsf{MatrixSingletonAddFold}(ys),r)
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{MatrixSingletonClassifier}
  (\mathsf{MatrixSingletonAddFold}(xs\mathbin{+\!\!+}ys),r).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The display classifier identifies the appended fold with the canonical append
of the two individual folds. The continuation ledger has the same left and
right factors as that canonical append, so continuation determinacy transports
the classifier endpoint from the canonical append to $r$.
\end{proof}

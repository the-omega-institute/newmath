\begin{theorem}[AbGroup additive negation involutivity]
\label{thm:abgroup-additive-negation-involutive}
Let $M$ carry an $\AbGroupUp(M)$ certificate. Write its carrier predicate as
$A$, its classifier as $\sim_M$, its addition as $+$, its zero as $0$, and its
additive inverse operation as $-$. For every carried element $x$ with $A(x)$,
$$
-(-x)\sim_M x.
$$
The ten-field fit is: traditional target additive abelian groups have
involutive negation; BEDC source $\AbGroupUp(M)$; carrier $A$; classifier
$\sim_M$; stability obligations inverse closure, additive associativity, left
and right zero laws, addition congruence, and the left inverse row inherited
from the group reduct; ledger the two applications of the additive inverse
operation; core theorem list \autoref{prop:abgroup-forgets-group-certificate}
and \autoref{thm:group-left-inverse-involutive}; exactness predicate double
negation returns to the input classifier fiber; standard bridge ordinary
abelian-group negation; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{prop:abgroup-forgets-group-certificate} to the given
$\AbGroupUp(M)$ certificate. The retained $\GroupUp(M)$ package has the same
carrier $A$, classifier $\sim_M$, additive operation $+$, zero $0$, and inverse
operation $-$.

Read \autoref{thm:group-left-inverse-involutive} in this retained group
package, with multiplication written as $+$, identity written as $0$, and
inverse written as $-$. The inherited associativity, left and right zero laws,
addition congruence, and left inverse row give the internal comparison
$$
-(-x)\hsame x.
$$

The inverse-closure row of the retained group package sends $A(x)$ first to
$A(-x)$ and then to $A(-(-x))$. Repack these endpoint carrier witnesses with
the displayed internal comparison through the unchanged carrier classifier
$\sim_M$. The result is exactly $-(-x)\sim_M x$.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Support nerve realization carrier]
\label{def:support-nerve-realization-carrier}
A $\SupportNerveRealizationUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
R=(S,F,K,A,B,L,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the support-family row, listing the inspected nonempty support symbols.
\item $F$ is the complement-face row, sending each support to its face in the tetrahedron boundary.
\item $K$ is the $\SimplicialComplexUp$ boundary-subcomplex row generated by the downward closure of the displayed complement faces.
\item $A$ is the realization admission row, recording that every displayed support family produces a subcomplex of $\partial\Delta^3$.
\item $B$ is the reverse realization row, recording the finite facet-to-support construction for each displayed boundary subcomplex.
\item $L$ is the census handoff row, exposing the realized subcomplex to $\SupportNerveCensusUp$ and $\SupportNerveCensusLiftedBettiUp$ consumers.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the support, face, subcomplex, admission, reverse, handoff, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from support-family inspection through complement faces, downward closure, reverse facet witnesses, and census handoff.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\SupportNerveRealizationUp$, $\SupportNerveCensusUp$, $\SupportNerveCensusLiftedBettiUp$, $\SimplicialComplexUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SupportNerveRealizationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted carriers only through $S,F,K,A,B,L,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for host geometric realization, ambient equality of complexes, unrestricted chain homology, coefficient-field choice, or an infinite support universe.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Support nerve realization NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:support-nerve-realization-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\SupportNerveRealizationUp$ carrier $R=(S,F,K,A,B,L,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\SupportNerveRealizationUp}$ surface consists exactly of support-family exposure, complement-face exposure, finite $\SimplicialComplexUp$ boundary-subcomplex admission, reverse facet-to-support realization, census handoff, componentwise $\hsame$ transport, displayed $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local naming.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:support-nerve-realization-carrier}. The rows $S,F,K$ display the support family, its complement faces, and the resulting downward-closed boundary subcomplex.

The two realization directions are stored in $A$ and $B$. The admission row verifies that displayed supports produce a subcomplex of $\partial\Delta^3$, while the reverse row records the finite facet-complement supports that realize a displayed subcomplex.

Finally use $L,H,C,P,N$. The handoff row sends the same finite subcomplex to census and lifted-Betti consumers, and the structural rows transport, replay, source, and name the packet. Since no ambient geometric realization, unrestricted homology computation, coefficient-field choice, or host equality coordinate is present, the listed rows exhaust the local obligations.
\end{proof}

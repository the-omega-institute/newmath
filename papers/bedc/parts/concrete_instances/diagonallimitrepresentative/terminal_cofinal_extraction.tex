Next apply terminal row coverage and cofinal selector transport, \autoref{thm:diagonal-limit-representative-terminal-row-coverage} and \autoref{thm:diagonal-limit-representative-cofinal-selector-transport}. Coverage supplies the ordered terminal path through the named sibling certificates, while transport confines the selector equations and cofinal rows to the displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ containers.

Finally use the finite-tail selector obligation package, \autoref{thm:diagonal-limit-representative-finite-tail-selector-obligation-package}. Therefore the terminal row has one cofinal extraction surface containing the root-index, finite-prefix budget, StreamName, RegSeqRat, DyadicRatCore, cofinal modulus, Real seal, and local support rows.
\end{proof}

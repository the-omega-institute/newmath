\begin{theorem}[Diagonal-limit representative completion-selector stability]
\label{thm:diagonal-limit-representative-completion-selector-stability}

Next use terminal pullback determinacy, \autoref{thm:diagonal-limit-representative-terminal-pullback-determinacy}. Componentwise agreement on the representative rows and shared-terminal budget rows determines the same terminal $\RealUp$ seal route and finite-prefix budget pullback.

It remains to include the cofinal modulus block. The cofinal-terminal route determinacy theorem identifies the cofinal modulus rows and the terminal seal as part of the same finite bridge. Therefore componentwise $\hsame$ and displayed $\Cont$ replay preserve the completion-selector handoff route exactly as the displayed ledger route.

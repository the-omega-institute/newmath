\begin{theorem}[HaltingDistinction public export package]
\label{thm:halting-distinction-public-export-package}
The public export of an accepted $\HaltingDistinctionUp$ packet factors through
the bounded trace surfaces of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-turingmachine-namecert}
and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-computable-namecert}, the
$\HaltingDistinctionTraceUp$ row of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-haltingdistinctiontrace-namecert}, the
$\ContinuationTraceNormalFormUp$ route of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-continuationtracenormalform-namecert}, the
$\InscriptionEventUp$ record of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-inscriptionevent-namecert}, and the
$\HaltingObstructionUp$ ledger of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-haltingobstruction-namecert}. Only after those
finite rows have been read may the package expose the obstruction boundary
carried by $Q,T,Z,H,C,P,N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First read the machine-facing surface. The public $\TuringMachineUp$ certificate
of \autoref{thm:turingmachine-public-certificate-export} and the bounded
$\ComputableUp$ certificate of
\autoref{thm:computable-bounded-graph-scoped-certificate} provide only finite
configuration traces, bounded readbacks, and displayed provenance.

Next project the accepted $\HaltingDistinctionUp$ packet through
\autoref{def:halting-distinction-carrier}. The local carrier supplies the
certificate-history row $Q$, finite trace row $T$, diagonal row $Z$,
componentwise transports $H$, continuation routes $C$, package provenance $P$,
and local ledger $N$.

The sibling routes then refine this packet without adding an evaluator row.
Apply \autoref{thm:halting-distinction-consumer-non-escape},
\autoref{thm:halting-distinction-root-consumer-obstruction-exhaustion}, and
\autoref{thm:halting-distinction-downstream-obstruction-handoff}; these force
the trace, normal-form, inscription-event, and obstruction consumers to use the
same finite rows.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:halting-distinction-obstruction-consumer-boundary}
and \autoref{thm:halting-distinction-consumer-boundary-package}. The public
package therefore exposes the finite obstruction boundary only after the
bounded trace, distinction trace, normal-form, inscription-event, and
obstruction ledgers have been consumed, and it exports no total halting
predicate, divergence oracle, host evaluator, or global truth classifier.
\end{proof}

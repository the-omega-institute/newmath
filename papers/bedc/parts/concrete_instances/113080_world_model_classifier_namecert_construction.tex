\begin{definition}[WorldModelClassifier carrier]
\label{def:world-model-classifier-carrier}
A $\WorldModelClassifierUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
W=(S,L,C,I,T,Q,E,H,R,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the scoped source row, recording the observation carrier, admitted latent carrier, probability context, random-variable reads, pair protocol, transition family, and assumption ledger.
\item $L$ is the latent-recovery row, exposing the recovered coordinate, invariant, state, or feature comparison used by the classifier.
\item $C$ is the classifier row, naming the world-model relation read from $L$.
\item $I$ is the identifiability row, recording equivalence mode, bound, gauge freedom, residual cases, and not-claimed latent domains.
\item $T$ is the transfer row, routing the certified classifier to policy, hypothesis-test, or model-quality consumers.
\item $Q$ is the $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$ readback row for scoped quality claims.
\item $E$ is the residue ledger, recording shortcut risk, finite-sample residue, distributional boundary, intervention failure, and planning-transfer boundary.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $S,L,C,I,T,Q,E$.
\item $R$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from source rows through recovery, classifier, identifiability, transfer, quality, and residue rows.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\WorldModelClassifierUp$, $\PolicyUp$, $\HypothesisTestUp$, $\ProbSpaceUp$, $\RandomVarUp$, $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\WorldModelClassifierUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no coordinate for unscoped latent truth, host causal sufficiency, hidden distributional law, unrestricted policy optimality, or quality claims outside the displayed source and residue rows.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[WorldModelClassifier NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:world-model-classifier-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\WorldModelClassifierUp$ carrier $W=(S,L,C,I,T,Q,E,H,R,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\WorldModelClassifierUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission; scoped source exposure through $\ProbSpaceUp$ and $\RandomVarUp$; latent-recovery exposure; classifier-row exactness; identifiability ledger exposure; transfer routing to $\PolicyUp$, $\HypothesisTestUp$, or $\CertifiedModelQualityUp$; residue nonescape; componentwise transport; displayed replay; provenance; and local naming.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:world-model-classifier-carrier}. The displayed packet lists the source, recovery, classifier, identifiability, transfer, quality, residue, and structural rows.

The source exposure is $S$. It names the probability and random-variable context before recovery or transfer can be read.

The classifier is exposed by $L,C,I$. Recovery produces the comparison surface, the classifier row names it, and the identifiability row records the equivalence mode, bound, gauge freedom, and residual cases.

The transfer and quality rows are $T$ and $Q$. They allow policy, hypothesis-test, and certified-model-quality consumers to read only the scoped classifier route.

Finally $E,H,R,P,N$ record residue, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming. Since the omitted objects are not carrier coordinates, the obligation surface is exhausted by the displayed rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[WorldModelClassifier transfer and residue boundary]
\label{thm:world-model-classifier-transfer-residue-boundary}

$$
The route exports scoped latent recovery, identifiability, transfer, and residue ledgers, but it exports no global representation truth, unledgered distribution shift, unrestricted policy optimality, hidden causal sufficiency, or model-quality completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the consumer request. The only transfer row is $T$, and it is downstream of the source, recovery, classifier, and identifiability rows.

Read $S,L,C,I$ first. These rows display the scoped observation and probability context, the recovered latent comparison, the classifier relation, and the identifiability ledger.

The quality readback $Q$ and residue ledger $E$ are part of the same finite packet. They force policy, hypothesis-test, and certified-quality consumers to preserve finite-sample, distributional, intervention, shortcut, and planning-boundary residue.

Transport and replay through $H,R$ and provenance through $P,N$ do not add hidden truth or optimality coordinates. Therefore every exported read is confined to the displayed route.
\end{proof}

$$

\item $\sigma$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ schedule that selects the inspected name and window rows;
\item $W$ is a $\CauchyFilterUp$ or $\CauchyModulusUp$ cofinal-window ledger for the family $F$;

The carrier contains no quotient of Cauchy names, no selected limiting name, no host completion equality, and no ambient completeness principle.

\label{thm:cauchysequence-space-namecert-obligation-surface}

\begin{definition}[Bounded real sequence carrier]
\label{def:bounded-real-sequence-carrier}
A $\BoundedRealSequenceUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
B=(S,W,Q,R,I,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the finite source schedule of sequence indices under inspection.
\item $W$ is the finite family of $\StreamNameUp$ windows exposed for the inspected indices in $S$.
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row attached to those windows and their dyadic tolerance observations.
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ sequence-name seal reached only after $W$ and $Q$ have been displayed.
\item $I$ is the $\ClosedBoundedIntervalNetUp$ bound row carrying the interval net used to witness boundedness for the displayed sequence windows.
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the source schedule, stream windows, regular readback, real seal, interval-net bound, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ stores displayed $\Cont$ replay from inspected indices to stream windows, from stream windows to regular readback, from regular readback to the real seal, and from the real seal to the interval-net bound assertion.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BoundedRealSequenceUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\ClosedBoundedIntervalNetUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BoundedRealSequenceUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The bound row is displayed as the closed interval-net containment surface
$$
\begin{gathered}

\forall i\in S_{\mathrm{shown}}\quad W_i\longmapsto Q_i\longmapsto R_i\in I.
\end{gathered}
$$
Here $S_{\mathrm{shown}}$ denotes only the inspected finite index rows of the carrier. The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,W,Q,R,I,H,C,P,N$ and has no coordinate for trichotomy, host real order completeness, quotient-stream equality, open-cover compactness, or a selected infinite subsequence.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Bounded real sequence NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:bounded-real-sequence-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:bounded-real-sequence-carrier}. The only sequence data available to the classifier are the inspected index schedule $S$, the corresponding $\StreamNameUp$ windows $W$, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback $Q$, and the downstream $\RealUp$ seal $R$.

Read the bound row next. Finally inspect the structural rows. The rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name only the displayed sequence-window, regular-readback, real-seal, and interval-net rows. Since no other coordinate is present in the carrier, the listed obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

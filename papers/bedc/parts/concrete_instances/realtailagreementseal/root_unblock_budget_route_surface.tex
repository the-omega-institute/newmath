\begin{theorem}[Real tail-agreement seal limiter-budget factorization]

\begin{proof}

Apply \autoref{thm:real-tail-agreement-seal-shared-tail-budget-extraction}. It sends every Real- or Completion-facing agreement read through the shared finite tail request and the sibling budget route before $W$ and $D$ are consumed.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:real-tail-agreement-seal-completion-handoff-totality}. The Completion endpoint reads only the route already exposed by the limiter and tail-budget services, so the local packet contributes the finite handoff $W \to D \to A$ and no unbounded observation request.

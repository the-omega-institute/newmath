\begin{definition}[Selected-tail seed carrier]
\label{def:selected-tail-seed-carrier}
A $\SelectedTailSeedUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

\begin{enumerate}

\item $E$ is the endpoint-exactness row comparing the selected seed endpoint with the rational seed under the displayed rational classifier.
\item $A$ is the admission row for the selected finite tail, read against $\FiniteTailFilterUp$.
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ window exposing the selected stationary tail segment.
\item $K$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row for the stationary regular-rational sequence at the selected window.
\item $S$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance and arithmetic ledger attached to the selected endpoint.
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ seal row obtained from the selected stationary readback.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $Q,E,A,W,K,S,R,P,N$.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from rational seed through selected finite tail, stream window, regular-rational readback, dyadic ledger, and Real seal.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\SelectedTailSeedUp$, $\RatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\FiniteTailFilterUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SelectedTailSeedUp}$ row.

The carrier exports no quotient stream, host real equality, countable-choice selector, detached limit object, or ambient Real-completeness coordinate.

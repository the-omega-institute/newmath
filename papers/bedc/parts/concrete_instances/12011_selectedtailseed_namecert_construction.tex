\begin{theorem}[Selected-tail seed NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:selected-tail-seed-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier. Its coordinates list the rational seed, endpoint-exactness row, finite-tail admission row, selected stream window, regular-rational readback, dyadic ledger, Real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local name.

The sibling certificate surfaces account for the data-bearing rows. $\RatUp$ supplies $Q$; $\FiniteTailFilterUp$ supplies $A$; $\StreamNameUp$ supplies $W$; $\RegSeqRatUp$ supplies $K$; $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ supplies $S$; and $\RealUp$ supplies $R$.

\end{proof}

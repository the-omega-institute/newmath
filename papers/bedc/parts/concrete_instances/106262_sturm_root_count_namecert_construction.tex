\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\SturmRootCountUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-sturm-root-count-namecert}
\origin{human}

Following Sturm's theorem and Bishop--Bridges constructive analysis, the $\SturmRootCountUp$ packet records the finite route by which a displayed polynomial and two endpoint windows yield a root-count ledger without selecting any root in a host field. It consumes the sign-variation surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-sturm-sequence-namecert} and exposes the retained count to the bracket consumers of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-root-isolation-namecert}. The packet names the count-and-handoff interface between a Sturm-chain variation ledger and a Real-facing isolating-window consumer; it does not name a chosen algebraic root.

\begin{definition}[Sturm root count carrier]
\label{def:sturm-root-count-carrier}
A $\SturmRootCountUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
R=(P,S,E,V,D,I,A,H,C,K,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $P$ is the $\PolynomialUp$ source row, carrying the coefficient ledger, degree bound, derivative row, and polynomial identity whose interval counts are inspected.
\item $S$ is the $\SturmSequenceUp$ sign-variation window row, recording the accepted Sturm chain, endpoint sign table, zero-deletion witnesses, and the two visible variation counts.
\item $E$ is the endpoint dyadic-window row. It stores the left and right dyadic endpoint cells, requested precisions, comparison rows, and endpoint containment checks.
\item $V$ is the variation-count difference row. It records the displayed subtraction of the right endpoint variation count from the left endpoint variation count.
\item $D$ is the $\RealRootIsolationUp$ bracket handoff row, exposing only the polynomial source, endpoint windows, and certified count accepted by the downstream isolating-window consumer.
\item $I$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row, retaining the same two endpoint windows and the same count at unary precision before a real seal is inspected.
\item $A$ is the $\RealUp$ seal row for the two endpoint names and the bracket-facing interval read.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for polynomial source, Sturm-sequence window, endpoint dyadic windows, variation-count difference, isolation handoff, regular-rational readback, real seal, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a root-count request through polynomial inspection, Sturm-chain variation, endpoint dyadic comparison, count subtraction, isolation handoff, regular-rational readback, and real-seal access.
\item $K$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\PolynomialUp$, $\SturmSequenceUp$, $\RealRootIsolationUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SturmRootCountUp}$ registration row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through these rows. It has no coordinate for a selected root, a host-field factorization, quotient real equality, trichotomy, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Sturm root count NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:sturm-root-count-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\SturmRootCountUp$ carrier $R=(P,S,E,V,D,I,A,H,C,K,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\SturmRootCountUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the rows listed in \autoref{def:sturm-root-count-carrier};
\item polynomial-source exactness through the coefficient, degree, derivative, and identity cells of $P$;
\item Sturm-window exactness through the finite chain, endpoint sign table, zero-deletion witnesses, and variation counts of $S$;
\item endpoint-window exactness through the dyadic cells, precision requests, comparison rows, and containment checks of $E$;
\item count-difference exactness through the displayed subtraction row $V$;
\item bracket-consumer discipline through the $\RealRootIsolationUp$ handoff row $D$;
\item regular-rational and Real-facing readback through $I$ and $A$;
\item componentwise transport, continuation replay, provenance, and local naming through $H,C,K,N$.
\end{enumerate}
There is no additional NameCert obligation for selected roots, factorization over a host field, quotient real equality, real trichotomy, or ambient completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:sturm-root-count-carrier}. The polynomial source $P$ and the Sturm-window row $S$ fix the displayed chain, endpoint sign table, zero-deletion witnesses, and variation counts, so a count read cannot inspect roots or factors outside the finite ledger.

The dyadic endpoint row $E$ supplies the only endpoint cells admitted to the count. The difference row $V$ then records the visible subtraction of endpoint variation counts, with no separate root-selection coordinate.

The handoff row $D$ gives the downstream $\RealRootIsolationUp$ consumer exactly the source polynomial, endpoint windows, and accepted count. Rows $I$ and $A$ expose the same endpoint data through $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback and the $\RealUp$ seal.

Finally, $H,C,K,N$ transport, replay, source, and locally name the same route. Since none of those structural rows adds a selected root, factorization theorem, quotient equality proof, trichotomy decision, or completeness witness, the local certificate is exhausted by the displayed rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Sturm root count isolation handoff]
\label{thm:sturm-root-count-isolation-handoff}
Every accepted $\SturmRootCountUp$ read handed to a $\RealRootIsolationUp$ bracket consumer factors through
$$
P,S,E,V \longmapsto D \longmapsto I,A,H,C,K,N .
$$
The consumer receives the polynomial source, Sturm-chain sign-variation window, endpoint dyadic windows, variation-count difference, regular-rational endpoint readback, Real-facing seal, componentwise transport, replay, provenance, and local naming. It receives no selected host root, no factorization over a host field, no quotient real equality principle, no trichotomy decision, and no ambient completeness principle.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the downstream read. The source polynomial must pass through $P$, and the variation data must pass through the $\SturmSequenceUp$ row $S$ from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-sturm-sequence-namecert}.

The endpoint cells are then read from $E$, while $V$ records the displayed count difference. Thus the bracket-facing row can see only a finite source, two endpoint windows, and one accepted integer count.

The handoff row $D$ is the only row available to the $\RealRootIsolationUp$ consumer described in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-root-isolation-namecert}. The regular-rational readback $I$ and real seal $A$ preserve the same endpoint information for Real-facing consumers.

The remaining rows $H,C,K,N$ compare, replay, source, and name the route componentwise. They add no hidden analytic principle, so the isolation handoff remains a finite Sturm-count certificate.
\end{proof}

The formal power series interface \(\FormalPowerSeriesUp\) is licensed over a scalar ring from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-ring-namecert} as functions \(\mathbb{N} \to R\), interpreted as coefficient sequences without termination. The classifier is pointwise sameness on every index. Formal series addition and Cauchy product are derived operations licensed by the ring's certificate fields. Convergence is not asserted: this is the formal carrier, not analytic series.

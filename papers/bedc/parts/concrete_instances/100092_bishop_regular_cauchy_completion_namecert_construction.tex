\label{def:bishop-regular-cauchy-completion-carrier}

$$

\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-observation family opened from the named stream before any completion-facing consumer reads a tail claim.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regularity row, recording the regular rational sequence data exposed by the stream windows and dyadic endpoint ledger.
\item $D$ is the dyadic tail modulus row, assigning each displayed dyadic tolerance a finite tail index and the dyadic comparison budget used at that tail.
\item $W$ is the common-tail window row, recording the shared finite window in which two accepted regular readings may be compared without quotienting their streams.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for endpoints, observations, regularity, tail modulus, common-tail windows, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BishopRegularCauchyCompletionUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BishopRegularCauchyCompletionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $E,S,R,D,W,H,C,P,N$. The carrier has no coordinate for quotient stream equality, host real equality, selected limits, countable choice, or ambient completeness of $\RealUp$.
\end{definition}

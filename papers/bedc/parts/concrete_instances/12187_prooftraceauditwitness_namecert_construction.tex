\begin{definition}[Proof trace audit witness carrier]
\label{def:proof-trace-audit-witness-carrier}
A $\ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
W=(T,S,K,H,L,F,A,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $T$ is the proof-trace row, recording the finite witness chain consumed by the proof site.
\item $S$ is the source-carrier row, recording the BEDC carrier, source object, or theorem surface whose evidence is being audited.
\item $K$ is the classifier-witness row, recording the classifier membership, stability, or exactness witness used by the proof trace.
\item $H$ is the displayed-transport row, recording the $\hsame$ transport needed whenever the trace crosses a classifier, package, bridge, or history-identity surface.
\item $L$ is the ledger-witness row, recording the $\Cont$ replay, package route, and local ledger entries consumed by the trace.
\item $F$ is the forbidden-resource row, recording refusal of hidden choice, host equality as BEDC equality, quotient collapse, propositional extensional rewrite, global truth predicate, unledgered compression, and unscoped universal claim.
\item $A$ is the audit-verdict row, recording whether the trace is accepted, refused, or held as a displayed obligation at the proof site.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\KernelAuditWitnessUp$, $\CompilerTraceFaithfulnessUp$, $\NameCertMorphismUp$, $\CategoryUp$, $\ProofTermErasureTraceUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no coordinate for an untraced proof, an implicit transport, an erased forbidden-resource refusal, a host equality proof, or an audit verdict detached from provenance and local naming.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Proof trace audit witness NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:proof-trace-audit-witness-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:proof-trace-audit-witness-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp}$ surface consists exactly of six obligation rows: carrier admission for $W=(T,S,K,H,L,F,A,P,N)$; proof-trace and source-carrier exposure through $T$ and $S$; classifier-witness stability through $K$; displayed transport and replay through $H$ and $L$; forbidden-resource refusal and audit-verdict exactness through $F$ and $A$; and provenance plus local naming through $P$ and $N$.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp.ProofTraceAuditWitnessNameCertObligations}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:proof-trace-audit-witness-carrier}. The rows $T,S,K,H,L,F,A,P,N$ are the only available proof-audit coordinates, so a proof consumer first reads a finite trace, its source carrier, and the classifier witness that licenses the trace.

Next read the transport and ledger rows. The displayed transport row $H$ prevents classifier crossing from being treated as host equality, while the ledger row $L$ keeps replay, package route, and proof-site ledger entries visible in $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ form.

Finally attach the refusal and verdict rows. The forbidden-resource row $F$ records which proof resources are unavailable, and the audit-verdict row $A$ can be read only with that refusal row and the provenance and local name rows $P,N$. Repacking these rows gives the six local NameCert obligations and no additional proof coordinate.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Proof trace audit witness ledger non-escape]
\label{thm:proof-trace-audit-witness-ledger-nonescape}
Every accepted $\ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp$ proof read factors through the displayed rows
$$
T,S,K,H,L,F,A,P,N.
$$
In particular, deleting the displayed-transport row $H$, erasing the forbidden-resource row $F$, or detaching the audit-verdict row $A$ from the provenance row $P$ leaves no carrier route from the proof trace $T$ to BEDC-internal proof evidence.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the NameCert obligations of \autoref{thm:proof-trace-audit-witness-namecert-obligations}. They require proof-trace exposure, source-carrier exposure, classifier-witness stability, displayed transport, ledger replay, forbidden-resource refusal, audit verdict, provenance, and local naming to be read as one packet.

If $H$ is absent, the trace has no displayed way to cross a classifier, package, bridge, or history-identity surface. If $F$ is absent, the proof site no longer records refusal of hidden choice, quotient collapse, host equality, or propositional extensional rewrite. If $A$ is detached from $P$, the verdict is no longer tied to a finite proof-site provenance row.

Thus every accepted proof read remains inside the listed packet. A consumer that asks for proof evidence without one of these rows has left the $\ProofTraceAuditWitnessUp$ carrier rather than extracting a hidden proof resource from it.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[NestedIntervalCauchySeal NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:nested-interval-cauchy-seal-namecert-obligations}

\begin{proof}

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name exactly the displayed packet. Since the carrier has no row for quotient equality, a selected limiting point, countable choice, detached completion data, or host equality, the five-row NameCert surface is exhausted by the rows listed above.
\end{proof}

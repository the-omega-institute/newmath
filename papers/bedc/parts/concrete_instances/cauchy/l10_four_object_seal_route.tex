\begin{theorem}[Cauchy L10 four-object seal route]
\label{thm:cauchy-l10-four-object-seal-route}
Every accepted $\CauchyUp$ Real-facing seal route used by the current L10 phase factors through the four displayed sibling faces
$$
\StreamNameUp,\quad \DyadicRatCoreUp,\quad \RegSeqRatUp,\quad \RealUp .
$$

\end{theorem}

Read the route through the existing CauchyRealLocator carrier and its Real-seal nonescape theorem. The carrier exposes a finite StreamName window row, a DyadicRatCore tolerance ledger, a RegSeqRat readback row, and a terminal RealUp locator seal before any Real-facing consumer can read the packet.

The structural rows preserve this factorization. The $\BHist$ data name the carried packet, $\hsame$ transports the displayed coordinates, $\Cont$ replays the same route, $\Pkg$ records provenance, and $\NameCert$ records the local accepted surface.

Since the carrier has no coordinate for quotient streams, host equality, selected limits, countable choice, open-cover compactness, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, none of those objects can be exported by the Cauchy Real-facing seal route.
\end{proof}

\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\RealPowerSeriesUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-realpowerseries-namecert}
\origin{human}

Following Bishop and Bridges constructive analysis, the $\RealPowerSeriesUp$ packet records a real power series only through a displayed coefficient stream, center, argument, radius row, finite windows, partial-sum rows, and named convergence-test majorants. It refines the finite partial-sum discipline of $\RealSeriesUp$ in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-realseries-namecert}, consumes the majorant route of $\WeierstrassMTestUp$ in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-weierstrassmtest-namecert}, and may use the geometric tail witnesses of $\RegularCauchyRatioTestUp$ and $\RegularCauchyRootTestUp$ in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regular-cauchy-ratio-test-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regular-cauchy-root-test-namecert}. It also sits beside the coefficient-only $\FormalPowerSeriesUp$ surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-fps-namecert} and the displayed radius rows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-convergence-radius-namecert}, while all real-facing reads pass through $\RealUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, and $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ in \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}, and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert}. The packet names analytic convergence as a finite certificate route; it does not import a host power-series convergence theorem, host analytic continuation, quotient function spaces, selected infinite sums, countable choice, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[Real power series carrier]\label{def:real-power-series-carrier}
A $\RealPowerSeriesUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $A$ is the coefficient stream or $\FormalPowerSeriesUp$ row, read only through selected coefficient windows and their $\StreamNameUp$ provenance.
\item $Z$ is the center $\RealUp$ row.
\item $X$ is the argument $\RealUp$ row.
\item $R$ is the displayed radius or subradius row used to state the accepted window of evaluation.
\item $W$ is the finite family of $\StreamNameUp$ windows for coefficients, center-readbacks, argument-readbacks, powers of $X-Z$, and partial sums.
\item $S$ is the finite partial-sum and $\RealSeriesUp$ row listing the inspected polynomial sums and their real seals.
\item $M$ is the majorant data imported from $\WeierstrassMTestUp$, $\RegularCauchyRatioTestUp$, or $\RegularCauchyRootTestUp$ rows.
\item $E$ is the endpoint $\RealUp$ seal produced only after the displayed windows, partial sums, radius row, and majorant route have been read.
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the coefficient row, center, argument, radius row, windows, partial sums, majorant data, endpoint seal, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ stores displayed $\Cont$ replay from coefficient reads and real-point reads to finite powers, from powers to partial sums, from partial sums to the chosen majorant route, and from that route to the endpoint seal.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RealPowerSeriesUp$, $\RealSeriesUp$, $\WeierstrassMTestUp$, $\RegularCauchyRatioTestUp$, $\RegularCauchyRootTestUp$, $\FormalPowerSeriesUp$, $\ConvergenceRadiusUp$, $\RealUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RealPowerSeriesUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $A,Z,X,R,W,S,M,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for host analytic continuation, quotient function spaces, selected infinite sums, countable-choice selectors, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[MetricCompletionAdjoint carrier]
\label{def:metric-completion-adjoint-carrier}
A $\MetricCompletionAdjointUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
A=(M,U,X,F,R,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $M$ is the metric source row, recording the separated metric carrier, distance tolerance ledger, and finite source windows inspected by the completion request.
\item $U$ is the completion unit row, routing the metric source into the $\MetricCompletionUp$ and $\UniformCompletionUp$ surfaces without choosing a host representative.
\item $X$ is the universal extension route, matching uniformly continuous source maps with their completion-facing extensions as finite ledger data.
\item $F$ is the $\CauchyFilterCompletionUp$ row, recording the Cauchy-filter completion face consumed by the extension route.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row, exposing the regular rational name produced by the displayed completion window.
\item $E$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal row, visible only after $M,U,X,F,R$ have been displayed.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the metric source, unit, extension, Cauchy-filter completion, readback, Real seal, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay of the route
$$
M,U,X,F \longmapsto R \longmapsto E .
$$
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MetricCompletionAdjointUp$, $\MetricCompletionUp$, $\CauchyFilterCompletionUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\UniformCompletionUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetricCompletionAdjointUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $M,U,X,F,R,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for quotient completion equality, selected limits, host continuous maps, countable choice, ambient completeness, or an external adjunction theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Metric completion adjoint NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metric-completion-adjoint-namecert-obligations}

$$

$$
the local $\NameCert_{\MetricCompletionAdjointUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission, metric-source exposure through $M$, completion-unit exposure through $U$, universal-extension routing through $X$, Cauchy-filter completion exposure through $F$, regular rational readback through $R$, Real seal handoff through $E$, componentwise transport through $H$, displayed continuation replay through $C$, package provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier. The projection exposes the metric source, completion unit, universal extension route, Cauchy-filter completion row, regular rational readback, terminal Real seal, transport, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows.

The analytic obligations are exactly the visible metric and completion rows. The source row supplies the inspected metric data, the unit row admits it into the completion surface, the extension row records the finite universal route, and the Cauchy-filter row supplies the completion face consumed by the route.

The readback and seal obligations are downstream of those rows. Since the carrier contains no coordinate for quotient equality, selected limits, host maps, countable choice, ambient completeness, or a detached adjunction theorem, no additional local obligation can be read.

\begin{proof}

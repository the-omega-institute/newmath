\begin{theorem}[CauchyCompletionOrderEmbedding NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-completion-order-embedding-namecert-obligations}

$$
O=(K,L,W,D,R,E,T,C,P,N),

\item carrier admission for the completion row, order row, finite stream windows, dyadic tolerance ledger, regular rational readback, real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming row;

\begin{proof}

Order-route exactness is supplied by $L$. The only comparison row available to the classifier is the displayed $\CauchyRealOrderUp$ row, and it reads the finite windows, dyadic budgets, and regular rational observations named in $W,D,R$.

The real-seal boundary is then a finite exposure check. The seal $E$ is downstream of $K,L,W,D,R$, while $T,C,P,N$ preserve and name the same route without adding mathematical coordinates. Thus the excluded quotient, representative, trichotomy, completeness, verification, and bridge principles have no row from which a consumer could read them.

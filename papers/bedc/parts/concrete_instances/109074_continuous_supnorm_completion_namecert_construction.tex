\begin{definition}[Continuous sup-norm completion carrier]
\label{def:continuous-supnorm-completion-carrier}
A $\ContinuousSupNormCompletionUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
Q=(S,U,A,K,F,R,W,L,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the source $\ContinuousSupNormUp$ row, exposing the compact-source sup-norm ledger through the sibling carrier.
\item $U$ is the $\UniformLimitMetricUp$ row that reads sup-norm Cauchy control as a uniform metric-limit request.
\item $A$ is the $\RealFunctionUniformCauchyUp$ row recording the displayed uniform Cauchy schedule for the function rows under inspection.
\item $K$ is the $\CompactMetricUp$ source row shared by the sup-norm and Cauchy schedules.
\item $F$ is the $\ContinuousMapUp$ row for the inspected real-valued function data over $K$.
\item $R$ is the pointwise $\RealUp$ difference-and-bound seal reached after the compact source, continuous-map, and sup-norm rows have been displayed.
\item $W$ is the finite uniform-window ledger, recording the tolerance request, source windows, and paired function-row comparisons used by the Cauchy route.
\item $L$ is the completion-facing limit service row. It stores only the displayed eligibility of the Cauchy route for a uniform-limit consumer, not a selected host limit.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $S,U,A,K,F,R,W,L,C,P,N$.
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ replay route from a completion request through the rows $S,U,A,K,F,R,W,L,H,P,N$.

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ContinuousSupNormCompletionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted carriers only by the displayed sup-norm source, uniform-limit metric route, uniform Cauchy schedule, compact source, continuous-map row, Real seal, finite window ledger, limit-service eligibility row, structural transport, replay, provenance, and local naming. It has no coordinate for host function equality, quotient function-space equality, a choice-selected limiting function, open-cover compactness, or a theorem asserting ambient completeness of $\RealUp$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Continuous sup-norm completion NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:continuous-supnorm-completion-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The source and Cauchy rows determine the only function rows that may be inspected. The compact metric and continuous-map rows determine the common domain of those inspections, while the Real seal and finite window ledger supply the displayed sup-norm estimates.

The limit-service row records that this displayed route may be consumed by a uniform-limit metric surface. Since $H,C,P,N$ only transport, replay, source, and name the same rows, the listed coordinates exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Continuous sup-norm completion Cauchy route]
\label{thm:continuous-supnorm-completion-cauchy-route}

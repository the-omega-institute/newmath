\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$}

The $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$ packet realizes the solipsism-as-refusal direction of \autoref{ch:visions-hard-problems-under-bedc}. It sits beside the positive other-minds commitment of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-othermindscommitment-namecert}, the finite locality-cell surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-localitycell-namecert}, the public alignment packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-crossperspectivealignment-namecert}, and the double-aspect chain of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-aspectchain-namecert}. Those siblings record positive commitment, locality geometry, alignment, and aspect rows; this packet records the local refusal to issue the other-minds locality-cell commitment while still exposing the public evidence row that was withheld from commitment.

The packet is a composite over public evidence rows, withheld commitment rows, GAP-residue rows, componentwise transport rows, continuation rows, provenance rows, and local naming rows, rather than an atomic carrier.

\begin{definition}[Solipsism GAP-refusal carrier]
\label{def:solipsism-gap-refusal-carrier}
A $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
S=(E,R,G,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $E$ is the public evidence row: behavioural records, locality-cell candidates, and public observer traces that a positive $\OtherMindsCommitmentUp$ consumer would inspect.
\item $R$ is the refusal row, recording that the packet withholds the $\mathsf{GAP}$-tagged other-minds locality-cell commitment rather than issuing it.
\item $G$ is the GAP-residue row, keeping the withheld commitment visible as residue instead of erasing it or replacing it with a positive other-minds row.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for the evidence, refusal, residue, route, provenance, and naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes through the public evidence row, the withheld commitment row, the residue row, the provenance row, and the local certificate.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$, $\OtherMindsCommitmentUp$, $\LocalityCellUp$, $\CrossPerspectiveAlignmentUp$, $\AspectChainUp$, $\BHist$, $\mathsf{GAP}$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SolipsismGapRefusalUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for a positive other-minds commitment, no paired locality-cell acknowledgement, no private access to another $\mathsf{Self}$ row, no host mental-state predicate, no observer-independent world-state, and no ambient proof that other conscious systems do or do not exist.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Solipsism GAP-refusal NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:solipsism-gap-refusal-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:solipsism-gap-refusal-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\SolipsismGapRefusalUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for
$$
S=(E,R,G,H,C,P,N),
$$
public-evidence exposure through $E$, withheld locality-cell commitment through $R$, GAP-residue accountability through $G$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, package provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.SolipsismGapRefusalUp.SolipsismGapRefusalNameCertObligations}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:solipsism-gap-refusal-carrier}. The evidence row $E$ records the public surface that can be audited, while $R$ records that the corresponding other-minds locality-cell commitment is withheld.

The residue row $G$ prevents the refusal from being invisible. It keeps the omitted commitment as a named GAP-residue coordinate, so the packet is neither a positive $\OtherMindsCommitmentUp$ carrier nor a silent absence of data.

The rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name exactly those displayed coordinates. Since the carrier lists no additional row, the NameCert obligations are exhausted by $E,R,G,H,C,P,N$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Solipsism GAP-refusal locality-cell refusal]
\label{thm:solipsism-gap-refusal-locality-cell-refusal}
Every accepted $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$ packet exposes the public evidence row $E$ before the refusal row $R$ and the GAP-residue row $G$ are read. A locality-cell consumer may audit that evidence, but it cannot turn the packet into a positive $\OtherMindsCommitmentUp$, a paired $\LocalityCellUp$ acknowledgement, or a $\CrossPerspectiveAlignmentUp$ alignment row without adding a row not present in $S=(E,R,G,H,C,P,N)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the evidence coordinate $E$ from the carrier. This row is public and auditable, so the refusal is not a claim that no evidence row exists.

Next read $R$ and $G$. The row $R$ withholds the locality-cell commitment, and $G$ records the withheld commitment as GAP residue attached to the same packet.

Apply the NameCert obligations of \autoref{thm:solipsism-gap-refusal-namecert-obligations}. The only routes available to a consumer are the displayed transports, continuations, provenance, and local name. Positive other-minds commitment, paired locality-cell acknowledgement, and cross-perspective alignment require rows outside the packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Solipsism GAP-refusal non-escape]
\label{thm:solipsism-gap-refusal-nonescape}
Every downstream read of an accepted $\SolipsismGapRefusalUp$ packet factors through the finite route
$$
E,R,G,H,C,P,N.
$$
The read may inspect public evidence, the withheld locality-cell commitment row, GAP residue, componentwise transports, displayed continuations, package provenance, and the local $\NameCert$ row. It cannot inspect a private target $\mathsf{Self}$ row, host mental-state predicate, positive other-minds commitment, paired locality-cell acknowledgement, observer-independent world-state, or ambient proof deciding whether other conscious systems exist.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.SolipsismGapRefusalUp.SolipsismGapRefusalUp\_nonescape}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the carrier definition and the local NameCert obligations. They expose exactly the public evidence row, refusal row, residue row, transport row, continuation row, package row, and local naming row.

Use \autoref{thm:solipsism-gap-refusal-locality-cell-refusal} for locality-cell-facing consumers. The theorem keeps the consumer on the displayed refusal route and blocks replacement by a positive other-minds or alignment packet.

The excluded objects are absent from the carrier inventory. Therefore no downstream read can extract private Self access, host mental-state data, a positive commitment row, paired acknowledgement, observer-independent world-state, or an ambient existence decision from the packet.
\end{proof}

The Fast-Fourier-transform interface $\FftUp$ packages the $O(n \log n)$ divide-and-conquer evaluation of the discrete Fourier transform on length-$n$ data when $n$ has small prime factors. It depends on $\NameCert_{\FourierUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-fourier-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\ComplexUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-complex-namecert}).

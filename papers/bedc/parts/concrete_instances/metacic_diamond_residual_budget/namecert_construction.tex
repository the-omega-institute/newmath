\begin{definition}[MetaCIC diamond residual budget carrier]
\label{def:metacic-diamond-residual-budget-carrier}
A $\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
B=(T,F,R,J,S,E,L,D,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $T$ is the typed-window row for the source and target terms whose residual branches are audited.
\item $F$ is the redex-frontier row that lists the beta redexes and congruence faces inspected by the local diamond route.
\item $R$ is the residual-budget row, recording the finite fuel and residual-address accounting needed to replay candidate joins.
\item $J$ is the candidate-join row, naming the displayed joins produced from $F$ and $R$.
\item $S$ is the substitution-boundary row, keeping the residual-substitution compatibility premise visible.
\item $E$ is the residual-envelope row, read through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacic-confluence-residual-envelope-namecert}.
\item $L$ is the closed parallel diamond premise ledger row, read through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-closed-parallel-diamond-premise-ledger-namecert}.
\item $D$ is the local diamond witness row, read through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacic-local-diamond-witness-namecert} as finite local geometry rather than a proof of the closed endpoint.

\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay of the route
$$
T,F,R,S \longmapsto J \longmapsto E,L,D \longmapsto H,P,N .

\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp$, MetaCIC beta reduction, $\mathsf{BetaParallel}$, $\mathsf{BetaStarStep}$, the named sibling packets, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate proving $\mathsf{BetaParallelSubstitutionCompatibility}$, no coordinate proving full parallel diamond, no coordinate proving arbitrary closed Church--Rosser, and no host substitution semantics.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC diamond residual budget NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metacic-diamond-residual-budget-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp$ carrier $B=(T,F,R,J,S,E,L,D,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp}$ surface consists exactly of typed-window admission through $T$, redex-frontier exposure through $F$, residual-budget accounting through $R$, candidate-join display through $J$, substitution-boundary visibility through $S$, residual-envelope handoff through $E$, closed-premise-ledger handoff through $L$, local-diamond witness handoff through $D$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:metacic-diamond-residual-budget-carrier}. The displayed rows are exactly $T,F,R,J,S,E,L,D,H,C,P,N$, so carrier admission is a finite projection.

The residual budget is read before any candidate join is exported. Rows $T$ and $F$ name the typed window and redex frontier, while $R$ records the finite residual-address and fuel accounting required by the join row $J$.

The substitution row $S$ remains visible downstream. The residual envelope $E$, premise ledger $L$, and local diamond witness $D$ are sibling handoffs, not discharges of $S$ or proofs of the full diamond endpoint.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same rows. With no coordinate for the refused endpoint claims, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC diamond residual budget endpoint boundary]
\label{thm:metacic-diamond-residual-budget-endpoint-boundary}
Any confluence consumer of an accepted $\MetaCICDiamondResidualBudgetUp$ packet may cite only the displayed residual-budget route
$$
T,F,R,S \longmapsto J \longmapsto E,L,D \longmapsto H,P,N .
$$
It may not promote the candidate-join row or residual-envelope row into $\mathsf{BetaParallelSubstitutionCompatibility}$, full parallel diamond, or arbitrary closed Church--Rosser.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the displayed route. The carrier gives the consumer typed windows, redex-frontier data, residual-budget accounting, a substitution-boundary row, candidate joins, the residual envelope, the closed-premise ledger, and the local diamond witness.

Use \autoref{thm:metacic-diamond-residual-budget-namecert-obligations}. Its substitution-boundary visibility keeps $S$ attached whenever $J$ is exported, and its sibling rows keep $E,L,D$ as contextual handoffs rather than endpoint proofs.

Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming preserve the same finite route. Therefore the packet can expose residual-budget coverage and candidate joins, but it cannot supply a premise-free parallel diamond theorem or arbitrary closed Church--Rosser theorem.
\end{proof}

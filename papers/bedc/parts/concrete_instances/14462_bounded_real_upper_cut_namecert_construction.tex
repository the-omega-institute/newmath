\begin{theorem}[BoundedRealUpperCut NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:bounded-real-upper-cut-namecert-obligations}

\item upper-observation exactness, stating that each row of $O$ is read from the same Real seal $R$ through the finite windows $S$ and dyadic comparison budget $D$;
\item boundedness handoff, stating that the Archimedean row $A$ bounds every upper-observation row recorded in $O$ through the finite ledger $B$;
\item seal non-escape, stating that no consumer reaches a host supremum, Dedekind quotient, trichotomy branch, selected limit, or $\RealUp$ completeness principle from the packet.

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The boundedness handoff is supplied by $A$ and $B$. The Archimedean row gives the explicit rational-bound surface, and the ledger $B$ ties every accepted upper observation to that surface without selecting a least upper bound or a supremum.

Seal non-escape follows from the row inventory. The provenance row mentions the sibling lower-cut, bounded-family, and located-supremum boundaries only as observation disciplines; no row carries a Dedekind construction, trichotomy branch, quotient equality, selected limit, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness coordinate.
\end{proof}

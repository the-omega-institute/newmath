\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\StreamNameUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}

The stream-name interface $\StreamNameUp$ packages an abstract sequential carrier underlying the Bishop-style L10 real-number construction. It is the lowest-level data on which $\NameCert_{\TotallyBoundedUp}$, $\NameCert_{\CompleteMetricUp}$, $\NameCert_{\CompactMetricUp}$, and $\NameCert_{\ContinuousMapUp}$ are built.

\section{Carrier definition}
\label{sec:streamname-carrier}

\begin{definition}[Rational stream-name carrier]
\label{def:rat-stream-name-carrier}
For a stream $s:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, the rational stream-name carrier is the
pointwise rational-history carrier:
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s)
\Longleftrightarrow
\forall n,\ \mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(s(n)).
$$
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[Rational stream-name classifier]
\label{def:rat-stream-name-classifier}
For streams $s,t:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, the rational stream-name classifier is
the pointwise rational-history classifier:
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)
\Longleftrightarrow
\forall n,\ \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(n),t(n)).
$$
\end{definition}

\section{The certificate}
\label{sec:streamname-certificate}

\begin{theorem}[Rational stream-name certificate fields]
\label{thm:rat-stream-name-certificate-fields}
For streams $s,t,u:\mathbb{N}\to\Hist$, the carrier and classifier above
supply the rational stream-name certificate fields:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(s)$ implies
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,s)$.
  \item $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ implies
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(t,s)$.
  \item $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ and
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(t,u)$ imply
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,u)$.
  \item $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ implies, for every $n$, both
  $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(s(n))$ and
  $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(t(n))$.
  \item If $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s,t)$ and streams $s',t'$ satisfy
  $\forall n,\ \hsame(s(n),s'(n))$ and
  $\forall n,\ \hsame(t(n),t'(n))$, then
  $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For reflexivity, fix an observation index $n$. The carrier gives
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(s(n))$, and RatUp classifier reflexivity turns that
single observation witness into
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s(n),s(n))$.

For symmetry, again fix $n$ and read the supplied stream classifier at that
index. RatUp rational-history classifier symmetry reverses the point
classifier, so the reversed stream classifier is obtained pointwise.

For transitivity, fix $n$ and read the two stream classifiers at the same
observation. RatUp rational-history classifier transitivity composes those two
point classifiers, giving the classifier between $s(n)$ and $u(n)$.

For endpoint carriers, fix $n$ and unpack the rational-history classifier
witness between $s(n)$ and $t(n)$. Its stored endpoint data are exactly the
two rational-history carrier witnesses required at that observation.

For transport, fix $n$. Apply
$\mathsf{BEDC.Derived.RatUp.RatHistoryClassifier\_hsame\_transport}$ to the
point classifier at $n$ and to the two supplied endpoint history-sameness
witnesses at $n$. The resulting point classifier is the required observation of
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(s',t')$.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Constant rational stream name]
\label{def:rat-stream-name-constant}
For a history $d:\Hist$, write $\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d(n)=d$ for every unary
observation index $n$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Constant rational stream-name witness]
\label{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$, if $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$, then
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d)$ and
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d)$. If $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$, then
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e)$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unfold $\mathsf{RatConstStream}$ and
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}$. For any observation index $n$, the stream value
is $d$, so the supplied $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ is the required
pointwise carrier witness.

For the self-classifier, unfold $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$ and fix
$n$. The two endpoint observations are both $d$, and RatUp carrier-reflexivity
turns the certified rational observation into
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,d)$.

For a classifier between two constant streams, unfold
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$ and fix $n$. The endpoint observations are
$d$ and $e$, so the supplied $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$ is already the
required pointwise classifier witness.
\end{proof}

The certificate template has ten fields: traditional target certified
countable rational-observation names; BEDC source $\RatUp$ over
$\mathsf{BHist}$; carrier $\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}$; classifier
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$; stability obligations as the fields of
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-certificate-fields}; ledger finite observation
index plus pointwise rational witnesses, with the constant stream ledger storing
no data beyond the certified rational observation; core theorems
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-certificate-fields} and
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness}; exactness pointwise over the
RatUp classifier; standard bridge ordinary rational streams; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

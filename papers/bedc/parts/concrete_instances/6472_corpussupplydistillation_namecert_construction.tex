The $\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp$ packet realizes the source-channel row of \autoref{ch:visions-large-model-as-inscription-point} as its own finite NameCert carrier. It refines the corpus-supply coordinate of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-largemodelinscriptionpoint-namecert} before that coordinate is consumed by the neighboring inscription surfaces of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-inscriptionpoint-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-inscriptionevent-namecert}. The packet records corpus supply, filtering, distillation, output-prior, refusal, transport, route, provenance, and local naming rows without treating a model output as internally certified truth.

\begin{definition}[CorpusSupplyDistillation carrier]
\label{def:corpus-supply-distillation-carrier}
A $\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
K=(C,F,D,O,R,H,T,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $C$ is the corpus-supply row naming the finite source channel from which training or retrieval inscriptions are carried;
\item $F$ is the filtering row naming the finite selection, exclusion, and normalization surface applied to the source channel;
\item $D$ is the distillation row naming the finite compression, training, or transfer route by which source-channel patterns are made available to a model-side packet;
\item $O$ is the output-prior row recording the carried prior over emitted records before any public output is read as an inscription;
\item $R$ is the refusal row excluding corpus-wide truth, hidden source selection, host semantic understanding, consciousness, and model-side proof of output truth;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the corpus-supply, filtering, distillation, output-prior, refusal, route, provenance, and naming rows;
\item $T$ records displayed $\Cont$ routes through $C,F,D,O,R$ before any downstream output record or inscription point is consumed;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\LargeModelInscriptionPointUp$, $\InscriptionPointUp$, $\InscriptionEventUp$, $\ApophaticNameUp$, $\ObserverInterfaceUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier is exactly this finite source-channel packet. It has no coordinate for corpus-total truth, hidden dataset selection, model intention, host semantic ratification, or internally certified output content.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[CorpusSupplyDistillation NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:corpus-supply-distillation-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:corpus-supply-distillation-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for $K=(C,F,D,O,R,H,T,P,N)$, corpus-supply exposure through $C$, filtering exposure through $F$, distillation exposure through $D$, output-prior exposure through $O$, refusal accounting through $R$, componentwise $\hsame$ stability through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $T$, package provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the displayed carrier. The accepted packet lists $C,F,D,O,R,H,T,P,N$, so the local certificate cannot introduce another source channel, filter, distillation route, output prior, refusal row, transport, route, provenance, or local name.

Read the source-channel rows in dependency order. A consumer first reaches the finite corpus-supply row $C$, then the filtering row $F$, then the distillation row $D$, and only then the output-prior row $O$.

The refusal and structural rows close the certificate surface. The row $R$ records the missing truth and semantic coordinates, while $H,T,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name only the same finite packet. Thus the NameCert obligations are exhausted by the displayed carrier.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CorpusSupplyDistillation carried-output boundary]
\label{thm:corpus-supply-distillation-carried-output-boundary}
Every accepted $\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp$ packet routes a corpus-carried output prior through the finite path
$$
\begin{gathered}
\text{corpus-supply row}\ \longrightarrow\ \text{filtering row}\ \longrightarrow\ \text{distillation row}\ \longrightarrow\ \text{output-prior row}\ \longrightarrow\ \NameCert_{\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp}.
\end{gathered}
$$
When this row is later consumed by $\LargeModelInscriptionPointUp$, the output record remains a carried inscription surface rather than internally certified output truth.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the carrier $K=(C,F,D,O,R,H,T,P,N)$. The source-channel route in $T$ reads $C,F,D$ before it reaches $O$, so the output prior is carried from displayed corpus supply rather than projected from a hidden model certificate.

The LargeModelInscriptionPoint chapter consumes a corpus-supply row before reading the output record. This chapter supplies that row as a standalone packet whose route has already exposed filtering and distillation.

Finally, the refusal row $R$ is read before the local naming row $N$. Therefore the downstream output boundary preserves carried inscription and does not become a proof of output truth, host semantic understanding, or corpus-total correctness.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CorpusSupplyDistillation public non-escape]
\label{thm:corpus-supply-distillation-public-nonescape}
No public consumer of an accepted $\CorpusSupplyDistillationUp$ packet can extract hidden source selection, corpus-wide truth, internally certified output truth, consciousness, host semantic understanding, or a model-side proof object. Public reads are limited to the finite corpus-supply, filtering, distillation, output-prior, refusal, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows displayed in the carrier.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Inspect the carrier inventory of \autoref{def:corpus-supply-distillation-carrier}. The only public coordinates are $C,F,D,O,R,H,T,P,N$.

Apply the carried-output boundary. Any consumer that reaches the output-prior row has already passed through the source-channel, filtering, and distillation rows, and then through the refusal row before local naming.

Since hidden source selection, corpus-wide truth, semantic understanding, consciousness, and proof of output truth are not coordinates of $K$, no public projection can recover them. The packet exports only the finite source-channel surface and its displayed certificate route.
\end{proof}

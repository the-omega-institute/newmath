$$

\item $B$ records a finite bound comparison for every displayed causal witness in $X$, relating its slot-local witness row to the same unary bound row $U$;

The carrier exports only a finite configuration row, finite causal witness rows, one unary bound row, displayed bound comparisons, transport rows, continuation routes, package provenance, and local naming data.

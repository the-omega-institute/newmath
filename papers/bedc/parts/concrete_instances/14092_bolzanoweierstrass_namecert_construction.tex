The construction reads boundedness through the finite mesh surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-closedboundedintervalnet-namecert}, the interval modulus surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-closed-bounded-interval-uniform-modulus-namecert}, the monotone-cofinal extraction row of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regular-cauchy-subsequence-namecert}, the regular rational readback of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, and the completed real-name seal of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}. $$

\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the bounded sequence rows, interval nets, extracted subsequence rows, regular rational readback, and real cluster seal;

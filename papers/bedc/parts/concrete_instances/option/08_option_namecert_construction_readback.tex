$$

$\mathsf{TaggedOptionSourceHsameCompatible}(S,\sim_S)$, and assume

$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionPayloadDescentImageClassifier}_{\delta}
(S,T,\rho_{\delta};k,\ell)

$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionPayloadDescentImageClassifier}_{\delta}
(S,T,\rho_{\delta};\ell,m)

\hsame(p,\Eone(u'))\land

\hsame(u,\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))\land
\hsame(v,\delta.\mathsf{map}(b))\land
\hsame(u',\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))\land
\hsame(v',\delta.\mathsf{map}(b)).

\begin{proof}
First combine the two image-classifier witnesses. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-reflective-transitivity}

compatibility and source reflection, to obtain the direct image classifier from

Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-hsame-transport}
to the direct classifier and the endpoint witnesses $\hsame(k,p)$ and
$\hsame(m,q)$. This yields the same image-classifier witness at the normalized
endpoint pair $(p,q)$.

Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-visible-payload-readback}
to the normalized classifier and the first visible payload presentation at
$(p,q)$. It returns source histories $a,b$ with $S(a)$, $S(b)$, $a\sim_S b$,
target carrier facts for $\delta.\mathsf{map}(a)$ and $\delta.\mathsf{map}(b)$,
and the readbacks $\hsame(u,\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))$ and
$\hsame(v,\delta.\mathsf{map}(b))$.

It remains to compare the second normalized visible presentation with the first.
At endpoint $p$, apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-history-present-payload-determinism} to
$\hsame(p,\Eone(u))$ and $\hsame(p,\Eone(u'))$. By symmetry and transitivity of
$\hsame$, the resulting one-constructor inversion gives
$\hsame(u',\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))$.

At endpoint $q$, the same visible endpoint determinacy applied to
$\hsame(q,\Eone(v))$ and $\hsame(q,\Eone(v'))$ gives
$\hsame(v',v)$. Composing this with $\hsame(v,\delta.\mathsf{map}(b))$ yields
$\hsame(v',\delta.\mathsf{map}(b))$.

Repack the original source pair $a,b$ with the two source-carrier witnesses, the
source classifier relation, the transported target-carrier witnesses, and the
four visible payload readbacks. These are precisely the fields of the displayed
existential conclusion.
\end{proof}

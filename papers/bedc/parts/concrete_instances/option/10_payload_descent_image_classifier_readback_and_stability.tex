\section{Payload descent image classifier visible payload exactness}

\begin{theorem}[Tagged option payload descent image classifier visible payload exactness]
\label{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-visible-payload-exactness}
Let $S,T:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be BEDC history source carriers, let
$\sim_S$ and $\sim_T$ be source classifiers, let
$\delta:\mathsf{DescentCertificate}(\mathsf{BHist},\mathsf{BHist},\sim_S,\sim_T)$,
and assume payload preservation
$$
\rho_{\delta}:\forall a.\;S(a)\to T(\delta.\mathsf{map}(a)).
$$
Let $\mathsf{cert}:\NameCert(S,\sim_S)$ and assume
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionSourceHsameCompatible}(S,\sim_S)$. For target endpoints
$k,k':\mathsf{BHist}$ and visible target payloads $u,v:\mathsf{BHist}$, the
visible endpoint classifier is exact:
$$
\bigl(
\mathsf{TaggedOptionPayloadDescentImageClassifier}_{\delta}
(S,T,\rho_{\delta};k,k')
\land T(u)\land T(v)\land
\hsame(k,\Eone(u))\land
\hsame(k',\Eone(v))
\bigr)
\Longleftrightarrow
$$
$$
T(u)\land T(v)\land
\hsame(k,\Eone(u))\land
\hsame(k',\Eone(v))\land
\exists a,b:\mathsf{BHist}.\;
S(a)\land S(b)\land a\sim_S b\land
T(\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))\land T(\delta.\mathsf{map}(b))\land
\hsame(u,\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))\land
\hsame(v,\delta.\mathsf{map}(b)).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the forward direction, keep the visible target facts
$T(u)$, $T(v)$, $\hsame(k,\Eone(u))$, and $\hsame(k',\Eone(v))$. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-visible-payload-readback}
to the classifier witness and these visible facts. This supplies source
payloads $a,b$ with the displayed source carrier witnesses, source classifier
witness, transported target witnesses, and payload identifications.

Combining the retained visible facts with that existential witness gives the
right-hand side of the equivalence.

For the reverse direction, unpack the right-hand side. Keep the visible target
facts and choose witnesses $a,b$ from the existential clause.

Compose $\hsame(k,\Eone(u))$ with the $\Eone$ congruence of
$\hsame(u,\delta.\mathsf{map}(a))$ to obtain
$\hsame(k,\Eone(\delta.\mathsf{map}(a)))$. The same transitivity and congruence
argument at the right endpoint gives
$\hsame(k',\Eone(\delta.\mathsf{map}(b)))$.

The source witnesses $S(a)$ and $S(b)$, the relation $a\sim_S b$, the transported
target witnesses, and these two transported endpoint presentations form the
present branch in
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-payload-descent-image-classifier-branch-exactness}.
Apply its reverse direction to rebuild
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionPayloadDescentImageClassifier}_{\delta}
(S,T,\rho_{\delta};k,k')$. Together with the retained visible facts, this gives
the left-hand side.
\end{proof}

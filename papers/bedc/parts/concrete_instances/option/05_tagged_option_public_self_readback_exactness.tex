\begin{theorem}[Tagged option public self-readback exactness]
\label{thm:tagged-option-public-self-readback-exactness}
This theorem fits the per-object template as follows: the traditional target is
optional identity readback; the BEDC source is $\mathsf{BHist}$ with a source
$\NameCert$; the carrier is $\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryCarrier}$; the
classifier is $\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}$; the stability
obligations are semantic certification, carrier-relative reflexivity,
endpoint-carrier inversion, and single-valued branch readback; the ledger is the
visible absent/present tag plus payload evidence; the core theorem is public
self-readback; exactness is the self-classifier iff carrier with exclusive
branch readback; the standard bridge is $\mathsf{none}$/$\mathsf{some}$
identity; and the status is $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$. Let
$S:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a source carrier, let $\sim_S$ be a source
classifier, let $\mathsf{cert}:\NameCert(S,\sim_S)$ be a source certificate,
and assume $\mathsf{TaggedOptionSourceHsameCompatible}(S,\sim_S)$. Then
$$
\mathsf{SemanticNameCert}(\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryCarrier}_S,
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryPattern}_S,
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryLedger}_S,
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{S,\sim_S})
$$
holds, and every history $h$ satisfies the public identity exactness clause
$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{S,\sim_S}(h,h)
\Longleftrightarrow
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryCarrier}_S(h).
$$
Moreover, for every history $h$, every carrier witness yields the
self-classifier readback
$$
\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryCarrier}_S(h)\to
\Bigl(
\bigl(
\hsame(h,\emp)\land
\neg\bigl(\exists a:\mathsf{BHist}.\; S(a)\land\hsame(h,\Eone(a))\bigr)
\bigr)
\lor
\bigl(
\exists a,b:\mathsf{BHist}.\;
S(a)\land S(b)\land
\hsame(h,\Eone(a))\land
\hsame(h,\Eone(b))\land
a\sim_S b\land
\neg\hsame(h,\emp)\land
\bigl(\forall a',b':\mathsf{BHist}.\;
\bigl(
S(a')\land S(b')\land
\hsame(h,\Eone(a'))\land
\hsame(h,\Eone(b'))
\bigr)\to
\hsame(a,a')\land\hsame(b,b')\bigr)
\bigr)
\Bigr).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-history-semantic-certificate} to
$\mathsf{cert}$ and the displayed source hsame compatibility hypothesis. This
gives the public semantic certificate for the tagged option carrier, pattern,
ledger, and classifier.

For the forward direction of the identity exactness clause, assume
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{S,\sim_S}(h,h)$. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-history-classifier-endpoint-carriers} and take
either endpoint carrier.

For the reverse direction, assume $\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryCarrier}_S(h)$.
Use the carrier-relative reflexivity field of
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-history-stability-fields} to obtain
$\mathsf{TaggedOptionHistoryClassifier}_{S,\sim_S}(h,h)$.

For the readback clause, start from a carrier witness for $h$ and build the
self-classifier by the same carrier-relative reflexivity field. Apply
Theorem~\ref{thm:tagged-option-public-branch-readback-single-valued} to that
self-classifier with the two endpoints both equal to $h$, then contract the
duplicated absent endpoint, duplicated present endpoint, and duplicated
non-absent endpoint into the displayed self-readback disjunction.
\end{proof}

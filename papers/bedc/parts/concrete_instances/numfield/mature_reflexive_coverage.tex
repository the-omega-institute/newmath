\begin{theorem}[NumFieldUp mature reflexive coverage]
\label{thm:numfield-mature-reflexive-coverage}
The degree-one RatUp reflexive $\NumFieldUp$ package exhausts the paper-side
mature treatment currently claimed for this chapter. Its visible surface is
exactly the carrier and classifier row, FieldExt consumption row,
finite-extension witness, singleton basis, coordinate readback, ledger
exactness rows, public interface, and exactness package for the reflexive
rational package. Nontrivial number fields and algebraic-integer theory remain
outside this claim.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Read the carrier and classifier from
\autoref{thm:numfield-reflexive-carrier-classifier}, and read the FieldExt
consumption row from \autoref{thm:numfield-reflexive-fieldext-consumption}.

The finite-extension witness and singleton basis are supplied by
\autoref{thm:numfield-reflexive-rational-finite-extension-witness} and
\autoref{thm:numfield-reflexive-rational-singleton-basis-exhaustion}.
Coordinate readback is then fixed by the coordinate exactness, readback, and
degree-one exhaustion rows in the reflexive certificate file.

Finally apply the ledger exactness rows, the public-interface row, and
\autoref{thm:numfield-reflexive-rational-exactness-package}. These rows repack
the displayed reflexive rational package without adding a nontrivial extension,
integral basis, class group, or algebraic-integer field.
\end{proof}

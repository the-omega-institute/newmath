\begin{theorem}[GroundCompiler recognizer-flow NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:groundcompiler-recognizer-flow-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\GroundCompilerRecognizerFlowUp$ carrier
$$

the local $\NameCert_{\GroundCompilerRecognizerFlowUp}$ surface consists exactly of event-flow readback through $E$, source-channel lossless routing through $S$, generated-recognizer mediation through $R$, certificate-gate acceptance through $Q$, non-evidence refusal through $B$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier. The rows $E,S,R,Q,B$ are the public data coordinates, and $H,C,P,N$ transport, route, locate, and name those same coordinates.

Read the route in order. The non-evidence row records every excluded channel: source text, carried metadata, metric reports, motif reports, compiler reports, proof text, host state, and raw classifier quotients.

\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for \(\ContinuousFunctionUp\)}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-continuous-namecert}

The continuous function interface \(\ContinuousFunctionUp\) has certificate obligations between two metric spaces from \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metric-namecert}. The carrier is the predicate "\(f : X \to Y\) is uniformly continuous on every totally bounded subset of \(X\), with explicit modulus of continuity \(\delta : \mathbb{N} \to \mathbb{N}\)". Bishop's choice of uniform continuity over pointwise continuity is required for 0-axiom workability: pointwise continuity in BEDC would require LEM to discharge the standard \(\epsilon\)-\(\delta\) argument.

\section{Carrier definition}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction carrier]
\label{def:continuous-carrier}
Use a function \(f:X\to Y\) between metric spaces plus explicit uniform-continuity moduli on every totally bounded subset.
\end{definition}

\section{The certificate}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction source specification]
\label{def:continuous-source-specification}
The source is a pair of certified metric spaces and the totally bounded subset discipline on the domain.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction pattern specification]
\label{def:continuous-pattern-specification}
The pattern is function-with-modulus formation, attaching a modulus \(\delta\) for each requested precision context.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction classifier specification]
\label{def:continuous-classifier-specification}
The classifier relates continuous-function witnesses pointwise by codomain classifier on every domain witness.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction stability certificate]
\label{def:continuous-stability-certificate}
The stability certificate consists of:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item pointwise classifier reflexivity
  \item pointwise classifier transitivity
  \item modulus validity for uniform continuity
  \item stability of modulus under function classifier replacement
  \item closure under identity
  \item closure under composition by composing moduli
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[ContinuousFunction ledger policy]
\label{def:continuous-ledger-policy}
Record the function identifier, metric ledgers, subset witness, and continuity modulus.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[\(\ContinuousFunctionUp\) certificate obligations]
\label{def:continuous-certificate-obligations}
The carrier, source, pattern, classifier, stability, and ledger data listed above are the obligations required for the interface name \(\ContinuousFunctionUp\).
\end{definition}

\paragraph{Primitive boundary.}
\(\ContinuousFunctionUp\) is not a BEDC primitive. It is admitted only when those obligations are supplied.

\begin{remark}[Zero-axiom encoding note]
\label{rem:continuous-zero-axiom-note}
Avoids funext by pointwise classifier sameness and replaces pointwise classical continuity with explicit uniform moduli.
\end{remark}

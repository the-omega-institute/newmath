\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp bracketing selector independence package]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-independence-package}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and
histories $h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and
$C_D^u(\ell)$, let $E_s(h,k,\ell)$ be the endpoint selector of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector}. If
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then for all selectors
$s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$, every context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$, and all classifier
contexts $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$, setting
$a:=E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $b:=E_t(h,k,\ell)$ gives the following package:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item
  $$
  a\hsame b;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  R_D^u(a,b);
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(a,b);
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}(\mathsf{append}(L,a),\mathsf{append}(R,b))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,a))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(a,r))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,a));
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(a,r)).
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,b))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(b,r))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,b));
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(b,r)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector-same} to the
selectors $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$. This gives the endpoint sameness row
$E_s(h,k,\ell)\hsame E_t(h,k,\ell)$.

Next apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-denominator-classifier-coverage}
and \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-rat-classifier-coverage} to
the same carrier witnesses and the same
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ witness. These give the denominator
classifier row $R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$ and the Rat classifier row
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$.

Apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-choice-classifier-exclusion} to
the same denominator tuple, the carrier witnesses, the
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ witness, the selectors $s,t$, and the
classifier contexts $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$. This gives the selected-pair
singleton-classifier exclusion.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-contextual-nonzero-package} to
the selector $s$ and the context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. Its four rows give the two
one-sided singleton-carrier exclusions and the two corresponding
$\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}$ witnesses for $E_s(h,k,\ell)$.

Apply the same theorem again to the selector $t$ and the same context $r$. The
resulting four rows give the matching singleton-carrier exclusions and
$\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}$ witnesses for $E_t(h,k,\ell)$. Replacing
the temporary names $a$ and $b$ by their selector definitions assembles the
displayed package.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Field zero linear factor signed branch selection]
\label{thm:field-zero-linear-factor-signed-branch-selection}
For BEDC history operations $add$, $mul$, $neg$, and $zero$ satisfying the
hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:commring-zero-linear-factor-equal-squares} and
\autoref{thm:field-equal-squares-signed-branch-determinacy}, every
$a,b:\mathsf{BHist}$ satisfies the following two signed zero-factor clauses:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item if $(a+(-b))\hsame0$ and $p_+:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+b)$, then
  $a\hsame b$ and $\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+(-b))\to\mathsf{False}$;
  \item if $(a+b)\hsame0$ and $p_-:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+(-b))$, then
  $a\hsame -b$ and $\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+b)\to\mathsf{False}$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume first the zero factor $(a+(-b))\hsame0$ and the signed nonzero witness
$p_+:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+b)$. Apply
\autoref{thm:commring-zero-linear-factor-equal-squares} to the displayed
right signed zero factor, read as the second disjunct. This gives the equal
square row $a\cdot a\hsame b\cdot b$.

Now apply the positive branch of
\autoref{thm:field-equal-squares-signed-branch-determinacy} to that square
row and the witness $p_+$. Its conclusion is exactly $a\hsame b$ together with
$\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+(-b))\to\mathsf{False}$.

For the opposite branch, assume the zero factor $(a+b)\hsame0$ and the signed
nonzero witness $p_-:\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+(-b))$. Apply
\autoref{thm:commring-zero-linear-factor-equal-squares} to the displayed left
signed zero factor, read as the first disjunct. This again gives
$a\cdot a\hsame b\cdot b$.

The negative branch of
\autoref{thm:field-equal-squares-signed-branch-determinacy}, applied to this
square row and to $p_-$, gives $a\hsame -b$ and excludes every witness
$\mathsf{NonZero}_F(a+b)$.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target constructive signed square-root branch
selection; BEDC source $\FieldUp+\CommRingUp$ over $\mathsf{BHist}$; carrier
$\mathsf{BHist}$; classifier $\hsame$; stability obligations zero-linear-factor
equal-square production and equal-square signed branch determinacy; ledger the
zero factor and signed nonzero witness; core theorem this block; exactness
predicate branch endpoint plus opposite-witness exclusion; downstream consumer
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ affine solution and square-root branch ledgers;
status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

Per-object template: traditional target bracketing-selector independence inside
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\mathsf{BMark}$,
$\mathsf{RatUp}$, the denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$,
and selectors $E_s,E_t$; carrier $C_D^u$ and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$;
classifier $R_D^u$ and $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$; stability obligations
selector sameness, selector denominator coverage, selector Rat coverage, and
contextual nonzero package rows for both selected endpoints on both context
sides; ledger selector choices plus both context sides; exactness predicate
selector-independent row coverage; standard bridge deferred; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp final certificate selector noncollapse boundary]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-selector-noncollapse-boundary}
For Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}, the concrete
denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$, histories
$h,k,\ell:\mathsf{BHist}$ with $C_D^u(h)$, $C_D^u(k)$, and $C_D^u(\ell)$,
selectors $s,t:\mathsf{BMark}$, and contexts $L,R,r:\mathsf{BHist}$, let
$E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $E_t(h,k,\ell)$ be the corresponding bracketing endpoint
selectors of Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-endpoint-selector}. If
$\mathsf{ThreeSupp}_D^u(h,k,\ell)$ holds, then one concrete
$\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ exit boundary simultaneously contains the following rows:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the final certificate noncollapse action package of
  \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-noncollapse-action-package};
  \item $C_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell))$, $C_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell))$, and
  $R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$;
  \item $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$;
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyClassifier}
  (\mathsf{append}(L,E_s(h,k,\ell)),\mathsf{append}(R,E_t(h,k,\ell)))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_s(h,k,\ell)))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(\mathsf{append}(E_s(h,k,\ell),r))\to\bot;
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(r,E_s(h,k,\ell)));
  $$
  \item
  $$
  \mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}(\mathsf{append}(E_s(h,k,\ell),r)).
  $$
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-final-certificate-noncollapse-action-package}
to Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}. This fixes the
concrete $\FieldUp(\RatUp)$ certificate boundary whose source object and action
rows are consumed by the hierarchy exit criterion.

Next apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-exit-subpackage} to
the same denominator tuple, histories, carrier witnesses, support witness,
selectors, and contexts $L,R:\mathsf{BHist}$. This contributes the two selected
carrier rows, the selected denominator-classifier row, and the arbitrary-context
singleton-classifier exclusion for the pair
$(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$.

Apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-bracketing-selector-independence-package} with
the same selector pair and the context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. Its
selector-independent row package supplies
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$ together with
contextual nonzero rows for the selected left endpoint.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-selector-contextual-nonzero-package} to
the selector $s$ and context $r:\mathsf{BHist}$. This reads the one-sided
singleton-carrier exclusions and the two
$\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyNonZero}$ witnesses as rows of the same concrete
exit boundary, so the final certificate and selector bracketing data are
consumed as one FieldUp object.
\end{proof}

Per-object template: traditional target final selector noncollapse boundary
inside $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$; BEDC source $\mathsf{BHist}$,
$\mathsf{BMark}$, $\mathsf{RatUp}$, and
Definition~\ref{def:ratup-fieldup-concrete-exit-object}; denominator tuple
$(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$; selected endpoints
$E_s(h,k,\ell)$ and $E_t(h,k,\ell)$; carrier rows $C_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell))$,
$C_D^u(E_t(h,k,\ell))$, and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}$; classifier rows
$R_D^u(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$ and
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(E_s(h,k,\ell),E_t(h,k,\ell))$; stability
obligations final noncollapse action, selector exit subpackage, selector
independence, and contextual nonzero rows; ledger selector pair plus contexts
$L,R,r$; exactness predicate one concrete FieldUp exit boundary; standard bridge
deferred; status $\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

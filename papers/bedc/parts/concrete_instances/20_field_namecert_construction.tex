\begin{definition}[Concrete Rat denominator FieldUp multiplicative exactness instance]
\label{def:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-exactness-instance}
The concrete $\mathsf{BHist}$ denominator-side multiplicative exactness instance
for $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ is the tuple
$$
\mathcal{M}_D^\ast:=(C_D^\ast,R_D^\ast,\mu_D^\ast,e_D^\ast,N_D^\ast)
$$
where $C_D^\ast$ and $R_D^\ast$ are the carrier and classifier of
\autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-unit-envelope},
$$
\mu_D^\ast(h,k):=\mathsf{append}(h,k),\qquad
e_D^\ast:=\emp,\qquad
N_D^\ast(h):=\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(h).
$$
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Rat denominator FieldUp multiplicative exactness boundary]
\label{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-exactness-boundary}
The concrete instance
\autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-exactness-instance}
satisfies the Rat denominator FieldUp multiplicative certificate boundary. In
addition, for all $h,k,t:\mathsf{BHist}$, if $C_D^\ast(h)$, $C_D^\ast(k)$,
and $N_D^\ast(t)$, then
$$
R_D^\ast(\mu_D^\ast(h,t),\mu_D^\ast(k,t))
\Longleftrightarrow R_D^\ast(h,k),
$$
and
$$
R_D^\ast(\mu_D^\ast(t,h),\mu_D^\ast(t,k))
\Longleftrightarrow R_D^\ast(h,k).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The displayed tuple is the same concrete carrier, classifier, multiplication,
unit, and strict support used by
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-certificate-boundary}.
That theorem supplies the packaged carrier, classifier, monoid, commutativity,
empty-unit uniqueness, and carried-endpoint clauses for the denominator-side
FieldUp boundary.

The strict-support component is the predicate
$N_D^\ast(h):=\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(h)$. The transport and product-support
parts of the boundary are therefore the corresponding clauses of
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-unit-envelope-strict-support-laws}.

It remains to identify strict common-factor cancellation at the same concrete
classifier. Since $N_D^\ast(t)$ is by definition
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(t)$, \autoref{def:field-rat-denominator-unit-envelope}
places $t$ in $C_D^\ast$ through the carried alternative.

Under $C_D^\ast(h)$, $C_D^\ast(k)$, and this derived carrier witness for $t$,
the right and left cancellation equivalences are exactly the two displayed
clauses of
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-unit-envelope-cancellation-exactness}. Hence
the concrete tuple carries both the certificate boundary and the strict
factor-cancellation exactness required for the denominator side of
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp denominator certificate assembly]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-denominator-certificate-assembly}
Let the denominator-side data for $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ be the concrete
external-unit envelope tuple
$(C_D^\ast,R_D^\ast,\mu_D^\ast,e_D^\ast,N_D^\ast)$, with
$\mu_D^\ast(h,k):=\mathsf{append}(h,k)$, $e_D^\ast:=\emp$, and
$N_D^\ast(h):=\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(h)$ on the strict carried endpoint
branch. Then this tuple is the denominator subcertificate consumed by
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$: the envelope classifier is exact, the
denominator operation carries the commutative monoid laws, strict support
transports across $R_D^\ast$, strict support is product-exact for
$\mu_D^\ast$, strict common factors give exact left and right cancellation,
fixed multiplication is classifier-exact inside empty external contexts, the
external unit is unique, and $e_D^\ast$ is not a carried denominator unit.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The exact envelope boundary is
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-unit-envelope-exact-field-boundary}. It
supplies the concrete carrier, classifier, operation, unit, strict support,
commutative monoid laws, support transport, support product law, unique
external endpoint, and strict-factor cancellation clauses for
$(C_D^\ast,R_D^\ast,\mu_D^\ast,e_D^\ast,N_D^\ast)$.

The remaining contextual multiplication field is
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-empty-unit-contextual-multiplication-classifier-exactness}.
It states that fixed denominator multiplication preserves and reflects the
classifier after empty external histories are attached on both sides. The
empty histories are exactly the endpoint contexts used by the external-unit
envelope, so this field reads on the same $\mathsf{BHist}$ append operation.

Finally,
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-multiplicative-certificate-boundary}
collects the denominator-side multiplicative certificate boundary for the
displayed tuple. Combining that boundary with the contextual multiplication
exactness clause and the exact envelope theorem gives the advertised
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ denominator subcertificate. The statement that no
carried denominator unit remains is the strict-support reading of
$e_D^\ast=\emp$ together with the carried-endpoint separation already included
in the exact envelope boundary.
\end{proof}

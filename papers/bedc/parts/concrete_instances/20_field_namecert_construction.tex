\begin{theorem}[RatUp FieldUp transported strict-support non-singleton endpoint]
\label{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-strict-support-non-singleton}
For the concrete denominator tuple $(C_D^u,R_D^u,\mu_D^u,e_D^u,N_D^u)$ and the
contextual action $\Gamma$ of
\autoref{def:ratup-fieldup-denominator-contextual-action-instance}, let
$x:=\Gamma_{p',q',s',t'}(\Gamma_{p,q,s,t}(h))$. Under the hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-transported-support-neutrality-row}, suppose also
that $N_D^u(h)$ holds. Then the concrete $\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit
certificate contains all of the following endpoint data:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $C_D^u(x)$;
  \item $N_D^u(x)$;
  \item $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(x)$;
  \item $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(x)\to\bot$.
\end{enumerate}
Thus the transported strict-support endpoint of the
$\FieldUp(\mathsf{RatUp})$ exit certificate cannot collapse into the singleton
empty-history field carrier.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:ratup-fieldup-exit-transported-support-nondegenerate} at the same
transported endpoint. It gives the carried endpoint clause $C_D^u(x)$ and the
strict-support equivalence
$$
N_D^u(x)\Longleftrightarrow N_D^u(h).
$$

Use the reverse direction of this equivalence with the displayed witness
$N_D^u(h)$. This produces the transported strict-support witness $N_D^u(x)$.

Now read the denominator tuple row from
\autoref{thm:field-rat-denominator-fieldup-contextual-multiplicative-boundary}.
There $N_D^u(y)=\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(y)$ for every denominator history
$y$, so the transported support witness gives $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(x)$.

Finally apply
\autoref{thm:field-singleton-schema-disjoint-from-rat-history-carrier} to
$x$. Any witness of $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(x)$, together with the
Rat history carrier witness just obtained, yields contradiction. This is the
displayed map $\mathsf{fieldSingletonEmptyCarrier}(x)\to\bot$.
\end{proof}

\label{def:hochster-nerve-betti-reduction-carrier}

$$

\item $F$ is the nerve-face row, recording the finite face and nonface tests that produce the nerve complex used by the reduction.
\item $N$ is the homology-range row, recording the displayed dimensions in which reduced homology can be read.

\begin{theorem}[Hochster nerve Betti reduction obligations]
\label{thm:hochster-nerve-betti-reduction-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:hochster-nerve-betti-reduction-carrier}. The finite support, nerve-face, homology-range, finite homology, Betti-strand, transport, replay, provenance, and local name rows exhaust the accepted packet.

The reduction is read along the finite route. The support row $V$ fixes the displayed vertex window, the face row $F$ builds the nerve complex, and the range row $N$ restricts the homology degrees that can be queried.

The Betti ledger is downstream of finite homology. The row $H_0$ is read through homology and finite simplicial-chain carriers, and $B_0$ records only the strand entries obtained from that range. The structural rows $G,T,P,L$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite route, so no field-faithfulness theorem, nontrivial coefficient instance, unrestricted resolution, or host Betti-table equality is exported.
\end{proof}

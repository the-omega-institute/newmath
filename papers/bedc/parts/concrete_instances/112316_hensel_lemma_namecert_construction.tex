\begin{definition}[Hensel lemma carrier]
\label{def:hensel-lemma-carrier}
A $\HenselLemmaUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
H=(K,R,F,P,A,V,N,E,T,C,Q,L)

with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $K$ is the $\PadicUp$ source row, exposing only the finite valuation and congruence windows inspected by the lifting packet.
\item $R$ is the $\LocalFieldUp$ coefficient row, supplying the local-field arithmetic reads used by the polynomial and derivative windows.
\item $F$ is the $\FieldUp$ compatibility row for the nonzero derivative and unit comparisons needed by the simple-root test.
\item $P$ is the $\PolynomialUp$ row, listing the displayed polynomial, derivative, coefficient windows, and evaluation reads at the approximate root.
\item $A$ is the approximate-root row, recording the chosen finite residue-window representative and the displayed smallness comparison for the polynomial value.
\item $V$ is the valuation-separation row, recording that the derivative unit window and the polynomial-value window are separated enough to start the lifting route.
\item $N$ is the Newton-correction ledger, listing the finite correction windows, derivative reads, and valuation gains that the packet promises to replay.

\item $Q$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\HenselLemmaUp$, $\PadicUp$, $\LocalFieldUp$, $\PolynomialUp$, $\FieldUp$, $\RingUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $L$ is the local $\NameCert_{\HenselLemmaUp}$ row.

The classifier compares accepted packets only through $K,R,F,P,A,V,N,E,T,C,Q,L$. It has no coordinate for selected roots, quotient roots, countable choice, a full algebraic closure, or an ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Hensel lemma NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:hensel-lemma-namecert-obligations}

Every lifting read must expose these finite rows before it can be consumed as a Hensel-style certificate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:hensel-lemma-carrier}. The arithmetic source is visible only through $K,R,F$, while the polynomial and derivative reads are visible only through $P$ and the approximate-root row $A$.

The lifting route then passes through $V,N$, and $E$. The valuation-separation row starts the correction schedule, the Newton ledger records the displayed correction windows, and the nonescape row keeps those windows inside the same $p$-adic ball.

The structural rows $T,C,Q,L$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite packet. Since no further coordinate occurs in the carrier, the certificate cannot export a selected root function, quotient-root semantics, countable choice, full algebraic closure, or an ambient completeness principle.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:hensel-lemma-newton-witness-nonescape}

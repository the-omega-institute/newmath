\begin{definition}[Bounded monotone convergence seal carrier]
\label{def:bounded-monotone-convergence-seal-carrier}
A $\BoundedMonotoneConvergenceSealUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
S=(W,M,K,F,\sigma,D,L,R,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $W$ is the accepted $\BoundedMonotoneCauchyWitnessUp$ row that supplies the bounded source, tail witness, dyadic ledger, located trap, and real-seal precursor;
\item $M$ is the public $\MonotoneCauchyUp$ tail-window certificate through which the bounded monotone source is read;
\item $K$ is the public $\CauchyCriterionUp$ packet through which tolerance requests, tail windows, and dyadic bounds are read;
\item $F$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff row exported by the witness and criterion packets;
\item $\sigma$ is the finite $\StreamNameUp$ window ledger used by the tail and seal reads;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance and upper-bound ledger consumed before any convergence seal is visible;
\item $L$ is the $\CauchyLimitSealUp$ compatibility row tying the witnessed Cauchy route to the completion-facing seal boundary;
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ convergence seal row reached only after $W,M,K,F,\sigma,D,L$ have been displayed;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for all displayed rows;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes by which consumers read the witness, monotone tail, criterion, regular-rational, stream-window, dyadic, limit-seal, and Real rows;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BoundedMonotoneCauchyWitnessUp$, $\MonotoneCauchyUp$, $\CauchyCriterionUp$, $\CauchyLimitSealUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BoundedMonotoneConvergenceSealUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no selected limit representative, quotient stream equality, host real equality, trichotomy row, open-cover compactness row, detached tail oracle, or ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Bounded monotone convergence seal public handoff]
\label{thm:bounded-monotone-convergence-seal-public-handoff}
Every $\RealUp$ or completion-facing consumer of an accepted $\BoundedMonotoneConvergenceSealUp$ packet factors first through the bounded monotone Cauchy witness row $W$, then through the public $\MonotoneCauchyUp$ and $\CauchyCriterionUp$ rows $M$ and $K$, and only then through the displayed route
$$
F,\sigma,D,L,R,H,C,P,N.
$$
The consumer receives a finite Real convergence handoff assembled from $\BoundedMonotoneCauchyWitnessUp$, $\MonotoneCauchyUp$, $\CauchyCriterionUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\RealUp$ rows, with the $\CauchyLimitSealUp$ row $L$ acting only as the compatibility boundary for the sealed route. It receives no route that bypasses $W$ or $K$ to a selected limit, quotient stream, host real equality, ambient completeness witness, detached tail oracle, trichotomy row, or open-cover compactness row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the bounded monotone witness surface. By \autoref{thm:bounded-monotone-cauchy-witness-scoped-dependency-package}, every witness read is already scoped by the displayed regular-rational, stream-window, Cauchy witness, dyadic, located-trap, Real seal, transport, route, provenance, and naming rows.

Next use the bridge to public tail and criterion packets. The $\MonotoneCauchyUp$ row supplies the public tail-window certificate, while the $\CauchyCriterionUp$ row supplies the explicit tolerance, tail, dyadic, regular-rational, and seal route.

The remaining rows $F,\sigma,D,L,R,H,C,P,N$ carry the public convergence handoff to the Real-facing boundary. The limit-seal row records compatibility with the sealed completion route, but it does not add a selected representative or a host equality coordinate.

Thus every consumer route is exhausted by the displayed packet. A route that omits $W$ or $K$ has no access to the bounded witness or Cauchy criterion rows, and a route that asks for selected limits, quotient streams, host equality, ambient completeness, detached tail oracles, trichotomy, or compactness asks for data absent from the carrier.
\end{proof}

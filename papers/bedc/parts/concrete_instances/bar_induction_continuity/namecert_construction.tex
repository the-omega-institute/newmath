\item $F$ is the finite fan-depth row, bounding the prefix depth at which the accepted bar evidence must be read.

\begin{theorem}[Bar induction continuity NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:bar-induction-continuity-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\BarInductionContinuityUp$ carrier
$$

$$
the local $\NameCert_{\BarInductionContinuityUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for the barred-spread data, finite fan-depth admission, stream-prefix window exposure, regular rational readback, modulus-output exactness, componentwise $\hsame$ transport, displayed $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local naming. Every exported continuity bound factors through the finite route
$$
B,F,S,R \longmapsto M,H,C,P,N .
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:bar-induction-continuity-carrier}. The tuple displays the barred-spread row, finite fan depth, stream prefix windows, regular rational readback, modulus output, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming.

The bar-induction part supplies only finite prefix descent. The row $B$ decides the inspected bar entries, while $F$ bounds the depth at which those entries are read. Thus no selected infinite branch or countable-choice schedule is available.

The continuity output is then read through $S$ and $R$. The stream row exposes the prefix coordinates, and the regular rational row packages exactly the rational observations and regularity ledger obtained from that same prefix window.

Finally $M$ records the modulus extracted from the displayed route, and $H,C,P,N$ only transport, replay, source, and name it. Since the carrier has no coordinate for quotient stream equality, open-cover compactness, host real equality, or ambient completeness, row projection and finite prefix induction give precisely the stated obligations.
\end{proof}

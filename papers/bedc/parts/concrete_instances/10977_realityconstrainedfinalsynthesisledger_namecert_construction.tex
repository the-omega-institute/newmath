\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained final-synthesis-ledger obligations]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-final-synthesis-ledger-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:reality-constrained-final-synthesis-ledger-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\RealityConstrainedFinalSynthesisLedgerUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission, TruthCert preservation, methodology-ledger export preservation, open-fit failure exposure, model-selection-boundary visibility, observer-invariant public reading, synthesis-scope exactness, componentwise transport, displayed continuation replay, provenance, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier $Z=(T,R,M,F,B,O,S,H,C,P,N)$. The first seven rows expose the truth-certificate packet, the reality-constrained TruthCert packet, the methodology-ledger export, the open failure row, the model-selection boundary, the observer-invariance row, and the finite synthesis scope.

TruthCert preservation follows through $T$ and $R$. These rows retain open fit, tower descent, source, signature, classifier, stability, observer-invariant reading, ledger data, failure surface, and local naming before the final-synthesis export can be read.

Methodology and failure exposure follow through $M$ and $F$. The methodology row records export discipline, domain-leakage refusal, upgrade routes, and methodology defeats, while the open-fit failure row keeps defeating observations, domain exits, classifier mismatches, continuation scope, ledger entries, replay, and provenance visible.

The model-selection and public-reading obligations follow through $B,O,S$. The boundary row blocks a no-final-oracle selection from being replaced by final confirmation, the observer row keeps public reading invariant across admissible histories, and the scope row fixes the observation domain, endpoint, continuation class, and consumer route.

The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ then transport, replay, package, and name exactly the displayed rows. Since the carrier has no further coordinate, every accepted final-synthesis export is exhausted by this NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained final-synthesis-ledger sibling independence]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-final-synthesis-ledger-sibling-independence}
The $\RealityConstrainedFinalSynthesisLedgerUp$ carrier is not bijective to $\PhysicalTruthCertificateUp$, $\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp$, $\RealityConstrainedMethodologyLedgerUp$, $\RealityConstrainedFailureSurfaceUp$, or $\ModelSelectionBoundaryUp$. \end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Compare the displayed carrier fields. $\PhysicalTruthCertificateUp$ and $\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp$ supply truth-certificate rows, but they do not by themselves bind the methodology-ledger export row, model-selection boundary, and final synthesis scope.

$\RealityConstrainedFailureSurfaceUp$ supplies the defeat and open-fit failure rows, while $\ModelSelectionBoundaryUp$ supplies comparative fit and no-final-oracle selection. Neither carries the full truth-certificate and methodology-ledger export rows together with final public naming.

Therefore no listed sibling is bijective to $Z$. Each omits at least one displayed field required by \autoref{def:reality-constrained-final-synthesis-ledger-carrier}, and the local NameCert row accepts only the joined packet.
\end{proof}

Following Bishop and Bridges constructive analysis, the $\ApartnessCotransitivityUp$ packet records the finite cotransitive apartness read by which an apart pair of real names separates through a third displayed name on either the left branch or the right branch. It sits over the Bishop-style apartness surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-apartnessreal-namecert}, the located apartness classifier of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-located-apartness-namecert}, the regular Cauchy order/apartness route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regular-cauchy-apartness-order-namecert}, the regular rational handoff of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, the dyadic tolerance arithmetic of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert}, and the terminal real-name seal of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}. Its role is to expose cotransitivity as finite NameCert data without importing trichotomy, ambient real equality, quotient streams, selected representatives, countable choice, or $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[Apartness cotransitivity carrier]
\label{def:apartness-cotransitivity-carrier}
An $\ApartnessCotransitivityUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
K=(X,Y,Z,L,R,D,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $X$ is the left $\RealUp$ name whose apartness from $Y$ is being tested by a displayed regular Cauchy route.
\item $Y$ is the right $\RealUp$ name in the source apart pair.
\item $Z$ is the third $\RealUp$ name through which cotransitivity is read.
\item $L$ is the left-branch row. When active, it records the witnessed apartness route from $X$ to $Z$ through the located apartness classifier and the regular Cauchy apartness-order surface.
\item $R$ is the right-branch row. When active, it records the witnessed apartness route from $Z$ to $Y$ through the same classifier and regular Cauchy apartness-order surface.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance row. It records the positive dyadic separation budget, slack, and finite precision data consumed by whichever branch is displayed.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the three real-name rows, the two branch rows, the dyadic tolerance row, replay, provenance, and local naming.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from an apartness-cotransitivity request through $X,Y,Z,L,R,D,H,P,N$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ApartnessCotransitivityUp$, $\ApartnessRealUp$, $\LocatedApartnessUp$, $\RegularCauchyApartnessOrderUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ApartnessCotransitivityUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier admits exactly the finite packet in which the source apartness of $X$ and $Y$ is witnessed by one displayed branch, either $L$ or $R$, together with the tolerance and structural rows needed to replay that branch. It has no coordinate for trichotomy, host real equality, quotient streams, countable choice, selected representatives, ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, Lean verification, or bridge checking.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Apartness cotransitivity NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:apartness-cotransitivity-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\ApartnessCotransitivityUp$ carrier
$$
K=(X,Y,Z,L,R,D,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\ApartnessCotransitivityUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the two source real names, the third real name, the left and right branch rows, the dyadic tolerance row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item branch stability under classifier transport, requiring $H$ and $C$ to preserve the displayed left-or-right branch tag and to transport the active branch through the same located-apartness and regular Cauchy apartness-order route;
\item dyadic tolerance preservation, requiring $D$ to carry the positive separation budget, slack, and finite precision data consumed by the active branch before any $\RealUp$ seal is read;
\item $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff, requiring each active branch to pass through a displayed regular rational readback before it is treated as a real-name apartness read;
\item $\RealUp$ seal non-escape, requiring the terminal real-name rows $X,Y,Z$ to remain endpoints and branch parameters rather than sources of host equality, quotient equality, selected representatives, trichotomy, countable choice, or completeness;
\item ledger exclusion, requiring $P$ and $N$ to name exactly the displayed cotransitivity packet and to export no trichotomy, no host real equality, no quotient streams, no countable choice, no selected representatives, and no $\RealUp$ completeness theorem.
\end{enumerate}
No apartness-cotransitivity obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}

$$
The consumer receives exactly one displayed cotransitive branch: either the left branch witnessing $X$ apart from $Z$, or the right branch witnessing $Z$ apart from $Y$. In both cases the Real-facing consumer reaches the endpoint names only after the displayed branch, regular handoff, and dyadic tolerance data have been opened. The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name that same branch.

No unlisted principle can be read from the packet. Trichotomy, host equality, quotient streams, countable-choice branch selection, selected representatives, and ambient completeness are absent from the carrier and from the local NameCert obligations.

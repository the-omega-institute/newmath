\begin{corollary}[Framed finite-spine paper certificate for hsame]
\label{cor:framed-list-carrier-certificate-hsame}
Let $A:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a BEDC history source carrier and let
$\mathsf{cert}:\NameCert(A,\hsame)$. Define the framed carrier by
$$
\mathsf{FramedListHistoryCarrier}_A(h)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\exists xs:\mathsf{HistSpine}.\;\mathsf{FramedListSpineRep}_A(h,xs),
$$
and define the hsame framed bridge classifier by
$$
\mathsf{FramedListBridgeClassifier}_{A,\hsame}(h,k)
\quad\Longleftrightarrow\quad
\exists xs\,ys.\;
\mathsf{FramedListSpineRep}_A(h,xs)\land
\mathsf{FramedListSpineRep}_A(k,ys)\land
\mathsf{ListClassifierSpec}(\hsame,xs,ys).
$$
With pattern and ledger predicates both given by
$\mathsf{FramedListHistoryCarrier}_A$, these fields form the paper certificate
target
$$
\mathsf{SemanticNameCert}(\mathsf{FramedListHistoryCarrier}_A,
\mathsf{FramedListHistoryCarrier}_A,
\mathsf{FramedListHistoryCarrier}_A,
\mathsf{FramedListBridgeClassifier}_{A,\hsame}).
$$
\end{corollary}
\begin{proof}
Apply Corollary~\ref{cor:framed-list-carrier-certificate} with
source classifier $\hsame$. The compatibility premise is exactly
Theorem~\ref{thm:list-source-hsame-compatible-hsame}, and the remaining source
certificate input is the displayed $\mathsf{cert}:\NameCert(A,\hsame)$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[List spine bridge visible endpoint transport]
\label{thm:list-spine-bridge-visible-endpoint-transport}
Let $A:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a BEDC history source carrier and let
$\sim_A$ be a source classifier. Visible endpoint transport holds for the framed
list bridge classifier:
$$
\mathsf{FramedListBridgeClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(\Eone(h),\Eone(k))
\to \hsame(h,h')\to \hsame(k,k')\to
\mathsf{FramedListBridgeClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(\Eone(h'),\Eone(k')).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Unpack the bridge witness into finite spines, framed representations of the two
visible endpoints, and the finite-spine classifier witness. Apply visible
history congruence to the endpoint equalities $\hsame(h,h')$ and
$\hsame(k,k')$, then transport the two framed spine representations along the
resulting endpoint equalities. The finite-spine classifier witness is unchanged,
so the same spines rebuild the bridge classifier at the transported visible
endpoints.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[List public finite-data exit package]
\label{thm:list-public-finite-data-exit-package}
Let $A:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a BEDC history source carrier, let
$\sim_A$ be a source classifier, and let
$\mathsf{cert}:\NameCert(A,\sim_A)$. If
$\mathsf{ListConsBoundaryCoherent}_{A,\sim_A}$ holds, then the public
$\ListUp$ object carries the Phase Finite Data exit package:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item
  $\mathsf{SemanticNameCert}(\mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A,
  \mathsf{ListHistoryPattern}_A,
  \mathsf{ListHistoryLedger}_A,
  \mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A})$.
  \item
  The public carrier is exact against both the finite pattern predicate and the
  ledger predicate:
  $$
  \forall h.\;
  \mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A(h)
  \Longleftrightarrow
  \mathsf{ListHistoryPattern}_A(h)
  $$
  and
  $$
  \forall h.\;
  \mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A(h)
  \Longleftrightarrow
  \mathsf{ListHistoryLedger}_A(h).
  $$
  \item
  Public length is total and well-defined:
  $$
  \forall h.\;\mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A(h)\to
  \exists n.\;\mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(h,n)
  $$
  and
  $$
  \forall h\,n\,m.\;\mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(h,n)\to
  \mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(h,m)\to n=m.
  $$
  \item
  Classified endpoints share public length:
  $$
  \forall h\,k.\;\mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(h,k)\to
  \exists n.\;\mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(h,n)\land
  \mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(k,n)
  $$
  and
  $$
  \forall h\,k\,n\,m.\;
  \mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(h,k)\to
  \mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(h,n)\to
  \mathsf{ListPublicLength}_A(k,m)\to n=m.
  $$
  \item
  Every classified endpoint pair exposes finite spine ledgers:
  $$
  \forall h\,k.\;\mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(h,k)\to
  \left(
  \begin{array}{l}
  (\exists xs:\mathsf{HistSpine}.\;\mathsf{ListSpineRep}_A(h,xs))\\
  \land\\
  (\exists ys:\mathsf{HistSpine}.\;\mathsf{ListSpineRep}_A(k,ys))
  \end{array}
  \right).
  $$
  \item
  The same classifier is exactly the nil-nil or cons-cons constructor audit:
  $$
  \forall h\,k.\;
  \mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(h,k)
  \Longleftrightarrow
  \begin{array}{l}
  (\hsame(h,\emp)\land \hsame(k,\emp))\\
  \lor\\
  \exists a\,b\,t\,u\,p\,q:\mathsf{BHist}.\;
  \left(
  \begin{array}{l}
  A(a)\land A(b)\land
  \mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A(t)\land
  \mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A(u)\land\\
  \ContR(a,t,p)\land \ContR(b,u,q)\land
  \hsame(h,\Eone(p))\land \hsame(k,\Eone(q))\land\\
  \sim_A(a,b)\land
  \mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(t,u)
  \end{array}
  \right).
  \end{array}
  $$
  \item
  The classified successor component readback package is independent of the
  chosen six-tuple witness, in the sense of
  Theorem~\ref{thm:list-classified-successor-component-unique-existence}.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:list-public-object-certificate-with-length-readback} to
$\mathsf{cert}$ and the cons-boundary coherence witness. Its five displayed
fields give the semantic certificate, totality of public length, well-definedness
of public length, shared length for classified endpoints, and equality of public
length values across classified endpoints.

Apply Theorem~\ref{thm:list-history-carrier-pattern-ledger-equivalence} with
$S=A$. This supplies the exactness of $\mathsf{ListHistoryCarrier}_A$ against
both $\mathsf{ListHistoryPattern}_A$ and $\mathsf{ListHistoryLedger}_A$.

Next apply Theorem~\ref{thm:list-public-object-constructor-audit}. This supplies
the endpoint spine-ledger exposure for every
$\mathsf{ListSpineHistoryClassifier}_{A,\sim_A}(h,k)$ witness and the nil-or-cons
constructor audit for the same classifier.

Finally use
Theorem~\ref{thm:list-classified-successor-component-unique-existence}. It
turns the classified successor exactness clause into a witness-choice
independent component package for the public object. Repack the three theorem
outputs into the seven displayed fields.
\end{proof}

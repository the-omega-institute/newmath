The $\RandomVarUp$ packet sits between the source probability surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-probspace-namecert} and the distribution readback surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-distribution-namecert}. This dependency is substantive: the source sample carrier and probability ledger come from $\ProbSpaceUp$, while the measurable-output route supplies the pushforward and readback surface later consumed by $\DistributionUp$. Thus a carried random variable is not an isolated measurable map; it is the finite route by which source probability rows are made available to distribution-facing consumers.

\begin{theorem}[LieGroup singleton standard bridge]
\label{thm:liegroup-singleton-standard-bridge}
The singleton BEDC $\LieGroupUp$ packet of
\autoref{def:liegroup-singleton-public-smooth-group-package} interprets as the
standard one-point Lie group. The bridge sends the carrier $L_{\emp}$ to the
singleton carrier, reads multiplication and inverse from the singleton
$\GroupUp$ rows, and reads smoothness from the singleton $\ManifoldUp$ chart
rows. It carries no non-singleton Lie theory, exponential theory,
representation theory, root-system theory, or host quotient data.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The carrier bridge is the singleton carrier of
\autoref{thm:liegroup-singleton-carrier-obligation}. Every accepted history is
$R_{\emp}$-classified with $\emp$, so the target carrier has one point.

The algebraic operation bridge is supplied by
\autoref{thm:liegroup-singleton-multiplication-smooth-homomorphism} and
\autoref{thm:liegroup-singleton-inverse-smooth-endomap}. Multiplication and
inverse stay in $L_{\emp}$ and are classifier-equal to the singleton endpoint.

The smooth bridge is supplied by
\autoref{thm:liegroup-singleton-chart-operation-readback}. The only chart is
the singleton chart, and every operation readback is the constant smooth
self-overlap row. Repacking these rows gives the standard one-point Lie group
interpretation and no further Lie-group structure.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Compact-uniform finite-cover selector obligations]
\label{thm:compact-uniform-finite-cover-selector-obligations}
For every accepted $\CompactUniformFiniteCoverSelectorUp$ carrier $S=(K,F,E,M,R,U,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\CompactUniformFiniteCoverSelectorUp}$ surface consists exactly of five obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the compact source, continuous-function row, finite cover selector, pointwise modulus ledger, rational lower-bound fold, uniform handoff, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item finite-cover accountability, requiring the selected centers and half-radius coverage rows in $E$ to be read from the displayed compact source $K$;
\item pointwise-modulus compatibility, requiring every selected center in $E$ to carry the matching pointwise modulus row in $M$ for the same continuous-function row $F$;
\item rational-fold exactness and uniform handoff, requiring $R$ to be a certified lower bound for the displayed radii before $U$ exports the tolerance-only precision row;
\item ledger refusal, stating that countable choice, external finite minima, global modulus oracles, host open-cover compactness, and ambient completeness are not carrier coordinates.
\end{enumerate}
No accepted selector packet carries any additional NameCert obligation.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier. The compact source, continuous-function row, finite cover selector, pointwise modulus ledger, rational fold, and uniform handoff are precisely $K,F,E,M,R,U$.

The finite-cover accountability row is read first. Row $E$ may list only centers and half-radius coverage witnesses supplied by the compact source row $K$.

The pointwise modulus row is then checked at those centers. Compatibility requires the ledger $M$ to use the same continuous-function row $F$ and the same selected centers exposed by $E$.

The rational fold $R$ is read before the uniform handoff $U$. It certifies one positive precision history below the displayed half-radii, so $U$ exports a tolerance-only uniform-modulus row without selecting a radius outside the finite ledger.

Rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name this route. Since no further coordinate occurs in $S$, the refused external principles cannot be exported by the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\section{Cauchy witness ledger classifier route coverage}
\label{sec:cauchy-witness-ledger-classifier-route-coverage}

\begin{theorem}[Cauchy witness ledger classifier route coverage]
\label{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-classifier-route-coverage}
Let $L=(Q,B,S,K,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\CauchyWitnessLedgerUp$ packet.
Every public consumer of the completion-seal classifier row $K$ factors through
the displayed Real-facing route
$$
Q \longmapsto B \longmapsto S \longmapsto K
$$
together with the structural rows $H,C,P,N$. In particular, no classifier
consumer can replace that route by quotient stream equality, host $\RealUp$
equality, selected external limits, countable choice, ambient completeness,
hidden tails, or an alternate $\RealUp$ seal.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.CauchyWitnessLedgerUp.CauchyWitnessLedgerCarrier\_classifier\_route\_coverage}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted packet by
\autoref{def:cauchy-witness-ledger-carrier}. Its public coordinates are
exactly the diagonal budget $Q$, observation budget $B$, synchronizer $S$,
completion-seal classifier $K$, and structural rows $H,C,P,N$.

Apply \autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-namecert-obligations} and
\autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-budget-factorization}. The NameCert surface
certifies the displayed rows, while the budget factorization orders the
Real-facing route as $Q \longmapsto B \longmapsto S \longmapsto K$ before any
classifier read is available to a public consumer.

Next use \autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-budget-source-coherence} and
\autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-synchronizer-classifier-lock}. The source
coherence identifies the finite stream, dyadic, regular-rational, and Real
faces consumed by $Q$ and $B$; the synchronizer-classifier lock carries that
same source through $S$ into $K$ without adding a second classifier route.

It remains to connect this finite route to the Real-facing seal. Apply
\autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-route-determinacy} and
\autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-real-completion-handoff}. Once the route
and structural rows are fixed, the completion-seal classifier read is
determined, and every $\RealUp$-facing handoff reaches $K$ only through the
displayed budget, observation, and synchronizer rows.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:cauchy-witness-ledger-forbidden-coordinate-exclusion}.
The excluded objects have no carrier coordinate, no NameCert entry, and no
structural row in $H,C,P,N$. A public consumer of $K$ therefore cannot replace
the displayed route by quotient stream equality, host $\RealUp$ equality,
selected external limits, countable choice, ambient completeness, hidden tails,
or an alternate $\RealUp$ seal.
\end{proof}

The $\FiniteCauchyApproximationUp$ interface isolates the finite observation packet used when a regular rational source is read at one displayed precision before a $\RealUp$ consumer crosses the Cauchy-limit boundary. It records the selected threshold, stream window, dyadic observation ledger, error row, and handoff route as finite $\BHist$ data, so later completion arguments can cite a named approximation surface without importing a selected real value or an ambient completeness principle.

\begin{definition}[Finite Cauchy approximation carrier]
\label{def:finite-cauchy-approximation-carrier}
A $\FiniteCauchyApproximationUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
F=(R,n,k,W,D,E,S,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed coordinates:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $R$ is the source $\RegSeqRatUp$ row whose rational stream is read through an attached $\StreamNameUp$ surface;
\item $n$ is the unary precision row naming the requested finite tolerance level;
\item $k$ is the $\CauchyModulusUp$ threshold row displayed for precision $n$;
\item $W$ is the finite $\StreamNameUp$ window selected at and beyond the threshold row $k$;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation ledger recording the dyadic representatives read from $W$;
\item $E$ is the explicit dyadic error-bound row used to compare the selected observations;
\item $S$ is the $\RealUp$ and $\CauchyLimitSealUp$ handoff row that exposes the finite approximation to a real-facing consumer boundary;
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transports for the source, precision row, threshold, window, dyadic ledger, error bound, handoff, provenance, and local name;
\item $C$ stores the displayed $\Cont$ routes from $R$ to the stream window, from $n$ to $k$, from $W$ to $D$ and $E$, and from the finite packet to the handoff row $S$;
\item $P$ records $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\CauchyModulusUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\DyadicAbsoluteValueUp$, $\CauchyModulusMeetUp$, $\CauchyLimitSealUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\FiniteCauchyApproximationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for quotient stream equality, selected real values, infinite tails, host real metrics, or $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

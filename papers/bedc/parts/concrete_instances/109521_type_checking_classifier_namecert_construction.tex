\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-type-checking-classifier-namecert}
\origin{human}

The $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$ packet realizes the type-checking-as-$\Ext$-membership vision of \autoref{ch:visions-type-checking-as-classifier-membership} as one finite NameCert carrier. It sits beside the classifier-membership packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typeclassifiermembership-namecert}, the typing-classifier route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-classifiertyping-namecert}, the subject-reduction surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-subjectreduction-namecert}, the derivation-trace packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typecheckingderivationtrace-namecert}, and the proof-erasure route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-prooftermerasuretrace-namecert}. The packet records type checking as finite membership data: it does not export a host conversion oracle, semantic equality of terms, normalization theorem, hidden proof object, quotient syntax, or ambient metatheory.

\begin{definition}[TypeCheckingClassifier carrier]
\label{def:type-checking-classifier-carrier}
A $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
Q=(T,J,E,D,S,R,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $T$ is the finite term row. It names the inspected term histories and their syntactic classifier.
\item $J$ is the typing-ledger row. It records the finite judgment entries used by the checker.
\item $E$ is the $\Ext$-membership row, identifying accepted typed terms as classifier members.
\item $D$ is the derivation-trace row linking each accepted membership read to a displayed typing derivation.
\item $S$ is the subject-reduction row, routing accepted $\Ext$ membership through displayed $\Cont$ steps.
\item $R$ is the erasure row, separating proof-term content from the erased computational term while preserving the displayed membership route.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for term, typing-ledger, $\Ext$-membership, derivation-trace, subject-reduction, erasure, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a type-checking request through membership, derivation, subject-reduction, and erasure rows.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$, $\TypeClassifierMembershipUp$, $\ClassifierTypingUp$, $\SubjectReductionUp$, $\TypeCheckingDerivationTraceUp$, $\ProofTermErasureTraceUp$, $\BHist$, $\Ext$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\TypeCheckingClassifierUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for host conversion, semantic equality of terms, an ambient normalization theorem, hidden proof objects, quotient syntax, or external metatheoretic consistency.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[TypeCheckingClassifier NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:type-checking-classifier-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$ carrier $Q=(T,J,E,D,S,R,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\TypeCheckingClassifierUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for the term row, typing ledger, $\Ext$-membership row, derivation trace, subject-reduction row, erasure row, componentwise transport, continuation replay, provenance, and local naming. Every accepted type-checking read must expose membership through $E$, derivation through $D$, subject reduction through $S$, and erasure separation through $R$ before $H,C,P,N$ package the row. No obligation is stored in host conversion, semantic equality of terms, normalization, quotient syntax, hidden proof objects, or ambient metatheory.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:type-checking-classifier-carrier}. The tuple lists exactly the term, judgment, membership, derivation, subject-reduction, erasure, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

Replay the continuation row $C$. A type-checking request first reads the finite term row $T$ and typing ledger $J$, then the $\Ext$-membership row $E$ and derivation trace $D$. Subject reduction is read from $S$, and proof-term erasure is kept in the displayed row $R$.

Transport by $H$ is componentwise over those rows. The provenance and naming rows $P,N$ therefore package only the finite classifier route, leaving no coordinate from which a host oracle, quotient syntax, hidden proof object, or ambient metatheorem could be exported.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[TypeCheckingClassifier membership soundness]
\label{thm:type-checking-classifier-membership-soundness}
Let $Q=(T,J,E,D,S,R,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\TypeCheckingClassifierUp$ packet. Every successful type-checking answer exported by $Q$ is an $\Ext$-membership read supported by the displayed typing ledger and derivation trace:
$$
T,J \longmapsto E,D \longmapsto S,R,H,C,P,N .
$$
The subject-reduction row preserves the membership read along displayed $\Cont$ replay, and the erasure row separates proof-term evidence from erased computational content. The handoff preserves $T,J,E,D,S,R,H,C,P,N$ and exports no host conversion oracle, semantic term equality, normalization theorem, hidden proof object, quotient syntax, or ambient consistency principle.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with a successful type-checking answer. By carrier admission, the answer can only be read through the rows of \autoref{def:type-checking-classifier-carrier}.

The finite term row $T$ and typing ledger $J$ provide the displayed input to the classifier. The $\Ext$ row $E$ records membership, and the derivation trace $D$ records the finite route witnessing the accepted judgment.

Subject reduction is then read as the displayed row $S$ along $\Cont$ replay, while $R$ keeps proof-term erasure separate from the erased computational term. Componentwise transport, provenance, and naming preserve this route through $H,C,P,N$ without adding the excluded host-level principles.
\end{proof}

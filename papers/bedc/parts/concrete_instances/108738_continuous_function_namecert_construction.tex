\begin{definition}[Continuous function carrier]
\label{def:continuous-function-carrier}
A $\ContinuousFunctionUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
F=(S,T,M,Q,W,R,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the source-window row, exposing the finite $\StreamNameUp$ observations and source-domain histories inspected by the continuity request.
\item $T$ is the target-readback row, exposing the target $\RealUp$ or sibling endpoint readback reached by the displayed source window.
\item $M$ is the finite modulus row, translating each target tolerance request into the source-window budget used by the packet.
\item $Q$ is the continuity-certificate row binding $S,T,M$ to the displayed request.
\item $W$ is the shared finite window ledger over $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and any $\FormalBallUp$ radius window opened by the same request.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ and $\RealUp$ readback row used when a continuous-function consumer asks for a real-valued endpoint.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for source, target, modulus, certificate, window, readback, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a source-window request through $S,T,M,Q,W,R$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ContinuousFunctionUp$, $\ContinuousFunctionCarrierUp$, $\ContinuousMapUp$, $\RealUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\FormalBallUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ContinuousFunctionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,T,M,Q,W,R,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for host function extensionality, countable choice, quotient equality of maps or reals, selected modulus functions, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Continuous function NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:continuous-function-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\ContinuousFunctionUp$ carrier $F=(S,T,M,Q,W,R,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\ContinuousFunctionUp}$ surface consists exactly of source-window admission through $S$, target readback through $T$, finite modulus exposure through $M$, continuity-certificate binding through $Q$, shared $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\FormalBallUp$ window routing through $W$, real-facing readback through $R$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:continuous-function-carrier}. The rows $S,T,M,Q,W,R,H,C,P,N$ are the only local coordinates, so source admission, target readback, modulus exposure, continuity binding, window routing, readback, transport, replay, provenance, and naming must all be read there.

The analytic route is finite. The modulus row $M$ translates a target tolerance into a source-window budget, $Q$ binds that budget to the displayed source and target rows, and $W,R$ expose the StreamName, RegSeqRat, FormalBall, and Real-facing readbacks attached to the same request.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ preserve and name the same route. Since the carrier has no host-function, function-extensionality, quotient-equality, choice, selected-modulus, or ambient-completeness coordinate, the listed rows exhaust the NameCert obligation surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Continuous function Real-map handoff]
\label{thm:continuous-function-real-map-handoff}
Every $\ContinuousMapUp$, compact-uniform-continuity, or real-valued modulus consumer of an accepted $\ContinuousFunctionUp$ packet receives the map only through
$$
S,M,Q \longmapsto W \longmapsto T,R \longmapsto H,C,P,N .
$$
Thus the consumer may use the source window, finite modulus, continuity certificate, shared $\StreamNameUp$ and $\RegSeqRatUp$ windows, optional $\FormalBallUp$ radius interface, target readback, real seal, componentwise transport, displayed replay, provenance, and local naming rows. It receives no host function object, function extensionality principle, countable-choice selector, quotient equality of maps or reals, selected limit, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness witness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Next read the finite windows. The row $W$ exposes the relevant $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\FormalBallUp$ faces, while $T$ and $R$ expose the target and real-facing readbacks reached by that same finite route.

The rows $H,C,P,N$ then transport, replay, source, and name the handoff. Row exhaustion in \autoref{def:continuous-function-carrier} excludes every host-function, function-extensionality, quotient-equality, choice, selected-limit, and ambient-completeness escape route.
\end{proof}

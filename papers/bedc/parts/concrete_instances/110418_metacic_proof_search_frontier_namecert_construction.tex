\begin{definition}[MetaCIC proof-search frontier carrier]
\label{def:metacic-proof-search-frontier-carrier}

$$

\item $D$ is the $\SubjectReductionDischargeSocketUp$ row, recording the socket through which proof-search evidence must pass before a closed-consistency consumer may read the subject-reduction boundary.
\item $N$ is the $\MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp$ row, exposing candidate evidence, closed-candidate evidence, finished-normal endpoints, and retained normalization obstruction data.

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC proof-search frontier NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metacic-proof-search-frontier-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MetaCICProofSearchFrontierUp$ carrier $F=(K,D,N,B,A,E,O,T,C,P,L)$, the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICProofSearchFrontierUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item critical-path exposure through $K$;
\item subject-reduction discharge-socket exposure through $D$;
\item normalization-frontier exposure through $N$;
\item bounded normal-conversion bridge exposure through $B$;
\item candidate-evidence admission through $A$;
\item discharge-socket evidence pairing through $E$;
\item obstruction retention through $O$;
\item componentwise transport through $T$;
\item displayed replay through $C$;
\item provenance through $P$ and local naming through $L$.
\end{enumerate}
No accepted proof-search read may skip the discharge socket row, and no local obligation is stored outside $K,D,N,B,A,E,O,T,C,P,L$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:metacic-proof-search-frontier-carrier}. Rows $K,D,N,B$ are the already named frontier surfaces, while $A$ and $E$ store the local candidate and discharge-socket evidence.

The closed-consistency-facing read must pass through $D$ and $E$. Candidate evidence in $A$ may reach a bounded normal endpoint through $N$ and $B$, but it is not eligible for the consistency handoff until the discharge socket row has been exposed.

The obstruction row $O$ remains part of the packet throughout the read. It prevents the bounded proof-search frontier from being cited as unrestricted subject reduction, unrestricted normalization, global conversion decidability, kernel consistency, or proof-search completeness.

Finally $T,C,P,L$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite route. Since the carrier has no coordinate for the excluded endpoints or for an unmediated closed-consistency read, the listed rows exhaust the NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

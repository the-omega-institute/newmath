It extends the untyped syntax and substitution corridor of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-lambdacalc-namecert}, keeps computability comparisons separate from the machine models of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-computable-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-turingmachine-namecert}, and routes downstream dependent typing through the displayed MetaCIC typing, substitution, and subject-reduction surfaces of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-typing-judgment-classifier-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-closed-substitution-boundary-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-subject-reduction-discharge-socket-namecert}, and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacic-subject-reduction-frontier-namecert}. \begin{theorem}[CalculusOfConstructions NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:calculus-of-constructions-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:calculus-of-constructions-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\CalculusOfConstructionsUp}$ surface consists exactly of finite obligations for the sort row $S$, dependent-product row $\Pi$, lambda row $L$, application row $A$, context row $\Gamma$, substitution row $\Sigma$, beta-boundary row $B$, subject-reduction boundary row $R$, componentwise transport $H$, displayed replay $K$, provenance $P$, and local naming $N$. Any accepted public read of the packet must pass through these rows and cannot replace them by a normalization theorem, a consistency theorem, a quotient conversion oracle, or host function extensionality.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier $C$. Its primitive calculus data are exactly $S,\Pi,L,A,\Gamma,\Sigma$, so the syntax, context, and substitution obligations are finite rows rather than ambient metatheoretic assumptions.

The boundary rows $B$ and $R$ add only local beta computation and the parameterised subject-reduction route. They do not add a closed subject-reduction theorem, because the route is read through the existing discharge socket and frontier.

Finally inspect $H,K,P,N$. Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming preserve the displayed row inventory. Since no other coordinate is present, a consumer has no place from which to extract consistency, normalization, quotient term equality, or host extensionality.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[CalculusOfConstructions substitution typing route]
\label{thm:calculus-of-constructions-substitution-typing-route}
Every accepted $\CalculusOfConstructionsUp$ consumer that asks for a substitution-stable typing route must read the carrier in the order
$$
\Gamma,\Sigma \longrightarrow S,\Pi,L,A \longrightarrow B,R \longrightarrow H,K,P,N.
$$
The context and substitution rows supply the finite substitution ledger, the sort and term-former rows supply the dependent typing surface, the beta and subject-reduction rows expose only the existing MetaCIC boundary, and the transport, replay, provenance, and naming rows keep the route inside the same finite packet. \end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Next read $S,\Pi,L,A$. The sort, dependent-product, lambda, and application rows provide the dependent typing surface on which the substitution ledger acts. This is the calculus surface inherited from the Coquand--Huet presentation and routed through the $\LambdaCalcUp$ syntax corridor.

The rows $B,R$ then expose the boundary to MetaCIC: beta computation is local to the displayed substitution row, and subject reduction is only the parameterised discharge route named by the socket and frontier chapters.

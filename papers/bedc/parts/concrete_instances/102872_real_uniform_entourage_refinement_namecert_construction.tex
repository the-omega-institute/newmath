\begin{theorem}[Real uniform entourage-refinement NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:real-uniform-entourage-refinement-obligations}
For every accepted $\RealUniformEntourageRefinementUp$ carrier
$$
E_{\mathrm{ref}}=(R,U,\epsilon,\delta,B,Y,F,S,Q,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\RealUniformEntourageRefinementUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed Real uniform source, requested entourage, dyadic request radius, smaller dyadic radius, basis containment row, symmetric-shrink row, Cauchy-filter handoff, StreamName windows, RegSeqRat readback, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming row;
\item requested-entourage grounding, requiring $U$ to be read from the Real uniform source row $R$ and named by the positive dyadic request radius $\epsilon$;
\item smaller-radius selection, requiring $\delta$ to be a displayed positive dyadic radius with $2\delta\leq\epsilon$ in the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ ledger;
\item containment refinement, requiring $B$ to exhibit the $\delta$-ball Real basis entourage as a contained sub-entourage of $U$;
\item symmetric shrink, requiring $Y$ to replay the $\delta$-ball self-composition inside the requested $\epsilon$-entourage;
\item Cauchy-filter handoff, requiring every completion-facing read in $F$ to consume $B$ and $Y$ before reading the finite rows $S$ and $Q$;
\item classifier transport, requiring $H$ and $C$ to preserve the displayed route $R,U,\epsilon,\delta,B,Y,F,S,Q$ componentwise under $\hsame$ and replay it under $\Cont$;
\item provenance non-escape, requiring $P$ and $N$ to export no quotient completion, selected limit, open-cover compactness, countable-choice selector, trichotomy, bridge-checked claim, Lean-verified claim, or ambient Real completeness principle.

No local NameCert obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is the finite projection from \autoref{def:real-uniform-entourage-refinement-carrier}. The projection exposes exactly the Real uniform source, the requested entourage, the two dyadic radius rows, the basis containment row, the symmetric-shrink row, the Cauchy-filter handoff, finite windows, readback, and the structural rows $H,C,P,N$.

The requested-entourage row is grounded in the Real uniform source. A consumer first reads $R$, then reads the request $U$, and then identifies the positive dyadic radius $\epsilon$ that names the displayed Real metric-ball request.

The smaller-radius row is internal to the dyadic ledger. The row $\delta$ carries the positive-radius witness and the comparison $2\delta\leq\epsilon$, so the containment and symmetric-shrink rows do not need a choice principle or an ambient completeness principle.

Containment refinement is supplied by the Real entourage-base packet. The row $B$ reads the $\delta$-ball basis entourage and displays the witness that this basis row lies inside the requested entourage $U$.

The symmetric-shrink row is the finite composition witness. It records that two $\delta$-steps remain within the $\epsilon$ request, and it is replayed before any completion-facing consumer reaches the Cauchy-filter handoff.

The handoff and readback rows are downstream of the refinement surface. A consumer reads $F$ only after $B$ and $Y$ have been replayed, and the finite observations $S$ and $Q$ expose StreamName windows and RegSeqRat comparisons for that same route.

Finally, transport and provenance add no new mathematical primitive. The row $H$ preserves each displayed coordinate componentwise, $C$ replays the same finite route, and $P,N$ name only the rows already present in $E_{\mathrm{ref}}$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Real uniform entourage-refinement completion handoff]
\label{thm:real-uniform-entourage-refinement-handoff}

\begin{proof}

\begin{theorem}[RealDyadicOrderEmbedding NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:real-dyadic-order-embedding-namecert-obligations}

\item carrier admission for the displayed dyadic endpoint rows, order row, embedding rows, stream windows, regular readbacks, density and located-Archimedean handoff, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming;
\item dyadic-order exactness, requiring $O$ to read the comparison of the endpoint rows $D_0,D_1$ from the same $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ ledger rather than from a detached host order;

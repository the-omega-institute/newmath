\begin{definition}[Located positive real carrier]
\label{def:located-positive-real-carrier}
A $\LocatedPositiveRealUp$ carrier is a finite packet
$$
X=(R,P,A,L,D,W,Q,H,C,S,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $R$ is the sealed $\RealUp$ row for the real-name presentation being certified.
\item $P$ is the $\PositiveRealUp$ row naming positivity by apartness from the zero real-name row.
\item $A$ is the $\ApartnessRealUp$ separation-to-zero row whose endpoints are the zero real-name row and $R$.
\item $L$ is the $\LocatedApartnessUp$ handoff row displaying how the separation witness is inspected at the requested finite window.
\item $D$ is a positive $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ radius row used by $A$ and exposed again by $L$.
\item $W$ is the finite $\StreamNameUp$ window through which the zero row, $R$, and the radius comparison are observed.
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row attached to the windows in $W$ and to the rational observations used by the radius comparison.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the real seal, positive row, apartness row, located handoff, dyadic radius, stream window, readback, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay of every consumer route through
$$
P,A,L,D,W,Q \longmapsto R .
$$
\item $S$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\LocatedPositiveRealUp$, $\PositiveRealUp$, $\ApartnessRealUp$, $\LocatedApartnessUp$, $\LocatedRealUp$, $\RealUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\LocatedPositiveRealUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $R,P,A,L,D,W,Q,H,C,S,N$. It has no coordinate for trichotomy, a total order on constructive reals, quotient stream equality, a selected limit, ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, Lean verification, or a standard bridge.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Located positive real NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:located-positive-real-namecert-obligations}

$$

\item carrier admission for the displayed real seal, positive-real row, apartness-to-zero row, located-apartness handoff, positive dyadic radius, stream window, regular readback, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item positivity alignment, requiring $P$ to read its positive witness only through the same $A,D,W,Q$ rows displayed by the carrier;
\item located-apartness exposure, requiring $L$ to display the inspection route for $A$ before any metric, order, or completion-facing consumer can use the separation from zero;
\item radius exactness, requiring $D$ to be the unique positive dyadic radius row exported by both the apartness and located-apartness coordinates;

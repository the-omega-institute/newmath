\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.CauchyLimitSealUp.CauchyLimitSealCarrier\_regular\_tail\_selector\_sibling\_link}

The non-escape clauses of the cited selector package and meet route exclude quotient stream identity, host real equality, choice-selected limits, hidden family limits, and ambient completeness.

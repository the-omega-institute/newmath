\label{thm:cauchy-limit-seal-tail-budget-meet}

$$

$$

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with tail extraction from \autoref{thm:cauchy-limit-seal-tail-extraction}. It projects every admitted tail read of $L$ from the displayed regular family, stream schedule, dyadic observation ledger, diagonal row, seal row, and visible transport route.

Attach the $\RealObservationBudgetUp$ side using \autoref{thm:cauchy-limit-seal-real-observation-budget-consumer-package}. Finally use budget root source totality from \autoref{thm:cauchy-limit-seal-budget-root-source-totality}. \end{proof}

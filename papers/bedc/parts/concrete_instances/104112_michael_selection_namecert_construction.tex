\begin{theorem}[Michael selection NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:michael-selection-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:michael-selection-carrier}. The rows $D,V,K,L,S,F,H,C,P,N$ are the whole packet, so carrier admission and the visible obligation rows are read directly from the displayed tuple.

The analytic surface is the finite route $D,V,K,L,S$. The paracompact row supplies the cover and refinement ledger, the lower-semicontinuous row supplies value-window exposure, the convex row supplies closed-convex target witnesses, and the local ledger records the finite selection data over displayed cover cells.

Only after those rows are present may the continuous-map row $F$ be read. The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same route, so the excluded choice and host-topology principles have no carrier coordinate and cannot be obtained from the local NameCert.
\end{proof}

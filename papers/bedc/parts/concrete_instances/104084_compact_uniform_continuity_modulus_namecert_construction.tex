\begin{theorem}[Compact uniform continuity modulus NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:compact-uniform-continuity-modulus-namecert-obligations}

The selected rational lower-bound fold is carried by $M$ and checked through $D$. The $\RealUp$ seal boundary $R$ is read only after these dyadic comparisons have been displayed.

The downstream $\UniformModulusUp$ row is therefore a tolerance-only modulus obtained from finite packet data.

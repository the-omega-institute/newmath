\begin{definition}[Diagonal cofinal-tail carrier]
\label{def:diagonal-cofinal-tail-carrier}
A $\DiagonalCofinalTailUp$ carrier is a finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ packet
$$
T=(Q,S,G,D,R,W,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $Q$ is the paired stationary $\RatUp$ seed row, including the two rational history carriers and their $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ row;
\item $S$ is the paired constant $\StreamNameUp$ window row induced by $Q$;
\item $G$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ tail readback row for the selected diagonal window;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ finite-window ledger for the same selected tail window;
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ diagonal representative row read only through $S,G,D$;
\item $W$ records the transported stationary window seal whose RatUp, StreamNameUp, RegSeqRatUp, DyadicRatCoreUp, and RealUp readings are the four public readings of \autoref{thm:real-completeness-transported-stationary-window-seal-exhaustion};
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $Q,S,G,D,R,W$ and the structural rows;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes from $Q$ to $S$, from $S$ to $G$, from $G$ to $D$, from $D$ to $R$, and from $W$ to each public window reading;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\DiagonalCofinalTailUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier is finite. It has no coordinate for an infinite window family, external equality identification, selected real limit, unrecorded selector, or ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

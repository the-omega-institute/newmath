The $\DiagonalCofinalTailUp$ packet realizes the cofinal-tail reading of the transported stationary diagonal window as a finite $\NameCert$ surface. It keeps the rational seed classifier, the constant stream windows, the regular-rational tail readbacks, the dyadic finite-window ledgers, the real-facing seal row, transport rows, continuation routes, provenance, and local naming in one displayed packet. It does not export an external equality identification, a selected limit, an unrecorded window selector, or an ambient completeness principle.

\begin{definition}[Diagonal cofinal-tail carrier]
\label{def:diagonal-cofinal-tail-carrier}
A $\DiagonalCofinalTailUp$ carrier is a finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ packet
$$
T=(Q,S,G,D,R,W,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $Q$ is the paired stationary $\RatUp$ seed row, including the two rational history carriers and their $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}$ row;
\item $S$ is the paired constant $\StreamNameUp$ window row induced by $Q$;
\item $G$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ tail readback row for the selected diagonal window;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ finite-window ledger for the same selected tail window;
\item $R$ is the $\RealUp$ diagonal representative row read only through $S,G,D$;
\item $W$ records the transported stationary window seal whose RatUp, StreamNameUp, RegSeqRatUp, DyadicRatCoreUp, and RealUp readings are the four public readings of \autoref{thm:real-completeness-transported-stationary-window-seal-exhaustion};
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $Q,S,G,D,R,W$ and the structural rows;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes from $Q$ to $S$, from $S$ to $G$, from $G$ to $D$, from $D$ to $R$, and from $W$ to each public window reading;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\DiagonalCofinalTailUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier is finite. It has no coordinate for an infinite window family, external equality identification, selected real limit, unrecorded selector, or ambient completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Diagonal cofinal-tail NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:diagonal-cofinal-tail-namecert-obligations}
The local $\NameCert_{\DiagonalCofinalTailUp}$ surface for \autoref{def:diagonal-cofinal-tail-carrier} consists exactly of carrier admission for $T=(Q,S,G,D,R,W,H,C,P,N)$, stationary $\RatUp$ seed exposure through $Q$, constant $\StreamNameUp$ window exposure through $S$, $\RegSeqRatUp$ cofinal-tail readback exposure through $G$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ finite-window ledger exposure through $D$, $\RealUp$ diagonal representative exposure through $R$, the transported stationary seal row $W$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ route replay through $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from the displayed packet $T$. The rows $Q,S,G,D,R$ expose the rational seeds, constant stream windows, regular-rational readbacks, dyadic window ledgers, and real-facing representatives.

The seal row $W$ is read through the finite-window seal exhaustion theorem in \autoref{thm:real-completeness-transported-stationary-window-seal-exhaustion}. Its public readings are already the RatUp seed classifier, the StreamNameUp constant-window classifier, the RegSeqRatUp finite-window seal consumption row, the DyadicRatCoreUp observation ledger, and the RealUp finite-window classifier.

The rows $H,C,P,N$ account for transport, route replay, package provenance, and local naming. Since the excluded analytic objects have no coordinate in $T$, the listed rows exhaust the local $\NameCert$ obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Diagonal cofinal-tail window exhaustion]
\label{thm:diagonal-cofinal-tail-window-exhaustion}
Every certified finite cofinal-tail observation exported by an accepted $\DiagonalCofinalTailUp$ carrier factors through the finite rows
$$

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier and its local naming surface. The observation rows are $Q,S,G,D,R,W$, and the structural rows are $H,C,P,N$.

Apply \autoref{thm:real-completeness-transported-stationary-window-seal-exhaustion} to the window read by $W$. It says that the finite StreamNameUp, RegSeqRatUp, DyadicRatCoreUp, and RealUp readings all agree with the same RatUp seed classifier.

The continuations in $C$ replay only those readings, and $H$ transports them componentwise. No carrier coordinate remains for an unbounded window family, so every cofinal-tail observation is exhausted by the displayed finite packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Diagonal cofinal-tail selector lock]

Now use \autoref{thm:diagonal-cofinal-tail-real-seal-handoff}. The $\RealUp$-facing read is the transported stationary diagonal row named by the $\RatUp$ seed classifier, so the real-facing selector and the $\RegSeqRatUp$ tail readback are the same finite route.

Since the carrier contains no coordinate for an unrecorded selector, quotient-stream equality, selected limit, or ambient completeness datum, no consumer-visible route remains that could choose a different cofinal tail.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[DoubleCauchyDiagonal NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:double-cauchy-diagonal-namecert-obligations}

No DoubleCauchyDiagonal obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

A diagonal consumer may inspect only the finite row and column windows already displayed by $W$, and the regular observations in $R$ are read through those windows.

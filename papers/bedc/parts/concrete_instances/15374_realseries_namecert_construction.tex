Following Bishop and Bridges constructive analysis, the $\RealSeriesUp$ packet records the finite partial-sum and Cauchy-tail surface for a real-valued term stream. It sits after the $\RealUp$ seal of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, the displayed limit interface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-sequence-limit-namecert}, the bounded Cauchy schedule of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-cauchy-sequence-bounded-namecert}, the regular rational rows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, the finite stream windows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}, and the dyadic tolerance ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dyadicratcore-namecert}. The packet names a series only through displayed finite sums and a shared tail threshold; it does not import host infinite sums, selected limits, quotient equality, countable choice, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[Real series carrier]\label{def:real-series-carrier}
A $\RealSeriesUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
K=(T,S,W,Q,D,M,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $T$ is the real-valued term stream, read only through selected $\StreamNameUp$ windows and $\RegSeqRatUp$ rows for the displayed terms.
\item $S$ is the partial-sum window row, listing the finite initial indices, their accumulated $\RealUp$ sum seals, and the adjacent difference reads induced by $T$.
\item $W$ is the finite family of $\StreamNameUp$ windows used for the terms and for the displayed partial sums.
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row for every selected term and partial-sum window.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance ledger that records finite addition errors, comparison radii, and tail-request tolerances.
\item $M$ is the shared Cauchy-tail threshold row: one displayed threshold governs every partial-sum comparison requested by the packet.
\item $E$ is the $\RealUp$ endpoint seal reached only after the term windows, partial-sum windows, regular readbacks, dyadic ledger, and shared tail threshold have been displayed.
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the term stream, partial-sum window, selected stream windows, regular readbacks, dyadic ledger, tail threshold, endpoint seal, continuation, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ stores displayed $\Cont$ replay from term reads to partial sums, from partial sums to regular readback, from readback to the dyadic ledger, from the ledger to the shared tail threshold, and from that threshold to the endpoint seal.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RealSeriesUp$, $\RealUp$, $\RealSequenceLimitUp$, $\CauchySequenceBoundedUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RealSeriesUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $T,S,W,Q,D,M,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for a host infinite sum, selected sequence limit, quotient equality of real streams, countable-choice selector, trichotomy, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

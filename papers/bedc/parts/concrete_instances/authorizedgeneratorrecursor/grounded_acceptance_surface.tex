The output-facing route must pass through the source signature, emitted generator, motive, branch package, descent, output, audit, transport, continuation, provenance, boundary, and local certificate rows before any consumer may read the output.

Finally apply the root carrier and route surfaces in \autoref{thm:authorized-generator-recursor-root-carrier-admission} and \autoref{thm:authorized-generator-recursor-ground-compiler-route-totality}. \end{proof}

\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\GeneratorFixedPointUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-generatorfixedpoint-namecert}
\origin{ai}

The $\GeneratorFixedPointUp$ packet names the finite certificate surface where an authorized generator returns a displayed output that remains fixed under its own certified continuation boundary. It refines the $\GeneratorClosureUp$ classifier of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-generatorclosure-namecert}: the constructor-list provenance and classifier row are consumed from that sibling before the fixed-output witness is read. A continuation without that witness is not a fixed point of the generator; it is recorded as a $\mathsf{GAP}$ boundary.

\begin{definition}[Generator fixed-point carrier]
\label{def:generator-fixed-point-carrier}
A $\GeneratorFixedPointUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
F=(G,L,Q,W,O,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ is the authorized generator row whose constructor-list closure supplies the only admitted source of generated outputs;
\item $L$ is the constructor-list provenance for $G$, including the displayed witness that the relevant continuation remains inside that list;
\item $Q$ is the $\GeneratorClosureUp$ classifier row that compares generator-internal continuations with boundary attempts;
\item $W$ is the fixed-output witness, recording that the displayed generator continuation returns the same output row under the certified route;
\item $O$ is the displayed output row whose fixed-output status is witnessed by $W$;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transports for $G,L,Q,W,O,P,N$ and the local packet shape;
\item $C$ records the finite $\Cont$ routes by which consumers read the generator row, constructor-list provenance, classifier row, fixed-output witness, output row, package provenance, and local naming row;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BHist$, authorized generator rows, constructor-list provenance, $\GeneratorClosureUp$, fixed-output witnesses, displayed outputs, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and boundary ledger rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\GeneratorFixedPointUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no host-level fixed-point theorem, no self-application oracle, and no induction rule beyond the constructor-list provenance displayed in $L$.
\end{definition}

\label{thm:generator-fixed-point-namecert-obligations}

\item classifier stability stating that the $\GeneratorClosureUp$ row $Q$ is preserved by componentwise $\hsame$ transport of the displayed generator, constructor-list provenance, fixed witness, output row, routes, package provenance, and local certificate;
\item fixed-witness persistence stating that $W$ remains attached to $O$ under the displayed generator-internal continuation routes;

\item ledger exactness stating that every public read of the certificate factors through $G,L,Q,W,O,H,C,P,N$ and no unrecorded fixed-point principle is exported.
\end{enumerate}

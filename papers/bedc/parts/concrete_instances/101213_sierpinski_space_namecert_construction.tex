\begin{definition}[Sierpinski space carrier]
\label{def:sierpinski-space-carrier}
A $\SierpinskiSpaceUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
S=(B,O,W,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $B$ is the $\BoolUp$ truth row, exposing the two accepted observable truth marks and their classifier transport.
\item $O$ is the $\TopologyUp$ open-set row, recording the open under inspection as a displayed observation row rather than as a host proposition.
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-observation row, recording the finite windows through which a countable name reports whether the open has been observed.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for truth rows, open rows, finite-observation windows, replay rows, provenance, and the local naming row.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from an observable-open request through $B,O,W,H,P,N$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\SierpinskiSpaceUp$, $\BoolUp$, $\TopologyUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SierpinskiSpaceUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $B,O,W,H,C,P,N$. It contains no coordinate for host propositions, excluded middle, selected limits, quotient stream equality, countable choice, or a proof of ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Observable open-set classifier]
\label{thm:sierpinski-space-open-set-classifier}
Let $S=(B,O,W,H,C,P,N)$ be an accepted $\SierpinskiSpaceUp$ carrier. Any two accepted observable-open requests are compared only through the displayed open-truth rows $B,O,W$ and the specialization-order readback they determine. The classifier has no access to host propositions, proof irrelevance for ambient opens, excluded middle, or a hidden truth-value evaluator outside the packet.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier. The truth row $B$ supplies the only accepted observable truth marks, and the open row $O$ records the open under inspection as displayed topology data.

The finite-observation row $W$ is the only route by which a countable name can report an observation of that open. Thus a comparison may read whether one displayed observation is below another in the specialization order generated by the observable truth rows, but it cannot inspect an external proposition asserting open membership.

The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name that same finite route. Since none of them stores excluded middle, proof irrelevance, a hidden truth evaluator, selected limits, quotient streams, or ambient Real completeness, the classifier is exhausted by observable open-truth readback.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Sierpinski space NameCert obligation surface]
\label{thm:sierpinski-space-namecert-obligation-surface}
For every accepted $\SierpinskiSpaceUp$ carrier
$$
S=(B,O,W,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\SierpinskiSpaceUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed Bool truth row, Topology open-set row, StreamName finite-observation row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming row;
\item open-truth classifier exactness, requiring every observable-open comparison to read $B,O,W$ before any structural row can be consumed;
\item specialization-order stability, requiring $\hsame$ transport to preserve the observable order read from the two truth marks and the displayed open row;
\item finite-observation stability, requiring every StreamName window in $W$ to report only the displayed observable truth row attached to $O$;
\item ledger non-escape, requiring $H,C,P,N$ to export no excluded middle, selected limits, quotient streams, countable choice, host proposition equality, ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, Lean verification, or bridge credential.
\end{enumerate}
No local observable-open obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:sierpinski-space-carrier}. The accepted packet displays exactly the Bool truth row, the Topology open-set row, finite StreamName observations, and the structural transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

Open-truth classifier exactness follows from the order of the displayed rows. A request can compare observable opens only after reading the truth row $B$, the open row $O$, and the finite observation row $W$.

Specialization-order stability is the componentwise transport clause in $H$. Transport preserves the displayed truth marks and the open row, so the specialization readback determined by them is preserved as well.

Finite-observation stability is the projection to $W$. Each window reports the observable truth row attached to $O$, and no row selects a second stream representative or a detached open-membership proposition.

Finally, ledger non-escape is exhausted by $H,C,P,N$. These rows transport, replay, source, and name the displayed observable-open route only, and none stores excluded middle, selected limits, quotient streams, countable choice, host proposition equality, ambient Real completeness, Lean verification, or a bridge credential.
\end{proof}

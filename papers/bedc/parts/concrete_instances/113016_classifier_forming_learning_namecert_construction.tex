The $\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$ packet realizes the classifier-forming
learning route of \autoref{sec:visions-philosophy-classifier-forming-learning}
as finite NameCert data. It records parameter motion, loss observations,
classifier shift, training residue, audit export, and TasteGate commitments
before any consumer may call the episode certified learning discovery. The
packet is downstream from the registry export surface of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-registry-export-surface-namecert}, the
machine-readable audit interface of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-machine-readable-audit-interface-namecert}, the

GroundCompiler audit-map surface of
\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-groundcompilerauditmap-namecert}.

\begin{definition}[ClassifierFormingLearning carrier]
\label{def:classifier-forming-learning-carrier}
A $\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
L=(D,A,S,Q,R,G,T,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the dataset or task row whose accepted observations are read only
  through displayed histories;
\item $A$ is the parameter-motion row recording the before-and-after training
  state without treating motion itself as discovery;
\item $S$ is the classifier-shift row, naming the finite decision boundary or
  equivalence-class change certified by the packet;
\item $Q$ is the loss, score, or benchmark residue row, kept as evidence
  residue rather than as the learning object;
\item $R$ is the registry-export row tying the learning claim to public
  status and refusal boundaries;
\item $G$ is the audit-interface and GroundCompiler map row that makes the
  packet machine-readable for later checking;
\item $T$ is the $\TasteGate$ row requiring nontriviality and field
  faithfulness for the displayed classifier-shift carrier;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for dataset, parameter,
  classifier-shift, residue, registry, audit, TasteGate, provenance, and
  naming rows;
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from training evidence through
  classifier shift, residue ledger, registry export, audit map, TasteGate,
  provenance, and local naming;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$,
  $\RegistryExportSurfaceUp$, $\MachineReadableAuditInterfaceUp$,
  positive-interpretability-discovery-facing packets,
  $\GroundCompilerAuditMapUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$,
  $\TasteGate$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ClassifierFormingLearningUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through
$D,A,S,Q,R,G,T,H,C,P,N$. Parameter motion and loss reduction are admissible
evidence rows, but they are not identified with learning unless the
classifier-shift row $S$ and the TasteGate row $T$ are present.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[ClassifierFormingLearning NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:classifier-forming-learning-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$ carrier
$L=(D,A,S,Q,R,G,T,H,C,P,N)$, the local
$\NameCert_{\ClassifierFormingLearningUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier
admission, dataset/task exposure, parameter-motion residue, classifier-shift
exposure, loss and benchmark residue, registry export, machine-readable audit
handoff, GroundCompiler audit-map handoff, TasteGate nontriviality and field
faithfulness commitments, componentwise $\hsame$ transport, displayed $\Cont$
replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local naming.

No obligation may be discharged by parameter motion alone, lower loss alone,
benchmark improvement alone, memorized records, shortcut performance, or
unregistered interpretability prose.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of
\autoref{def:classifier-forming-learning-carrier}. The displayed inventory is
exactly $D,A,S,Q,R,G,T,H,C,P,N$, so every accepted learning claim must be read
from those finite rows.

The parameter row $A$ and residue row $Q$ are evidence ledgers. They can
support the claim only after the classifier-shift row $S$ is present, because
the NameCert surface names classifier formation rather than motion or lower
loss by itself.

The registry and audit rows $R,G$ make the claim exportable and
machine-readable. The TasteGate row $T$ records that later Lean work must
provide nontriviality and field-faithfulness instances for this displayed
carrier.

Finally $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, package, and name the same route. Since
the excluded substitutes have no carrier coordinate, they cannot discharge the
NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[ClassifierFormingLearning metric nonescape]
\label{thm:classifier-forming-learning-metric-nonescape}
Every metric-facing read exported by an accepted
$\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$ carrier factors through
$$
D,A,S,Q,R,G,T,H,C,P,N .
$$
Parameter motion, loss reduction, and benchmark movement remain ledger rows
inside the packet. They become certified learning discovery only when the
classifier-shift row, registry export, machine-readable audit handoff,
GroundCompiler audit-map handoff, TasteGate commitments, transport, replay,
provenance, and local naming rows are all displayed.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with a metric-facing read of the carrier. The definition exposes
parameter and loss data through $A$ and $Q$, but those rows are downstream
evidence and not the classifier named by the packet.

The classifier-shift row $S$ is the decisive learning row. The route then
passes through registry export $R$ and audit handoff $G$, so the claim remains
publicly checkable and machine-readable rather than a private score report.

The TasteGate row $T$ keeps nontriviality and field faithfulness attached to
the same carrier. Componentwise transport and continuation replay preserve
the rows, and $P,N$ package the accepted route.

Therefore metric improvement cannot escape the packet boundary. Without the
classifier-shift and TasteGate rows, the read remains residue rather than a
certified $\ClassifierFormingLearningUp$ claim.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[MonotoneClass carrier]
\label{def:monotone-class-carrier}
A $\MonotoneClassUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $G$ is the generator family over $\BHist$, read as displayed subset rows through $\SetLikeUp$ and as generated-set boundary data through $\BorelSetUp$ when the source is Borel-facing.
\item $U$ records increasing-union closure rows for finite observed chains and their named limit event request.
\item $I$ records decreasing-intersection closure rows for finite observed chains and their named limit event request.
\item $A$ records measurable-event admission through the $\BorelSetUp$ and $\MeasureUp$ sibling surfaces.
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $G,U,I,A$ and the structural rows.
\item $C$ stores displayed $\Cont$ replay from a generator request through increasing-union closure, decreasing-intersection closure, measurable-event admission, provenance, and local naming.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MonotoneClassUp$, $\MeasureUp$, $\BorelSetUp$, $\SetLikeUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MonotoneClassUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $G,U,I,A,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for completed sigma-algebras, chosen countable unions, host subset equality, measure extension, or an ambient monotone-class theorem.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MonotoneClass NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:monotone-class-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:monotone-class-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\MonotoneClassUp}$ surface consists exactly of generator admission through $G$, increasing-union closure through $U$, decreasing-intersection closure through $I$, measurable-event admission through $A$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, package provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$. Every accepted $\MeasureUp$, $\BorelSetUp$, or $\SetLikeUp$ consumer that reads a monotone-class conclusion must expose those rows before it treats the generated family as closed under the requested operation.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier $M=(G,U,I,A,H,C,P,N)$. The generator row $G$ is the only entry point for displayed set requests, so carrier admission is exhausted by the generator family and its sibling $\SetLikeUp$ or $\BorelSetUp$ boundary.

Read the closure rows next. The row $U$ supplies increasing-union closure for the named observed chain, and $I$ supplies decreasing-intersection closure for the named observed chain. The measurable-event admission row $A$ is the only route from those closures to the $\BorelSetUp$ and $\MeasureUp$ surfaces.

Finally replay the structural rows. Transport through $H$, continuation through $C$, provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$ preserve the same finite route. Since no carrier coordinate contains completed sigma-algebras, selected countable chains, quotient subset equality, measure extension, or an ambient monotone-class theorem, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\begin{proof}

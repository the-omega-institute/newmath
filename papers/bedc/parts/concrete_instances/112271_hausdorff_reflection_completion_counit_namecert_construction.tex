\begin{theorem}[Hausdorff reflection completion counit obligations]
\label{thm:hausdorff-reflection-completion-counit-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The completion-facing part is the route through $U,V,R,S$. Finally, $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the displayed rows. Since no further coordinate exists, the local certificate exports no quotient completion, selected representative, host equality, countable-choice schedule, or ambient completeness principle.

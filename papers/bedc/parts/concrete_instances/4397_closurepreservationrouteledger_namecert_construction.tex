\begin{definition}[Closure-preservation route ledger carrier]
\label{def:closure-preservation-route-ledger-carrier}

$$

\begin{enumerate}

\item $S$ is the shift-preservation row for $\mathsf{shift\_one\_preserves\_closed\_succ}$;
\item $U$ is the substitution-preservation row covering the variable and full-substitution closedness routes;

\item $Q$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes by which consumers reach the shift, substitution, beta-step, and beta-star rows from the source row;

\begin{theorem}[Closure-preservation route ledger NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:closure-preservation-route-ledger-namecert-obligations}
For the carrier of \autoref{def:closure-preservation-route-ledger-carrier}, the local $\NameCert_{\ClosurePreservationRouteLedgerUp}$ surface consists exactly of five obligation families: source closedness admission through $C$, shift preservation through $S$, substitution preservation through $U$, beta-step preservation through $B$, and beta-star preservation through $F$. The auxiliary rows $H,Q,P,N$ transport, replay, account for, and name those five families without adding a sixth preservation claim.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier $L=(C,S,U,B,F,H,Q,P,N)$. The source row $C$ fixes the displayed $\mathsf{ClosedAt}$ boundary, and the four route rows $S,U,B,F$ are the closedness-preservation routes named by \autoref{ch:metacic-closure-preservation-audit-map}.

Read the transport and route rows next. The $\hsame$ row $H$ can only identify corresponding displayed coordinates, and the $\Cont$ row $Q$ can only replay the four listed preservation routes from the source boundary.

Finally inspect provenance and naming. The package row $P$ records the audit-map and sibling-packet sources, while $N$ names the local certificate. Since no further coordinate is present, the five obligation families exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Closure-preservation route ledger non-escape]
\label{thm:closure-preservation-route-ledger-nonescape}

$$
C,S,U,B,F,H,Q,P,N.
$$
It does not export typed subject reduction, host conversion, an unrestricted substitution calculus, or full consistency.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The substitution route is therefore the finite audited route through $U$, not a calculus for arbitrary substitution outside the listed closedness hypotheses. The beta-step and beta-star routes similarly preserve $\mathsf{ClosedAt}$ and do not introduce typing or conversion coordinates.

Rows $H,Q,P,N$ preserve access discipline for the same finite packet. They transport, replay, account for, and name the visible rows, so typed subject reduction, host conversion, unrestricted substitution, and full consistency remain outside the public surface.
\end{proof}

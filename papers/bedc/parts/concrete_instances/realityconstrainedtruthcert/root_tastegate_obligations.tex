\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained TruthCert root TasteGate obligation]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-tastegate-obligation}
For an accepted $\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp$ packet
$$
(S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N),
$$
the root TasteGate obligation is determined by exactly the displayed source,
signature, classifier, tower, stability, descent, invariant, ledger, failure,
and local-name rows. The Lean TasteGate instance reads this finite packet and
does not read a hidden truth coordinate, a hidden classifier choice, or an
off-packet source of reality.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Use the carrier and field-coverage rows,
\autoref{def:reality-constrained-truth-cert-carrier} and
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-field-coverage}. They make
$(S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N)$ the whole admitted TruthCert packet before the
local name is exposed.

Next apply sibling dependency, root readiness, and root consumer totality,
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-sibling-dependency},
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-readiness-packet}, and
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-consumer-totality}. These
results force every root consumer to pass through the displayed sibling routes
and through the same finite carrier.

Finally use root classifier stability and root ledger nonescape,
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-classifier-stability} and
\autoref{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-ledger-nonescape}. The
classifier row $K$, ledger row $L$, and failure row $F$ remain internal to the
packet, so the TasteGate read has no coordinate beyond
$S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N$.
\end{proof}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp.RealityConstrainedTruthCertRootTasteGateObligation}

\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained TruthCert root nontrivial readiness]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-nontrivial-readiness}
The root nontriviality obligation for $\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp$ is
witnessed at the packet surface
$$
(S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N).
$$
A nontrivial TasteGate reading must distinguish accepted packet presence from
off-packet absence by the displayed carrier, field coverage, sibling
dependency, root readiness, root consumer totality, root classifier stability,
and root ledger nonescape rows, not by a separate truth object.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission and field coverage first supply the finite packet and its
mandatory rows. Thus packet presence is read through
$S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N$, while off-packet absence has no local
$\NameCert_{\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp}$ row to expose.

Sibling dependency, root readiness, and root consumer totality then determine
which downstream reads count as root-facing reads. A consumer that omits one
of the displayed sibling routes or displayed rows receives only a projection,
so it cannot be the nontrivial root packet.

Root classifier stability and root ledger nonescape close the distinction at
the classifier and ledger boundary. The rows $K,L,F,N$ remain part of the same
finite packet, so the Lean TasteGate instance may witness nontriviality from
packet presence versus off-packet absence without consulting any hidden truth
coordinate.
\end{proof}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp.RealityConstrainedTruthCertRootNontrivialReadiness}

\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained TruthCert root field-faithful readiness]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-truth-cert-root-field-faithful-readiness}
The root field-faithfulness obligation for $\RealityConstrainedTruthCertUp$ is
ready exactly when every TasteGate read of
$$
(S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N)
$$
preserves the displayed rows as the TruthCert fields. In particular, the
source, signature, classifier, tower, stability, descent, invariant, ledger,
failure, and local-name rows are not compressed into a hidden truth
coordinate, and no root consumer may replace their field order by an ambient
object.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the carrier and field-coverage theorems. They identify the accepted
packet rows and make them the TruthCert fields that must be preserved before
$N$ becomes readable.

Apply sibling dependency, root readiness, and root consumer totality. These
theorems route the open-fit, objectivity, explanation, physical-induction, and
tower-compression dependencies through the same displayed packet, so a
root-facing read cannot rearrange the fields through a second consumer path.

Finally use root classifier stability and root ledger nonescape. They keep
$K$ attached to signature comparison and keep $L,F$ attached to ledger and
failure discipline. Hence a field-faithful Lean TasteGate instance reads
exactly $S,\Sigma,K,T,U,D,I,L,F,N$ and nothing outside that finite packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[DyadicApproximation carrier]

A $\DyadicApproximationUp$ carrier is a finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ packet
$$

\begin{enumerate}
\item $n$ is a unary precision row naming the requested dyadic accuracy;

\end{definition}

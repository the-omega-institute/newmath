\begin{definition}[PeriodicContinuedFraction carrier]
\label{def:periodic-continued-fraction-carrier}
A $\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
F=(I,B,L,Q,D,S,G,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $I$ is the partial-quotient prefix row, recording the finite initial block and the inspected continued-fraction source inherited from $\ContinuedFractionUp$.
\item $B$ is the nonempty period-block row, recording the finite repeating block together with the local phase coordinate used to unfold displayed windows.
\item $L$ is the recurrence and convergent ledger, recording numerator and denominator recurrence rows for the prefix followed by repetitions of $B$.
\item $Q$ is the rational normalization row, recording the normalized rational convergents exported by the recurrence ledger.
\item $D$ is the denominator-bound comparison row, recording the finite denominator budgets and comparison witnesses used by the Dirichlet-style approximation surface.
\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ window row, recording exactly which unfolded prefix-and-period windows may be read by downstream consumers.
\item $G$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff row, reached from $I,B,L,Q,D,S$ before any real-facing seal is visible.
\item $E$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal row, exposed only after the prefix, period, recurrence, rational normalization, denominator comparison, StreamName window, and RegSeqRat rows have been displayed.
\item $H$ is the componentwise $\hsame$ transport row for the prefix, period block, recurrence ledger, rational normalization, denominator comparisons, StreamName windows, RegSeqRat handoff, Real seal, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ is the displayed $\Cont$ replay row from a finite periodic-window request through the recurrence ledger, rational normalization, denominator comparison, regular handoff, and terminal seal.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp$, $\ContinuedFractionUp$, $\ContinuedFractionConvergentsUp$, $\DirichletApproximationUp$, $\SternBrocotUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier predicate accepts exactly these finite prefix-and-period packets. It has no coordinate for a host quadratic irrational, a chosen infinite continued fraction, quotient real equality, trichotomy, global irrationality measure, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness principle.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[PeriodicContinuedFraction NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:periodic-continued-fraction-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp$ carrier $F=(I,B,L,Q,D,S,G,E,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed prefix, nonempty period block, recurrence ledger, rational normalization, denominator comparison, StreamName window, RegSeqRat handoff, Real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item periodic-block stability, requiring every unfolded window in $S$ to read the same nonempty row $B$ with its displayed phase coordinate;
\item recurrence exactness, requiring every convergent in $L$ and $Q$ to be obtained from the displayed prefix and period rows by the finite numerator and denominator recurrence;
\item convergent-window compatibility, requiring the rational normalization and denominator-bound comparisons in $Q,D$ to address only the windows exposed by $S$;
\item Real-seal nonescape, requiring $E$ to be downstream of $G$ and to expose no host quadratic irrational, selected infinite expansion, quotient real equality, trichotomy branch, or ambient completeness theorem;
\item provenance exhaustion, requiring $H,C,P,N$ to transport, replay, source, and name exactly the finite route recorded by $I,B,L,Q,D,S,G,E$.
\end{enumerate}
No local obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:periodic-continued-fraction-carrier}. The accepted packet displays exactly the prefix, period block, recurrence ledger, rational normalization, denominator comparison, StreamName windows, regular handoff, terminal Real seal, and structural rows.

The periodic-block and recurrence obligations are finite. The row $B$ is nonempty and supplies the only repeated block, while $L$ unfolds that row after the prefix $I$ by the displayed numerator and denominator recurrence.

The rational approximation obligations then read only displayed windows. The row $Q$ normalizes the convergents from $L$, the row $D$ compares their denominators against the finite budgets, and the row $S$ records the finite StreamName windows to which those comparisons apply.

The handoff and seal are terminal. The row $G$ is downstream of $I,B,L,Q,D,S$, and the row $E$ is downstream of $G$, so a Real-facing consumer cannot bypass the regular rational route.

Finally, $H,C,P,N$ preserve the same packet componentwise. Since the carrier contains no further coordinate, the six listed clauses exhaust the local $\NameCert_{\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp}$ surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[PeriodicContinuedFraction quadratic boundary]
\label{thm:periodic-continued-fraction-quadratic-boundary}
An accepted $\PeriodicContinuedFractionUp$ carrier exports only the finite periodic-block-to-convergent boundary
$$
I,B \longmapsto L,Q,D,S \longmapsto G \longmapsto E .
$$
Consequently the packet may be consumed as a finite rational-approximation source for $\RegSeqRatUp$ and $\RealUp$, but it does not export a host quadratic irrational, selected infinite expansion, quotient real equality, trichotomy, irrationality-measure bound, or $\RealUp$ completeness witness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from an accepted carrier $F=(I,B,L,Q,D,S,G,E,H,C,P,N)$. The only periodic data are the finite prefix row $I$ and the nonempty block row $B$.

The rows $L,Q,D,S$ turn this finite source into convergent and comparison data. They record recurrence, normalization, denominator bounds, and finite windows, but they do not add an ambient completed expansion.

The rows $G$ and $E$ provide the only regular rational and Real-facing exits. Their order forces every such exit through the finite convergent boundary before the terminal seal is visible.

The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same route. Since no row stores a host quadratic irrational, quotient equality, trichotomy branch, irrationality-measure theorem, or completeness witness, none is exported by the packet.
\end{proof}

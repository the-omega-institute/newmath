\begin{definition}[BHist pullback open row]
\label{def:topology-bhist-pullback-open-row}
Let $\mathcal{T}_X$ be an indexed open topology carrier for $\TopologyUp$ over $X$, and let $(i,U,h_i,\lambda_i)$ be a topology public open row in the sense of \autoref{def:topology-public-open-row}. Let $Y$ be a $\mathsf{BHist}$-carried source with classifier $\mathsf{same}_Y$, and let
$$
f:Y\to X
$$
be a displayed $\mathsf{BHist}$ map respecting classifiers:
$$
\mathsf{same}_Y(y_0,y_1)
\Longrightarrow
\mathsf{same}_X(f(y_0),f(y_1)).
$$
The BHist pullback open row of $(i,U,h_i,\lambda_i)$ along $f$ is the row
$$
(i,U\circ f,h_i\circ f,\lambda_i^{f})
$$
whose pulled-back predicate is
$$
(U\circ f)(y):\Longleftrightarrow U(f(y)).
$$
The carried-index route remains the same target index $i$ viewed through the displayed map $f$: the carrying equation at $y:Y$ is obtained by applying $h_i$ at $f(y)$, and the ledger row $\lambda_i^{f}$ records the original visible $\mathsf{BHist}$ provenance together with the map row for $f$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Topology pullback open row classifier transport]
\label{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-classifier-transport}
Let $(i,U,h_i,\lambda_i)$ be a topology public open row of $\mathcal{T}_X$ over $X$, and let $f:Y\to X$ be a classifier-respecting $\mathsf{BHist}$ map as in \autoref{def:topology-bhist-pullback-open-row}. Then membership in the pulled-back predicate transports along the source classifier:
$$
\mathsf{same}_Y(y_0,y_1)
\Longrightarrow
\bigl((U\circ f)(y_0)\Longleftrightarrow(U\circ f)(y_1)\bigr).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Assume $\mathsf{same}_Y(y_0,y_1)$. The map row for $f$ sends this source classifier witness to $\mathsf{same}_X(f(y_0),f(y_1))$.

Apply \autoref{thm:topology-public-open-row-classifier-transport} to the public open row of \autoref{def:topology-public-open-row}. It transports $U(f(y_0))$ to $U(f(y_1))$ along the obtained target classifier witness.

Unfold the pulled-back predicate of \autoref{def:topology-bhist-pullback-open-row}. The displayed equivalence is exactly membership transport for $U\circ f$ on the source carrier.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Topology pullback open row finite-meet]
\label{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-finite-meet}
Let $(i,U,h_i,\lambda_i)$ and $(j,V,h_j,\lambda_j)$ be topology public open rows of the same indexed open topology carrier $\mathcal{T}_X$, and let $f:Y\to X$ be a classifier-respecting $\mathsf{BHist}$ map. Pullback commutes with the displayed finite meet:
$$
\bigl((U\cap V)\circ f\bigr)(y)
\Longleftrightarrow
\bigl((U\circ f)(y)\wedge(V\circ f)(y)\bigr)
$$
for every $y:Y$, and the carried index for this pulled-back meet is $i\wedge_{\TopologyUp}j$ with ledger provenance obtained from the finite-meet row and the two pulled-back source rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:topology-indexed-open-finite-intersection} to the target carried indices $i$ and $j$ and the carrying witnesses from \autoref{def:topology-public-open-row}. It gives the target meet index $i\wedge_{\TopologyUp}j$ carrying the pointwise predicate $U\cap V$.

Now evaluate the carrying equation at the target point $f(y)$. The left side is membership of $(U\cap V)(f(y))$, and the theorem identifies it with $U(f(y))\wedge V(f(y))$.

Unfold precomposition. The resulting statement is exactly $((U\cap V)\circ f)(y)$ if and only if $(U\circ f)(y)\wedge(V\circ f)(y)$. The ledger is the visible $\mathsf{BHist}$ row that records the finite-meet constructor together with the two pulled-back input rows.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Topology pullback NameCert obligation surface]
\label{thm:topology-pullback-namecert-obligation-surface}
Let $\mathcal{T}_X$ be a $\NameCert_{\TopologyUp}$ obligation package over $X$ as in \autoref{thm:topology-namecert-obligation-package}, and let $f:Y\to X$ be a classifier-respecting $\mathsf{BHist}$ map from a displayed source carrier $Y$. The pullback rows of \autoref{def:topology-bhist-pullback-open-row} assemble the downstream pullback-open obligation surface: the source carrier $Y$, the source classifier $\mathsf{same}_Y$, the carried-index equation obtained by evaluating target carrying witnesses after $f$, finite-meet preservation from \autoref{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-finite-meet}, classifier transport from \autoref{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-classifier-transport}, and visible $\mathsf{BHist}$ ledger provenance for the map row and the target open row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The carrier and classifier are the displayed source rows $Y$ and $\mathsf{same}_Y$. The map row for $f$ is the only route used to move a source classifier witness into the target classifier.

For each carried target open, \autoref{def:topology-bhist-pullback-open-row} gives the pulled-back predicate and the carried-index equation by applying the target witness at $f(y)$. Classifier transport is then exactly \autoref{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-classifier-transport}.

For finite meets, use \autoref{thm:topology-pullback-open-row-finite-meet}. It applies the target finite-intersection row of \autoref{thm:topology-indexed-open-finite-intersection} before precomposition, so no host powerset open is selected on $Y$.

Finally invoke the package shape of \autoref{thm:topology-namecert-obligation-package}. Its carrier, classifier, finite-meet, and ledger clauses are preserved by the displayed pullback rows, with provenance recording both the target public open row and the $\mathsf{BHist}$ map row.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Transported stationary window-seal carrier]
\label{def:transported-stationary-window-seal-carrier}

$$

\item $S$ is the constant $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window classifier induced by $R$;
\item $G$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ finite-window seal row read from the same constant stream window;
\item $W$ is the $\FiniteWindowEnvelopeUp$ packet naming the selected anchor, window, dyadic ledger, regular-rational seal, and endpoint classifier;
\item $E$ is the $\RealUp$ seal-facing row obtained from $G$ and $W$;

\item $C$ stores the displayed $\hsame$ and $\Cont$ transports for the seed classifier, stream window, regular-rational seal, finite envelope, and real seal;

The local classifier relates two carriers exactly when their transported $\RatUp$ classifier rows agree and the induced $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\FiniteWindowEnvelopeUp$, $\RealUp$, transport, package, and local certificate rows compare componentwise after the displayed routes in $C$.

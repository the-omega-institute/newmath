It joins the membership row of $\TypeClassifierMembershipUp$, the classifier typing row of $\ClassifierTypingUp$, the subject-reduction handoff of $\SubjectReductionUp$, the finite derivation route of $\TypeCheckingDerivationTraceUp$, and the proof-erasure readback of $\ProofTermErasureTraceUp$ into one locally named packet.

\begin{definition}[TypeCheckingMembershipTrace carrier]
\label{def:typechecking-membership-trace-carrier}
A $\TypeCheckingMembershipTraceUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
T=(M,D,R,S,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $M$ is the $\Ext$-membership row read as the local type-classifier decision;
\item $D$ is the finite derivation trace row recording the classifier typing route;
\item $R$ is the displayed $\Cont$ subject-reduction route attached to the same membership read;
\item $S$ is the $\SigRel$ readback row relating the derivation route to the visible classifier name;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $M,D,R,S$ and the local naming row;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ replay by which consumers reread membership, derivation, subject-reduction, and readback rows;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance row over $\TypeClassifierMembershipUp$, $\ClassifierTypingUp$, $\SubjectReductionUp$, $\TypeCheckingDerivationTraceUp$, $\ProofTermErasureTraceUp$, $\Ext$, $\Cont$, $\SigRel$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\TypeCheckingMembershipTraceUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The local classifier compares two accepted carriers only through the displayed membership, derivation, subject-reduction, readback, transport, continuation, package, and naming rows.
\end{definition}

\begin{definition}[MetaCIC candidate normalization confluence handoff carrier]
\label{def:metacic-candidate-normalization-confluence-handoff-carrier}
A $\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
H=(A,K,N,F,C,D,B,T,R,P,L)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $A$ is the audit-map anchor row, binding the packet to the candidate-mediated reading of \autoref{ch:metacic-normalization-audit-map}.
\item $K$ is the candidate-evidence row, storing Candidate, ClosedCandidate, or CandidateSet membership data before any strong-normalization read is exported.
\item $N$ is the normal-endpoint row, recording the bounded normal endpoint or no-infinite-reduction projection obtained from $K$.
\item $F$ is the $\MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp$ row that keeps candidate evidence and finished-normal evidence inside the displayed frontier packet.
\item $C$ is the confluence-facing row, read through the residual envelope of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metacic-confluence-residual-envelope-namecert}.
\item $D$ is the $\MetaCICDecidableBoundaryUp$ row, limiting decidability reads to bounded checker output and normal endpoint comparison.
\item $B$ is the blocked-edge row, retaining subject-reduction and residual-substitution obligations as visible boundaries rather than discharged theorems.
\item $T$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the audit, candidate, normal endpoint, frontier, confluence, decidability, blocked-edge, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $R$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a MetaCIC consumer request through $A,K,N,F,C,D,B,T$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp$, $\MetaCICCriticalPathUp$, $\MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp$, $\MetacicConfluenceAuditWitnessUp$, $\MetaCICDecidableBoundaryUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $L$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $A,K,N,F,C,D,B,T,R,P,L$. It has no coordinate for evidence-free normalization, a global parallel diamond, unrestricted subject reduction, quotient syntax equality, host evaluator soundness, or closed consistency.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC candidate normalization confluence handoff boundary]
\label{thm:metacic-candidate-normalization-confluence-handoff-boundary}
For every accepted $\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp$ carrier
$$
H=(A,K,N,F,C,D,B,T,R,P,L),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the audit anchor, candidate evidence, normal endpoint, normalization frontier, residual confluence envelope, decidable boundary, blocked-edge row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming;
\item candidate-gate exactness, requiring every normalization read to pass through $K$ and the frontier row $F$ before $N$ is consumed;
\item confluence handoff discipline, requiring every residual or join read to pass through $C$ while retaining $B$ as a visible blocked edge;
\item decidable-boundary discipline, requiring every conversion-facing read to pass through $D$ after $N$ and before any bounded normal comparison is exported;
\item frontier non-collapse, requiring $T,R,P,L$ to preserve the same rows without promoting candidate-mediated SN into an unconditional closed-term theorem or residual-envelope rows into full Church--Rosser.
\end{enumerate}
No handoff obligation is stored outside $A,K,N,F,C,D,B,T,R,P,L$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:metacic-candidate-normalization-confluence-handoff-carrier}. The accepted packet displays exactly the audit anchor, candidate evidence, normal endpoint, normalization frontier, residual confluence envelope, decidable boundary, blocked-edge, and structural rows.

The normalization stage is $A,K,N,F$. The audit anchor fixes the candidate-mediated reading; the candidate row $K$ is exposed before the endpoint row $N$, and the frontier row $F$ keeps both inside the public normalization boundary.

The confluence and decidability stage is $C,D,B$. A confluence-facing consumer may read residual-envelope data through $C$, but the blocked-edge row $B$ remains visible. A conversion-facing consumer may read bounded checker output through $D$ only after the normal endpoint has been displayed.

Finally $T,R,P,L$ transport, replay, source, and name the same finite route. Since the carrier contains no evidence-free normalization coordinate, global diamond theorem, unrestricted subject-reduction proof, quotient syntax equality, host evaluator soundness, or closed-consistency endpoint, the listed rows exhaust the local obligation surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC candidate handoff consumer boundary]
\label{thm:metacic-candidate-normalization-confluence-handoff-consumer-boundary}
Every public consumer of an accepted $\MetacicCandidateNormalizationConfluenceHandoffUp$ packet factors through
$$
\MetaCICCriticalPathUp \longmapsto \MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp \longmapsto C,D,B \longmapsto T,R,P,L .
$$
The consumer receives candidate-mediated normalization evidence, bounded normal endpoint data, residual-envelope visibility, decidable-boundary routing, and blocked-edge retention. It receives no unconditional strong-normalization theorem, full Church--Rosser theorem, unrestricted subject-reduction theorem, global conversion decider, or completed closed-consistency proof.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the carrier projection. The audit and critical-path rows determine which candidate-mediated normalization and boundary rows are visible to the consumer.

Apply \autoref{thm:metacic-candidate-normalization-confluence-handoff-boundary}. The candidate gate exposes $K,F,N$ before any normal endpoint is available, while $C,D,B$ retain the confluence and decidability boundaries as displayed rows.

The structural rows $T,R,P,L$ do not add another route. They preserve the same carrier under componentwise transport, replay it for downstream consumers, record provenance, and name the packet locally. The absent global theorems cannot be projected from those rows.
\end{proof}

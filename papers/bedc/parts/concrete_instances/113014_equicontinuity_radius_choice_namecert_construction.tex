\begin{theorem}[Equicontinuity radius choice NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:equicontinuity-radius-choice-namecert-obligations}

\item finite-family exposure, requiring every radius choice to be indexed by the displayed row $F$;

\begin{proof}

Finally $U,A,H,C,P,N$ receive, seal, transport, replay, source, and name the same packet. Since none of those rows adds choice data, the listed obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.

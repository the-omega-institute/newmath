\begin{theorem}[Bounded monotone Cauchy witness terminal-window split exactness]
\label{thm:bounded-monotone-cauchy-witness-terminal-window-split-exactness}
Let
$$
W=(S,F,\sigma,\mu,\lambda,I,Q,H,C,P,N)
$$
be an accepted $\BoundedMonotoneCauchyWitnessUp$ carrier. Every terminal tail service exported by $W$ for an accepted packet factors in the exact finite order
$$
S,F,\sigma,\mu,\lambda \longmapsto I \longmapsto Q \longmapsto H,C,P,N.
$$
The first stage is the bounded-source, regular-handoff, finite-observation, Cauchy-witness, and dyadic-ledger window. The second stage is the located trap $I$. The third stage is the $\RealUp$ seal row $Q$. Only after these three stages are visible may the consumer read componentwise $\hsame$ transport, displayed $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ naming.

Thus a terminal tail answer is not a detached threshold, selected subsequence, quotient real, host equality, trichotomy split, ambient completeness principle, open-cover compactness proof, or choice-selected limit. It is exactly the displayed route from finite observation through regular handoff and Real seal to the structural suffix.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the finite observation stage. By \autoref{thm:bounded-monotone-cauchy-witness-source-tail-synchronization} and \autoref{thm:bounded-monotone-cauchy-witness-cauchy-modulus-extraction-factorization}, a terminal request that reads $\sigma$, $\mu$, or $\lambda$ keeps the bounded source row $S$ and regular handoff row $F$ in the same accepted packet.

Next pass to the located trap. The located interval trap certificate fixes $I$ as the pre-seal enclosure obtained after the finite observation, modulus, and dyadic ledger rows have been consumed. No terminal service can replace that row by an ambient interval choice because the carrier has no such coordinate.

Then read the Real seal. Apply \autoref{thm:bounded-monotone-cauchy-witness-real-completion-export}: any Real-completion-facing consumer must pass through the finite observation budget, regular rational handoff, trap, seal, transport, continuation, provenance, and naming rows before a completion-facing export is visible.

Finally compare the suffix. The convergence-seal terminal synchronization theorem forces the same displayed route for seal-facing consumers, while ledger exhaustion leaves $H,C,P,N$ as transport, replay, provenance, and naming only. Therefore every accepted terminal tail service splits exactly through the finite observation stage, the regular handoff and located trap, the Real seal, and the structural suffix named in the statement.
\end{proof}

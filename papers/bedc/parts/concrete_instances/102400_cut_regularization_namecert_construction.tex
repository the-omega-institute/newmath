Following Bishop and Bridges on constructive real numbers, the $\CutRegularizationUp$ packet records the finite route from rounded located cut observations to a regular-Cauchy $\RealUp$ name. It sits after the rounded lower and upper presentation of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dedekindcut-namecert}, the located dyadic window surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-locatedcut-namecert}, and the approximation interface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-located-cut-approximation-namecert}. The chapter names only finite carrier rows and their non-escape boundary; it is not a quotient of cuts, a selected supremum, a trichotomy principle, a host-completion theorem, or an appeal to choice.

\begin{definition}[Cut regularization carrier]
\label{def:cut-regularization-carrier}
A $\CutRegularizationUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
Q=(D,L,A,\Delta,W,S,R,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the rounded $\DedekindCutUp$ source row, exposing the lower and upper rational observations as finite BEDC data.
\item $L$ is the $\LocatedCutUp$ located-window row, binding each inspected precision request to lower and upper dyadic witnesses.
\item $A$ is the $\LocatedCutApproximationUp$ approximation row, storing the visible midpoint, endpoint, or bracket readback obtained from $D$ and $L$.
\item $\Delta$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance ledger, recording dyadic radii, endpoint slack, and width comparisons for the regularization route.
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ window family through which the finite brackets are read in precision order.
\item $S$ is the stabilization row requiring the displayed windows to be nested or refined enough to feed the regular-Cauchy modulus demanded downstream.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff row obtained from $A,\Delta,W,S$.
\item $E$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal row reached only through the regular-Cauchy handoff $R$.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for all source, window, tolerance, handoff, seal, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay of the route
$$
D,L \longmapsto A \longmapsto \Delta \longmapsto W \longmapsto S \longmapsto R \longmapsto E .
$$
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\CutRegularizationUp$, $\DedekindCutUp$, $\LocatedCutUp$, $\LocatedCutApproximationUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CutRegularizationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $D,L,A,\Delta,W,S,R,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for quotient-cut equality, selected suprema, trichotomy, host real equality, ambient completeness, $\mathsf{Quot.sound}$, propext, classical choice, Lean verification, or bridge checking.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Cut regularization NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cut-regularization-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\CutRegularizationUp$ carrier
$$
Q=(D,L,A,\Delta,W,S,R,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\CutRegularizationUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the displayed rounded cut source, located window, approximation row, dyadic tolerance ledger, stream windows, stabilization row, regular-Cauchy handoff, Real seal, and structural rows $H,C,P,N$;
\item cut-source exposure, requiring every regularization request to read the rounded $\DedekindCutUp$ source only through the displayed lower and upper rational observations;
\item located-window exposure, requiring the $\LocatedCutUp$ row $L$ to bind each inspected precision to finite lower and upper dyadic witnesses before any stream or regular-Cauchy row is consumed;
\item approximation exactness, requiring the $\LocatedCutApproximationUp$ row $A$ to store only finite bracket data obtained from $D,L$ and the tolerance ledger $\Delta$;
\item tolerance accounting, requiring $\Delta$ to record the dyadic radius and endpoint slack used by each displayed stream window and stabilization read;
\item stream stabilization, requiring $W$ and $S$ to expose the finite $\StreamNameUp$ windows whose refinement feeds the regular-Cauchy modulus;
\item regular-Cauchy handoff, requiring $R$ to be the only route by which the rounded located cut data becomes a $\RegSeqRatUp$ row;
\item Real-seal factorization, requiring every downstream read of $E$ to pass through $D,L,A,\Delta,W,S,R$ and the structural rows;
\item transport, replay, and provenance non-escape, requiring $H,C,P,N$ to preserve and name exactly the displayed rows, with no quotient-cut equality, selected supremum, trichotomy, host real equality, ambient completeness, $\mathsf{Quot.sound}$, propext, classical choice, Lean verification, or bridge credential.
\end{enumerate}
No cut-regularization obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted packet $Q=(D,L,A,\Delta,W,S,R,E,H,C,P,N)$. Carrier admission exposes exactly the rounded cut source, located window, approximation row, dyadic tolerance ledger, stream windows, stabilization row, regular-Cauchy handoff, Real seal, and four structural rows.

The cut-source and located-window obligations are the projections to $D$ and $L$. The source row supplies the rounded lower and upper rational observations, and the located row binds each inspected precision to finite dyadic witnesses before any Cauchy-facing route is read.

Approximation exactness and tolerance accounting are the projections to $A$ and $\Delta$. The approximation row stores only bracket data read from the displayed source and located windows, while the tolerance ledger records the dyadic radius, endpoint slack, and width comparisons used by that read.

The stream and stabilization obligations are the projections to $W$ and $S$. The stream row exposes the finite windows in precision order, and the stabilization row records the refinement needed to feed the regular-Cauchy modulus without selecting a quotient cut or a supremum.

Finally, the regular handoff, Real seal, and structural rows exhaust the boundary. The row $R$ is downstream of $D,L,A,\Delta,W,S$, the seal $E$ is downstream of $R$, and $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, name, and source only the displayed rows. Hence no quotient-cut equality, selected supremum, trichotomy, host real equality, ambient completeness, $\mathsf{Quot.sound}$, propext, classical choice, Lean verification, or bridge credential is exported.
\end{proof}

The Iwasawa-theory interface $\IwasawaUp$ packages the projective limit of class groups of cyclotomic $\mathbb{Z}_p$-extensions of a number field together with the Iwasawa main conjecture relating it to a $p$-adic L-function. It depends on $\NameCert_{\CyclotomicUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-cyclotomic-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\IdealClassUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-idealclass-namecert}).

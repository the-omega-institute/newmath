\label{thm:realseries-scoped-certificate-closure}
The $\RealSeriesUp$ chapter closes the finite certificate scope generated by its existing child files. \end{theorem}

The product child closes the remaining root product consumer. It reads product-facing partial sums only through the same finite windows, dyadic ledger, regular readback, shared threshold, and structural rows. Thus the listed child files exhaust the scoped $\RealSeriesUp$ certificate surface.
\end{proof}

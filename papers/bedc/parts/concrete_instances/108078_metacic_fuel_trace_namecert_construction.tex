\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:metacic-fuel-trace-carrier}. The computational side is $F,I,O$: a displayed fuel value, represented inputs, and the bounded normalizer outputs obtained at that fuel.

The equality rows $Q,B$ then compare only the bounded outputs. They are interpreted through the structural equality and \texttt{decideClosedBetaConv} rows named by the decidability audit map.

\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC fuel trace bounded-conversion nonescape]
\label{thm:metacic-fuel-trace-bounded-conversion-nonescape}

$$

It exports no fuel-erased global normalization theorem, no subject-reduction theorem, no confluence theorem, no arbitrary closedness decider, no unrestricted beta-conversion oracle, and no host quotient equality of represented terms.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Because the carrier has no coordinate for stronger metatheory, no consumer can reinterpret the bounded fuel trace as global normalization, subject reduction, confluence, arbitrary closedness decidability, unrestricted beta-conversion, or quotient equality.

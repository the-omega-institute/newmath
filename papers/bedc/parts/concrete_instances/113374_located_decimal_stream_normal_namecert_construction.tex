\label{thm:located-decimal-stream-normal-namecert-obligations}

\begin{proof}

The endpoint and schedule rows then fix the normal-form boundary. The row $E$ records the finite endpoint normalization decisions, while $W$ lists the stages and positions at which those decisions are visible.

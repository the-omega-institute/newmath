\begin{theorem}[StreamName constant observation map row]
\label{thm:streamname-constant-observation-map-row}
For any certified point row over the L10 source, the constant observation schedule determines a $\StreamNameUp$ carrier row whose pointwise classifier, $\hsame$ transport, $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ row are stable under every unary finite observation. The row is available to $\RegSeqRatUp$ and $\RealUp$ as a finite StreamName handoff, without claiming real completeness or quotient stream equality.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the constant-stream and constant-window boundary rows. They identify a certified point with the constant StreamName carrier read over each finite observation window, so a unary finite observation changes only the displayed schedule.

Apply constant-window transport totality. The pointwise classifier and $\hsame$ transport are stable under the unary observation because the seed point row is unchanged and only the finite window is reindexed.

Now use the scheduled constant observation package. Its $\Cont$ replay and $\Pkg$ provenance record the same constant schedule as a finite StreamNameUp packet, and the local $\NameCert$ row names that packet before downstream consumption.

Finally read the consumers. $\RegSeqRatUp$ and $\RealUp$ may consume the finite StreamName carrier, classifier, transport, replay, provenance, and naming rows supplied here; they do not receive a real-completeness claim, quotient stream equality, or hidden completion coordinate from this map row.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[StreamName pointwise map stability]\label{thm:streamname-pointwise-map-stability}
Let $r$ be a unary reindexing map on finite histories, and let $F$ be a pointwise map on rational-history observations. If $r$ sends every unary window to a unary window, $F$ preserves the $\RatHistoryUp$ carrier, and $F$ preserves the $\RatHistoryUp$ classifier, then the $\StreamNameUp$ carrier and classifier rows are stable both under reindexing by $r$ and under pointwise mapping by $F$. The exported route remains a finite StreamName row: it contains only the StreamName carrier, classifier, $\hsame$ transport, $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ data, and it does not introduce quotient stream equality or real-completeness data.
\end{theorem}

\begin{theorem}[StreamNameUp public countable-name interface]
\label{thm:streamname-public-countable-name-interface}
The public $\StreamNameUp$ interface over $\mathsf{BHist}$ exports exactly the
countable rational stream-name data assembled from $\UnaryHistory$, $\hsame$,
$\RatUp$, and the stream carrier/classifier rows.  Its carrier is
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}$, its classifier is
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$, and its public rows are precisely the
RatUp pointwise carrier/classifier fields, the constant stream witness, empty
constant-stream point exactness, constant point exactness, reindexing and
RatUp-preserving map stability, and the composition law for unary observation
reindexing.

Equivalently, a downstream chapter may cite this interface as a single
countable-name package whose exported surface consists of
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-certificate-fields},
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness},
\autoref{thm:rat-const-stream-empty-point-exactness},
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-point-exactness},
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-reindexing-and-map-stability}, and
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-reindexing-composition-law}.  The export does
not supply quotient representatives, completion data, RealUp completeness,
metric data, or a standard bridge.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
The carrier and classifier fields are the two unfolded stream rows in
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-certificate-fields}.  That theorem also supplies
the pointwise RatUp equivalence and transport clauses over every certified
unary observation in $\mathsf{BHist}$, so the public package does not introduce
any classifier beyond $\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}$.

The inhabited constant-name row is
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-witness}.  Empty observation exactness
comes from \autoref{thm:rat-const-stream-empty-point-exactness}, and pointwise
constant exactness comes from
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-constant-point-exactness}.  These three rows
identify the visible constant stream data with the underlying $\RatUp$ seed
without adding any quotient representative or completion object.

The structural action rows are exactly
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-reindexing-and-map-stability} and
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-reindexing-composition-law}.  The first handles a
single unary observation reindexer together with a RatUp-preserving history
map, while the second handles composition of such reindexers.  Repacking these
six named rows gives the public countable-name interface and leaves all
completion, metric, RealUp-completeness, and standard-bridge claims outside
the export.
\end{proof}

\begin{proof}
The public countable-name spine is
\autoref{thm:streamname-public-countable-name-interface}.  It fixes the carrier

carrier/classifier rows over certified unary observations in $\mathsf{BHist}$.

finite-window classifier is exactly the restriction of the stream-name
classifier to bundled certified unary observations.  Thus
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-from-classifier} projects every
public stream-name classifier to an arbitrary probe bundle, while

\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-structural-laws}.  Bundle membership

For selected endpoints, apply

\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[StreamNameUp finite-window restriction export]
\label{thm:streamname-finite-window-restriction-export}
Let $s,t$ be certified $\StreamNameUp$ observation maps, and let $B'$ and $B$
be $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ windows. Suppose every certified unary observation
carried by $B'$ is an $\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of $B$. Then
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,B)
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,B').
$$
The restriction export has the ten-field fit used by downstream
$\RegSeqRat$ and $\RealUp$ consumers: the BEDC source is
$\StreamNameUp$ together with $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$, the carrier consists of
bundled certified unary observations, the classifier is the pointwise
$\RatUp$ classification row, the ledger retains exactly the observation rows
selected by $B'$, and exactness is supplied by bundle inclusion.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Unfold Definition~\ref{def:rat-stream-name-finite-window-classifier}.  A
finite-window classifier over $B$ is a pointwise classifier row for each
certified unary observation carried by $B$.

Fix an observation carried by $B'$.  The displayed inclusion witness presents
that same certified unary observation as an $\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of
$B$, so the classifier over $B$ supplies the corresponding pointwise
$\RatUp$ row for $s$ and $t$.

Repack these rows over all observations of $B'$.  The carrier remains the
same bundled certified-unary-observation carrier, the ledger is the retained
subfamily of observation rows, and no completion, quotient representative, or
regularity row is introduced.  This is precisely the finite-window classifier
over $B'$.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[StreamNameUp finite-window reindexing stability]
\label{thm:streamname-finite-window-reindexing-stability}
Let $s,t$ be certified $\StreamNameUp$ observation maps, let $B$ and $B'$ be
$\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ windows, and let $r$ be a certified unary reindexer.
Suppose that every certified unary observation carried by $B'$ is sent by $r$
to an $\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of $B$. Then
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,B)
\quad\Longrightarrow\quad
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s\circ r,t\circ r,B').
$$
The restricted window consumes exactly the reindexed pointwise $\RatUp$
classifier rows and the finite observation ledger named by $B'$. It introduces
no $\RealUp$ completion, no $\RegSeqRat$ regularity row, no quotient
representative, and no metric row.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Start from the public finite-window interface of
\autoref{thm:streamname-public-finite-window-interface}. It makes the
finite-window classifier a pointwise $\RatUp$ classifier over bundled certified
unary observations and includes the certified reindexing rows from
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-reindexed-map-coverage}.

Fix a certified unary observation carried by $B'$. The reindexer sends it to an
$\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of $B$, so the classifier over $B$ supplies the
pointwise $\RatUp$ row for $s$ and $t$ at the reindexed observation. Transport
that row along the certified point map to read the classifier for $s\circ r$ and
$t\circ r$ at the original observation.

Now repack these transported rows over all observations of $B'$. This is the
same restriction shape as
\autoref{thm:streamname-finite-window-restriction-export}, with the inclusion
provided by the reindexed-map coverage row rather than by an identity
subwindow. The carrier, classifier, and finite ledger remain those of the
public finite-window interface.

Nothing in the construction asks for a completion object, a regular sequence
condition, a quotient representative, or a metric witness. The resulting
classifier is therefore precisely the restricted probe window needed by
$\RealUp$ and $\RegSeqRat$ boundary consumers.
\end{proof}

\section{StreamName finite-request RealUp seal lock}
\label{sec:streamname-finite-request-real-seal-lock}

\begin{theorem}[StreamName finite request section RealUp seal lock]
\label{thm:streamname-finite-request-section-real-seal-lock}
Any finite request section packet accepted by $\StreamNameUp$ and consumed by a
$\RegSeqRatUp$-to-$\RealUp$ seal factors through the displayed
$\StreamNameUp$ schedule rows, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ endpoint and radius
rows, the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regularity ledger, componentwise $\hsame$ transport,
the displayed $\Cont$ routes, $\Pkg$ provenance, and the local $\NameCert$
rows. The seal lock exports no quotient stream, no host real equality, no
ambient completion point, and no $\RealUp$ completeness datum.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with the finite request section packet of
\autoref{thm:streamname-real-endpoint-finite-request-section} and
\autoref{thm:streamname-regseqrat-window-budget-section}. These results make
the accepted request a finite StreamName section whose public rows are the
schedule, endpoint, readback, budget, transport, continuation, package, and
local naming rows.

Next use the RegSeqRat-to-RealUp handoff rows of
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-real-seal-handoff},
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-real-seal-endpoint-coverage}, and
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-finite-request-real-seal-correspondence}. They place
the Real seal behind the same regularity ledger and finite request rows rather
than behind a host sequence equality or selected completed limit.

Then compose with the DyadicRatCore endpoint and radius ledger reads recorded
by \autoref{thm:regseqrat-dyadic-radius-observation},
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-dyadic-radius-ledger-readback}, and
\autoref{thm:regseqrat-streamname-dyadic-real-handoff-exactness}. The
endpoint and radius rows are therefore the displayed dyadic rows carried by
the finite section, not independent ambient metric data.

Finally apply the non-escape exclusions of
\autoref{thm:streamname-real-seal-request-section-nonescape} and
\autoref{thm:streamname-regseqrat-real-budget-nonescape}. Since the composed
route has coordinates only for the StreamName schedule, DyadicRatCore
endpoint-radius ledger, RegSeqRat regularity ledger, $\hsame$, $\Cont$,
$\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$, it cannot export quotient streams, host real
equality, ambient completion points, or RealUp completeness data.
\end{proof}

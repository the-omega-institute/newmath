\label{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-transported-selected-full-endpoint-package}
For certified observation maps $s,t,s',t':\Hist\to\Hist$, a probe bundle
$\mathcal{P}:\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$, a selected bundled unary observation $n$,
and histories $a,b,z_s,z_t:\Hist$, suppose
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,\mathcal{P}),
\quad
\mathsf{InBundle}(n,\mathcal{P}),
\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n),
$$
and suppose the bundled pointwise transport rows
$$
\forall m,\ \mathsf{InBundle}(m,\mathcal{P})\to
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(m)\to\hsame(s(m),s'(m))
$$
and
$$
\forall m,\ \mathsf{InBundle}(m,\mathcal{P})\to
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(m)\to\hsame(t(m),t'(m))
$$
hold. If the transported selected observations are displayed as one-extension
endpoints
$$
\hsame(s'(n),\Eone(a))\quad\text{and}\quad \hsame(t'(n),\Eone(b)),
$$
then the transported selected finite-window endpoints carry the full endpoint
package:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(s'(n),t'(n))$.
  \item $\mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(s'(n))\land
  \mathsf{PositiveUnaryDenominator}(t'(n))$.
  \item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(s'(n))\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(t'(n))$.
  \item $(\hsame(s'(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})\land
  (\hsame(t'(n),\emp)\to\mathsf{False})$.
  \item $(\hsame(s'(n),\Ezero(z_s))\to\mathsf{False})\land
  (\hsame(t'(n),\Ezero(z_t))\to\mathsf{False})$.
  \item $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(a)\land\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(b)\land
  \hsame(a,b)$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First transport the finite-window classifier. For every bundled unary
observation $m$, use the given transport row at $m$ for the left endpoint and
the corresponding row at $m$ for the right endpoint. The endpoint-transport
field of \autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-stability-fields} then
gives
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s',t',\mathcal{P}).
$$

Now apply
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-selected-full-endpoint-package} to
this transported classifier, the selected bundle membership of $n$, the unary
witness for $n$, and the displayed one-extension endpoints of $s'(n)$ and
$t'(n)$. Its six conclusions are exactly the transported selected endpoint
package listed above.
\end{proof}

observation endpoint package; BEDC source
$\RatUp+\StreamNameUp+\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$; carrier certified observation
maps; classifier finite-window restriction; stability bundled $\hsame$
transport; ledger selected bundled unary probe observation and displayed
transported endpoints; core theorems

\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-selected-full-endpoint-package};
exactness transported selected RatUp point classification with positive
denominators, shape exclusions, and one-tail agreement; standard bridge
ordinary finite rational stream endpoint transport; status
$\mathsf{ProofTarget}$.

\begin{theorem}[StreamName two-route selected endpoint agreement]
\label{thm:streamname-two-route-selected-endpoint-agreement}
For certified observation maps $s,t,u,s',t',u':\Hist\to\Hist$, a common probe
bundle $\mathcal{P}:\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$, a selected bundled unary
observation $n$, and displayed one-extension endpoint tails
$a,b,c,z_s,z_t,z_u:\Hist$, suppose the hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-transported-selected-full-endpoint-package}
hold for the route from $s$ to $t$ with transported endpoints $s'$ and $t'$:
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,\mathcal{P}),
\quad
\mathsf{InBundle}(n,\mathcal{P}),
\quad
\mathsf{UnaryHistory}(n),
$$
the bundled pointwise transport rows for $s$ to $s'$ and $t$ to $t'$, and the
displayed endpoint rows
$$
\hsame(s'(n),\Eone(a))\quad\text{and}\quad
\hsame(t'(n),\Eone(b)).
$$
Suppose also that the same selected unary observation and common bundle satisfy
the hypotheses of
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-transported-selected-full-endpoint-package}
for the route from $s$ to $u$ with transported endpoints $s'$ and $u'$:
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,u,\mathcal{P}),
$$
the bundled pointwise transport rows for $s$ to $s'$ and $u$ to $u'$, and the
displayed endpoint rows
$$
\hsame(s'(n),\Eone(a))\quad\text{and}\quad
\hsame(u'(n),\Eone(c)).
$$
Then the two exported right tails of the selected transported readbacks agree:
$$
\hsame(b,c).
$$
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-finite-window-transported-selected-full-endpoint-package}
to the $s$ to $t$ route. Its selected endpoint package includes the
one-tail agreement row $\hsame(a,b)$ for the displayed endpoints of $s'(n)$
and $t'(n)$.

Apply the same theorem to the $s$ to $u$ route over the same bundle and
selected unary observation. The selected endpoint package for this route
includes the one-tail agreement row $\hsame(a,c)$ for $s'(n)$ and $u'(n)$.

Use symmetry of $\hsame$ on $\hsame(a,b)$ to obtain $\hsame(b,a)$, and then
compose it by transitivity with $\hsame(a,c)$. The resulting relation is
$\hsame(b,c)$, which is exactly the agreement of the two exported right tails.
\end{proof}

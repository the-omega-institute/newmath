\begin{theorem}[StreamName successor-window projection determinacy]
\label{thm:streamname-successor-window-projection-determinacy}
Let $s,t:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{BHist}$ be certified StreamName observation
maps, let $n:\mathsf{BHist}$ be a certified unary observation, and let
$B:\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ be a finite observation bundle such that
$\mathsf{BHist.e1}(n)$ is an $\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of $B$. If the public
finite-window classifier
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}(s,t,B)
$$
holds, then it determines the projected singleton successor-window classifier
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameFiniteWindowClassifier}
\bigl(s,t,\mathsf{ProbeBundle.Bcons}(\mathsf{BHist.e1}(n),\mathsf{ProbeBundle.Bnil})\bigr).
$$
The projected row is exactly the successor observation row consumed by the
scheduled constant observation package of
\autoref{def:streamname-constant-scheduled-observation-package}, by the
scheduled window reindex stability row of
\autoref{thm:streamname-scheduled-window-reindex-stability}, and by the RealUp
finite-window boundary rows
\autoref{thm:realup-finite-window-exhaustion-obligation} and
\autoref{thm:realup-streamname-finite-window-ledger-boundary}. It adds no host
$\mathsf{Nat}$, quotient representative, completion object, metric row, or
$\RealUp$ completeness datum.
\end{theorem}

\begin{proof}
Unfold the public finite-window interface in
\autoref{thm:streamname-public-finite-window-interface}. The classifier over
$B$ is a finite family of pointwise $\RatUp$ classifier rows indexed by the
certified unary observations carried by $B$.

The hypothesis that $\mathsf{BHist.e1}(n)$ is an $\mathsf{InBundle}$ member of
$B$ gives the inclusion of the singleton successor bundle into $B$. Apply the
finite-window restriction export of
\autoref{thm:streamname-finite-window-restriction-export} to retain exactly the
row indexed by $\mathsf{BHist.e1}(n)$ and to discard every other bundled
observation.

Now read that singleton row as a scheduled successor observation. The
successor reindexing stability row for StreamName observations identifies the
same pointwise classifier after reindexing by the successor observation, and
\autoref{thm:streamname-scheduled-window-reindex-stability} repacks it as the
finite input of the scheduled constant observation package.

Finally pass the projected scheduled row through
\autoref{thm:streamname-scheduled-window-realup-feed} and
\autoref{thm:streamname-regseqrat-realup-finite-window-feed}. These rows are
the RealUp finite-window feed rows already cited by
\autoref{thm:realup-finite-window-exhaustion-obligation} and
\autoref{thm:realup-streamname-finite-window-ledger-boundary}. The projection
therefore changes only the finite $\mathsf{ProbeBundle}$ ledger and introduces
no host $\mathsf{Nat}$ index, quotient representative, completion object,
metric row, or $\RealUp$ completeness datum.
\end{proof}

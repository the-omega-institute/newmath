\begin{definition}[Scheduled constant observation package]
\label{def:streamname-constant-scheduled-observation-package}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$ and observation schedules $r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, the
scheduled constant observation package is the finite StreamNameUp-facing row
tuple
$$
\mathsf{SchedConstObs}(d,e,r,q)
$$
consisting of the RatUp seed carrier rows
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ and $\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(e)$, the RatUp
seed classifier row $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$, and the two
scheduled constant stream rows
$$
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\qquad
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q.
$$
Its StreamNameUp carrier entries are
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r)$ and
$\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q)$, and its
StreamNameUp classifier entry is
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q).
$$
The schedules $r$ and $q$ are retained only as observation rows; the rational
seed histories remain the only RatUp endpoints exported by the package.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Scheduled constant observation exactness]
\label{thm:streamname-constant-scheduled-observation-exactness}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$ and schedules $r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, the scheduled
constant observation package of
\autoref{def:streamname-constant-scheduled-observation-package} supplies
exactly the following StreamNameUp rows:
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameCarrier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r)
$$
and
$$
\mathsf{RatStreamNameClassifier}(\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r,
\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q),
$$
if and only if the corresponding RatUp seed rows
$\mathsf{RatHistoryCarrier}(d)$ and $\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$ hold.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
For the carrier row, unfold $\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r$. Every certified
unary observation is sent back to the same seed history $d$, so the
StreamNameUp carrier clause is precisely the RatUp carrier clause for $d$.

For the classifier row, apply
\autoref{thm:rat-stream-name-independent-reindexed-constant-point-exactness}.
Its independently scheduled classifier clause identifies the classifier of
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r$ against
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q$ with the seed classifier
$\mathsf{RatHistoryClassifier}(d,e)$.

Combining these two readings gives the forward direction by projection from
the scheduled package. The reverse direction repacks the RatUp carrier and
classifier rows as the two displayed StreamNameUp rows, with $r$ and $q$
serving only as observation schedules.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Scheduled constant observation RealUp feed]
\label{thm:streamname-constant-scheduled-real-feed}
For histories $d,e:\Hist$ and schedules $r,q:\Hist\to\Hist$, the scheduled
constant observation package is the StreamNameUp-side input consumed by the
RealUp independently scheduled constant rational embedding certificate of
\autoref{thm:real-independent-reindexed-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}.
Equivalently, its two scheduled stream rows and its exact classifier row are
the StreamNameUp coordinates used by the downstream target
$\mathsf{RealIndependentReindexedConstant\_rational\_embedding\_certificate}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Read the RealUp package in
\autoref{thm:real-independent-reindexed-constant-rational-embedding-certificate}.
Its two StreamNameUp coordinates are exactly
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_d\circ r$ and
$\mathsf{RatConstStream}_e\circ q$, and its classifier chain passes through
the independently scheduled StreamNameUp classifier between those rows.

By \autoref{thm:streamname-constant-scheduled-observation-exactness}, those
are precisely the carrier and classifier rows delivered by the scheduled
constant observation package. Thus RealUp consumes no additional StreamNameUp
data beyond this scheduled package when forming the constant rational
embedding certificate.
\end{proof}

\section{StreamName Open-Phase Exit Boundary}
\label{sec:streamname-open-phase-exit-boundary}

\begin{theorem}[StreamName open-phase exit boundary]
\label{thm:streamname-open-phase-exit-boundary}
Every current Phase Real and Completion consumer whose $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window route reaches the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback, the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ ledger, and the $\RealUp$ seal factors through exactly one finite BEDC exit boundary. The boundary is the StreamName window, the RegSeqRat readback, the DyadicRatCore ledger, the RealUp seal, displayed $\BHist$ observations, componentwise $\hsame$ transports, displayed $\Cont$ routes, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ rows on those finite surfaces.

The boundary exports no quotient stream, no host sequence equality, no selected limit, no ambient $\RealUp$ completeness row, no metric-completeness datum, and no standard bridge row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First apply \autoref{thm:streamname-open-phase-terminal-route}. It places every such Phase Real and Completion consumer on the terminal finite packet and identifies the StreamName window, RegSeqRat readback, DyadicRatCore ledger, RealUp seal, displayed observations, transports, routes, provenance, and local naming rows.

Next use \autoref{thm:streamname-open-phase-terminal-seal-exactness} and \autoref{thm:streamname-open-phase-terminal-readback-determinacy}. Terminal seal exactness fixes the four-face route, and readback determinacy says that two consumers with the same four faces and displayed support rows determine the same terminal packet.

Now invoke \autoref{thm:streamname-dyadicratcore-window-anchor}. It anchors every StreamName finite-window read consumed by RegSeqRat or by the Real seal through the DyadicRatCore observation and tolerance rows before the regular-rational handoff.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:streamname-regseqrat-real-open-phase-dependency-lattice}. The open-phase lattice exhausts the public consumer coordinates by the finite route and its displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ support. Therefore the exit boundary is exactly that finite BEDC packet, and no quotient stream, host sequence equality, selected limit, ambient completeness, metric-completeness datum, or standard bridge row is exported.
\end{proof}

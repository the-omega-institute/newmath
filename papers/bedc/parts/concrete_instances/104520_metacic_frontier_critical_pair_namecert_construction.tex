\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC frontier critical-pair NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:metacic-frontier-critical-pair-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MetaCICFrontierCriticalPairUp$ carrier $K=(R,F,J,E,U,S,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\MetaCICFrontierCriticalPairUp}$ surface consists exactly of these finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the redex-frontier row, finite critical-pair table, local-join row, bounded-checker row, substitution-boundary row, route-classifier row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming row;
\item frontier enumeration, requiring every accepted critical pair to be read from $R$ and $F$ before a local join is consulted;
\item local-join exactness, requiring $J$ to display the join candidate before the bounded checker $E$ is used;
\item substitution-boundary visibility, requiring $U$ to remain visible whenever the audit-map boundary is cited;
\item route separation, requiring $S$ to keep subject-reduction discharge outside the confluence critical-pair route;
\item nonescape, excluding global confluence, arbitrary closed-term Church--Rosser, full parallel diamond, residual-substitution compatibility, unchecked subject-reduction discharge, and audit-map reads outside $R,F,J,E,U,S,H,C,P,N$.
\end{enumerate}
No frontier critical-pair obligation is stored outside those rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:metacic-frontier-critical-pair-carrier}. The projection exposes exactly $R,F,J,E,U,S,H,C,P,N$.

Frontier enumeration reads $R$ and $F$ first. Therefore the packet can inspect only finite frontier overlaps that appear in the displayed critical-pair table.

The local-join obligation reads $J$ before the bounded checker $E$. This keeps bounded normal-equality checking downstream of a displayed join candidate rather than turning the checker into a global confluence proof.

The boundary and route-separation obligations are carried by $U$ and $S$. They keep substitution compatibility and subject-reduction discharge outside the packet's confluence route.

The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name only the displayed route. Since the excluded confluence and typing claims are absent from the carrier, the local NameCert surface is exhausted by the stated rows.
\end{proof}

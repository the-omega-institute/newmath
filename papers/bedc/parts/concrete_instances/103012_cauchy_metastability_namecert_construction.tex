\begin{definition}[Cauchy metastability carrier]\label{def:cauchy-metastability-carrier}
A $\CauchyMetastabilityUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
M=(p,g,W,D,Q,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $p$ is the precision request. It names the dyadic tolerance that the packet must answer.
\item $g$ is the control-function row. It is read only through the finite requests displayed in the packet, not as a countable choice object.
\item $W$ is the finite tail-window row. It displays a bound $n$ and the finite interval of indices inspected between $n$ and $g(n)$.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ tolerance ledger shared by the precision request, the tail-window comparisons, the rational readback, and the terminal real seal.
\item $Q$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row. It supplies the rational values actually compared inside the window named by $W$.
\item $E$ is the $\RealUp$ seal, exposed only after $p,g,W,D,Q$ have been displayed.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the precision request, control row, tail window, tolerance ledger, readback, seal, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay along the finite route
$$
p,g,W,D,Q \longmapsto E,H,P,N .
$$
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\CauchyMetastabilityUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\CauchyMetastabilityUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $p,g,W,D,Q,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for countable choice, quotient stream equality, a selected limit, host real equality, trichotomy, or an ambient $\RealUp$ completeness witness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Cauchy metastability NameCert obligations]\label{thm:cauchy-metastability-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\CauchyMetastabilityUp$ carrier
$$
M=(p,g,W,D,Q,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\CauchyMetastabilityUp}$ surface consists exactly of nine finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the precision request, control row, tail-window row, tolerance ledger, rational readback, real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item control-window exactness, requiring $W$ to answer the displayed control row $g$ at the displayed precision request $p$;
\item dyadic tolerance accounting, requiring every comparison inside $W$ to use the single ledger $D$;
\item $\RegSeqRatUp$ handoff, requiring each compared value in the finite window to be read from $Q$;
\item $\RealUp$ seal boundary, requiring $E$ to be downstream of $p,g,W,D,Q$ and not an independent completion coordinate;
\item $\hsame$ transport, requiring accepted comparisons to preserve $p,g,W,D,Q,E,C,P,N$ componentwise;
\item $\Cont$ replay, requiring every endpoint-facing read to replay the displayed finite route;
\item $\Pkg$ provenance, requiring the packet to cite only the listed source chapters and kernel objects;
\item local naming, requiring $N$ to name precisely the finite metastability packet and no hidden limit data.
\end{enumerate}
No NameCert obligation is stored outside these rows, and none exports countable choice, quotient stream equality, a selected limit, host real equality, trichotomy, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:cauchy-metastability-carrier}. The accepted packet exposes exactly $p,g,W,D,Q,E,H,C,P,N$, so the local certificate has no detached coordinate for a completed limit or infinite selector.

Control-window exactness is read from $g$ and $W$. The control row may ask for a tail interval, but the carrier answers it only by the displayed finite window row and the indices named there.

The tolerance and readback obligations are then projections to $D$ and $Q$. The dyadic ledger is the only tolerance account available to the packet, and the regular rational readback supplies the only values compared inside the named window.

The endpoint boundary is supplied by $E$ together with $H,C,P,N$. Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming preserve the displayed route from request to window to readback to seal, and they introduce no row from which the excluded principles can be read.
\end{proof}

Consequently the packet witnesses finite Cauchy tail stability without countable choice, quotient stream equality, a selected limit, host real equality, trichotomy, or an ambient $\RealUp$ completeness coordinate.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

An accepted control-window request is therefore handled inside $W$. The row $g$ determines which finite interval must be inspected, and $W$ displays the bound and window that answer that request.

\begin{theorem}[Measure real-stream dependency lattice]
\label{thm:measure-real-stream-dependency-lattice}
Every $\MeasureUp$ real-valued or countable-name consumer factors through the $\StreamNameUp$ finite observation rows of \autoref{def:measure-streamname-endpoint-family}, the $\RealUp$ seal rows attached to those observations, the finite event-algebra rows of the root public surface, the displayed $\BHist$ ledger, componentwise $\hsame$, $\Cont$ replay, $\Pkg$ provenance, and local $\NameCert$ rows before any countable premeasure or relative-difference read is accepted. The route is the common dependency lattice supplied by \autoref{thm:measure-streamname-endpoint-carrier-classifier}, \autoref{thm:measure-countable-name-carrier-classifier-obligation}, \autoref{thm:measure-root-real-stream-premeasure-carrier-obligation}, and \autoref{thm:measure-dependency-lattice}.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the StreamName endpoint family. By \autoref{thm:measure-streamname-endpoint-carrier-classifier}, each unary observation is carried by the displayed $\StreamNameUp$ row and transports its measurable-event row and $\RealUp$ endpoint row through the same componentwise classifier data.

Next read the countable-name surface. Apply \autoref{thm:measure-countable-name-carrier-classifier-obligation}; it requires the visible countable family, event rows, endpoint comparisons, and pointwise StreamName classifier rows to remain attached to one $\BHist$ measure code.

Now use the real-stream premeasure bridge. The carrier obligation of \autoref{thm:measure-root-real-stream-premeasure-carrier-obligation} admits a countable premeasure row only after the displayed event rows, StreamName observation family, Real endpoint comparisons, and $\NameCert$ rows have been read.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:measure-dependency-lattice}. Its value, integral, and probability-facing dependencies bind the same finite event-value boundary to $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and local naming data. Therefore a countable premeasure or relative-difference consumer cannot bypass the StreamName observations, Real seals, finite event-algebra rows, or structural certificate rows.
\end{proof}

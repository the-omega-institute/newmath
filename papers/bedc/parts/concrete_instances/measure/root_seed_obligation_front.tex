\begin{definition}[Measure root seed obligation front]
\label{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front}
The $\MeasureUp$ root seed obligation front is the ten-field surface that
places the root measure certificate before its downstream consumers:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item traditional target: a constructive measure space whose measurable
  predicates form a $\sigma$-algebra and whose measure takes values in
  $\RealUp$;
  \item BEDC source: $\mathsf{BHist}$ presentations, the certificate
  $\NameCert_{\RealUp}$, and the additive $\AbGroupUp$ row on real endpoints;
  \item carrier: the predicate $\mathsf{MeasCode}$ on $\mathsf{BHist}$;
  \item classifier: the root classifier $\sim_M$ on measure codes, with
  endpoint comparisons read through the $\RealUp$ classifier $\sim_R$;
  \item stability rows: transport of $\mathsf{MeasCode}$, visible event
  rows, measurable-family rows, endpoint rows, countable-sum rows, and
  head-tail readback along $\sim_M$ and componentwise $\hsame$;
  \item ledger rows: measurable membership, relative-difference,
  disjointness, endpoint-value, and countable-sum witnesses attached to the
  visible $\mathsf{BHist}$ rows they justify;
  \item core theorem list:
  \autoref{thm:measure-root-carrier-classifier-public-obligation},
  \autoref{thm:measure-root-sigma-additive-public-obligation}, and
  \autoref{thm:measure-root-downstream-unblock-package};
  \item exactness: visible-row exhaustion by
  \autoref{def:measure-event-row-coverage} and
  \autoref{thm:measure-ledger-exactness-obligation};
  \item standard bridge: no $\StdBridge$ is asserted at this paper site;
  \item status: $\seedClosure$ on the theory axis, with the theorem-shaped
  target surface for $\formalTargetV$ under $\mathsf{BEDC.Derived.MeasureUp}$.
\end{enumerate}
This front is read through the carrier rows of
\autoref{def:measure-sigma-algebra-carrier-surface}, the certificate rows of
\autoref{def:measure-certificate-surface}, the root public surface of
\autoref{def:measure-root-namecert-public-surface}, and the root consumer
package of \autoref{def:measure-root-consumer-package}.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root seed carrier-classifier obligation]
\label{thm:measure-root-seed-carrier-classifier-obligation}
The seed front of \autoref{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front} supplies
the root carrier and classifier obligation for $\MeasureUp$. If
$h,k:\mathsf{BHist}$ satisfy $\mathsf{MeasCode}(h)$ and $h\sim_M k$, then the
front transports $\mathsf{MeasCode}(k)$, every displayed event row, every
ledger row, and every $\RealUp$ endpoint comparison attached to the carried
measure code. The endpoint movement is always read through
$\NameCert_{\RealUp}$ and the classifier $\sim_R$, while event movement is
read through componentwise $\hsame$ on the $\mathsf{BHist}$ rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the carrier and classifier fields of
\autoref{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front}. They are the
$\mathsf{MeasCode}$ and $\sim_M$ fields of
\autoref{def:measure-certificate-surface}, read over the visible rows listed
in \autoref{def:measure-sigma-algebra-carrier-surface}.

Apply \autoref{thm:measure-obligation-carrier-transport}. It transports the
carried measure code and permits every displayed row indexed by that code to
be read at the target history $k$ after the $\sim_M$ comparison is supplied.

Next apply \autoref{thm:measure-root-carrier-classifier-public-obligation}.
Its public form identifies the transported rows as the root carrier,
classifier, ledger, and endpoint rows of the $\MeasureUp$ surface. The event
rows move by their componentwise $\hsame$ witnesses, and the endpoint rows
move through $\NameCert_{\RealUp}$ and $\sim_R$.

Finally use the root exactness clause from
\autoref{thm:measure-ledger-exactness-obligation}. Since every consumed
membership, relative-difference, disjointness, value, and countable-sum
witness is attached to a visible row, the transported visible rows exhaust the
root carrier-classifier obligation.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root seed sigma-additive obligation]
\label{thm:measure-root-seed-sigma-additive-obligation}
The seed front of \autoref{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front} supplies
the root sigma-additive obligation for $\MeasureUp$. For every covered
pairwise-disjoint measurable sequence $E_\bullet$ in a carried
$\mathsf{BHist}$ fiber with covered countable union $U$, the front supplies
the comparison
$$
\mu(U)\sim_R\Sigma_\mu(E_\bullet).
$$
It also supplies the empty-zero, finite disjoint-union, relative-difference,
head-tail, and countable-sum classifier rows needed to read this comparison
through the additive $\AbGroupUp$ real row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the sigma-algebra, countable-union, countable-sum, head-tail, and
classifier-stability rows from \autoref{def:measure-certificate-surface}. The
coverage predicate of \autoref{def:measure-event-row-coverage} places the
sequence $E_\bullet$, its union $U$, its disjointness witness, and its
endpoint rows in the visible $\mathsf{BHist}$ ledger.

Apply \autoref{thm:measure-root-sigma-additive-public-obligation}. Its first
conclusion is precisely the displayed comparison
$\mu(U)\sim_R\Sigma_\mu(E_\bullet)$, with both endpoints classified through
$\sim_R$.

The same public obligation invokes
\autoref{thm:measureup-sigma-additive-certificate-obligations},
\autoref{thm:measure-empty-set-zero-from-sigma-additivity},
\autoref{thm:measure-finite-disjoint-union-additivity}, and
\autoref{thm:measure-relative-difference-additivity}. These rows provide the
empty-zero, finite readback, relative-difference, head-tail, and
countable-sum classifier consequences.

The additive structure used in those readbacks is the $\AbGroupUp$ real row
named in \autoref{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front}. Thus the
sigma-additive obligation is a root $\mathsf{BHist}$ and $\RealUp$ endpoint
statement, not a separate host-level measure-space equality.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root seed consumer threshold]
\label{thm:measure-root-seed-consumer-threshold}
The seed front of \autoref{def:measure-root-seed-obligation-front} reaches
the root consumer threshold for $\IntegralUp$, $\ProbSpaceUp$, and
$\FunctionalAnalysisUp$. The threshold exports exactly the covered
measurable-event rows, $\RealUp$ endpoint classifier rows, countable-sum
rows, head-tail readback, empty-zero, finite disjoint-union readback,
relative-difference additivity, monotone inclusion, nonnegative endpoint,
total-event normalization, and the explicit no-$\StdBridge$ row exposed by
the root $\MeasureUp$ package.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the root public surface of
\autoref{def:measure-root-namecert-public-surface} and the root consumer
package of \autoref{def:measure-root-consumer-package}. Together they expose
the $\mathsf{BHist}$ event ledger, the $\sim_M$ carrier classifier, and the
$\NameCert_{\RealUp}$ endpoint classifier rows named by the seed front.

Apply \autoref{thm:measure-root-downstream-unblock-package}. It packages the
carrier-classifier rows of
\autoref{thm:measure-root-carrier-classifier-public-obligation} with the
sigma-additive rows of
\autoref{thm:measure-root-sigma-additive-public-obligation}, while keeping
the standard-bridge field absent.

For the three outgoing faces, apply
\autoref{thm:measure-integral-consumer-rows},
\autoref{thm:measure-probability-consumer-rows}, and
\autoref{thm:measure-functionalanalysis-consumer-rows}. These theorems read
the integral, probability, and functional-analysis projections from the same
root consumer package and list the rows each projection may consume.

Finally compare those projections with
\autoref{thm:measure-root-bhist-real-endpoint-threshold},
\autoref{thm:measure-root-bhist-probspace-threshold}, and
\autoref{thm:measure-root-bhist-integral-functional-threshold}. The threshold
contains no host set equality, quotient event equality, analytic completion
object, or $\StdBridge$ field, so the exported consumer surface is exactly the
one stated above.
\end{proof}

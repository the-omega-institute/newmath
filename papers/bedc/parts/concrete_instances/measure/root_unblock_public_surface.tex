\begin{definition}[Measure root NameCert public surface]
\label{def:measure-root-namecert-public-surface}
The root $\MeasureUp$ NameCert public surface is the ten-field carrier surface
of \autoref{def:measure-sigma-algebra-carrier-surface}, read together with the
certificate surface of \autoref{def:measure-certificate-surface}, the exact
event-row coverage predicate of \autoref{def:measure-event-row-coverage}, and
the no-$\StdBridge$ row recorded in the standard-bridge field of the carrier
surface. Concretely, its public rows are:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item the traditional constructive measure-space target;
  \item the BEDC source rows over $\mathsf{BHist}$, $\NameCert_{\RealUp}$, and
  the additive $\AbGroupUp$ real row;
  \item the visible carrier rows for measurable events, empty event, binary
  union, countable union, complement/relative-difference, disjoint sequence
  readback, $\RealUp$ endpoints, and countable sums;
  \item the componentwise $\hsame$ classifier, with $\RealUp$ endpoint
  comparison supplied by the real classifier;
  \item the stability rows for every displayed event, value, and countable-sum
  component;
  \item the ledger rows attached to hidden membership, relative-difference,
  and countable-sum witnesses;
  \item the core consequence rows named in the carrier surface;
  \item the exactness predicate asserting that downstream consumers read only
  the displayed rows;
  \item the explicit absence of a standard bridge at this paper site;
  \item the public status row that exposes the resulting package as the
  $\MeasureUp$ NameCert obligation surface.
\end{enumerate}
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root carrier-classifier public obligation]
\label{thm:measure-root-carrier-classifier-public-obligation}
The root $\MeasureUp$ NameCert public surface of
\autoref{def:measure-root-namecert-public-surface} supplies the public carrier
and classifier transport rows required by the $\MeasureUp$ obligation surface.
For any two presented histories $h,k:\mathsf{BHist}$ in the surface, a carried
$\MeasureUp$ code at $h$, and a public classifier comparison $h\sim_M k$, the
surface transports the carrier code, each visible event row, each ledger row,
and each $\RealUp$ endpoint row from $h$ to $k$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Begin with the root public surface and project its carrier and classifier
fields. These fields are exactly the carrier slots and componentwise
classifier slots listed in
\autoref{def:measure-sigma-algebra-carrier-surface}.

Apply \autoref{thm:measure-carrier-classifier-obligation}. It transports the
displayed measurable-event, empty-event, binary-union, countable-union,
relative-difference, disjointness, endpoint, and countable-sum rows along the
public classifier. The $\RealUp$ endpoint movement is read through the
displayed real classifier, while the event rows move by their $\hsame$
components.

It remains to expose the stability rows as public obligation rows. Apply
\autoref{thm:measureup-classifier-stability-obligations}. Its projections
identify the same classifier-stable rows at the certificate level, so the
transported carrier, classifier, ledger, and endpoint data repack as the
root carrier-classifier public obligation.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root sigma-additive public obligation]
\label{thm:measure-root-sigma-additive-public-obligation}
The root $\MeasureUp$ NameCert public surface supplies the public
sigma-additive, empty-zero, finite readback, and relative-difference rows.
For every covered pairwise-disjoint measurable sequence $E_\bullet$ with
countable union $U$, the surface supplies
$$
\mu(U)\sim_R\Sigma_\mu(E_\bullet).
$$
It also supplies $\mu(\varnothing)\sim_R0_R$, finite disjoint-union readback
for covered disjoint pairs, and relative-difference additivity for covered
inclusions of measurable events in the same fiber.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the sigma-algebra, countable-union, head-tail, countable-sum
classifier, and additive $\AbGroupUp$ rows from the root public surface. The
coverage predicate ensures that the measurable sequence, its union, its
disjointness witness, and the resulting endpoints are all visible
$\mathsf{BHist}$ ledger rows.

Apply \autoref{thm:measureup-sigma-additive-certificate-obligations} to read
the certificate rows as the public sigma-additive obligation. Its
sigma-additivity projection gives the displayed comparison for
$E_\bullet$ and $U$.

Next apply \autoref{thm:measure-empty-set-zero-from-sigma-additivity}. The
constant-empty measurable sequence is covered by the empty-event and
countable-union rows, and the additive $\AbGroupUp$ row supplies the zero and
congruence laws used by the theorem.

Apply \autoref{thm:measure-finite-disjoint-union-additivity} to a covered
disjoint pair. The same visible binary-union, endpoint, and head-tail rows
give the finite readback comparison.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:measure-relative-difference-additivity}. The
relative-difference row is one of the displayed carrier rows, so the inclusion
witness, disjointness witness, binary-union readback, and endpoint comparison
repack as the public relative-difference obligation.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Measure root downstream unblock package]
\label{thm:measure-root-downstream-unblock-package}
The carrier-classifier rows of
\autoref{thm:measure-root-carrier-classifier-public-obligation} and the
sigma-additive rows of
\autoref{thm:measure-root-sigma-additive-public-obligation} form the public
$\MeasureUp$ package consumed by $\IntegralUp$, $\ProbSpaceUp$, and
$\FunctionalAnalysisUp$. The package exposes only covered measurable-event
rows, $\RealUp$ endpoint rows, countable-sum rows, classifier-stable
head-tail readback, empty-zero, finite disjoint-union readback,
relative-difference additivity, and the explicit no-$\StdBridge$ row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start from the root public surface and apply
\autoref{thm:measure-namecert-obligation-package}. This repacks the carrier,
classifier, sigma-additivity, ledger exactness, nonnegative value, monotone
inclusion, and measure consequence rows as the public $\MeasureUp$ NameCert
obligation package.

For $\ProbSpaceUp$, apply
\autoref{thm:measure-probability-normalization-row}. It consumes the total
event endpoint together with the empty-zero and finite disjoint-union rows
already exposed by the root sigma-additive public obligation.

For $\IntegralUp$ and $\FunctionalAnalysisUp$, apply
\autoref{thm:measure-integral-premeasure-consumption}. Its consumed rows are
exactly the covered measurable-event rows, endpoint classifier rows,
countable-sum rows, head-tail readback rows, and finite disjoint-union rows
projected above.

The event-row coverage predicate prevents any extra carrier from entering the
downstream package. The standard-bridge field is the no-$\StdBridge$ row from
the root surface, so the public package is a NameCert surface only and makes
no standard-bridge claim.
\end{proof}

\begin{definition}[Regular Cauchy located completion carrier]
\label{def:regular-cauchy-located-completion-carrier}
A $\RegularCauchyLocatedCompletionUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
L=(S,R,D,Q,K,E,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $S$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window row through which the Cauchy name is inspected at requested precision scales.
\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ regular rational readback row attached to those windows.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ arithmetic ledger for tolerance comparison, shrinkage, and rational distance estimates.
\item $Q$ is the located completion row, recording the displayed located-distance and approximation data extracted from $S,R,D$.
\item $K$ is the $\CompleteMetricUp$ service row used only as a finite completion handoff for the displayed located data.
\item $E$ is the terminal $\RealUp$ seal row reached after the regular readback and located completion rows have been displayed.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for stream windows, regular rational readbacks, dyadic arithmetic, located completion data, completion service, real seal, replay, provenance, and local naming.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a located-completion request through $S,R,D,Q,K,E,H,P,N$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RegularCauchyLocatedCompletionUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\CompleteMetricUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RegularCauchyLocatedCompletionUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $S,R,D,Q,K,E,H,C,P,N$. The carrier has no coordinate for quotient completion equality, selected representatives, countable choice, host order completeness, ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, Lean verification, or bridge checking.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Regular Cauchy located completion obligations]
\label{thm:regular-cauchy-located-completion-obligations}
For every accepted $\RegularCauchyLocatedCompletionUp$ carrier
$$
L=(S,R,D,Q,K,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\RegularCauchyLocatedCompletionUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the stream-window row, regular rational readback row, dyadic arithmetic ledger, located completion row, complete-metric handoff, real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local name;
\item window-readback coherence, requiring $S$ to expose the finite inspection windows before $R$ may be consumed as a regular rational name;
\item dyadic locatedness, requiring $D$ to supply the tolerance comparisons and distance estimates used by the located completion row $Q$;
\item completion handoff ordering, requiring $Q$ to be displayed before the $\CompleteMetricUp$ row $K$ is exposed;
\item real-seal ordering, requiring $E$ to be read only after $S,R,D,Q,K$ have been displayed;
\item classifier stability, requiring componentwise $\hsame$ transport and displayed $\Cont$ replay to preserve all visible rows;
\item non-escape, requiring $H,C,P,N$ to transport, replay, source, and name no quotient completion equality, selected representative, countable choice, host order completeness, ambient $\RealUp$ completeness, Lean verification, or bridge credential.
\end{enumerate}
No local NameCert obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:regular-cauchy-located-completion-carrier}. Its rows list the finite stream windows, regular rational readback, dyadic arithmetic ledger, located completion data, complete-metric handoff, real seal, and the structural rows.

Window-readback coherence follows because $R$ has no independent observation coordinate in this packet. The regular rational read must pass through $S$, and the tolerance computations used by that read are then checked against the dyadic row $D$.

The located completion row $Q$ is the only row that records located-distance data. The completion handoff $K$ consumes that displayed row, and the real seal $E$ is exposed only after the handoff has been displayed.

Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming are exhausted by $H,C,P,N$. Since quotient completion equality, selected representatives, countable choice, host order completeness, and ambient $\RealUp$ completeness are absent from the carrier, no NameCert obligation can export them.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Regular Cauchy located completion Real seal route]
\label{thm:regular-cauchy-located-completion-real-seal-route}

$$
S,R,D \longmapsto Q \longmapsto K \longmapsto E \longmapsto H,C,P,N .
$$

It does not export quotient completion equality, selected representatives, countable choice, host order completeness, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

Finally the seal row $E$ exposes the $\RealUp$ boundary, and $H,C,P,N$ transport, replay, source, and name the same displayed route. No remaining carrier coordinate can provide quotient completion equality, selected representatives, choice data, host order completeness, or ambient real completeness.
\end{proof}

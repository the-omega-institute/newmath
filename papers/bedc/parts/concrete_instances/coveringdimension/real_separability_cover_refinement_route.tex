\begin{lemma}[Covering dimension separability cover-refinement route]

\end{lemma}

Finally the structural rows $H,T,P,N$ preserve the same route under $\hsame$ transport, $\Cont$ replay, package provenance, and local naming. Since the carrier has no detached host open cover, choice-selected refinement, or external multiplicity oracle, none can be exported to the separability-facing consumer.
\end{proof}

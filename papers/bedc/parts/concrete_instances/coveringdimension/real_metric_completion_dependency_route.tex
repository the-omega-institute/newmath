The $\CoveringDimensionUp$ carrier consumes $\RealSeparabilityUp$ through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-separability-namecert} when countable dense windows are refined into finite cover samples, and it consumes $\MetricCompletionUp$ through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metriccompletion-namecert} when those finite refinements are transported across completion-stable metric rows. Thus the dimension packet records a real separability and metric completion dependency route without turning dense covers into selected points or completion stability into ambient compactness.

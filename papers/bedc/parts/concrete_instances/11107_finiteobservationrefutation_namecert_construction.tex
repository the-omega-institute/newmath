\begin{definition}[FiniteObservationRefutation carrier]
\label{def:finite-observation-refutation-carrier}

$$

$$
with the following visible rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $T$ is the finite trace row through which the observation is read.
\item $\Pi$ is the observation-bundle row naming the finite questions used for the read.
\item $O$ is the observed-signature row extracted from $T$ through $\Pi$.
\item $M$ is the model-signature row generated over the same finite trace and bundle.
\item $F$ is the finite-fit row whose acceptance is being tested.
\item $D$ is the displayed defeating-observation row, storing the finite observation that makes $O$ and $M$ fail the accepted classifier.
\item $V$ is the verdict row separating finite defeat, current undefeatedness, and final-fit refusal.
\item $L$ is the ledger row recording the finite fit scope, the defeating observation, and the refusal to promote the prefix into final confirmation.
\item $E$ is the open-continuation row recording admissible observation extensions still outside the finite packet.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for $T,\Pi,O,M,F,D,V,L,E,C,P,N$.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay through the trace, bundle, signature, fit, defeat, verdict, ledger, and continuation rows.

\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\FiniteObservationRefutationUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for total model identity, host physical truth, all-prefix confirmation, or a final-fit oracle.
\end{definition}

\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\GeomQuantizationUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-geomquantization-namecert}

The geometric-quantization interface $\GeomQuantizationUp$ packages the construction of a Hilbert space from a symplectic manifold via a prequantum line bundle, polarisation, and metaplectic correction. It depends on $\NameCert_{\SymplecticUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-symplectic-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\HilbertUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-hilbert-namecert}), and bridges geometric and operator-algebraic certificates through a common polarisation classifier.

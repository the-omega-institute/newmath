$A$ exact for accepted results only after the finite trace-step row in $T$ has
been read from $I$.

packet. By \autoref{thm:finite-trace-evaluator-public-consumer-package} and
\autoref{thm:finite-trace-evaluator-runtime-checkpoint-boundary}, the public

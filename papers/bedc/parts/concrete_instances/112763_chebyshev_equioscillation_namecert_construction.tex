\begin{definition}[Chebyshev equioscillation carrier]
\label{def:chebyshev-equioscillation-carrier}

$$

\item $F$ is the continuous-map row on $I$, read through the same compact-source windows as the $\ContinuousSupNormUp$ packet.
\item $\nu$ is the requested polynomial degree bound and coefficient-window budget.
\item $P$ is the displayed $\PolynomialUp$ row whose finite coefficient ledger has degree at most $\nu$.
\item $S$ is the finite alternating support ledger, listing inspected points of $I$ in order together with the endpoint and interior-window data used to compare them.
\item $\Sigma$ is the sign pattern row, recording alternating signs along $S$.
\item $A$ is the signed extremal-error ledger, saying that the inspected errors $F-P$ reach the same displayed sup-norm magnitude with the signs in $\Sigma$.
\item $R$ is the minimax refusal row, recording that every admissible lower-degree perturbation read by the packet fails to reduce the displayed sup-norm bound on the same support windows.

\begin{theorem}[TypeCheckingClassifierMembership NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:type-checking-classifier-membership-namecert-obligations}

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

The derivation and preservation rows are also displayed. The row $D$ records the finite type-checking derivation trace, and $S$ records the subject-reduction handoff that may be consumed only through the visible preservation boundary.

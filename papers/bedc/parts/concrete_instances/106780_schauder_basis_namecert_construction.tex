\begin{definition}[Schauder basis carrier]
\label{def:schauder-basis-carrier}
A $\SchauderBasisUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
K=(V,Nrm,B,C,S,E,R,H,T,P,L)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $V$ is the $\VecSpaceUp$ row naming the vector-space carrier, addition, scalar action, zero vector, and finite linear-combination syntax.
\item $Nrm$ is the $\NormUp$ row naming the norm, triangle budget, scalar-compatibility budget, and zero-detection surface.
\item $B$ is the basis-vector stream row, a $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window record whose inspected entries name the vectors $b_0,b_1,\ldots$ available to the packet.
\item $C$ is the coordinate-readback row, recording finite coordinate requests and their $\RegSeqRatUp$ rational names.
\item $S$ is the partial-sum row, recording finite sums $\sum_{i<n} c_i b_i$ together with the requested truncation index.
\item $E$ is the error-control row, a $\RealUp$-facing norm estimate for the tail between the named vector and the displayed partial sum.
\item $R$ is the reconstruction row, recording that each accepted vector read is recovered through the displayed partial sums and tail estimates.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the vector, norm, basis stream, coordinate, partial-sum, error, reconstruction, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $T$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a coordinate request through $B,C,S,E,R$.
\item $P$ records $\Pkg$ provenance over $\SchauderBasisUp$, $\BanachUp$, $\VecSpaceUp$, $\NormUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $L$ is the local $\NameCert_{\SchauderBasisUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $V,Nrm,B,C,S,E,R,H,T,P,L$. It has no coordinate for a Hamel basis choice, an arbitrary linear functional, a detached coefficient oracle, ambient compactness, countable choice, or a global unconditional-basis assertion.
\end{definition}

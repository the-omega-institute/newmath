\begin{theorem}[Concrete unary multiplication MulUp certificate spine]
\label{thm:unary-multiplication-mulup-certificate}
The concrete unary multiplication candidate for $\mathsf{MulUp}$ uses carrier
$\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$ over $\mathsf{BHist}$, classifier $\hsame$, operation
relation $\mathsf{NatMul}$, and unit $\Eone(\emp)$. Its certificate spine is the
following finite law package.
\begin{enumerate}
\item Totality: for unary histories $d$ and $q$, there is a unary $n$ with
$\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,n)$.
\item Result closure: every $\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,n)$ with unary $d$ has unary
result $n$.
\item Functionality: any two results of $\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,-)$ for unary
inputs are $\hsame$.
\item Unit laws: $\mathsf{NatMul}(\Eone(\emp),q,n)$ gives $n\hsame q$, and
$\mathsf{NatMul}(d,\Eone(\emp),n)$ gives $n\hsame d$.
\item Commutativity: products $\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,n)$ and
$\mathsf{NatMul}(q,d,m)$ over unary inputs have $n\hsame m$.
\item Associativity: for unary $a$, $b$, and $c$, products
$\mathsf{NatMul}(a,b,ab)$, $\mathsf{NatMul}(ab,c,\ell)$,
$\mathsf{NatMul}(b,c,bc)$, and $\mathsf{NatMul}(a,bc,r)$ have
$\ell\hsame r$.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
The carrier, classifier, operation, and unit are the concrete objects already
used by $\mathsf{NatMul}$. Totality, result closure, functionality, the two unit
laws, and commutativity are exactly the checked unary multiplication laws in
this section.

For associativity, induct on the middle multiplication spine. The base case is
the zero multiplier. The successor case rewrites the right-associated product
as an appended multiplier product, applies
\autoref{thm:natmul-append-continuation-closure}, and then uses functionality
to compare it with the displayed left-associated result.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Unary multiplication MulUp semantic exit package]
\label{thm:unary-multiplication-mulup-semantic-exit-package}
For unary histories $d$ and $q$, the result carrier
$\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,-)$ has a $\mathsf{SemanticNameCert}$ with classifier
$\hsame$. Together with the concrete carrier $\mathsf{UnaryHistory}$ over
$\mathsf{BHist}$, classifier $\hsame$, operation relation $\mathsf{NatMul}$,
unit $\Eone(\emp)$, totality, result closure, functionality, the two unit
laws, commutativity, and associativity, this supplies the $\mathsf{MulUp}$
phase exit package over $\mathsf{BHist}$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Apply \autoref{thm:unary-multiplication-mulup-certificate} for the algebraic
spine. Its rows are precisely the concrete carrier, classifier, operation,
unit, totality, result closure, functionality, left and right unit laws,
commutativity, and associativity for unary multiplication.

The result carrier certificate is the checked
$\mathsf{NatMul\_result\_semanticNameCert}$ theorem. It takes the unary inputs
$d$ and $q$ and returns a $\mathsf{SemanticNameCert}$ for the predicate
$\mathsf{NatMul}(d,q,-)$ with classifier $\hsame$.

The dependency list is therefore the checked multiplication family
$\mathsf{NatMul\_total}$, $\mathsf{NatMul\_result\_unary}$,
$\mathsf{NatMul\_functional}$, $\mathsf{NatMul\_unit\_left\_hsame}$,
$\mathsf{NatMul\_unit\_right\_hsame}$, $\mathsf{NatMul\_comm\_hsame}$,
$\mathsf{NatMul\_assoc\_hsame}$, and the result-name certificate. These rows
are exactly the finite exit data needed for the full natural-arithmetic phase
object to consume $\mathsf{MulUp}$ as a single semantic package.
\end{proof}

The Koszul-duality interface $\KoszulDualityUp$ packages the contravariant derived equivalence between graded algebras of finite global dimension and their Koszul-dual algebras. It depends on $\NameCert_{\DerivedCatUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-derivedcat-namecert}) and $\NameCert_{\TensorProductUp}$ (\autoref{ch:concrete-instances-tensorproduct-namecert}), and bridges algebraic and homological certificates through the same Ext-classifier.

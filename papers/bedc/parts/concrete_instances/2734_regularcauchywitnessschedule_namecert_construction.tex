\begin{definition}[Regular Cauchy witness schedule carrier]
\label{def:regular-cauchy-witness-schedule-carrier}
A $\RegularCauchyWitnessScheduleUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$

$$
with the following displayed coordinates:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $X$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ family row for the regular Cauchy input;
\item $\mu$ is the explicit modulus row selecting the regular-Cauchy source index for a requested tolerance;
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window schedule at the selected modulus index;
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ observation ledger recording the endpoint, radius, tolerance, and comparison rows used by the scheduled read;
\item $R$ is the diagonal readback row naming the rational observation read from $X_{\mu}$ through $W$ and $D$;
\item $T$ is the $\RealUp$ seal handoff row reached after the displayed diagonal readback;
\item $H$ stores componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the family row, modulus row, window schedule, dyadic ledger, readback row, seal row, provenance, and local name;
\item $C$ stores the displayed $\Cont$ routes from the modulus request to $W$, from $W$ to $D$, from $D$ to $R$, and from $R$ to $T$;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\RegularCauchyWitnessScheduleUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier is a finite witness packet for the modulus-selected diagonal observation route. It contains no unbounded stream object, quotient equality, selected limit, host real equality, or completeness theorem.
\end{definition}

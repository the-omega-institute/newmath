\begin{definition}[ObserverDistinctionTrace carrier]
\label{def:observer-distinction-trace-carrier}
An $\ObserverDistinctionTraceUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
D=(H,T,K,C,L,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $H$ is the observed $\Hist$ source row whose distinction trace is being read;
\item $T$ is the finite ordered trace of $\Eone$ and $\Ezero$ distinction-step rows above the source surface;
\item $K$ is the trace-growth ledger, recording the successive $\Hist$ rows reached by the ordered distinction steps;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ routes that read each step of $T$ into the corresponding growth row in $K$;
\item $L$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the source, trace, growth, route, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\ObserverHistIdentityUp$, $\ObserverSubjectBoundaryUp$, $\HistTimeStreamUp$, $\LocalClockBudgetUp$, $\BHist$, $\Hist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ObserverDistinctionTraceUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for an observer-substance, a host equality of observers, a global clock, or a trace value detached from the displayed $\Hist$ and $\Cont$ rows.
\end{definition}

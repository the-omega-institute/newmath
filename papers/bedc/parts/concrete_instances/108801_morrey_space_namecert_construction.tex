\begin{definition}[MorreySpace carrier]
\label{def:morrey-space-carrier}
A $\MorreySpaceUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
M=(X,B,F,I,R,T,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $X$ is the source $\MetricSpaceUp$ row, including the distance classifier used by local ball consumers.
\item $B$ is the finite metric-ball window ledger, listing the inspected center histories, radius histories, and ball-membership witnesses.
\item $F$ is the function-observation row, read through the same $\RealUp$ value seal that Sobolev and integral consumers use.
\item $I$ is the local $\IntegralUp$ or $\MeasureUp$-facing budget row, recording the finite real-valued oscillation or integral magnitude attached to each inspected ball.
\item $R$ is the radius-scale ledger, comparing the displayed radius with the exponent and dimension budget through $\RealUp$ and $\RatUp$ readback rows.
\item $T$ is the transport row that keeps the ball window, function observation, integral budget, and radius-scale ledger attached under the carrier classifier.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the metric, ball-window, function, local-budget, scale-ledger, transport, replay, provenance, and local-name rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a Morrey-space request through metric-ball inspection, local budget readback, radius scaling, and exported consumer access.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\MorreySpaceUp$, $\MetricSpaceUp$, $\RealUp$, $\MeasureUp$, $\IntegralUp$, $\SobolevUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\MorreySpaceUp}$ row.

\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[MorreySpace NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:morrey-space-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\MorreySpaceUp$ carrier $M=(X,B,F,I,R,T,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\MorreySpaceUp}$ surface consists exactly of metric-source admission through $X$, metric-ball window exposure through $B$, Real-valued function observation through $F$, local oscillation or integral-budget exposure through $I$, radius-scale ledger comparison through $R$, classifier transport through $T$ and $H$, displayed $\Cont$ replay through $C$, $\Pkg$ provenance through $P$, and local naming through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:morrey-space-carrier}. The tuple displays the metric source, finite ball windows, Real-valued function observations, local budget row, radius-scale ledger, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming.

The analytic-facing part of the packet is finite. The metric row $X$ and ball-window row $B$ determine which local windows may be inspected, while $F$ and $I$ attach the Real-valued oscillation or integral budget to those same windows.

The scale row $R$ records the radius comparison available to a Morrey consumer. The transport rows $T,H$ and replay row $C$ preserve this same route under classifier comparison and downstream access, and $P,N$ source and name the route.

Since no remaining coordinate stores measure completion, quotient function equality, Hahn--Banach separation, a selected representative, or a PDE regularity theorem, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

\label{thm:morrey-space-sobolev-neighbour-handoff}

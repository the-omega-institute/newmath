\begin{theorem}[Axis carry route separation boundary]
\label{thm:axis-carry-route-separation-boundary}

\end{theorem}

Finally use coordinate exhaustion. Since $Q$ contains no host quotient, no global rewrite, and no coordinate that identifies $S$ with $T$ by $\hsame$, every public read remains within the finite carry route, normal-target route, and refusal ledger displayed above.
\end{proof}

Now apply \autoref{thm:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-root-route-totality} and \autoref{thm:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-root-route-nonescape}. Finally, \autoref{thm:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-root-authority-carrier-totality} keeps the authority read on $L$ itself. Thus the open-fit read cannot close empirical fit or certify model truth outside the local ledger.
\end{proof}

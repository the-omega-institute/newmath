\begin{theorem}[Reality-constrained methodology-ledger TruthCert reverse dependency]
\label{thm:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-truthcert-reverse-dependency}
Let $L=(X,A,O,T,I,S,D,U,F,H,C,Q,N)$ be an accepted $\RealityConstrainedMethodologyLedgerUp$ packet. If $L$ is read as TruthCert methodology authority, then the read is consumed through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-reality-constrained-truth-cert-namecert} and preserves the displayed rows
$$
X,A,O,T,I,S,D,U,F,H,C,Q,N.
$$
It may not be read as freestanding methodology authority, final model truth, hidden observer privilege, closed empirical fit, or off-ledger proof.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-carrier}. The only available methodology-ledger coordinates are the displayed rows $X,A,O,T,I,S,D,U,F,H,C,Q,N$, and the carrier explicitly omits final model truth, hidden observer privilege, closed empirical fit, and off-ledger export.

Apply the TruthCert consumer handoff theorem, \autoref{thm:reality-constrained-methodology-ledger-truthcert-consumer-handoff}. A TruthCert-facing methodology read receives the same finite ledger after carrier admission, local $\NameCert$ obligations, failure-consumer coverage, formal-target route totality, and TasteGate readback have displayed it.

The consumer is therefore routed through the TruthCert carrier chapter, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-reality-constrained-truth-cert-namecert}, rather than through a freestanding authority surface. Since that chapter consumes the methodology-ledger handoff as a TruthCert-facing export, any accepted read must preserve all rows of $L$ and cannot replace them by an absent truth, observer, fit, or proof coordinate.
\end{proof}

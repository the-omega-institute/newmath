\begin{definition}[List cons-boundary coherence]
\label{def:list-cons-boundary-coherent}
Let $A:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a BEDC history source carrier and let $\sim_A$ be a source classifier. Define $\mathsf{ListConsBoundaryCoherent}_{A,\sim_A}$ to mean that any two displayed cons presentations of the same list endpoint classify the displayed heads and tails. Concretely, for histories $m,a,a',t,t',p,p'$ and finite spines $xs,xs'$, assume $A(a)$, $A(a')$, $\mathsf{ListSpineRep}_A(t,xs)$, $\mathsf{ListSpineRep}_A(t',xs')$, $\ContR(a,t,p)$, $\ContR(a',t',p')$, $\hsame(m,\Eone(p))$, and $\hsame(m,\Eone(p'))$. The required outputs are exactly $a\sim_A a'$ and $\mathsf{ListClassifierSpec}(\sim_A,xs,xs')$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[List spine coherence from cons-boundary coherence]
\label{thm:list-spine-coherence-from-cons-boundary-coherence}
Let $A:\mathsf{BHist}\to\mathsf{Prop}$ be a BEDC history source carrier and let $\sim_A$ be a source classifier. If $\mathsf{ListConsBoundaryCoherent}_{A,\sim_A}$ holds, then $\mathsf{ListSpineRepCoherent}_{A,\sim_A}$ holds.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Compare two finite-spine presentations of the same history by cases on the displayed spines. The nil-nil case is the nil constructor of $\mathsf{ListClassifierSpec}$. The mixed nil-cons cases contradict Theorem~\ref{thm:list-spine-rep-nil-cons-no-confusion}. In the cons-cons case, both presentations expose the same list endpoint together with displayed head histories, tail spines, tail representations, continuation witnesses, and endpoint witnesses. Definition~\ref{def:list-cons-boundary-coherent} supplies exactly the head classifier witness and the tail finite-spine classifier witness, and these are the two constructor arguments needed for $\mathsf{ListClassifierSpec}(\sim_A,-,-)$ on the whole cons spines.
\end{proof}

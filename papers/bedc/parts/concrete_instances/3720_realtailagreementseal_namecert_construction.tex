\begin{theorem}[Real tail-agreement seal NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:real-tail-agreement-seal-namecert-obligations}

The RealUp handoff and non-escape rows are then package clauses. The provenance $P$ and local naming row $N$ expose only $R,S,W,D,A,H,C,P,N$, so any downstream Real or Completion consumer receives the finite tail-agreement route and no external analytic object.

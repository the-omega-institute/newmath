The terminal read is downstream of regular rational observation. The row $R$ supplies rational centers, radii, and tolerances before $E$ is consumed, and $H,C,Q,N$ preserve the same route. Since the excluded objects have no carrier coordinate, the listed rows exhaust the local NameCert surface.

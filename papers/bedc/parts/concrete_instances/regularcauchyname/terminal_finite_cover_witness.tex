\begin{theorem}[RegularCauchyName terminal finite-cover witness]
\label{thm:regularcauchyname-terminal-finite-cover-witness}
Every accepted $\RegularCauchyNameUp$ packet with displayed root dependency manifest and four-face support minimality admits a terminal finite-cover witness whose support consists exactly of the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows named in \autoref{def:regularcauchyname-root-dependency-manifest}. Every terminal $\RealUp$ read or completion-facing read factors through that witness, and the witness exports no quotient stream, host sequence equality, selected limit, independent real carrier, ambient completeness, metric completeness, countable-choice, or bridge row.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
First locate the source and consumer reads. The root dependency manifest exhaustion theorem \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-root-dependency-manifest-exhaustion} places every downstream source, finite-window, readback, $\RealUp$ seal, and completion consumer read inside the displayed manifest of \autoref{def:regularcauchyname-root-dependency-manifest}.

Next pass from the manifest to the terminal exit. The manifest exit descent exactness theorem \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-manifest-exit-descent-exactness} identifies the terminal route, and the Phase Real and Completion exit interface theorem \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-phase-real-completion-exit-interface} reads that route through the four faces $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, and $\RealUp$ together with the displayed $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, $\Pkg$, and $\NameCert$ rows.

Finally apply the four-face support minimality theorem \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-four-face-support-minimality} and the terminal no-extra-support theorem \autoref{thm:regularcauchyname-terminal-exit-no-extra-support}. Minimality pins the witness to exactly those four faces and support rows, while no-extra-support refuses quotient stream data, host sequence equality, selected limits, independent real carriers, ambient or metric completeness, countable-choice coordinates, and bridge rows. Thus every terminal $\RealUp$ or completion-facing read factors through the displayed finite-cover witness and no other support row is exported.
\end{proof}

\section{Located Cauchy extraction carrier}
\label{sec:located-cauchy-extraction-carrier}

\begin{definition}[Located Cauchy extraction carrier]
\label{def:located-cauchy-extraction-carrier}
A $\LocatedCauchyExtractionUp$ carrier is a finite packet
$(D,S,Q,L,U,M,E,H,C,P,N)$ with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$, a $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ precision ledger for the positive dyadic
budgets used by the extraction;
\item $S$, a $\StreamNameUp$ observation window for the rational name being
read;
\item $Q$, a $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row exposing the regular Cauchy rational
values visible on $S$;
\item $L$ and $U$, lower and upper $\LocatedRealComparisonUp$ rows tied to the
same window and dyadic ledger;
\item $M$, the $\RegularCauchyLocatedModulusUp$ row assigning displayed
precision budgets to finite window depths;
\item $E$, the $\RealUp$ seal row that receives only the extracted finite
regular-Cauchy read;
\item $H$, the componentwise $\hsame$ transport rows for $D,S,Q,L,U,M,E$;
\item $C$, the displayed $\Cont$ replay routes preserving the extraction
window, located brackets, modulus row, and real seal;
\item $P$, the $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance rows for the dyadic ledger, stream
window, regular readback, located comparison evidence, located modulus, and
real seal;
\item $N$, the local $\NameCert$ row binding these ten rows as one located
Cauchy extraction certificate.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier contains no quotient equality of Cauchy names, no selected limit,
no trichotomy, no host real order predicate, and no ambient completeness
principle.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Located Cauchy extraction NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:located-cauchy-extraction-namecert-obligations}
The local $\NameCert_{\LocatedCauchyExtractionUp}$ surface for
\autoref{def:located-cauchy-extraction-carrier} consists of finite admission of
the rows $(D,S,Q,L,U,M,E,H,C,P,N)$, shared-window alignment for $S$, dyadic
precision exactness through $D$, regular readback confinement through $Q$,
located bracket export through $L,U$, modulus handoff through $M$, real sealing
through $E$, componentwise $\hsame$ transport through $H$, displayed $\Cont$
replay through $C$, $\mathsf{Pkg}$ provenance through $P$, and local naming
through $N$.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of
\autoref{def:located-cauchy-extraction-carrier}. The projection exposes exactly
the rows $D,S,Q,L,U,M,E,H,C,P,N$.

The extraction route is fixed by the shared window and ledger. Row $S$ is the
only observation window, row $D$ is the only dyadic precision source, and row
$Q$ is the only regular rational readback exposed to the located comparison and
modulus rows.

The located handoff is then forced through $L,U,M$ before the real seal $E$ is
read. Componentwise transport and replay through $H$ and $C$ preserve those
rows, while $P$ and $N$ record provenance and local naming.

No carrier coordinate names quotient equality, a selected limit, trichotomy, a
host order predicate, or ambient completeness. Hence the stated obligations
exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Located Cauchy extraction Real-seal route]
\label{thm:located-cauchy-extraction-real-seal-route}
Every accepted $\LocatedCauchyExtractionUp$ packet exports toward $\RealUp$
only the finite route
$$
D,S,Q,L,U,M \longmapsto E \longmapsto H,C,P,N .
$$
Any $\LocatedRealComparisonUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, or $\RealUp$ consumer of the
accepted packet must read through the displayed dyadic ledger, stream window,
regular rational readback, located comparison rows, located modulus row,
transport, replay, provenance, and local NameCert rows. The route supplies no
quotient Cauchy equality, selected limit, trichotomy, host order, or ambient
completeness.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with an accepted carrier and apply
\autoref{thm:located-cauchy-extraction-namecert-obligations}. The obligations
pin the regular rational readback to $Q$ over the displayed window $S$ and
dyadic ledger $D$.

The located comparison rows $L,U$ and the located-modulus row $M$ are the only
rows that can be exported before the seal $E$. Therefore every Real-facing
consumer reaches the seal by the displayed finite route and not by selecting a
limit outside the packet.

Transport, replay, provenance, and local naming are exactly $H,C,P,N$. Since
the refused objects are absent from the carrier coordinates, no downstream
consumer can recover quotient equality, trichotomy, host order, or ambient
completeness from the accepted packet.
\end{proof}

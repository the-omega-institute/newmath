\begin{definition}[RealBall finite-window carrier]
\label{def:realball-window-carrier}
A $\RealBallUp$ carrier is a finite $\mathsf{BHist}$ packet
$$
B=(c,r,W,P,L)
$$
with the following visible rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $c$ is a $\RealUp$ center name read through a scheduled $\RegSeqRat$ stream and its $\StreamNameUp$ finite-window requests;
\item $r$ is a positive dyadic-radius row from $\DyadicRatCoreUp$;
\item $W$ is a finite dyadic-window witness listing the displayed center approximants, the displayed radius row, and the $\DyadicIntervalUp$ interval rows that cover the visible portion of the ball;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance tying the carrier to $\RealUp$, $\RegSeqRat$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\DyadicRatCoreUp$, and $\DyadicIntervalUp$ source certificates;
\item $L$ is the ledger that hides only the unobserved tail beyond the displayed window and records that every public read is made through $W$ and $P$.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier predicate is $\mathsf{RealBallWindow}(c,r,W,P,L)$. Two accepted carriers classify together only when their center names, positive dyadic radii, visible interval rows, provenance rows, and tail ledgers transport by displayed $\hsame$ and by the same $\Cont$ routes named in $P$.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[RealBall radius transport]
\label{thm:realball-radius-transport}
Let $B=(c,r,W,P,L)$ be an accepted $\RealBallUp$ carrier, and let $B'=(c',r',W',P',L')$ be obtained by displayed $\hsame$ transport of the $\RealUp$ center row, the positive $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ radius row, the visible $\DyadicIntervalUp$ window rows, and the provenance and ledger rows. If the same $\Cont$ routes named by $P$ and $P'$ preserve the $\RegSeqRat$ and $\StreamNameUp$ reads of the center, then $B'$ is again an accepted $\RealBallUp$ carrier.

The transported classifier reads the same finite dyadic-radius neighborhood surface: it changes only the displayed representatives of $c$, $r$, $W$, $P$, and $L$, and it introduces no Dedekind cut, trichotomy row, completeness datum, host real ball, or open-cover topology.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.RealBallUp.RealBallWindowPacket\_radius\_transport}
\begin{proof}
Project the five coordinates of \autoref{def:realball-window-carrier}. The center coordinate is read through $\RealUp$, $\RegSeqRat$, and $\StreamNameUp$, so the stated $\hsame$ and $\Cont$ routes transport each displayed center approximant in the finite window.

Transport the positive dyadic-radius row through $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ and transport the displayed interval rows through $\DyadicIntervalUp$. The visible window witness $W'$ is obtained by repacking exactly these transported rows.

Finally transport the provenance row and the tail ledger. The ledger still hides only the unobserved tail beyond the displayed window, so the accepted packet has the same classifier surface and no additional topological or completeness coordinate.
\end{proof}

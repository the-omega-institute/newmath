\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\BoundedSetUp$}

Following Hausdorff--Kelley metric-space practice and Bishop--Bridges constructive analysis, the $\BoundedSetUp$ packet records bounded subset data as a finite NameCert surface rather than as an ambient host-set fact. It sits over the metric carrier of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metric-namecert}, the algebraic and order-facing real rows of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-alg-order-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}, the ball-containment ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metricball-namecert}, and the consumer routes of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-metric-diameter-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-uniformspace-namecert}. The packet exposes exactly the center, radius, subset classifier, and ball-containment evidence needed by diameter, finite-net, precompactness, and completion-facing arguments.

\begin{definition}[BoundedSet carrier]
\label{def:bounded-set-carrier}
A $\BoundedSetUp$ carrier is a finite $\BHist$ packet
$$
Q=(X,S,c,r,B,H,C,P,N)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $X$ is the $\MetricUp$ carrier whose distance rows are the only metric data available to the packet.
\item $S$ is the bounded subset classifier row. It records accepted membership reads as finite classifier entries over $X$.
\item $c$ is the displayed center row, or the displayed witness row naming the local center used by the boundedness certificate.
\item $r$ is the $\RealUp$ or rational bound row read through the $\RealAlgOrderUp$ comparison surface.
\item $B$ is the $\MetricBallUp$ containment ledger asserting that every accepted membership read of $S$ is routed through the displayed ball with center $c$ and bound $r$.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the metric carrier, subset classifier, center or witness, real or rational bound, ball-containment ledger, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.
\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a membership request through $X,S,c,r,B,H,P,N$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\BoundedSetUp$, $\MetricUp$, $\RealAlgOrderUp$, $\RealUp$, $\MetricBallUp$, $\MetricDiameterUp$, $\UniformSpaceUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\BoundedSetUp}$ row.
\end{enumerate}
The classifier compares accepted packets only through $X,S,c,r,B,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for an ambient host set, a supremum row, compactness, open-cover compactness, a selected maximum, trichotomy, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[BoundedSet NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:bounded-set-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\BoundedSetUp$ carrier $Q=(X,S,c,r,B,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\BoundedSetUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the $\MetricUp$ carrier, bounded subset classifier, displayed center or witness, $\RealUp$ or rational bound read through $\RealAlgOrderUp$, $\MetricBallUp$ containment ledger, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item subset classifier stability, requiring membership reads of $S$ to be preserved componentwise by the displayed transport row $H$;
\item ball containment, requiring each accepted membership read to pass through the $\MetricBallUp$ ledger $B$ with center $c$ and bound $r$;
\item real-bound nonescape, requiring the bound row $r$ to be read only through the displayed $\RealAlgOrderUp$ comparison surface and not as a host supremum, selected maximum, trichotomy proof, or completeness theorem;
\item transport and replay stability for the same $X,S,c,r,B,P,N$ route under $\hsame$ transport and $\Cont$ replay.
\end{enumerate}
No BoundedSet NameCert obligation is stored outside these rows.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier of \autoref{def:bounded-set-carrier}. The only analytic rows are the metric carrier $X$, subset classifier $S$, displayed center or witness $c$, bound row $r$, and ball-containment ledger $B$.

Carrier admission is therefore rowwise. A membership request first names the metric carrier and classifier, then reads the center or witness and the bound comparison row before any containment claim is available.

Subset classifier stability and transport stability are forced by the single transport row $H$. Since $H$ moves only the listed coordinates, it cannot replace the displayed classifier by a host subset or by an unrecorded predicate.

Ball containment is forced by $B$. The continuation row $C$ replays membership reads through $X,S,c,r,B$, so a downstream consumer sees the $\MetricBallUp$ containment route before it can use boundedness.

Finally, $r$ is a displayed $\RealUp$ or rational bound read through $\RealAlgOrderUp$. Because the carrier contains no coordinate for a supremum, selected maximum, trichotomy proof, compactness principle, open-cover argument, or ambient Real completeness, none of those objects can become a local NameCert obligation.
\end{proof}

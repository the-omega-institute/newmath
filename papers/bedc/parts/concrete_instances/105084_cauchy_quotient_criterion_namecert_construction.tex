\begin{theorem}[Cauchy quotient criterion NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:cauchy-quotient-criterion-namecert-obligations}

\begin{proof}

The consumer-facing rows are downstream. The handoff $R$ is available only after the product residual has passed through $W,D,P,K$, and the seal row $E$ is available only after $R$ has been displayed. The rows $H,C,L,N$ transport, replay, source, and name this same route.

\begin{definition}[ObservationFiberDigest carrier]
\label{def:observation-fiber-digest-carrier}
An $\ObservationFiberDigestUp$ carrier is a finite packet
$$
O=(D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H,C)
$$
with the following displayed rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item $D$ is the local visible digest row, naming the classifier-facing digest token available to a particular observer state;
\item $F$ is the observation-fiber membership row, recording the generated relation that binds the visible digest to compatible observation histories without identifying them;
\item $G$ is the $\mathsf{GAP}$ ledger row for missing source supply, far-end socketing, and unavailable exactness data;
\item $S$ is the signature provenance row, recording the $\mathsf{Sig}$ or classifier route that made the digest visible;
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\DigestProvenancePacketUp$, $\ApophaticNameUp$, $\DigestFiberLedgerUp$, observation histories, signatures, GAP rows, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$ rows;
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\ObservationFiberDigestUp}$ row;
\item $T$ is the far-end status row, recording whether the observation fiber terminates in a refused, socketed, or locally exact boundary;
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the digest, fiber, GAP, signature, package, local naming, far-end status, and replay rows;
\item $C$ records the displayed $\Cont$ replay routes by which accepted consumers inspect $D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H$.
\end{enumerate}
The carrier has no coordinate for digest-as-source equality, a global universe digest, object-level $\mathsf{T}$, quotient fiber equality, hidden source equality, host preimage recovery, or observer-independent totalization.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[ObservationFiberDigest NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:observation-fiber-digest-namecert-obligations}
For an accepted $\ObservationFiberDigestUp$ packet
$$
O=(D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H,C),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\ObservationFiberDigestUp}$ surface consists exactly of carrier admission for the nine displayed rows, visible digest exposure through $D$, observation-fiber membership through $F$, GAP ledger exposure through $G$, signature provenance through $S$, package provenance through $P$, local naming through $N$, far-end status through $T$, componentwise transport through $H$, replay locality through $C$, and non-escape from digest-as-source equality, global universe digest, object-level $\mathsf{T}$, quotient fiber equality, and hidden source equality.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the carrier of \autoref{def:observation-fiber-digest-carrier}. Its visible inventory is exactly $D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H,C$, so carrier admission and local naming attach to those rows and to no host digest function or hidden observation substrate.

The digest boundary is read first through $D$. The observation fiber is not the same datum: it is the generated membership row $F$, and source-sensitive reads must keep $F$ visible before the digest can be cited as an observation boundary.

The rows $G,S,P,N,T$ then provide the finite obligation surface. The GAP row records missing supply and unavailable exactness; the signature row records how the digest became visible; the package row records the sibling route through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-digestprovenancepacket-namecert}, \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-apophaticname-namecert}, and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-digestfiberledger-namecert}; the local NameCert row records the packet name; and the far-end row records whether the fiber boundary is refused, socketed, or locally exact.

Finally $H$ and $C$ preserve the same finite packet under transport and replay. Since no displayed row is a source payload, a universe digest, an object-level truth value, a quotient representative, or a hidden equality witness, the listed non-escape obligations exhaust the local NameCert surface.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[ObservationFiberDigest non-escape]
\label{thm:observation-fiber-digest-nonescape}
Every accepted $\ObservationFiberDigestUp$ consumer that uses the digest as an observation boundary must factor through the displayed rows
$$
D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H,C.
$$
The consumer obtains a local visible digest with fiber, ledger, signature, package, naming, far-end, transport, and replay evidence, but cannot obtain digest-as-source equality, a global universe digest, object-level $\mathsf{T}$, quotient fiber equality, hidden source equality, host preimage recovery, or observer-independent totalization from the packet.
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Start with an accepted packet $O=(D,F,G,S,P,N,T,H,C)$. A token-only read of $D$ obtains only the visible digest row, because \autoref{def:observation-fiber-digest-carrier} gives no source coordinate behind $D$.

If the consumer uses the digest as an observation boundary, the read must open the observation-fiber membership row $F$. Without $F$, the digest is merely a visible classifier-facing token and has no packet-local authority to delimit compatible observation histories.

Next the read must pass through $G,S,P,N,T$. These rows expose the GAP ledger, signature provenance, package provenance, local certificate, and far-end status. In particular, $T$ keeps the apophatic boundary visible rather than replacing it with an internal object.

The remaining rows $H$ and $C$ transport and replay the same packet. They do not add a preimage oracle, quotient collapse, hidden source equality, global universe digest, or object-level $\mathsf{T}$ coordinate. Therefore every accepted boundary use is confined to the displayed observation-fiber digest packet.
\end{proof}

\begin{theorem}[Baire ultrametric prefix stability]
\label{thm:baire-ultrametric-prefix-stability}

\begin{proof}

The structural rows $P,N$ and $P',N'$ provide provenance and local naming only for the already transported route. Since no excluded branch, quotient, choice, host equality, or ambient Real-completeness coordinate is present, both carriers determine the same finite common-prefix ultrametric surface.

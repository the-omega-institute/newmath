It sits over the Dini convergence packet of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dini-theorem-namecert}, the extracted uniform-modulus ledger of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-dini-uniform-limit-modulus-namecert}, the lower and upper semicontinuity boundaries of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-lowersemicontinuous-namecert} and \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-uppersemicontinuous-namecert}, and the located monotone-convergence route of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-located-monotone-convergence-namecert}. It also reads finite stream windows through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-streamname-namecert}, regular-rational tolerances through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-regseqrat-namecert}, and the terminal real boundary through \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-real-namecert}. The packet names only the finite route from displayed monotone data to a uniform-modulus handoff; it does not import open-cover compactness, countable choice, quotient real equality, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.

\begin{definition}[Dini uniform-monotone carrier]\label{def:dini-uniform-monotone-carrier}

$$

\item $M$ is the accepted $\DiniUniformLimitModulusUp$ row, exposing the finite net, rational tolerance budget, extracted uniform-modulus row, and uniform-limit handoff consumed by later rows.
\item $L$ is the $\LocatedMonotoneConvergenceUp$ handoff row, recording the located supremum route used by the monotone tail read before any real seal is exposed.
\item $S^{-}$ and $S^{+}$ are the $\LowerSemicontinuousUp$ and $\UpperSemicontinuousUp$ boundary rows, storing the displayed lower and upper semicontinuity checks at the finite window boundary.
\item $G$ is the monotone-window ledger, listing the inspected family indices, pointwise comparison rows, semicontinuity boundary rows, and the finite handoff from $D$ to $M$ and $L$.

\item $C$ records displayed $\Cont$ replay from a uniform-modulus request through $D,M,L,S^{-},S^{+},G,W,Q,E,R,H,P,N$.
\item $P$ is the $\Pkg$ provenance over $\DiniUniformMonotoneUp$, $\DiniTheoremUp$, $\DiniUniformLimitModulusUp$, $\LowerSemicontinuousUp$, $\UpperSemicontinuousUp$, $\LocatedMonotoneConvergenceUp$, $\StreamNameUp$, $\RegSeqRatUp$, $\RealUp$, $\BHist$, $\hsame$, $\Cont$, and $\NameCert$ rows.
\item $N$ is the local $\NameCert_{\DiniUniformMonotoneUp}$ row.

The classifier compares accepted packets only through $D,M,L,S^{-},S^{+},G,W,Q,R,E,H,C,P,N$. It has no coordinate for open-cover compactness, countable choice, quotient real equality, hidden selected limits, detached real representatives, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{definition}

\begin{theorem}[Dini uniform-monotone NameCert obligations]\label{thm:dini-uniform-monotone-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\DiniUniformMonotoneUp$ carrier
$$
U=(D,M,L,S^{-},S^{+},G,W,Q,R,E,H,C,P,N),
$$
the local $\NameCert_{\DiniUniformMonotoneUp}$ surface consists exactly of the following finite obligation rows:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the Dini finite-window row, extracted uniform-modulus row, located-monotone handoff, lower and upper semicontinuity boundary rows, monotone-window ledger, stream-window row, regular-rational tolerance row, Real seal, route row, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming;
\item monotone-window ledger admission, requiring every family index, comparison row, and tail request used by a consumer to occur in $G$ and to be read from $D,M,L$;
\item compact finite-modulus handoff, requiring the uniform-modulus row $M$ and the route row $E$ to mediate every tolerance request before any $\RealUp$ seal is exposed;
\item semicontinuity boundary admission, requiring $S^{-}$ and $S^{+}$ to record the lower and upper boundary checks attached to the same finite windows named by $G$ and $W$;
\item located-monotone convergence routing, requiring the handoff row $L$ to expose the located supremum route used for the monotone tail read;
\item transport and replay stability through $H$ and $C$, preserving the displayed rows $D,M,L,S^{-},S^{+},G,W,Q,E,R$ componentwise;
\item provenance and local naming through $P$ and $N$, with no obligation allowed to call on open-cover compactness, countable choice, quotient real equality, detached representatives, or ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Project the accepted carrier. Carrier admission exposes exactly the rows $D,M,L,S^{-},S^{+},G,W,Q,R,E,H,C,P,N$, so each obligation is read from a displayed coordinate of \autoref{def:dini-uniform-monotone-carrier}.

The monotone-window obligations are local to $D,M,L$ and $G$. The Dini row supplies the finite monotone convergence surface, the uniform-modulus row supplies the extracted finite-modulus handoff, the located-monotone row supplies the tail route, and $G$ records the inspected indices and comparisons.

The boundary obligations are local to $S^{-},S^{+},W,Q,E,R$. The lower and upper semicontinuity rows are attached to the same stream windows and regular-rational tolerances, while $E$ records the route that must be traversed before the terminal $\RealUp$ row $R$ can be read.

Finally, $H$ and $C$ transport and replay only the displayed rows, and $P,N$ source and name the same packet. Since the excluded compactness, choice, quotient, representative, and completeness data are absent from the carrier, they cannot occur among the local NameCert obligations.
\end{proof}

The structural rows $H,C,P,N$ preserve, replay, source, and name this same route. Since no alternative compactness, choice, quotient, selected-limit, or completeness coordinate is present, every accepted consumer factors through the displayed route before the Real seal.
\end{proof}

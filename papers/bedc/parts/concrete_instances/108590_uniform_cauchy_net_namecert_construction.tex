\begin{theorem}[UniformCauchyNet NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:uniform-cauchy-net-namecert-obligations}
For every accepted $\UniformCauchyNetUp$ carrier $U=(D,E,W,R,S,H,C,P,N)$, the local $\NameCert_{\UniformCauchyNetUp}$ surface consists exactly of five finite obligations:
\begin{enumerate}
\item carrier admission for the directed-index row, uniform entourage ledger, finite stream windows, regular rational readback, Real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local naming rows;
\item directed-tail stability, requiring cofinal tail comparisons to be read from $D$ before any entourage claim in $E$ is exposed;
\item uniform tolerance exactness, requiring every inspected entourage request to pass through the finite ledger $E$ and the corresponding $\StreamNameUp$ windows $W$;
\item completion-facing readback, requiring the regular rational row $R$ and the $\RealUp$ seal $S$ to be reached only through the displayed directed, entourage, and window route;
\item non-escape for quotient nets, selected limits, countable choice, open-cover compactness, hidden cofinal schedules, and ambient $\RealUp$ completeness.
\end{enumerate}
\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}
Carrier admission is projection from \autoref{def:uniform-cauchy-net-carrier}. The tuple displays the directed index data, entourage ledger, window family, regular readback, Real seal, transport, replay, provenance, and local name.

Directed-tail stability is confined to $D$. A consumer first reads the finite Moore--Smith comparison rows that justify the inspected tail, so the later entourage rows cannot be treated as an unindexed uniform assertion.

Uniform tolerance exactness is the role of $E$ and $W$. Each entourage request is paired with the displayed finite windows that witness the inspected tail, and no hidden cofinal schedule is available outside those rows.

The completion-facing readback then passes through $R$ and $S$. Since $R$ is obtained only after the directed and window data are displayed, and $S$ is the terminal seal for that route, the packet exports a finite uniform-net handoff rather than a selected completion point.

Non-escape follows by row exhaustion. Quotient nets, selected limits, countable choice, open-cover compactness, hidden cofinal schedules, and ambient $\RealUp$ completeness are absent from the carrier, so the local NameCert surface cannot export them.
\end{proof}

Together they force the selected precision read through the compact finite-net spine, pointwise-modulus rows, positive rational ledger, finite fold, and route rows before it reaches $\UniformModulusUp$.

Finally apply \autoref{thm:compact-net-modulus-selector-consumer-nonescape}. \end{proof}

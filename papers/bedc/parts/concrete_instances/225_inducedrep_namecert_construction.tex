\chapter{A Concrete Naming Certificate for $\InducedRepUp$}
\label{ch:concrete-instances-inducedrep-namecert}

The induced-representation interface $\InducedRepUp$ records a finite induction--restriction packet with consumed $\SubgroupUp$ and $\RepresentationRingUp$ certificate rows, Frobenius rows, classifier rows, continuation ledgers, and package provenance. The induction-restriction packet consumes the subgroup carrier of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-subgroup-namecert} and the character-ledger surface of \autoref{ch:concrete-instances-representationring-namecert} before the local Frobenius rows are named.

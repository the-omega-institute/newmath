\begin{theorem}[MetaCIC normalization frontier ground-compiler route]
\label{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-ground-compiler-route}
Every accepted $\MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp$ candidate-evidence boundary routes its TasteGate-facing conservativity and no-hidden-input obligations through the displayed ground-compiler recognition rows before any normalization boundary is exported. Concretely, a consumer of
$$
F=(C,K,E,N,S,H,R,P,L)
$$
must first read the carrier surface of \autoref{def:metacic-normalization-frontier-carrier}, then the candidate-evidence route of \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-candidate-evidence-totality}, and then the root normal-form boundary of \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-normal-form-boundary}. Only after those rows are visible may the consumer use the TasteGate row certified at the core surface, and that use exports no hidden normalizer, host normalization theorem, erased subject-reduction transport, or quotient-level term equality.
\end{theorem}
\leanchecked{BEDC.Derived.MetaCICNormalizationFrontierUp.MetaCICNormalizationFrontierGroundCompilerRoute}
\begin{proof}
Project the core carrier. The rows $C,K,E,N,S,H,R,P,L$ are the only accepted frontier coordinates, and the core surface records the TasteGate-facing packet through the same finite carrier rather than through an external normalization oracle.

Next read the candidate-evidence boundary. The route through $C,K$ is fixed by \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-candidate-evidence-totality}, while \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-seal-boundary} keeps the seal and audit-facing rows visible before closed-normal use.

The root normal-form boundary then forces finished-normal reads through $E,N$ with $S,H,R,P,L$ still displayed. Thus the conservativity and no-hidden-input obligations face the ground-compiler recognition rows before normalization is exported.

Finally apply the non-escape rows \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-normal-route-nonescape} and \autoref{thm:metacic-normalization-frontier-substitution-stability}. They exclude hidden normalizers, host normalization, erased subject-reduction transport, and quotient term equality, so the TasteGate-facing route remains a ground-compiler route over the displayed packet.
\end{proof}

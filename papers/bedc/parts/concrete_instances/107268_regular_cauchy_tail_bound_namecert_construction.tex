\label{def:regular-cauchy-tail-bound-carrier}

$$

\item $R$ is the $\RegSeqRatUp$ readback row, repacking those windows as regular rational Cauchy data.
\item $D$ is the $\DyadicRatCoreUp$ radius ledger, recording the dyadic tolerance and comparison rows used by the tail estimate.
\item $N$ is the unary tail-index row, naming the finite stage after which the displayed radius is to be enforced.
\item $T_0$ is the later-index comparison row, recording the finite comparisons that put every displayed later observation beyond $N$ and inside the dyadic radius from $D$.
\item $H$ records componentwise $\hsame$ transport for the stream, regular readback, dyadic radius, tail index, later-index comparison, replay, provenance, and local naming rows.

\begin{theorem}[Regular Cauchy tail-bound NameCert obligations]
\label{thm:regular-cauchy-tail-bound-namecert-obligations}

$$

\end{theorem}
\begin{proof}

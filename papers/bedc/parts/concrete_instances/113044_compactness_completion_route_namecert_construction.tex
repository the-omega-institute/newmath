\label{def:compactness-completion-route-carrier}

$$

\begin{enumerate}
\item $M$ is the $\CompactMetricUp$ source row, exposing only the finite metric observations and compact-source identifiers read by the packet.
\item $T$ is the $\TotallyBoundedUp$ finite-net coverage row, or equivalently the finite net schedule attached to the displayed compact metric source.
\item $C$ is the $\CompleteMetricUp$ or Cauchy-completion boundary row that records where completion-facing Cauchy requests may land after the finite-net route has been read.
\item $W$ is the $\StreamNameUp$ window row whose displayed observations are generated from the finite-net coverage schedule.

\section{Finite theorem target}

The finite proof target consists of the following named results:
\begin{enumerate}[label=\textbf{T\arabic*.}]
\item \(\MSame\) reflexivity, symmetry, transitivity.
\item mark no-confusion.
\item \(\HSame\) reflexivity, symmetry, transitivity.
\item history no-confusion.
\item \(\Ext\) determinacy and sameness-respect.
\item \(\Cont\) determinacy.
\item continuation left and right units.
\item continuation associativity, relational form.
\item signature determinacy under \(\mathsf{AskPolicy}\).
\item \(\SameSig\) equivalence under \(\mathsf{AskPolicy}\).
\item gap coverage under the three policies.
\item gap separation under the three policies.
\item composite gap coverage.
\item certificate admission for derived interfaces.
\item unary history and unary continuation interface seeds.
\end{enumerate}

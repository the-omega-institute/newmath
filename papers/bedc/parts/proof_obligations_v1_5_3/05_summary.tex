\chapter{v1.5.3 Summary}
\label{ch:v153-summary}

The v1.5.3 increment is a local proof patch. It does not add new derived-object theory. It makes two hidden proof requirements explicit and proves their low-risk consequences at paper level.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Package reflection now depends on \(\mathsf{TokUnique}(\Pi)\), the uniqueness of token introduction up to \(\hsame\).
  \item Concrete signature Globalize exactness is stated only under explicit domain, ask, token, package, and gap policies.
  \item Unary right-shift is decomposed into base and step lemmas, and unary continuation commutativity is proved from them.
  \item \(\AddUp\) remains a conditional license, but the needed commutativity certificate now has a precise proof spine.
\end{enumerate}

The next step should not be another broad theory extension. It should be a local machine-checking sprint for token uniqueness, unary right-shift, and concrete gap exactness.

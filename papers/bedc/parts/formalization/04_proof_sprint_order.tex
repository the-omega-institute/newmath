\section{Proof-sprint order}

The recommended order:
\begin{enumerate}
\item prove all \texttt{MSame} theorems;
\item prove all \texttt{HSame} theorems and no-confusion lemmas;
\item prove \texttt{Ext} determinacy and sameness-respect;
\item prove \texttt{Cont} determinacy, units, and associativity witness;
\item define bundles and signature generation;
\item prove signature determinacy under deterministic ask;
\item prove \texttt{SameSig} equivalence;
\item define package and gap policies;
\item prove gap coverage and separation;
\item prove composite gap coverage.
\end{enumerate}

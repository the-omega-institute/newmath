\chapter{Project Governance}
\label{ch:project-governance}

This paper is the active source of truth for the BEDC theory. Future development amends the paper in place; see Chapter~\ref{ch:theory-amendment-policy} for the amendment policy and \texttt{HOW\_INCREMENT\_WORKS.md} for the companion editing recipe.

The repository remains intentionally small: one paper tree and one Lean tree. Chapter-level and section-level claims are tracked in \texttt{claim-registry.md}. Lean-facing theorem status is rendered by the \texttt{\textbackslash leanchecked} family of macros and aligned with \texttt{correspondence.md}.

\begin{remark}
The master PDF should remain the public artifact. The source tree should remain the authoritative editable artifact.
\end{remark}
